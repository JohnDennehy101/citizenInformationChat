Leaving A Gift In Your Will

Donating to the Irish Kidney Association is a meaningful and enduring act of generosity. Your gift will continue to benefit renal patients and their families and carers, living with and affected by end-stage renal disease throughout Ireland, ensuring your legacy creates positive change for years to come.

We have teamed up with FreeWill.ie to provide you with a free online service to create or update your will using their
will drafting tool.
