Welcome to the Pensions Authority

The Pensions Authority regulates occupational pension schemes, trust retirement annuity contracts (RACs) and personal retirement savings accounts (PRSAs).

Our website provides you with access to a comprehensive range of information and guidance material to help you understand pensions. The information is organised under five separate personas to assist you access the most relevant information for you.

Our information and guidance help those involved in managing pensions, such as trustees, administrators, employers and advisers, understand their obligations under the Pensions Act and how to meet them.

If you can’t find the information you are looking for or have a specific question, please email our enquiry service at info@pensionsauthority.ie
