Doras is an independent organisation working to promote and protect the rights of people from a migrant background in Ireland.

Direct Support

Doras provides direct support to international protection applicants, refugees and migrants who need assistance accessing their rights and entitlements. We do this primarily at our headquarters in Limerick.

Learn more
Know your rights

Doras centres its work on human rights, seeking to promote and uphold the rights of all migrants and refugees. It does this through direct support and a dedicated commitment to driving systemic change.

Learn more
Programmes

In each programme, we aim to support underserved communities. We work to create lasting change to policies, perceptions and power structures that adversely affect communities through specific projects.
