The Impact of Your Donation

Your generous donation will help us save the lives of so many animals who desperately need us.

Your donation contributes to and helps support:

  • Our rescue service
  • Immediate and ongoing veterinary care for each animal we care for.
  • Spay/Neuter Programmes
  • Veterinary Equipment and Supplies
  • Our Education and Outreach Programmes
