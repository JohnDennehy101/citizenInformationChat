We offer a free, nationwide support service, dedicated to international visitors who experience a crime or other distressing incidents – available 7 days a week

How can we help?

Assisting a visitor? Select an option below:

Choose an option:
  • I work in tourism/hospitality
  • I am a member of the Garda Siochána
  • I am embassy staff

How can we help you?

We provide support and information to help you overcome the practical problems that result from a crime or other distressing incident, so you can put it behind you as quickly as possible, and get back to enjoying your holiday. Choose an option below

Were you the victim of a crime?

A safe environment to talk about your experience, help you report it to police, and help resolve practical problems.

Missing cash or bank/credit cards?

We can assist in contacting banks and credit card companies and organising money transfers

Missing passport/identity cards?

We can help you contact embassies and local police, to get replacement documents quickly

Do you require help with travel as a result of an incident?

We can help you make new travel arrangements with airlines, ferries and local transport services

Do you require medical assistance ?

We provide guidance and information on where to receive the medical care you may need

Have you had an accident – for example, a traffic accident, a fall?

We can assist you to get the support you need to resolve the problems you might have

Need help with a bereavement?

We can help during such a difficult time, with the practical problems that you may face

Do you require emergency accommodation assistance?

In some limited circumstances, we may be able to arrange short-term accommodation while you’re waiting for a money transfer
