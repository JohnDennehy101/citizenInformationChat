ABOUT US

The Central Credit Register is established by the Central Bank of Ireland under the Credit Reporting Act 2013 (as amended). See the background to the Central Credit Register and the legal framework.        

ABOUT

APPLY FOR A CREDIT REPORT

You can apply for your credit report online and you will need to complete an application form and provide three types of identification documents:

1. photo with signature (such as passport or driving licence)
2. proof of your address (must be no more than 6 months old)
3. proof of your PPSN
