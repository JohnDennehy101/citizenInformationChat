Our Work

Fostering Children’s Rights

We believe children, young people, and their guardians/representatives need to be aware of children’s rights in order to be able to use them.

Children's Rights in Laws, Policies and Practice

We monitor national and international developments and respond to serious threats to children’s rights.

Building Children's Futures

Funded by the European Commission, this Project aims to understand the impact of Covid-19 on services for children and young people.

Reducing Child Poverty and Social Exclusion

We are running a multi-year campaign to end child poverty in Ireland and advocate for improved outcomes for children and young people.

Educational Reform

We work to ensure the best interests of children and young people are at the heart of education reform.
