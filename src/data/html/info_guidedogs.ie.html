Our highly trained dogs positively impact so many lives every day

Guide Dogs

Our dogs help people who are vision impaired achieve greater independence and mobility.

Assistance Dogs

Our Assistance Dogs support a child and their family, helping to control and improve behaviour.

Independent Living Skills

Our Skills Programmes grant independence to carry out everyday tasks!

Community Dog

Our Community Dogs are specially trained to provide support in places like schools.

Orientation & Mobility

Supports our Guide Dog owners to help maintain the skills needed to remain independent.

Advocacy

Our Advocacy services ensure our Clients find their voice around areas such as rights, law, policies and responsibilities.
