#  New on Citizens Information 04-09-2023

4 September 2023

#####  Site updates

New [ child protection procedures in schools
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-
education/attendance-and-discipline-in-schools/child-protection-in-school/)
are effective from 1 September 2023. Information given to a school board must
not disclose the identity of the child or the person that the allegations are
being made against.

The [ free contraception scheme
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-
services/reproductive_health/contraception/) now includes women aged 17 to 30
(previously only available up to age 26).

[ The Electoral Commission ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/government-
in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/electoral-commission/) has published
its review of Ireland’s electoral constituencies. It recommends increases to
constituencies and TDs with 174 TDs representing 43 [ Dáil constituencies
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/government-in-ireland/houses-of-the-
oireachtas/dail-eireann/) , up from the current 160 TDs in 39 constituencies.
If accepted, the changes will come into effect before the next [ general
election ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/government-in-
ireland/elections-and-referenda/types-of-elections-and-referendums/the-
general-election/) .
