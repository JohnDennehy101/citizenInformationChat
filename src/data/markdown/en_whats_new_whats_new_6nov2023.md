#  What's New

6 November 2023

#####  Site updates

From 1 November 2023, new offences and increased sentences have been
introduced under the [ Criminal Justice (Miscellaneous Provisions) Act 2023
](https://www.gov.ie/en/press-release/7bce1-new-criminal-offences-and-tougher-
sentences-take-effect-from-today-minister-mcentee-announces/) .

New offences include:

  * Stalking or non-fatal strangulation (10 year maximum sentence) 
  * Non-fatal strangulation causing serious harm (maximum sentence of life in prison) 

Certain [ maximum prison sentences
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/justice/criminal-law/criminal-
offences/public-order-offenses-in-ireland/) have increased including:

  * 7 years to 12 years for assaulting an on-duty Garda or emergency service worker 
  * 5 years to 10 years for assault causing harm 
  * 10 years to life in prison for conspiracy to murder
