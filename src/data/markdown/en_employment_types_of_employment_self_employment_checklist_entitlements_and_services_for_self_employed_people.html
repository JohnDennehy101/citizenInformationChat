for self-employed people * Introduction ## Introduction If you are self-employed and have had a downturn in business and a reduction in your income you can use the links and lists below to check out your entitlements. Setting up a business | [ Becoming self-employed ](../../../../employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/self-employment-as-an-individual/) and [ Setting up a business ](../../../../employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/setting-up-a-business-in-ireland/) give you a broad overview of the factors involved in setting up a business, including information on types of self-employment, tax, PRSI, legal issues and sources of support. ---|--- Supports for unemployed people | [ Back to Work Enterprise Allowance ](../../../../social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/social-welfare-payments-and-work/back-to-work-enterprise-allowance/) [ Short-term Enterprise Allowance ](../../../../social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/social-welfare-payments-and-work/short-term-enterprise-allowance/) Problems with self-employment | If you are no longer earning enough from self-employment, or your [ business has to close down ](../../../../employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/closing-or-selling-a-business-in-ireland/) , you may [ qualify for a social welfare payment ](../../../../social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/unemployed-people/self-employed-and-unemployment/) . You **do not need to deregister as self-employed** to get a payment. [ Getting a social welfare payment ](../../../../social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment-1/) | [ Jobseeker's Benefit (Self-Employed) ](../../../../social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/unemployed-people/jobseekers-benefit-self-employed/) (for people with sufficient PRSI contributions) [ Jobseeker's Allowance ](../../../../social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/unemployed-people/jobseekers-allowance/) [ Claiming for an adult dependant ](../../../../social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/claiming-an-increase-in-your-social-welfare-payment-for-an-adult-dependant/) [ Claiming for a child dependant ](../../../../social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/claiming-and-increase-in-your-payment-for-a-child-dependant/) [ Signing on for the first time ](../../../../social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/unemployed-people/signing-on/) [ Supplementary Welfare Allowance Scheme ](../../../../social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/supplementary-welfare-schemes/supplementary-welfare-allowance-scheme/) | [ Additional Needs Payments ](../../../../social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/supplementary-welfare-schemes/additional-needs-payment/) Maintaining your social insurance record | [ Becoming a voluntary contributor ](../../../../social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-insurance-prsi/voluntarysocialinsurancecontributions/) [ Signing on for credits (in some cases self-employed people may be able to sign for credited PRSI contributions) ](../../../../social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-insurance-prsi/credited-social-insurance-contributions/) Getting credit | [ Microfinanceireland.ie ](http://www.microfinanceireland.ie/) is a website for small businesses looking for information about financing or funding their business. If you have a small or medium business and your application for credit is refused you may be able to [ apply to the Credit Review Office ](https://www.creditreview.ie/review-process/) to have your case reviewed. Checking your employment status | [ Code of Practice for determining Employment or Self- Employment status of Individuals (pdf) ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/tax-professionals/codes-of-practice/index.aspx) [ Revenue Guidelines for Determining Employment Status for Taxation Purposes (pdf) ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/tax-professionals/tdm/income-tax-capital-gains-tax-corporation-tax/part-05/05-01-30.pdf) [ Understanding your employment status ](../../../../employment/types-of-employment/understanding-your-employment-status/) [ Education and training ](../../../../education/) | [ Back to Education Programme ](../../../../social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/back-to-education/back-to-education-programme/) [ Back to Education Allowance ](../../../../social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/back-to-education/back-to-education-allowance/) [ Vocational Training Opportunities Scheme ](../../../../education/returning-to-education/vocational-training-opportunities-scheme/) Employment support schemes | There are a [ number of schemes ](../../../../employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/employment-support-schemes/schemes-to-support-employment/) to support people who are unemployed and want to get back to work.
