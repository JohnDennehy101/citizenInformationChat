#  Springboard now open for applications

28 June 2024

#####  News

You can now apply for free Springboard+ 2024-2025 courses. 👨‍🎓

If you’re unemployed, you

  * Must be looking and available for work. 🧑‍💼 
  * Can keep your existing social welfare payments 💸 

If you’re working, Level 6 courses are free.  
🧑‍💼
