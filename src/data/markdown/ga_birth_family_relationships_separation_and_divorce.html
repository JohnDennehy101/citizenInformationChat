Mura féidir leat teacht ar chomhaontú, conas iarratas a dhéanamh ar ordú cothabhála. * [ How pensions are assessed during a separation, divorce and dissolution ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/separation-and-divorce/sep-divorce-pensions/) When you end a relationship by judicial separation, divorce or dissolution, the court can decide to share your pension with your former partner and (or) any dependent children. This is called a pension adjustment order. * [ Cánacha Caipitiúla tar éis scartha/colscartha ](/ga/money-and-tax/tax/capital-taxes/capital-taxes-following-separation-or-divorce/) Cur síos gairid ar an tionchar a bhíonn ag scaradh/colscaradh ar chánacha Caipitiúla - cáin ar ghnóthacháin caipitiúla, cáin ar fháltais caipitiúla agus stampdhleacht. **This document is in:** [ Cánacha Caipitil ](/ga/money-and-tax/tax/capital- taxes/) ### Ábhar is mó éilimh i "Scaradh, colscaradh agus díscaoileadh" 1.
