Cabhair airgeadais le dul ar scoil # Cabhair airgeadais le dul ar scoil * cabhair a thabhairt do chostais scoile * Liúntas Éadaí agus Coisbhirt don Scoilbhliain Nua * Free schoolbooks * Táillí scrúdaithe * Iompar scoile * Riachtanais speisialta agus míchumas fisiceach * Tuilleadh faisnéise ## cabhair a thabhairt do chostais scoile B’fhéidir go bhfaighidh tú cabhair éigin leis an gcostas a bhaineann le do leanbh dul ar scoil. Ag brath ar do chás, is féidir leat tacaíocht airgeadais a fháil le haghaidh: * Éadaí scoile (éide scoile san áireamh) agus bróga scoile * Leabhair scoile * Táillí scrúdaithe * Táillí iompair scoile * Teicneolaíocht chúnta ## Liúntas Éadaí agus Coisbhirt don Scoilbhliain Nua Is féidir leis an [ Liúntas Éadaí agus Coisbhirt don Scoilbhliain Nua ](/ga/social-welfare/families-and-children/liuntas-eadai-agus-coisbhirt-don- scoilbhliain-nua/) cabhair a thabhairt do theaghlaigh costais éide scoile agus coisbhirt a íoc. Chun an Liúntas Éadaí agus Coisbhirt don Scoilbhliain Nua a fháil, ní mór d’ioncam teaghlaigh a bheith faoi bhun méid áirithe, agus ní mór duit a bheith: * Ag fáil íocaíocht leasa shóisialaigh áirithe, nó * Ag glacadh páirt i scéimeanna ceadaithe oideachais, oiliúna nó fostaíochta See a [ list of qualifying payments and schemes ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/86a6ba-operational-guidelines-back-to- school-clothing-and-footwear-scheme/#annex-a) on Gov.ie. Caithfidh do leanbh cáiliú don scéim freisin. Léigh tuilleadh faoi na daoine [ ar féidir leo an Liúntas Éadaí agus Coisbhirt don Scoilbhliain Nua a fháil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social- welfare/social-welfare-payments/families-and-children/back-to-school-clothing- and-footwear-allowance/#bb1587) . ## Free schoolbooks ### **Free primary schoolbooks** Since September 2023, parents of children in primary school and special schools do not have to pay for [ schoolbooks, workbooks and copybooks (pdf) ](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/250812/c0e12161-a64b-4dce-88d8-c7e6a6ed4a68.pdf#page=null) . Soláthraíonn an scoil na leabhair in aisce ar iasacht. Caithfidh tú na leabhair a thabhairt ar ais ag deireadh na scoilbhliana, nó má fhágann do leanbh an scoil. Ba chóir duit a chinntiú go gcoimeádtar na leabhair i riocht maith ionas gur féidir iad a athúsáid. Má bhíonn ort earraí breise a cheannach do do leanbh, cuirfidh an scoil é sin in iúl duit. Léigh tuilleadh faoin [ Scéim na Leabhair Bunscoile In Aisce (pdf) ](https://assets.gov.ie/250686/b1aa4f60-a8d7-4a8c-8257-6f585bb87a10.pdf) . ### **Iar-bhunscoileanna** #### **Leabhair saor in aisce don tsraith Shóisearach** Ó Mheán Fómhair 2024, cuirfidh scoileanna sa Scéim Saoroideachais leabhair scoile agus croí-acmhainní ranga ar fáil do gach dalta sa tSraith Shóisearach (iad siúd atá sa chéad 3 bliana den iar-bhunscoil). Core classroom resources include things like copybooks, journals, calculators and dictionaries. The school will provide the free books on loan. You must return the books at the end of the school year, or if your child leaves the school. If you must buy additional items for your child, the school will let you know. While the scheme includes eBooks, it does not cover the purchase of digital devices. If your child already started in first year of post-primary school, you may already have their Junior Cycle books. If your child needs additional books before starting second or third year in September 2024, the school will provide them for free. Read more about the [ Junior Cycle Schoolbooks Scheme (pdf) ](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/286441/2caaccc0-411a-437a-bf38-ad5c1ab6a310.pdf#page=null) . #### **Scéim cíosa leabhar don tSraith Shinsearach** Is féidir le hiar-bhunscoileanna sa Scéim Saoroideachais chun cabhair a thabhairt do dhaltaí na Sraithe Sinsearaí (iad siúd san Idirbhliain, sa chúigiú bliain agus sa séú bliain) le costas na leabhar scoile. Ligeann an scéim do scoileanna tacaíocht a thabhairt do mhic léinn ó theaghlaigh ar ioncam íseal nó ó theaghlaigh a bhfuil cruatan airgeadais orthu. Faoin scéim, is féidir le scoileanna: * Scéim cíosa leabhar a chur ar bun laistigh den scoil, nó * Cabhair a thabhairt do daltaí aonair leabhair a cheannach Ní mór duit táille bheag cíosa a íoc. Is féidir leat teagmháil a dhéanamh le scoil do linbh le fáil amach an bhfuil scéim leabhar ar cíos acu. Mura bhfuil, is féidir leat é a mholadh do phríomhoide na scoile. Is féidir leat an [ Treoir do Thuismitheoirí: Scéimeanna do Théacsleabhar ar cíos (pdf) ](https://s3-eu- west-1.amazonaws.com/govieassets/39088/f6a23749787e4ee88d4fd0bcc1c8e62d.pdf) a léamh freisin. Chun iarratas a dhéanamh ar chúnamh faoin scéim, déan teagmháil le príomhoide na scoile. Tá lánrogha ag an bpríomhoide maidir le cinneadh a dhéanamh ar na scoláirí is mó atá i ngá. Read more about [ the different schoolbooks schemes ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/e7372-schoolbooks-scheme-information-hub/) on Gov.ie. ## Táillí scrúdaithe **Note: There were no exam fees for students sitting the Junior Cycle or Leaving Certificate exams in 2024.** Ordinarily, students whose parent or guardian have a [ medical card ](/ga/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/medical-card/) do not have to pay the examination fees for [ Junior Cycle ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/state-examinations/junior- cycle-exam-and-results/#7652a5) or [ Leaving Certificate ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/state- examinations/examination-fees/) . You put the details of the medical card on the fee payment form and send it back to the school. Mar sin féin, má dhéanann tú iarratas tar éis an spriocdháta, beidh ort táillí déanacha a íoc fiú má tá cárta leighis ag do thuismitheoirí. ## Iompar scoile Soláthraíonn an scéim iompair scoile iompar do leanaí: * A bhfuil cónaí orthu 3.2 km nó níos mó óna mbunscoil áitiúil * A bhfuil cónaí orthu 4.8 km nó níos mó óna n-iar-bhunscoil áitiúil Go ginearálta, íocann tú táille as an scéim. Mar sin féin, d’fhéadfadh iompar scoile a bheith saor do shealbhóirí cárta leighis, má tá an scéim iompair scoile ag feidhmiú i do cheantar agus go bhfuil do leanbh incháilithe. Léigh tuilleadh faoi [ iompar scoile don bhunscoil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary- education/going-to-primary-school/primary-school-transport-scheme/) agus [ iompar scoile don iar-bhunscoil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary- education/going-to-post-primary-school/post-primary-school-transport-scheme/) . #### **Deontas Ceantar Iargúlta** Má cháilíonn dalta don scéim iompair scoile ach nach bhfuil seirbhís iompair ar fáil, is féidir Deontas Ceantar Iargúlta a íoc mar ranníoc i dtreo socruithe iompair phríobháidigh. Féadfar na deontais seo a íoc le mic léinn a bhfuil cónaí orthu 3.2 km nó níos mó ón bpointe bailithe is gaire d’iompar scoile. Read more about the [ Remote Area Grant ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/07a71-school- transport/?referrer=https://www.gov.ie/en/service/school-transport/#remote- area-grant) on Gov.ie. ### **Special educational needs school transport** Free [ transport is available to children with special educational needs ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary- education/going-to-primary-school/school-transport-for-children-with-special- needs/) to and from special schools and classes. If transport cannot be provided, you may be able to apply for a Special Transport Grant to help with the cost of making private transport arrangements. ### **Deontas Eislóistín Cheantar Iargúlta** Cuireann an Deontas Eislóistín Cheantar Iargúlta maoiniú ar fáil do mhic léinn chun áit chónaithe nó lóistín a fháil ionas gur féidir leo freastal ar scoil dara leibhéal saor in aisce. Chun cáiliú do dheontas bordála, ní mór duit: * I do chónaí 4.8km ar a laghad ó scoil dara leibhéal saor in aisce * I do chónaí níos mó ná 3.2 km ó phointe bailithe ar sheirbhís iompair go dtí scoil dara leibhéal saor in aisce * In ann áit a fháil i scoil dara leibhéal oiriúnach laistigh de 25km ón áit a bhfuil tú i do chónaí * Ag moladh freastal ar scoil dara leibhéal a chuireann oideachas saor in aisce ar fáil D’fhéadfadh mic léinn a bhfuil cónaí orthu ar oileáin amach ón gcósta nach bhfuil scoil acu a sholáthraíonn oideachas oiriúnach dara leibhéal in aisce cáiliú freisin. Is é an t-uasdeontas atá ar fáil ná €4,947 in aghaidh na bliana. Íoctar é seo leis an scoil, nó leis an tuismitheoir nó caomhnóir, tar éis gach téarma scoile a luaithe a bhíonn tinreamh an dalta fíoraithe ag an scoil i rith na bliana. Seans go mbeidh ort ráiteas a sholáthar ar na táillí lóistín nó lóistín a íocadh. Ba chóir iarratais do leanaí a thosaíonn i mí Mheán Fómhair gach bliain a chur isteach roimh 30 Aibreán na bliana sin. Ní féidir iarratais a gheofar tar éis an ama seo a phróiseáil faoi Mheán Fómhair. Ní féidir leat iarratas a dhéanamh ar líne. Read more about the [ Remote Area Boarding Grant ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/remote-area-boarding-grant/) on Gov.ie. You can [ find the application form in Circular 0016/2016 (pdf) ](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/12976/5eaeebd606a04fd7b3eb6cda354c8fe1.pdf#page=1) . ## Riachtanais speisialta agus míchumas fisiceach Is féidir le scoileanna [ Deontas Teicneolaíochta Cúnta ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary- education/educational-supports/assistive-technology-grant-students-with- disabilities/) a fháil ón Roinn Oideachais chun ríomhairí agus trealamh speisialta a cheannach do mhic léinn faoi mhíchumas. Tá deontais bhreise ar fáil, lena n-áirítear deontais le haghaidh * Teagasc baile * Teanga chomharthaíochta na hÉireann * Teagasc clóscríofa do mhic léinn lagamhairc Read more about [ special education needs in primary schools ](/ga/education/special-education-needs/special-needs-education-primary- schools/) and [ post-primary schools ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary- education/going-to-post-primary-school/post-primary-education-for-students- with-special-needs/) . You can also read more about [ special education ](https://www.gov.ie/en/organisation-information/b05378-special-education- section/) and [ Special Needs and Tuition Grants ](https://www.gov.ie/en/organisation-information/1b936-special-needs-and- tuition-grants/) on Gov.ie. ## Tuilleadh faisnéise ### An Roinn Oideachais Riarachán Bunoideachais 1 Corr na Madadh An Iarmhí N37 X659 Éire **Teil:** (090) 648 3600 **Láithreán Gréasáin:** [ https://www.gov.ie/en/organisation/department-of- education/ ](https://www.gov.ie/en/organisation/department-of-education/) **R-phost:** [ info@education.gov.ie ](mailto:info@education.gov.ie) _**Dáta an Leasaithe Deireanaigh:** 22 Lúnasa 2024 _ [ ](https://facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/ga/education/primary- and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/cabhair-airgeadais-le- scoil/?utm_source=sharebutton_facebook) [ ](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet/?text=Cabhair%20airgeadais%20le%20dul%20ar%20scoil&url=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/ga/education/primary- and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/cabhair-airgeadais-le- scoil/?utm_source=sharebutton_twitter) [ ](whatsapp://send?text=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/ga/education/primary- and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/cabhair-airgeadais-le- scoil/?utm_source=sharebutton_whatsapp) [ ](mailto:?subject=Cabhair%20airgeadais%20le%20dul%20ar%20scoil&body=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/ga/education/primary- and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/cabhair-airgeadais-le- scoil/?utm_source=sharebutton_email) [ ](javascript:void\(0\)) ### Téigh i dteagmháil linn Má tá ceist agat a bhaineann leis an ábhar seo, is féidir leat glao a chur ar an tSeirbhís Ghutháin um Fhaisnéis do Shaoránaigh [ _0818 07 4000_ ](tel:0818074000) (Luan go hAoine idir 9rn agus 8in).
