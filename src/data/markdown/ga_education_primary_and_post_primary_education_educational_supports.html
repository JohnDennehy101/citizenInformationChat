Oideachas bunscoile agus iar-bhunscoile ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/) > Tacaíocht Oideachais # Tacaíocht Oideachais * [ Acmhainní do dhaltaí nach cainteoirí Béarla iad ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/resources-for-non-english-speakers/) Tugann an cháipéis seo cur síos ar an tacaíocht oideachais le haghaidh daltaí nach cainteoirí Béarla iad i mbunscoileanna agus in iar-bhunscoileanna. * [ Seirbhís síceolaíochta i scoileanna ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/psychological-assessment-school/) Cuireann an tSeirbhís Náisiúnta Síceolaíochta Oideachais seirbhísí síceolaíochta ar fáil i mbunscoileanna agus in iar-bhunscoileanna. * [ Socraithe oideachais don Lucht Siúil ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/educational-arrangements-for-travellers/) Cur síos ar na socraithe oideachais atá ar fáil do pháistí an lucht siúil. * [ Deontais teicneolaíochta cúnta do mhic léinn faoi mhíchumas ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/assistive-technology-grant-students-with-disabilities/) Tá cur síos anseo ar scéim a chuireann deontas ar fáil do scoileanna chun cur ar an gcumas treallamh a cheannacht do scolairí faoi mhíchumas. * [ Cláir Luathfhágála Scoile ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/early-school-leavers-programmes/) Tá roinnt scéimeanna oideachais ann do dhaoine óga agus do dhaoine eile a d'fhág an scoil gan aon cháilíocht aitheanta. * [ Cabhair airgeadais le dul ar scoil ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/cabhair-airgeadais-le-scoil/) Tá roinnt scéimeanna ann chun cabhair a thabhairt do na costais a bhaineann le dul ar scoil. * [ Visiting Teacher Service ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/educational-supports/visiting-teacher-service/) The Department of Education operates a Visiting Teacher Service for deaf children and children with visual impairments. ### Ábhar is mó éilimh i "Tacaíocht Oideachais" 1.
