#  Obair a thosú

  * Overview 

[ Ag obair in Éirinn i ndiaidh cónaí thar lear ](/ga/returning-to-
ireland/starting-work/working-in-Ireland-after-living-abroad/)

Leagann sé amach na príomh-rudaí atá le breithniú má tá sé ar intinn agat dul
i mbun fostaíochta nuair a fhilleann tú ar Éirinn. Clúdaíonn sé post a aimsiú,
do chearta fostaíochta agus na cánacha a chaithfidh tú a íoc.

  * [ Aitheantas cáilíochtaí gairmiúla in Éirinn ](/ga/returning-to-ireland/starting-work/Recognition-of-professional-qualifications-in-ireland/)

Leagtar amach conas a rialaítear cáilíochtaí gairmiúla éagsúla in Éirinn agus
san AE, lena n-áirítear na gairmeacha seo a leanas: leictreoir, múinteoir,
ailtire, dochtúir, cuntasóir agus níos mó.

  * [ Returning to Ireland as a teacher ](/ga/returning-to-ireland/starting-work/returning-to-ireland-as-a-teacher/)

Explains the process for getting teaching qualifications recognised for those
teachers who have qualified outside of Ireland and are returning to the state.

  * [ Returning to Ireland and working in construction ](/ga/returning-to-ireland/starting-work/returning-to-ireland-and-working-in-construction/)

Provides information on careers in construction, Safepass, certifications, and
recognition of foreign qualifications, as well as details about trade unions,
wages, and pensions for those returning to Ireland and seeking to begin or
continue work in the construction industry.

  *   * [ Post a aimsiú ](/ga/employment/starting-work-and-changing-job/training-and-looking-for-work/finding-a-job/)

Eolas faoi conas post a chuardach, conas iarratas a dhéanamh ar phost agus
roinnt tacaíochtaí atá ar fáil do lucht cuardaigh fostaíochta.
