#  Driving tests

  * [ Driver theory test ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driver-theory-test/)

To get a learner permit in Ireland you may need to complete a driver theory
test. Find out more.

  * [ Driving tests ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driving-test/)

Driver testing in Ireland consists of an oral test and a practical test of
skills. It's a compulsory requirement to sit the test before you can get a
third or subsequent learner permit.
