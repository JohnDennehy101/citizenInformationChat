**English** | Gaeilge **You are here:** [ Home ](/en/) > [ What's New ](/en/whats-new/) > What's New # What's New 27 November 2023 ##### Site updates Some once-off cost of living payments announced in [ Budget 2024 ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/budgets/budget-2024/) will be paid this week: * If you get an [ increase for a qualified child ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/claiming-and-increase-in-your-payment-for-a-child-dependant/) , you will get a lump sum of €100 on the same day that you get your primary social welfare payment * If you get a [ Living Alone Increase ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/extra-social-welfare-benefits/living-alone-allowance/) , you will get a lump sum of €200 between 29 November 2023 and 1 December 2023 (you will get this on the same day that you get your primary payment) * If you get the [ Carer’s Support Grant ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/carers/carers-support-grant/) or [ Domiciliary Care Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/disability-and-illness/domiciliary-care-allowance/) , you will get a lump sum of €400 on 30 November 2023 You have a legal right to 5 days’ paid [ domestic violence leave ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and- conditions/leave-and-holidays/domestic-violence-leave/) if you need to take time off work because of the abuse. This is available from 27 November 2023. The Government has proposed a new [ Pay-Related Jobseeker’s Benefit System ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare- payments/unemployed-people/jobseekers-benefit/#75628e) to be introduced in late 2024. The scheme for reduced [ toll fares ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/roads-and- safety/national-toll-roads-in-ireland/) for [ electric vehicles ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/buying- or-selling-a-vehicle/electric-vehicles/#257223) will end on 31 December 2023. From 10 January 2025, it will be an offence to [ advertise alcohol ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/justice/criminal-law/criminal- offences/alcohol-and-the-law/#f6a10b) on: * Television between 3am and 9pm * Radio on a weekday between midnight and 10am, and between 3pm and midnight **New and recently edited** New page: * [ Domestic violence leave
