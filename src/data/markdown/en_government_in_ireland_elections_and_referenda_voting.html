](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/right-to-vote/) Who is eligible to vote? This page describes the nationality rules for the different types of elections and referendums that take place in Ireland. * [ Registering to vote ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/registering-to-vote/) In order to vote in an election or referendum in Ireland, you must be registered to vote. Find out how to register and how to update your details. * [ Voting in a local election ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/voting-in-a-local-election/) Find out what you need to do on the day of a local election and how to vote. * [ Voting in a European election ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/voting-procedure-in-a-european-election/) Outlines the procedures for voting in a European election in Ireland. Describes who can vote, how a person can vote, the organisation of the elections, how the votes are counted, how the results are announced and more. * [ Facilities for voters with disabilities ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/facilities-for-voters-with-disabilities/) Procedures to help people with disabilities to exercise their voting rights. * [ Homeless and registering to vote ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/homeless-and-registering-to-voting/) How to register to vote if you have no fixed address or you are homeless. * [ Proportional representation ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/proportional-representation/) Proportional representation is the system of voting used in all elections in Ireland. From quotas to recounts, this document explains how it all works. * [ Voting in a general election ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/voting-procedure-in-a-general-election/) Find out about what you need to do on the day of a general election and how to vote. * [ Voting in a referendum ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/voting-in-a-referendum/) Who can vote in a referendum in Ireland, where do you cast your vote and how do you cast your vote? * [ The regulation of elections ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/electoral-commission/) An Coimisiún Toghcháin (the Electoral Commission) oversees elections and referendums in Ireland. ### Most Read in "Voting" 1.
