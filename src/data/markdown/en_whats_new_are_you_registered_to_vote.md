#  Are you registered to vote?

20 February 2024

#####  Site updates

Are you registered to vote in the upcoming referendums?

To vote, you must be eligible and registered.

Today, 20 February 2024, is the last day to register to vote. You can register
online at [ checktheregister.ie ](http://checktheregister.ie/)

To register to vote you will need your:

  * PPS number 
  * Eircode 
  * Date of birth 

To be eligible to register you must:

  * Be at least 18 years old 
  * Be ordinarily resident at an address in Ireland 

Find out more about [ registering to vote in Ireland
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-
referenda/voting/registering-to-vote/)
