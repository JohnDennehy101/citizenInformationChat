**English** | Gaeilge **You are here:** [ Home ](/en/) > [ What's New ](/en/whats-new/) > Social welfare payments during St Patrick’s Day weekend ☘️ # Social welfare payments during St Patrick’s Day weekend ☘️ 13 March 2024 ##### News Are you expecting a social welfare payment on Monday, 18 March 2024? Monday, 18 March is a [ public holiday ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and- conditions/leave-and-holidays/public-holidays/) for St Patrick’s Day. If you are due to get a payment on Monday, you will be paid on Friday, 15 March 2024 instead. Payments will be paid as normal on Tuesday, 19 March 2024. All Intreo centres and local branch offices will be closed on Bank Holiday Monday, 18 March 2024. [ Intreo centres and local branch offices ](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) will reopen on Tuesday, 19 March 2024. Check gov.ie for more updates [ about St Patrick’s Day bank holiday payments ](https://www.gov.ie/en/news/67e0b4-customer-notices/#latest-updates) .
