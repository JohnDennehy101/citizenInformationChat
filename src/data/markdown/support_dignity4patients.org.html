#### Victim Rights If you have been a victim of sexual abuse, it is vital to know that you are not alone, and that help is available to you. We appreciate the importance of defending victims' rights and dignity, as well as ensuring that they receive the required support, help, and protection. We amplify survivors' voices, by advocating for their rights, to ensure you are supported. Recovery from sexual assault or abuse is a unique process that takes time and is personal to each individual — there is no set timeline for healing. We can assist you on your journey to seeking justice and restoration of your dignity.
