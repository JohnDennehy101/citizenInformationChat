#  Changes to Stamp 4 permit

7 May 2024

#####  News

  

Eligibility for a Stamp 4 has reduced from 2 years to 21 months for people
with the following Stamp 1 and Stamp 1H [ immigration permissions
](/en/moving-country/working-in-ireland/employment-permits/overview-
employment-permits/) :

  * [ Critical Skills Employment Permit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/moving-country/working-in-ireland/employment-permits/overview-employment-permits/#8cba39)
  * [ Hosting Agreement as a Researcher ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/moving-country/working-in-ireland/employment-permits/overview-employment-permits/#2f2e35)
  * Multi-Site General Employment Permit as a Non-Consultant Hospital Doctor 

For people on a [ general employment permit
](https://eur03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcitizensinformationboard.newsweaver.com%2Fhpazr9uwci%2F1dupgz9j2kydd102vo4l7u%2Fexternal%3Femail%3Dtrue%26a%3D5%26p%3D12444856%26t%3D2911414&data=05%7C02%7Cliz.martin%40ciboard.ie%7C69904c0ac77649f7435e08dc5fa8afef%7Cbb4e61ce39a3414abb5d6a0290c95fd9%7C0%7C0%7C638490425367281595%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=kPTYWqHiGwGomk3zaV880d%2Bi6Zh5TBSXhe9wDpp6Ga4%3D&reserved=0)
, eligibility for a Stamp 4 is reduced from 5 years to 57 months.
