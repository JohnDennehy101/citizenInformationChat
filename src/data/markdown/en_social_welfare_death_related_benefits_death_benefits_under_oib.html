related benefits ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/) > Death Benefits under the Occupational Injuries Scheme # Death Benefits under the Occupational Injuries Scheme * Introduction * Rules * Rates * How to apply * Where to apply ## Introduction If a person dies because of an accident at work or occupational disease, Death Benefit (under the [ Occupational Injuries Scheme ](/en/social- welfare/disability-and-illness/occupational-injuries-benefit-overview/) ) may be paid to their surviving spouse or civil partner or dependent child. Death Benefit may also be paid if, at the time of their death, the person was getting a [ Disablement Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and- illness/disablement-benefit/) assessed at 50% or more, regardless of the cause of death. The Death Benefit Scheme includes: * Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner's Pension * Orphan's Pension * Funeral Grant ## Rules ### Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner's Pension This is paid to the surviving spouse or civil partner of a person who died as a result of an accident at work, an occupational disease or who was getting a Disablement Pension assessed as 50% or more at the time of their death. You cannot get the pension if you remarry, enter into a civil partnership or cohabit with another person. The pension is not affected by any income you may have. Increases for qualified children are paid with the pension. [ The pension is taxable ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming- a-social-welfare-payment/taxation-of-social-welfare-payments/) . A Widow's Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner's Pension under the Death Benefit Scheme cannot be paid at the same time as a [ Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner's Contributory Pension ](/en/social-welfare/death- related-benefits/widowed-persons-contrib-pension/) . ### Orphan's Pension This is paid on behalf of a child if the parent, step-parent or person who supported the child died as a result of an accident at work, an occupational disease or was getting a Disablement Pension assessed as 50% or more at the time of their death. The orphan's guardian should apply for the payment. ### Funeral Grant If the deceased person meets the conditions a [ special Funeral Grant ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/special-funeral-grant/) can be paid to the person who pays for the funeral expenses. A Funeral Grant is not taxable. ## Rates Payments under the Death Benefit Scheme are paid at higher rates than a standard Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner's Contributory Pension or a Guardian's payment. **Death Benefit Scheme rates 2024** **Payment** | **Rate** | **Increase for a child dependant** ---|---|--- Widow, widower or surviving civil partner (under 66) | €262.50 | Child under 12 €46 (full rate), €23 (half rate) Child aged 12 or over €54 (full rate), €27 (half rate) Widow, widower or surviving civil partner (over 66) | €281.70 Widow, widower or surviving civil partner (over 80) | €291.70 Orphan's Pension | €218.80 | N/A [ Funeral Grant ](../../../../../../en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/death-related-benefits/special-funeral-grant/) (once-off lump sum) | €850 | N/A ## How to apply A claim for any of the payments under the Death Benefit Scheme should be made within 3 months of the date of death, otherwise you may lose some benefit. Complete claim form [ OB 61(pdf) ](https://assets.gov.ie/19635/5f3892d28336479bb97e7dc5e64676d5.pdf) which is available from your local social welfare office. Send your completed claim form to the Death Benefit Section at the address below. ## Where to apply ### Department of Social Protection Social Welfare Services Office Government Buildings Ballinalee Road Longford Ireland **Tel:** (043) 334 0000 or 0818 927 770 **Homepage:** [ https://www.gov.ie/en/organisation/department-of-social- protection/ ](https://www.gov.ie/en/organisation/department-of-social- protection/) _**Page edited:** 5 January 2024 _ [ ](https://facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social- welfare/death-related-benefits/death-benefits-under- oib/?utm_source=sharebutton_facebook) [ ](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet/?text=Death%20Benefits%20under%20the%20Occupational%20Injuries%20Scheme&url=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social- welfare/death-related-benefits/death-benefits-under- oib/?utm_source=sharebutton_twitter) [ ](whatsapp://send?text=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social- welfare/death-related-benefits/death-benefits-under- oib/?utm_source=sharebutton_whatsapp) [ ](mailto:?subject=Death%20Benefits%20under%20the%20Occupational%20Injuries%20Scheme&body=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social- welfare/death-related-benefits/death-benefits-under- oib/?utm_source=sharebutton_email) [ ](javascript:void\(0\)) ### Related documents * [ _Occupational Injuries Scheme -
