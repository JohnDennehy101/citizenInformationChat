#  Motor tax and insurance

  * [ Motor tax ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/motor-tax-and-insurance/motor-tax-rates/)

It is a legal requirement to pay motor tax if you want to drive your vehicle
in a public place. Details of motor tax requirements, rates and how to apply.
(Including the online motor tax payment service).

  * [ Motor insurance ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/motor-tax-and-insurance/motor-insurance/)

Motor insurance is a compulsory requirement to drive a car in a public place.
A current Certificate of Insurance is required to get motor tax.
