**English** | Gaeilge **You are here:** [ Home ](/en/) > [ What's New ](/en/whats-new/) > Deadline for taxation of married people and civil partners # Deadline for taxation of married people and civil partners 25 March 2024 ##### News For the years following your marriage or civil partnership, there are three options for taxation: * Assessment as a single person (you are both still taxed as single people) * Separate assessment * Joint assessment The deadline to [ request separate assessment ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/life-events-and-personal-circumstances/marital- status/marriage-and-civil-partnerships/separate-assessment.aspx) or to [ nominate an assessable spouse ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/life-events-and- personal-circumstances/marital-status/marriage-and-civil-partnerships/joint- assessment.aspx) or civil partner for 2024 is 31 March 2024.
