#  Dul chun scoile iar-bhunoideachais

  * [ Bunscoil a roghnú ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-post-primary-school/types-of-post-primary-school/)

Tá chineál scoile iarbhunoideachais ann. Baineann na príomhdhifríochtaí leis
an gcaoi a bhainistítear is a mhaoinítear na scoileanna agus cé leo iad.

  * [ Sraith Shinsearach ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-post-primary-school/senior-cycle/)

Cur síos ar an tSraith Sinsearach i scoileanna dara leibhéil.

  * [ Eagraíochtaí agus acmhainní le haghaidh iarbhunscoileanna ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-post-primary-school/organisations-and-resources-for-post-primary-schools/)

Liosta eagraíochtaí agus acmhainní idirlín é seo a chuireann faisnéis ar fáil
faoi chúrsaí oideachais ar an leibhéal iarbhunoideachais.

  * [ An tSraith Shóisearach ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-post-primary-school/an-tsraith-shoisearach/)

Tugtar an tSraith Shóisearach ar an gcéad tréimhse trí bliana d'oideachas dara
leibhéal.

  *   * [ Scéim iompair do scoileanna dara-leibhéil ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-post-primary-school/post-primary-school-transport-scheme/)

Cuireann an Roinn Oideachais cabhair airgid d’iompar scoile ar fáil do na
scoláirí atá intofa don scéim Iompair Scoile.

  * [ Transition Year ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-post-primary-school/transition-year/)

This document describes the Transition Year programme, a one-year, school-
based programme between Junior Cycle and Senior Cycle.

  * [ Oideachas riachtanas speisialta: oideachas iarbhunscoile ](/ga/education/special-education-needs/post-primary-education-for-students-with-special-needs/)

Cur síos ar oideachas iarbhunscoile do mhic léinn atá faoi mhíchumas agus a
bhfuil riachtanais speisialta oideachais acu.
