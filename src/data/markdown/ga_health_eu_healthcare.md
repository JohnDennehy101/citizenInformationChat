#  Cúram sláinte i Aontais Eorpaigh

  * [ Travelling from Ireland for healthcare in Europe ](/ga/health/eu-healthcare/travelling-for-healthcare/)

Introduction to the options for Irish residents who wish to access healthcare
in other countries in Europe.

  * [ Treatment Abroad Scheme ](/ga/health/eu-healthcare/treatment-abroad-scheme/)

If you are a public healthcare patient and require treatment that is not
available to you in Ireland, you may be able to use the Treatment Abroad
Scheme to get the treatment abroad.

  * [ An Treoir maidir le Cúram Sláinte Trasteorann ](/ga/health/eu-healthcare/cross-border-directive/)

Má tá tú i dteideal seirbhísí sláinte poiblí in Éirinn, féadfaidh tú roghnú
rochtain a fháil ar na seirbhísí sin i mballstát eile den Aontas Eorpach nó
den Limistéar Eorpach Eacnamaíoch.

  * [ Cárta Árachais Sláinte na hEorpa ](/ga/travel-and-recreation/travel-abroad/european-health-insurance-card/)

Tugann an Cárta Eorpach Árachas Sláinte rochtain ar leighis is gá, stát-ar
fáil cúram sláinte le linn fanacht go sealadach i dtír eile den AE / LEE.
