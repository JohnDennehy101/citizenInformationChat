Kinship care is the full-time parenting of children by grandparents, older siblings, aunts and uncles, other relatives, or close friends of the family when their parents are no longer able to look after them. I It occurs for many reasons, for example, the death of a parent, parental substance misuse, abandonment, illness, or imprisonment. I It can be temporary or permanent arrangement and can also be on a formal or informal basis. I [ Learn More ](https://www.kinshipcare.ie/what-is-kinship-care/) ![](https://www.kinshipcare.ie/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/KCI- Image-3-crop.jpg) ##### Are you raising a child for a relative or friend? Are you a grandparent, aunt, uncle, older brother, or sister, relative or family friend raising a child who is unable to live with their parents? I If so, you are a Kinship Carer.
