> Coming home with children # Coming home with children * Overview [ Returning to Ireland with children ](/en/returning-to-ireland/coming-home- with-children/returning-to-ireland-with-children/) Outlines the supports and services you may need to know about when returning home to Ireland with children. Covers employment rights, supports for people parenting alone and caring for a child with a disability. * [ Returning to Ireland and having a baby ](/en/returning-to-ireland/coming-home-with-children/returning-to-ireland-and-having-a-baby/) Outlines what public maternity care services you can access when you return to Ireland and your entitlements to take leave from work when you have a baby. * [ Irish citizenship and passports for children born abroad ](/en/returning-to-ireland/residency-and-citizenship/irish-citizenship-and-passports-for-children-born-abroad/) Outlines the rules about Irish citizenship for children who were born abroad and have an Irish parent. It also covers the documents you need when applying for an Irish passport for children born abroad. **This document is in:** [ Residency and citizenship ](/en/returning-to- ireland/residency-and-citizenship/) * [ Child Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/child-benefit/) Child Benefit is a monthly payment to all parents or guardians of children under 16, or under 19 if the child is in full-time education or has a disability. **This document is in:** [ Families and children ](/en/social- welfare/families-and-children/) * [ GP visit cards for children under 8 ](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/gp-visit-cards-for-under-8s/) Children under 8 are entitled to free visits to participating GPs. Find out how to register for the GP visit card for children. **This document is in:** [ Medical cards and GP visit cards ](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/) * [ Caring for a child with a disability ](/en/health/health-services/children-s-health/caring-for-a-child-with-a-disability/) Find out what supports and services are available to parents, guardians and carers of children with disabilities **This document is in:** [ Children's health ](/en/health/health- services/children-s-health/) ### Most Read in "Coming home with children" 1.
