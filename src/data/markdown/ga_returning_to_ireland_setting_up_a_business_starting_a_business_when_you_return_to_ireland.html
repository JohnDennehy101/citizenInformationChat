'Back for Business' * Do threoir maidir le gnó a thosú in Éirinn * Beithnithe airgeadais * Tuilleadh faisnéise ## Réamhrá Má tá tú ag filleadh ar Éirinn tar éis duit cónaí thar lear, b'fhéidir go gcinnfidh tú oibriú duit féin, seachas d'fhostóir. Féadfaidh an t-eolas agus na scileanna atá agat a fuarthas ón saol thar lear cabhrú leat chun rath a bhaint amach sa gheilleagar áitiúil. Tá faisnéis ar an leathanach seo maidir le conas a bheith i d'úinéir gnó in Éirinn, lena n-áirítear treoir ar conas plean gnó a scríobh, foireann a fhostú agus do chánacha a bhainistiú. Má tá saoránach neamh-Éireannach ag teacht go hÉirinn chun gnó a bhunú leat, b'fhéidir go mbeidh orthu iarratas a dhéanamh ar chead ar dtús. Léigh faoi [ theacht chun gnó a bhunú in Éirinn mar náisiúnach neamh-LEE, na RA nó na hEilvéise ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/moving-country/working-in- ireland/migrant-workers/coming-to-set-up-a-business-in-ireland/#lac4a4) . ## An clár 'Back for Business' Is clár forbartha saor in aisce é Back for Business chun cabhrú le saoránaigh Éireannacha atá tar éis filleadh ar Éirinn chun gnó a fhorbairt. Tá an clár maoinithe tríd an [ gClár Tacaíochta Eisimirceach ](https://www.dfa.ie/global- irish/support-overseas/emigrant-support-programme/) ag an Roinn Gnóthaí Eachtracha. Tabhair cuairt ar ár leathanach faoin gclár [ Back for Business ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/setting-up-a- business/back-for-business-programme/) . ## Do threoir maidir le gnó a thosú in Éirinn Cibé an raibh gnó ar siúl agat agus tú i do chónaí thar lear, nó má tá tú ag éirí fiontraí den chéad uair, is féidir leat an faisnéis seo a leanas a léamh faoi ghnó a thosú in Éirinn: * [ Treoir ar chéim maidir le gnó a bhunú in Éirinn ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/step-by-step-guide-to-setting-up-a-business-in-ireland/) * [ Conas plean gnó a scríobh ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/how-to-write-a-business-plan/) * [ Ainm do ghnó a chlárú ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/becoming-self-employed/#ad8994) * [ Cinneadh a dhéanamh ar struchtúr dlíthiúil do ghnó ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/starting-a-business/#9960e7) ## Beithnithe airgeadais Ní mó duit raon saincheisteanna airgeadais a mheas sula dtosaíonn tú ar ghnó. Mar shampla, athrófar do fhreagrachtaí cánach. D'fheadfadh go mbeadh deontas tosaithe uait freisin nó gur mhaith leat fostaithe a fhostú. Léigh ár bhfaisnéis ar: * [ Conas maoiniú a fháil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/starting-a-business/#6c48a3) * [ Cad atá le déanamh má tá deacrachtaí creidmheasa agat ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/starting-a-business/#d9e9f0) * [ Do cháin mar úinéir gnó a thuiscint ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/understanding-your-tax-as-a-business-owner/) * [ Daoine a fhostú i do ghnó ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/employing-people-in-your-business/) * [ Árachas nó cead pleanála a fháil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/becoming-self-employed/#54b49d) ## Tuilleadh faisnéise Is féidir leat [ an comhlacht ionadaíoch do thionscal ar leith a aimsiú ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self- employment/finding-information-on-starting-business/#8b20a5) chun treoir agus tacaíocht shaincheaptha a fháil. Má tá inbhuanaitheacht tábhachtach duit, féach ar an [ raon tacaíochtaí atá ar fáil chun do ghnó a dhéanamh neamhdhíobhálach don chomhshaol ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self- employment/supports-for-businesses-going-green/) . Féach ar ár [ liosta de shuímh Ghréasáin, de ranna Rialtais agus de ghníomhaireachtaí le faisnéis chun cabhrú leat gnó a thosú ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self- employment/finding-information-on-starting-business/#969d1a) in Éirinn.
