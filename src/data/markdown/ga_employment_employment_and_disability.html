míchumas # Fostaíocht agus míchumas * [ Deontais chun an láthair-oibre a chuir in oiriúint agus a áisiú do fhoireann faoi mhíchumas ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/workplace-equipment-adaptation-grant/) Tá cabhair deontais le fáil ag fostaithe faoi mhíchumas, fostóirí agus daoine féin-fhostaithe faoi mhíchumas, a dteastaíonn uathu oiriúnuithe a dhéanamh ar a láthair oibre, nó trealamh speisialta a cheannach. * [ Deontais léitheoirí pearsanta ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/personal-reader-grant/) Cuirtear an Deontas Léitheora Phearsanta ar fáil d'fhostaithe le amharc lagaithe nó atá dall agus a dteastaíonn roinnt cabhrach breise uathu le ábhar priontáilte a léamh ag an obair. * [ Scéim Fóirdheontas Tuarastail do fhostóirí le foireann mhíchumasaithe ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/wage-subsidy-scheme-for-people-with-disabilities/) Cuireann an Scéim Fóirdheontas Tuarastail tacaíocht airgid ar fáil d'fhostóirí a fhostaíonn daoine áirithe faoi mhíchumas. * [ Fostaíocht le tacaíocht do dhaoine faoi mhíchumas ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/supported-employment-for-people-with-disabilities/) Scéim é an tSeirbhís Infhostaitheachta a bhfuil sé mar aidhm aige cúnamh a thabhairt do dhaoine faoi mhíchumas fostaíocht le pá a fháil. * ![Life Event Document](/static/images/lifeevent.jpg) [ Daoine faoi mhíchumas agus an obair ](/ga/employment/employment-and- disability/working-with-a-disability/) Tá tacaíochtaí difriúla ar fáil do dhaoine faoi mhíchumas le cabhrú leo fostaíocht a fháil agus a choimeád. * [ Work and Access Programme ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/work-and-access-programme/) The new Work and Access employment programme offers various supports to help people with disabilities find or keep a job by removing or reducing workplace barriers. * [ Work and Access - Communication Support Grant ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/work-and-access-communication-support-grant/) The Communication Support Grant is part of the new Work and Access employment programme, designed to help people with disabilities to find or keep a job by removing or reducing workplace barriers. * [ Work and Access - Workplace Needs Assessment Grant ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/work-and-access-workplace-needs-assessment-grant/) The Workplace Needs Assessment Grant is part of the new Work and Access employment programme, designed to help people with disabilities to find or keep a job by removing or reducing workplace barriers. * [ Work and Access - In-Work Support Grant ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/work-and-access-in-work-support-grant/) The In-Work Support Grantis part of the new Work and Access employment programme, designed to help people with disabilities to find or keep a job by removing or reducing workplace barriers. * [ Work and Access - Workplace Equipment Grant ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/work-and-access-workplace-equipment-grant/) The Workplace Equipment Grant is part of the new Work and Access employment programme, designed to help people with disabilities find or keep a job by removing or reducing workplace barriers. * [ Work and Access - Disability Equality and Inclusion Training Grant ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/disability-equality-and-inclusion-training-grant/) The Disability Equality and Inclusion Training Grant is part of the new Work and Access employment programme, designed to help people with disabilities find or keep a job by removing or reducing workplace barriers. ### Ábhar is mó éilimh i "Fostaíocht agus míchumas" 1.
