related benefits ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/) > Special Funeral Grant # Special Funeral Grant * Introduction * Rules * Rates * How to apply * Where to apply ## Introduction If someone dies at work, a special Funeral Grant is available under the [ Occupational Injuries Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and- illness/occupational-injuries-benefit-overview/) scheme. ## Rules To be eligible for this grant, the death must have resulted from: * An accident at work, * An accident while travelling directly to or from work * An occupational disease. The person must have paid a minimum of one week's PRSI. This special funeral grant is paid under the Occupational Injuries Benefits scheme instead of the Widowed Person's Bereavement Grant. ## Rates Special Funeral Grant: â‚¬850. ## How to apply To apply [ download and complete claim form OB 61 (pdf). ](https://assets.gov.ie/19635/5f3892d28336479bb97e7dc5e64676d5.pdf) To apply you need: * The original [ Death Certificate ](/en/death/practical-arrangements-after-a-death/registering-a-death/) /interim Death Certificate * A copy of the death notice * A note of the funeral expenses. ##
