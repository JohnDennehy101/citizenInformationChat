#  Sport and leisure

  * [ Public library services ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/public-library-services/)

An outline of the services offered by the public library service and the
membership requirements.

  * [ Blue Flag beaches and marinas in Ireland ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/blue-flag-beaches-and-marinas-in-ireland/)

The "Blue Flag" is an international award made to beaches and marinas that
demonstrate high environmental standards and good sanitary and safety
facilities.

  * [ Walking and rambling in Ireland ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/walking-and-rambling-in-ireland/)

This page discusses the legal situation for walking or rambling across
privately owned land and gives information on walking trails.

  * [ Boat licences and safety regulations ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/licensing-of-boats-and-watercraft-in-ireland/)

If you want to operate a boat or watercraft in Ireland, there are certain
rules you need to follow. Find out if you need a boat licence and what safety
equipment you may need.

  * [ Fishing licences and permits ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/fishing-licences-and-permits/)

State fishing licences and fishing permits are required if you wish to take
part in some types of angling in Ireland. Find out how and where to apply for
a licence.

  * [ Deer hunting licences ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/deer-licences/)

Deer in Ireland are a protected species and it is against the law to shoot
them without a licence. Find out to apply for a licence and the restrictions
that are in place.

  * [ Sport for blind and visually impaired people ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/sport-for-blind-and-visually-impaired-people/)

Vision Sports Ireland is an organisation set up to encourage the participation
in sport at all levels of blind and visually impaired people.

  * [ Sport activities for older people ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/go-for-life/)

The Go for Life programme, is Ireland's national programme aimed at increasing
the level of participation by older people in sport and physical activity.

  * [ How to set up a new club ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/setting-up-a-new-club/)

This page outlines what is involved in setting up a new club and where you can
go to get more information.

  * [ Owning and operating drones in Ireland ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/owning-and-operating-a-drone/)

If you want to fly a drone, you must register as a drone operator in most
cases. This page has information on how to do this and the rules for flying
drones.

  * [ Recreational activities for young people ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/recreational-activities-for-young-people/)

The overall objective of the National Recreation Policy for Young People is to
provide a framework for the promotion of appropriate recreational
opportunities for young people between the ages of 12 and 18. Find out more.
