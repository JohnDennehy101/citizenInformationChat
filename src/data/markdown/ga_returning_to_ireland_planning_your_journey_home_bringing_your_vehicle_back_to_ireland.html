thabhairt go hÉirinn_ ](/ga/returning-to-ireland/residency-and-citizenship/ag-filleadh-ar-eirinn-le-do-phairti-de-facto/) Treoir céim ar chéim maidir leis an bpróiseas inimirce do do pháirtí neamh-LEE de facto. Tugann sé breac-chuntas ar an bpróiseas chun do pháirtí de facto nach as an LEE, an AE, an Ríocht Aontaithe ná an Eilvéis é, a thabhairt abhaile go hÉirinn chun cónaí agus oibriú ann. * [ _Do chéile nó do pháirtnéir neamh-LEE a thabhairt abhaile go hÉirinn_ ](/ga/returning-to-ireland/residency-and-citizenship/returning-to-ireland-with-your-non-eea-spouse/) A step-by-step guide to the immigration process for your non-EEA non-EEA spouse or civil partner. Outlines the process for bringing your spouse or civil partner who is not from the EEA, the EU or Switzerland, home to Ireland to live and work. * [ _Mo pháirtnéir neamh-LEE a thabhairt go hÉirinn chun pósadh_ ](/ga/returning-to-ireland/residency-and-citizenship/bringing-my-non-eea-partner-to-ireland-to-get-married/) Leagtar amach conas do pháirtí neamh-LEE, RA nó Eilvéiseach a thabhairt go hÉirinn chun pósadh. ### Téigh i dteagmháil linn Má tá ceist agat a bhaineann leis an ábhar seo, is féidir leat glao a chur ar an tSeirbhís Ghutháin um Fhaisnéis do Shaoránaigh [ _0818 07 4000_ ](tel:0818074000) (Luan go hAoine idir 9rn agus 8in).
