#  Cúrsaí Láithreachais agus smachta sna scoileanna

  * [ Téarmaí scoile i mbunscoileanna agus iarbhunscoileanna ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/attendance-and-discipline-in-schools/school-terms-in-primary-and-postprimary/)

Tá caighdeánú déanta ar scítheanna na Nollag, na Cásca agus ar na scítheanna
lárthéarma i scoileanna na hÉireann. Eolas faoi fhad na scoilbhliana agus
laethanta saoire ag an mbunleibhéal agus an dara leibhéal.

  * [ Smacht i scoileanna ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/attendance-and-discipline-in-schools/school-discipline/)

Cuireann an tAcht um Leas Oideachais (2000) dualgas ar Bhoird Bhainistíochta
na scoileanna cód iompraíochta a bheith acu maidir le smacht scoile.

  * [ Cosaint leanaí i scoileanna ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/attendance-and-discipline-in-schools/child-protection-in-school/)

  * [ Bulaíocht ar scoil ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/attendance-and-discipline-in-schools/bulaiocht-i-scoileanna-na-heireann/)

Is mór an fhadhb í an bhulaíocht i scoileanna agus tá dualgas ar údaráis
bhainistíochta na scoileanna déileáil leí.

  * [ Tineamh scoile ](/ga/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/attendance-and-discipline-in-schools/school-attendance/)

Cuntas faoi na rialacha maidir le tinreamh scoile, tinreamh scoile, cur i
bhfeidhm rialacha agus pionós, agus cearta oideachais-bhaile.

who are at risk of not reaching their educational potential.
