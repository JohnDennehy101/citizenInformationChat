**English** | Gaeilge **You are here:** [ Home ](/en/) > [ What's New ](/en/whats-new/) > New on Citizens Information 10-07-2023 # New on Citizens Information 10-07-2023 10 July 2023 ##### Site updates Since 3 July 2023, parts of the [ Work Life Balance (Miscellaneous Provisions) Act 2023 ](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2023/act/8/enacted/en/html) are now in effect: * [ Breastfeeding ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/after-your-baby-is-born/breastfeeding/) breaks have increased from 6 months to 2 years * If you are a carer or a parent of a child who needs serious medical care, you have the right to 5 days [ unpaid medical leave ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/unpaid-leave-for-medical-care/) * If you are a transgender man who has given birth, you have the right to take [ maternity leave ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/maternity-leave/) You can now apply for the new [ Enhanced Concrete Blocks Grant Scheme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/housing-grants-and- schemes/schemes-for-houses-affected-by-pyrite-or-mica/#aaf6f6) , if your home is in Clare, Donegal, Limerick or Mayo and has been affected by mica or pyrite. Since 28 June 2023, the Westport local electoral area is a [ Rent Pressure Zone ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/renting-a-home/rent- increases/) . If you are a [ non-EEA student coming to Ireland to study ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/moving-country/moving-to- ireland/studying-in-ireland/immigration-nonEEA-students/) , you must have evidence to show you can fund your stay in Ireland. If you are applying for a [ student visa ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/moving-country/visas- for-ireland/student-visas/) , you must show that you have access to €10,000 when you arrive in Ireland. If you are planning to study for up to 6 months, you must have €700 per month or €4,200 (whichever is lesser). Since 1 July 2023, [ EU Digital COVID Certificates (DCCs) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/travel- abroad/digital-covid-certificate/) are no longer available. The HSE will still give you [ evidence of your COVID-19 vaccination ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health- services/vaccinations/vaccines-for-covid19/) after you have been vaccinated.
