welfare payments and COVID-19 # Social welfare payments and COVID-19 * COVID-19 Pandemic Unemployment Payment * COVID-19 enhanced Illness Benefit ## COVID-19 Pandemic Unemployment Payment The [ COVID-19 Pandemic Unemployment Payment (PUP) ](/en/social- welfare/unemployed-people/covid19-pandemic-unemployment-payment/) was a social welfare payment for employees and self-employed people who lost all their employment due to the COVID-19 public health emergency. **The Scheme is closed.** ## COVID-19 enhanced Illness Benefit The [ enhanced Illness Benefit for COVID-19 absences ](/en/social- welfare/disability-and-illness/covid19-enhanced-illness-benefit/) ended on Friday, 30 September 2022. After 30 September 2022, if you are advised to self-isolate due to COVID-19 and have a medical certificate from your GP, you can apply for [ Illness Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and- illness/illness-benefit/) . _**Page edited:** 6 December 2022 _ [ ](https://facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social- welfare/covid19-and-social-welfare/?utm_source=sharebutton_facebook) [ ](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet/?text=Social%20welfare%20payments%20and%20COVID-19&url=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social- welfare/covid19-and-social-welfare/?utm_source=sharebutton_twitter) [ ](whatsapp://send?text=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social- welfare/covid19-and-social-welfare/?utm_source=sharebutton_whatsapp) [ ](mailto:?subject=Social%20welfare%20payments%20and%20COVID-19&body=https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social- welfare/covid19-and-social-welfare/?utm_source=sharebutton_email) [ ](javascript:void\(0\)) ### Related documents * [ _Social welfare payments and the student grant_ ](/en/social-welfare/back-to-education/social-welfare-payments-and-the-student-grant/) Information on claiming a social welfare payment, BTEA and the student grant. 2079.733 * [ _Maintenance and social welfare payments_ ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/means-test-for-social-welfare-payments/maintenance-and-social-welfare-payments/) If you have income from maintenance payments, this may be assessed in the means test for social welfare payments.
