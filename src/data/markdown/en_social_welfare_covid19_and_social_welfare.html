**English** | Gaeilge **You are here:** [ Home ](/en/) > [ Social Welfare ](/en/social-welfare/) > Social welfare payments and COVID-19 # Social welfare payments and COVID-19 * COVID-19 Pandemic Unemployment Payment * COVID-19 enhanced Illness Benefit ## COVID-19 Pandemic Unemployment Payment The [ COVID-19 Pandemic Unemployment Payment (PUP) ](/en/social- welfare/unemployed-people/covid19-pandemic-unemployment-payment/) was a social welfare payment for employees and self-employed people who lost all their employment due to the COVID-19 public health emergency. **The Scheme is closed.** ## COVID-19 enhanced Illness Benefit The [ enhanced Illness Benefit for COVID-19 absences ](/en/social- welfare/disability-and-illness/covid19-enhanced-illness-benefit/) ended on Friday, 30 September 2022.
