](/en/money-and-tax/tax/) > Duties and VAT # Duties and VAT * [ Stamp duty on financial cards ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/duties-and-vat/stamp-duty-on-financial-cards/) Stamp duty is charged on credit cards and charge cards annually. A transaction fee is charged on ATM withdrawals. * [ Value Added Tax ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/duties-and-vat/value-added-tax/) Value Added Tax (VAT) is included in the price of most products and services that we use every day. You may also be liable to pay VAT if you buy online. Find out more about VAT and how it is paid. * [ Excise duties ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/duties-and-vat/excise-duties/) A brief description of how excise duty is charged on alcohol, tobacco and other products. * [ Stamp duty on property ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-home/stamp-duty/) Stamp duty is paid on the transfer of property **This document is in:** [ Buying a home ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a- home/) * [ VAT refunds on aids and appliances used by people with disabilities ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/tax-credits-and-reliefs-for-people-with-disabilities/vat-refunds-on-aids-and-appliances-used-by-people-with-disabilities/) People with disabilities may claim refunds of Value-Added Tax (VAT) on certain aids and appliances. **This document is in:** [ Tax credits and reliefs for people with disabilities ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/tax-credits-and-reliefs-for-people-with- disabilities/) ### Most Read in "Duties and VAT" 1.
