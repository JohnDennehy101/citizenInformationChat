New 13 November 2023 ##### Site updates On 13 November 2023, the weekly income limit for the [ GP visit card ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit- cards/gp-visit-cards/) increased. You can check the current [ income limits for people aged under 70 ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit- cards/gp-visit-cards/#c2b86d) and see [ examples of how eligibility for the GP visit card is calculated ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit- cards/examples-of-calculating-eligibility-for-gp-visit-card-for-under-70s/) . The new age limit of 18 for [ Child Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare- payments/families-and-children/child-benefit/) will not apply before September 2024. From 1 December 2023, [ fines will increase ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/environment/pets-and-wildlife/control- of-dogs/#633f6f) for not having a dog licence, not having identification on a dog, allowing a dog to go stray, and for not keeping a dog under control. Applications for repairs to a protected structure under either the Historic Structures Fund or the Built Heritage Investment Scheme must be submitted to your local authority [ before 27 January 2024 ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/building-or-altering-a- home/protected-structures/#ae7f20) . If you are giving evidence in a Circuit Court case, you can now swear an [ affidavit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/justice/witnesses/affidavit/) for this by video conference. **New and
