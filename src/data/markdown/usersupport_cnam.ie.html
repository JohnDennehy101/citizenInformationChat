This means having a mix of different voices, opinions and sources of news. This means protecting children and all of us from harmful content. Our responsibilities are to: * Oversee the funding of and support the development of the wider media sector in Ireland. * Oversee the regulation of broadcasting and video-on-demand services. * Develop and enforce the Irish regulatory regime for online safety. ![](https://www.cnam.ie/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/CNAM-GROUP- MX-4-1-1024x683.jpg) Learn more [ about us ](https://www.cnam.ie/about-us/) and our Commissioners.
