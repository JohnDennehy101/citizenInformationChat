](/en/money-and-tax/budgets/) > Budget 2025 # Budget 2025 * Introduction ## Introduction **Budget 2025** will be announced on **Tuesday, 1 October 2024.** This document will summarise the main changes in taxation, cost of living supports, supports for energy costs, social welfare, health, housing, education, employment and other areas. ### More information on Budget 2025 You can access a range of information from gov.ie before the Budget: * [ Opening Statement by Minister Paschal Donohoe to the Committee on Budgetary Oversight ](https://www.gov.ie/en/speech/ad68c-minister-paschal-donohoe-opening-statement-to-the-committee-on-budgetary-oversight/) (18 September 2024) * [ The Budget Cycle ](https://www.gov.ie/en/policy-information/dc09f0-budget-cycle/) * [ Tax Strategy Group Papers (setting out options for tax policy changes) ](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/e9c8d-budget-2025-tax-strategy-group-papers/) * [ Where your money goes website ](https://whereyourmoneygoes.gov.ie/en/) * [ Pre-Budget submissions will be published on this page (not yet available for Budget 2025) ](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/1afd0-pre-budget-submissons/) You can also visit the [ Budget 2024 website (last year's Budget)
