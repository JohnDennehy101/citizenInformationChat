referendums on Friday 8 March 2024 # Voting in the referendums on Friday 8 March 2024 7 Márta 2024 ##### News ![Hand putting voting paper into ballot box](https://d2tha60j0slgae.cloudfront.net/images/Voting_paper_into_box.width-400.jpg) On Friday, 8 March 2024, Irish citizens are asked to [ vote in two constitutional referendums ](/ga/government-in-ireland/elections-and- referenda/voting/voting-in-a-referendum/) proposing to change the Constitution of Ireland. The changes are also known as the Family Amendment and the Care Amendment. * The Family Amendment will be on a white coloured ballot paper. * The Care Amendment will be on a green coloured ballot paper. You do not need a polling information card to vote. However, you may be asked for [ identification at the polling station ](/ga/government-in- ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/voting-in-a-referendum/) . The documents you can use for identification include your: * [ Passport ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/passports/) (either a passport card or passport book) * [ Driving licence ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/) * Workplace identity card (it must have a photograph) * Student identity card (it must have a photograph) * Travel document (it must have a photograph) * [ Public Services Card ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/public-services-card/) * Bank or credit union account book with your name and address in the constituency Find out more about the [ proposed changes to the Constitution
