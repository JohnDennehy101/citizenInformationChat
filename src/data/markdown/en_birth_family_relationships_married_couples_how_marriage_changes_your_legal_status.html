Marriage is a legal agreement with significant legal implications. It changes your legal status and affects your children's status as well, even after death. It also impacts your rights and responsibilities concerning: * [ Social welfare ](/en/birth-family-relationships/married-couples/marital-status-and-social-welfare-entitlements/) * [ Tax
