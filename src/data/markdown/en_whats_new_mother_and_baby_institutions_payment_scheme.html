**English** | Gaeilge **You are here:** [ Home ](/en/) > [ What's New ](/en/whats-new/) > Mother and Baby Institutions Payment Scheme # Mother and Baby Institutions Payment Scheme 9 April 2024 ##### News Did you spend time in a mother and baby home in Ireland? You may be able to get financial and health supports under the new [ Mother and Baby Institutions Payment Scheme. ](/en/birth-family- relationships/adoption-and-fostering/mother-and-baby-institutions-payment- scheme/) The payments will not affect: * 💶 [ Social welfare means tested ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/) payments * 🩺 [ Medical card ](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/medical-card/) * 👩🏻‍⚕️ [ GP visit card ](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/gp-visit-cards/) * 🏠 [ Fair Deal Scheme ](/en/health/health-services/health-services-for-older-people/fair-deal-scheme/) Find out how to apply and more 👉 [ Mother and Baby Institutions Payment Scheme. ](/en/birth-family-relationships/adoption-and-fostering/mother-and- baby-institutions-payment-scheme/) ### Follow Us * [ Facebook ](https://www.facebook.com/citizensinformation) * [ Twitter ](https://twitter.com/citizensinfo) * [ LinkedIn ](https://www.linkedin.com/company/citizens-information-board/) * [ Instagram ](https://www.instagram.com/citizensinformation/) ### Filter by tag * [ News ](/en/whats-new/?whatsnew_tags=news) * [ Videos ](/en/whats-new/?whatsnew_tags=videos) * [ Site updates ](/en/whats-new/?whatsnew_tags=site-updates) * [ Did you know?
