#  Vacant Property Refurbishment Grant

12 September 2023

#####  Videos

Want to refurbish a vacant or derelict property?

You can get a grant to help you. To qualify the property must have been:

✅ Vacant for 2 years or more

✅ Built before 2008

For more information, check out this video about the Vacant Property
Refurbishment Grant, or see our webpage:

[ Vacant Property Refurbishment Grant ](/en/housing/housing-grants-and-
schemes/local-authority-housing-grants-and-supports/vacant-property-
refurbishment-grant/)
