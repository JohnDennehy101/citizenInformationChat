New 11 December 2023 ##### Site updates From 1 January 2024, [ Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) ](/en/employment/employment- rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/sick-leave-and-sick-pay/) will increase from 3 to 5 days a year. You cannot get [ Illness Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare- payments/disability-and-illness/disability-benefit/) on the days that you get SSP. If your first illness in 2024 is less than 5 days and you qualify for Statutory Sick Pay, you don't need to apply for Illness Benefit. For illnesses lasting more than 5 days, Illness Benefit starts from day 6. If you use your 5 days of SSP in 2024 and you become ill again in the same year, you'll get Illness Benefit from day 4 of your illness, which is after the normal 3 waiting days. Two [ constitutional referendums ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and- referenda/types-of-elections-and-referendums/constitutional-referendum-in- ireland/) on [ family and care ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/government-in-ireland/irish- constitution-1/constitution-fundamental-rights/) will be held on 8 March 2024. If you are a [ refugee applying for a travel document ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/moving-country/asylum-seekers-and- refugees/refugee-status-and-leave-to-remain/travel-documents-for-refugees/) , you can do this online for a fee of €55. From April 2024, the [ Hot School Meals Programme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary- education/going-to-primary-school/giving-children-an-even-break-by-tackling- disadvantage/#d5392a) will be expanded to more schools. You can read the [ list of Schools approved for the Hot School Meals Programme from April 2024 ](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/937b9-list-of-schools-approved-for-the-hot- school-meals-programme-from-april-2024/) . From 1 January 2024, the maximum grant available under the [ Home Charger Grant Scheme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and- recreation/motoring/buying-or-selling-a-vehicle/electric-vehicles/#1f1908) will be €300. The Department of Education has set up [ a pilot counselling programme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary- education/educational-supports/psychological-assessment-school/) to a small number of primary schools in 7 counties. Since 6 December 2023, companies that [ provide mortgage protection insurance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a- home/insurance-protection-on-mortgages/) can ignore your cancer diagnosis if your treatment finished more than 7 years ago, or more than 5 years ago if you were diagnosed when you were under 18. The deadline for applications to preserve and maintain archaeological monuments under the [ Community Monument Fund ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/building-or-altering-a- home/protected-structures/) in 2024 will be set by your local authority, but this deadline should be no later than 1 February 2024. **New and recently edited** New pages: * [ Micro-generation
