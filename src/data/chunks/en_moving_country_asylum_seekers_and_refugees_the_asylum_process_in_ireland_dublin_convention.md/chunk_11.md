##  Transfers to other Dublin countries

Following a transfer decision, the [ Repatriation Unit
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/my-situation-has-changed-since-i-arrived-in-
ireland/spouse-civil-partner-of-irish-national-scheme/repatriation-division/)
of the Department of Justice will contact you with details of how your
transfer will take place.

If you appeal the transfer decision, you can stay in Ireland while you are
waiting for a decision on the appeal.
