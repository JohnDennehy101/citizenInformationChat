##  Rates

Instead of your usual payment, you will get a weekly allowance of:

  * €147 per week if you are single 
  * €239.70 per week if you are getting an increase for a qualified adult 

There are no increases in these rates for qualified children. The allowance is
paid regardless of how much you earn in your job. The allowance is not
taxable. However, your wages will be taxable in the usual way.
