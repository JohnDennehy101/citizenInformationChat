###  Work breaks and remote working

When you are [ working from home ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-
conditions/health-and-safety/working-at-home/) , you must get your daily and
weekly rest. The duty to comply with the rules on breaks and rest periods is
with the employer, not the employee. However, you should tell your employer if
you are not able to take rest periods or breaks and the reason why. You can
read more in the [ code of practice on the right to disconnect (pdf)
](https://www.workplacerelations.ie/en/what_you_should_know/codes_practice/code-
of-practice-for-employers-and-employees-on-the-right-to-disconnect.pdf) .
