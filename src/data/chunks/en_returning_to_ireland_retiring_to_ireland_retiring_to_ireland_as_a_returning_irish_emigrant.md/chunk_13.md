##  What other state benefits can I get?

You may be entitled to apply for other benefits in Ireland.

If you are getting an Irish state pension, you may also be entitled to:

  * [ Living Alone Increase ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/living-alone-allowance/)
  * [ Household Benefits Package ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/household-benefits-package/)
  * [ Fuel Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/fuel-allowance/)
  * [ Supplementary Welfare Allowance Scheme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social_welfare/social_welfare_payments/supplementary_welfare_schemes/)
  * [ Rent Supplement ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/rent-supplement/)
  * [ Island Increase ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/island-increase/)
  * [ Centenarian's Payment ](/en/social-welfare/older-and-retired-people/centenarians-bounty/)
  * [ Carer's Support Grant ](/en/social-welfare/carers/carers-support-grant/)
  * [ Half-rate Carer's Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/carers/half-rate-carers-allowance/)

Read about the [ benefits available with Contributory ](/en/social-
welfare/social-welfare-payments/older-and-retired-people/state-pension-
