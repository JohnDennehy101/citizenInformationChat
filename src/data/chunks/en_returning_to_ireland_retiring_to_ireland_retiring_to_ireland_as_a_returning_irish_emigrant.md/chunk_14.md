###  Winter Fuel Payment UK

If you get a UK state pension and you live in Ireland, you may be able to
claim the [ UK Winter Fuel Payment ](https://www.gov.uk/winter-fuel-
payment/if-you-live-
abroad#:~:text=If%20you%20do%20not%20live,having%20family%20in%20the%20UK) .

You qualify for the payment if:

  * You moved to Ireland before 1 January 2021 
  * You were born before 26 September 1956 
  * You have a genuine and sufficient link to the UK, for example, you previously lived or worked in the UK 

The UK Winter Fuel Payment is taken into account in the means test for Fuel
Allowance in Ireland.
