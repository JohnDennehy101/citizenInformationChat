###  **Cost of Education Allowance**

You get an annual Cost of Education Allowance of €500 if you are getting BTEA
and you have a dependent child.

The maximum Cost of Education Allowance available per household is €500, even
if you and a qualified adult both get BTEA.

You cannot get the Cost of Education Allowance if you are:

  * On the [ Part-Time Education Option ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/back-to-education/part-time-education-option/) , including Springboard+ and ICT skills courses 
  * Studying a full-time [ Springboard+ or Human Capital Initiative Pillar 1 ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/418e3f-back-to-education-allowance/#springboard-and-human-capital-initiative-courses) course 
  * On a Skillnet Ireland course
