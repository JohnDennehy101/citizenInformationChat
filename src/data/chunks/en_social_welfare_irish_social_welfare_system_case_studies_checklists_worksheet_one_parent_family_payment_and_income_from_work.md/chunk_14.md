####  Working Family Payment (WFP)

You can claim WFP if you are working at least 19 hours per week, or 38 hours
per fortnight, and your income falls below the income threshold for your
family size.

[ Working Family Payment ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/working-
family-payment/) is not taxable and does not affect secondary benefits.
