####  Other tax free allowances

You may be able to claim [ Mortgage Interest Relief ](/en/money-and-
tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/housing-tax-credits-and-reliefs/) and [
tax relief on medical expenses ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-
reliefs/taxation-and-medical-expenses/) .

If you are a tenant in private-rented accommodation, you may be able to claim
some [ tax relief for rent paid ](/en/housing/renting-a-home/help-with-
renting/tax-issues-for-renters/) .

If you are a widowed parent, you can claim a [ Widowed Parent Tax Credit
](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/income-tax-credits-and-
reliefs-following-a-death/) .

[ You can get more information about tax credits and reliefs ](/en/money-and-
tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/) .
