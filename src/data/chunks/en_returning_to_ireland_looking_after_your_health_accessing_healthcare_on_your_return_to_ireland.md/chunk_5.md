##  Find a GP (doctor) in Ireland

In Ireland, your family doctor is known as a [ GP (General Practitioner)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/gp-and-
hospital-services/gps-and-private-patients/) . A GP is usually the first
doctor you will see about non-emergency illnesses and health issues. They can
also refer you to hospitals and other medical services if you need urgent and
specialist treatment.

see if you are still registered with them, or if you can re-register. You can
