##  Compulsory subjects

Within these programmes, there are a number of compulsory subjects. The
subjects that are compulsory vary for different programmes and different types
of school. All third-level courses have [ minimum subject requirements for
entry ](/en/education/third-level-education/applying-to-college/application-
procedures-and-entry-requirements/) . These often include Maths, English and
Irish. You should check these minimum requirements before deciding which
subjects to take for the Leaving Certificate.

Irish is compulsory in most programmes. [ In some very limited circumstances,
a student may not have to study Irish ](/en/education/the-irish-education-
system/exemption-from-irish/) .
