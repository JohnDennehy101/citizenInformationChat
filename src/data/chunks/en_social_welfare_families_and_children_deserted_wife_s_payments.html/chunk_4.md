##  Rates

**Weekly** **Deserted Wife’s Benefit rate 2024**

**PRSI Contributions** |  **Rate per week (aged under 66)** |  **Rate per week (aged 66 or over)**  
---|---|---  
48 or over  |  €237.50  |  €277.30   
36-47  |  €233.90  |  €271.90   
24-35  |  €231.20  |  €265.50   
  
You may also get an [ increase in your payment for child dependants
](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-
payment/claiming-and-increase-in-your-payment-for-a-child-dependant/) and [
extra benefits such as Living Alone Allowance and Fuel Allowance ](/en/social-
welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/extra-social-welfare-benefits/) .

**Reduced rates of Deserted Wife's Benefit:**

Any new application for Deserted Wife’s Benefit received on or after 31 August
1992 is subject to an earnings limit as well as satisfying the PRSI
contribution condition. You will get one of the rates of Deserted Wife’s
Benefit in the table above if your yearly income is less than €20,000. You are
not entitled to Deserted Wife’s Benefit if your earnings are over €20,000.

It is important to note, a reduced rate of Deserted Wife's Benefit based on
earnings or insufficient PRSI contributions does not apply to claims made
before 31 August 1992.
