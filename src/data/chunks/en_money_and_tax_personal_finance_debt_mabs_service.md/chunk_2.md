##  Introduction

The [ Money Advice and Budgeting Service (MABS) ](http://www.mabs.ie/)
provides a free and confidential service if you are having problems managing
your money or problems with debt.

MABS money advisers can give advice and practical help to review your debts,
deal with your creditors and work out solutions.

MABS has a dedicated service to help with mortgage arrears. MABS is also
involved in the [ Abhaile ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/mortgage-
arrears/abhaile-mortgage-arrears-support/) scheme for people in serious
mortgage arrears.

MABS provides [ money management education ](https://www.mabs.ie/en/about/how-
we-help/partnership-work-in-education/) and produces a range of [ publications
](https://www.mabs.ie/en/about/publications/) . It also has [ money tools
](https://mabs.ie/money-tools/) you can use to help manage your money and
tackle any debts.
