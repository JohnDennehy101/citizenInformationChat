###  Proof of age and written permission from parents

Before starting a job, young workers must provide proof of age, such as a
birth certificate.

Those under 16 also need written permission from a parent or guardian, which
must be given to the employer.

The law on young workers is set out in the [ Protection of Young Persons
(Employment) Act 1996
](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1996/en/act/pub/0016/index.html) .
