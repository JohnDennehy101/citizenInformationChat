##  Tax implications

Residence and domicile are taken into account for a number of taxes including
[ income tax ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/how-your-tax-is-calculated/) ,
[ Deposit Interest Retention Tax (DIRT) ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/tax-on-
savings-and-investments/deposit-interest-retention-tax/) , [ gift tax
](/en/money-and-tax/tax/capital-taxes/capital-acquisitions-tax/) , [
inheritance tax ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/capital-taxes/capital-acquisitions-
tax/) and [ Capital Gains Tax
](http://www.revenue.ie/en/tax/cgt/leaflets/index.html) .
