##  Appealing a decision on habitual residency

If you are not happy with the decision made in your case, you can appeal the
decision to the independent [ Social Welfare Appeals Office ](/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-welfare-appeals/) . You must appeal
within 21 days of getting the decision. Read more about [ Social Welfare
Appeals ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-welfare-
appeals/) and see our [ checklist when appealing a social welfare decision
](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/case-studies-
checklists/checklist-when-appealing-a-social-welfare-decision/) .
