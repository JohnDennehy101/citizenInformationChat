##  Rates

If you satisfy the conditions for Carer’s Allowance it will be awarded at 50%
of the rate that would apply if you were not getting any other payment.

**Maximum** **half-rate Carer’s Allowance rates 2024**

**Half-rate Carer’s Allowance** |  **Under 66** |  **Age 66 and over**  
---|---|---  
Caring for one person  |  €124  |  €143   
Caring for two or more  |  €186  |  €214.50   
  
Any increase you may be entitled to, for example, an [ increase for a
qualified child ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-
social-welfare-payment/claiming-and-increase-in-your-payment-for-a-child-
dependant/) , living alone and living on an offshore island will be paid with
your main social welfare payment and **not** with the half-rate Carer’s
Allowance.
