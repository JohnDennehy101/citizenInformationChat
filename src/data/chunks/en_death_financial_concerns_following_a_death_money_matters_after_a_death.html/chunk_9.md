##  Help with money matters

If you are experiencing financial difficulties following a bereavement, you
can get advice from your local Money Advice and Budgeting Service (MABS). MABS
is a free, confidential, independent service staffed by trained money
advisers. Contact details are available in your local telephone directory or [
on the MABS website ](http://www.mabs.ie/) .
