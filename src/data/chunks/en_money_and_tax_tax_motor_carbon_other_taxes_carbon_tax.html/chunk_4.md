##  Further information

You can find [ detailed information about the Solid Fuel Carbon Tax
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/companies-and-charities/excise-and-
licences/energy-taxes/solid-fuel-carbon-tax/index.aspx) on the Revenue
website, including the [ amount of tax per tonne of solid fuel
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/companies-and-charities/excise-and-
licences/energy-taxes/solid-fuel-carbon-tax/rate-of-tax.aspx) .

You can also read more about [ Natural Gas Carbon Tax
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/companies-and-charities/excise-and-
licences/energy-taxes/natural-gas-carbon-tax/index.aspx) and [ Mineral Oil Tax
on vehicle gas ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/companies-and-charities/excise-and-
licences/mineral-oil-tax/vehicle-gas/index.aspx) .
