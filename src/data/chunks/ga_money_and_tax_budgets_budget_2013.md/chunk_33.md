####  Back to School Clothing and Footwear Allowance

The Back to School Clothing and Footwear Allowance will reduce by €50 per
child. This means that the Back to School Clothing and Footwear Allowance will
reduce from €250 to €200 for children aged 12-17 or aged 18-22 in full-time
education and from €150 to €100 for children aged 4-11 years. (2013)
