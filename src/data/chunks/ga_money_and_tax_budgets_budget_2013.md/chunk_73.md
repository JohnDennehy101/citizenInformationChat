####  Vehicle Registration Tax (VRT)

Rates of VRT are being increased with effect from 1 January 2013. There is a
revised structure for vehicles taxed on the basis of CO2 emissions.

The VRT reliefs currently in place for electric vehicles (up to €5,000), plug-
in hybrid electric vehicles (up to €2,500), and hybrid and flexible fuel
vehicles (up to €1,500) are being retained for a further 12 months to the end
of December 2013.
