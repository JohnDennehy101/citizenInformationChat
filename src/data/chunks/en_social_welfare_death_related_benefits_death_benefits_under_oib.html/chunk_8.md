##  How to apply

A claim for any of the payments under the Death Benefit Scheme should be made
within 3 months of the date of death, otherwise you may lose some benefit.

Complete claim form [ OB 61(pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/19635/5f3892d28336479bb97e7dc5e64676d5.pdf) which is
available from your local social welfare office. Send your completed claim
form to the Death Benefit Section at the address below.
