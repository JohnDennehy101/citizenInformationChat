##  Offences and penalties

If you break the rules of the road, you can be [ fined
](https://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/special-purpose-vehicles/powered-
personal-transportation#:~:text=No%20front%20lamp,warden%20stop%20sign.)
between €40 and €80.

You must not ride your e-bike under the influence of alcohol or drugs. Riding
under the influence can result in a court appearance and a fine of up to
€2,000.
