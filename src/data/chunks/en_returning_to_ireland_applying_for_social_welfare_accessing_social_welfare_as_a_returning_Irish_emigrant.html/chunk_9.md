##  How do I apply for a social welfare payment?

You must fill in an application form and provide other supporting
documentation, which the DSP assesses. The information and documentation you
provide is different depending on the payment type and your personal
circumstances.

You can [ apply online for some payments ](https://services.mywelfare.ie/) ,
such as Jobseeker’s allowance.

If you can’t apply online, you can get an application or claim form from:

  * The [ Department of Social Protection ](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/a9ac9-social-welfare-application-forms/)
  * Your local [ Intreo Centre or Social Welfare Branch Office ](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) , or 
  * Your [ local Citizens Information Centre ](http://centres.citizensinformation.ie/) . 

To apply for a social welfare payment, you must have a [ Personal Public
Service Number (PPS number) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-
to-ireland/applying-for-social-welfare/pps-numbers-for-returning-irish-
emigrants-and-families/) .
