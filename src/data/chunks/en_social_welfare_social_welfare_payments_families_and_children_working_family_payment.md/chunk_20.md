###  Income from rent

Rental income from the letting of property or land is assessed and you cannot
deduct mortgage payments or other expenses.

However, you can get up to [ €14,000 per year for renting out a room in your
own home ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/owning-a-home/home-
owners/renting-a-room-in-your-home/#b0e931) without it affecting your Working
Family Payment.

The maximum income disregard is €269.23 a week (€14,000 per year).
