##  Introduction

The [ Consumer Rights Act 2022 (pdf)
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2022/act/37/enacted/en/pdf) introduces
new rights when you have a problem with goods you bought in a shop or online.
This page explains these consumer rights. They only apply to goods you bought
**on or** **after 29 November 2022.**

You have different rights if you have a problem with goods you bought [ on or
before 28 November 2022
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/products-and-services-bought-
before-november-2022/your-rights-if-you-bought-in-a-shop-before-
november-2022/) .
