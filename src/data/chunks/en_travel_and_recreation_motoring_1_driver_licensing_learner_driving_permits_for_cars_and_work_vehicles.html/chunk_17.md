###  **Your first learner permit**

To get your first learner permit to drive a bus or truck, you must have a full
driving licence for a car (Category B) and a valid [ driver theory test
certificate ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driver-theory-
test/) for:

  * Category C if you want to drive a truck 
  * Category D if you want to drive a bus
