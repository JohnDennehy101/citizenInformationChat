###  Weekly payment

Fuel Allowance is usually paid from the end of September to April each year
for 28 weeks. In 2024/2025, the Fuel Allowance starts on Monday, 23 September
2024. Fuel Allowance is included in your weekly payment, if you are getting a
social welfare payment.

If you are not getting a social welfare payment, your Fuel Allowance can be
paid directly into your account in a financial institution or you can collect
it from your local post office.
