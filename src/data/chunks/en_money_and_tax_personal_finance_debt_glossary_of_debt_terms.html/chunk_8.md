###  Code of Conduct on Mortgage Arrears (CCMA)

The CCMA is a statutory code, issued by the Central Bank of Ireland, which
requires mortgage lenders to follow specific procedures when dealing with
borrowers who are facing **mortgage arrears** . Under this Code, each lender
must have a **Mortgage Arrears Resolution Process (MARP)** . [ Read more about
Consumer protection codes and mortgages ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/mortgage-
arrears/consumer-protection-codes-and-mortgages/) .
