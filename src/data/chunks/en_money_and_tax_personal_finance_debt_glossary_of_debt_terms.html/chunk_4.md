###  Abhaile Scheme

[ Abhaile ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/mortgage-arrears/abhaile-mortgage-
arrears-support/) is a free scheme that helps homeowners who are in **mortgage
arrears** and at risk of losing their homes. The [ Money Advice and Budgeting
Service (MABS) ](https://mabs.ie/) provides access to this service.
