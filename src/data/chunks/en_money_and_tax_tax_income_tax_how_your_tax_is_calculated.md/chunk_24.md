##  Contact

Contact [ Revenue’s PAYE jobs and pensions helpline
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-us/customer-service-contact/paye-jobs-and-
pensions-helpline.aspx) .

You can also [ make an enquiry or view and update your tax details online
](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/online-services-for-paye-taxpayers/) using
Revenue’s [ myAccount ](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-web/home.html) service.
