####  Step 1: Determine your taxable income

Subtract the following from your total income to get your taxable income:

  * Pension contributions 
  * Salary used for a [ Travel Pass Scheme ](/en/travel-and-recreation/public-transport/public-transport-commuter-tickets-and-tax/) or [ Bike to Work Scheme ](/en/travel-and-recreation/cycling/cycle-to-work-scheme/)
  * Payments to a [ Permanent Health Benefit Scheme ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-credits-reliefs-and-exemptions/health-and-age/permanent-health-benefit-contributions/index.aspx) (to a maximum of 10% of income).
