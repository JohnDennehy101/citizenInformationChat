##  Further information

Crosscare Irish Diaspora Support Project has a [ range of useful resources
](https://diasporasupport.ie/returning-to-ireland/returning-with-non-irish-
family/) to help you to bring your non-EEA family members home to Ireland.

Immigration Services Division (ISD) has a [ family reunification policy (pdf)
](http://www.justice.ie/en/JELR/Family%20Reunification%20Policy%20Document.pdf/Files/Family%20Reunification%20Policy%20Document.pdf)
, which sets out rules for how families from outside the EEA, UK and
Switzerland can join people who live in Ireland.
