####  CE, RSS and Tús

You can apply for Fuel Allowance at any point when you are on CE, RSS or Tús
if you meet all the relevant qualifying conditions for Fuel Allowance. If you
were getting a long-term qualifying payment before starting CE, RSS or Tús,
you satisfy the qualifying payment requirement.

For the 2024/2025 Fuel Allowance season, time spent on CE, Tús and RSS can be
used to meet the minimum number of days on a qualifying payment for Fuel
Allowance.
