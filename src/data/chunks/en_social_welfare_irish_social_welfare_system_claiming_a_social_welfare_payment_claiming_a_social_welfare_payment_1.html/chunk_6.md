####  **How long to process claims**

It may take some time for the Department to process your claim. As part of the
procedure, you may be interviewed in your home or asked to attend an interview
at your local social welfare office.

While your claim is being processed by the Department, you may qualify for [
Supplementary Welfare Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-
schemes/supplementary-welfare-allow/) .
