#####  News

Are you getting a [ social welfare payment ](/en/social-welfare/) ?

Over Easter, your payment dates may change because of the holidays.

If your payment is due on Good Friday, 29 March or bank holiday Monday, 1
April you will get it on Thursday, 28 March 2024 instead.

[ Payment dates ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/22d98-easter-payment-
arrangements-2024/) will go back to normal from Tuesday, 2 April.
