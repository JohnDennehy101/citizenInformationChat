#  Personal insolvency

  * Overview 

[ Personal insolvency options ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-
finance/debt/personal-insolvency/personal-insolvency-options/)

There are 3 debt resolution procedures to help people with unsustainable debt
to reach agreements with their creditors.

  * [ Debt Relief Notices ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/debt/personal-insolvency/debt-relief-notices/)

People on very low incomes who cannot pay their debts may apply for a Debt
Relief Notice.

  * [ Debt Settlement Arrangements ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/debt/personal-insolvency/debt-settlement-arrangements/)

People who cannot pay their unsecured debts can apply for a Debt Settlement
Arrangement.

  * [ Personal Insolvency Arrangements ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/debt/personal-insolvency/personal-insolvency-arrangements/)

People with secured debts who cannot pay their debts can apply for a Personal
Insolvency Arrangement.

  * [ What is bankruptcy? ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/debt/personal-insolvency/what-is-bankruptcy/)

If you cannot repay your debts and the personal insolvency alternatives are
not suitable, bankruptcy may be a way to settle your debts.
