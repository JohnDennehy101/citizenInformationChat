####  Expenses that are excluded

You cannot claim tax back for expenses refunded by:

  * A public or local authority, for example, the HSE (Health Service Executive) 
  * An insurance policy 
  * Any other source, for example, compensation 

If you have private health insurance, you can claim tax relief on the portion
of those qualifying expenses not covered by your insurer.

You cannot claim relief for cosmetic surgery costs, unless you need the
surgery as a result of a personal injury, disease or congenital abnormality.
