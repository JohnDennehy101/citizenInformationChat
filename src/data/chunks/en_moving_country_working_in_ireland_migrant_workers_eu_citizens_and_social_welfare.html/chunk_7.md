##  Claiming social welfare if you lose your job or you are sick

EU workers (including self-employed people) have the [ right to reside in
Ireland ](/en/moving-country/moving-to-ireland/rights-of-residence-in-
ireland/residence-rights-eu-national/) . This applies to EEA and Swiss
citizens also. If you stop working, you can keep your right to reside status
for a period in some circumstances.

You may need to claim a social welfare payment, if you become unemployed or
cannot work because you are sick or incapacitated.
