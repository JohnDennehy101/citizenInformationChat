#####  Did you know?

From 1 October 2024 it will be illegal to import, breed, rehome or resell an
XL Bully dog.

From 1 February 2025, it will be illegal to own an XL Bully unless you have a
Certificate of Exemption.

The regulations to bring in this ban are being developed.

For more information on how to get a Certificate of Exemption check our page
on [ dog licences and ownership ](/en/environment/pets-and-wildlife/control-
of-dogs/) .
