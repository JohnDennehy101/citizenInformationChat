###  How can I get a JAM Card?

You can get it in any of the following 3 ways:

  * [ Order a physical card online ](https://jamcard.org/register/get-a-card) . 
  * Phone the NOW Group (based in UK) on 0044 28 9043 6400. 
  * [ Download the app from the Play Store or Apple ](https://www.jamcard.org/jam-card-for-me) .
