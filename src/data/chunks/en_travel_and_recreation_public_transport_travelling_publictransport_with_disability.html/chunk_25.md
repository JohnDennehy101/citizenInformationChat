###  Disabled car parking at train stations

All station car parks have designated car parking spaces for people with a [
Disabled Person’s Parking Card ](/en/travel-and-recreation/traffic-and-
parking/disabled-persons-parking-card/) . You can get information on station
accessibility on [ each station’s webpage ](https://www.irishrail.ie/en-
ie/travel-information/find-a-station) .
