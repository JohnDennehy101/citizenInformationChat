##  How do I use Long-Term Carers Contributions to qualify for the State
Pension (Contributory)?

To get a State Pension (Contributory), you must have a certain number of paid
contributions. The number of contributions also determines your rate of
pension.

Once you have reached 20 years (1,040 weeks) of providing full-time care, you
can include Long-Term Carers Contributions in the calculation of your [ State
Pension (Contributory). ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/social-welfare-payments/older-and-retired-people/state-pension-
contributory/) If you are entitled to Long-Term Carers Contributions, you do
not need 10 years of employment contributions to qualify for State Pension
(Contributory).
