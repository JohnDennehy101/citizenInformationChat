##  What is the Dependent Relative Tax Credit?

If you pay income tax, [ a tax credit reduces your tax by the amount of the
credit
](../income_tax_credits_and_reliefs/introduction_to_income_tax_credits_and_reliefs.en.html#lb4129)
.

If you maintain a dependent relative, you may qualify for the Dependent
Relative Tax Credit. Maintaining a relative means meeting the costs of
everyday living.

To qualify for the tax credit, the income of your relative must be below a
limit.
