####  Back to Work Enterprise Allowance

Under the [ Back to Enterprise Work Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/social-
welfare-payments-and-work/back-to-work-enterprise-allowance/) , you can become
self-employed and keep a certain amount of your social welfare payment.

**Retaining Rent Supplement**

If you have been unemployed or not in full-time employment for at least 12
months and are assessed as in need of housing under the [ Rental Accommodation
Scheme ](/en/housing/local-authority-and-social-housing/rental-accommodation-
scheme/) you may be entitled to retain your [ Rent Supplement. ](/en/social-
welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/rent-supplement/)
