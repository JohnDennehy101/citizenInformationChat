###  Getting the work done safely

Under Regulations governing safety, health and welfare at work, homeowners
must ensure that construction work on their home is carried out by competent
people. The [ Health and Safety Authority ](http://www.hsa.ie/eng/) has
published a useful [ Guide for Homeowners: Getting construction work done
safely (pdf)
](http://www.hsa.ie/eng/Publications_and_Forms/Publications/Construction/homeowners_guidance.pdf)
and some [ Frequently Asked Questions
](http://www.hsa.ie/eng/Your_Industry/Construction/Construction_FAQ's/Homeowners_FAQs.html)
.
