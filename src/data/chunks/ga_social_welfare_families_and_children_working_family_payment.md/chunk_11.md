####  **Tuismitheoireacht** **ina n-aonar**

Más tuismitheoir aonair thú, d’fhéadfá a bheith i dteideal WFP de bhreis ar [
d’Íocaíocht Teaghlaigh Aontuismitheora
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/families-and-children/one-parent-family-payment/) , [ Sochar

Mharthanaigh (Ranníocach) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/social-welfare-payments/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-
