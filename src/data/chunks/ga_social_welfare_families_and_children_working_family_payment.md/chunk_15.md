####  **Íocaíochtaí** **nach** **féidir a éileamh le WFP**

Ní féidir leat WFP a fháil, má tá tú ar cheann de na scéimeanna seo nó
íocaíochtaí leasa shóisialaigh:

  * [ Scéim Fostaíochta Pobail ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/employment-support-schemes/community-employment-scheme/) , [ Scéim Shóisialta na Tuaithe ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/farming-and-fishing/rural-social-scheme/) , nó [ scéim Tús ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/employment-support-schemes/tus/) . 
  * [ Sochar Cuardaitheora Poist ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/unemployed-people/jobseekers-benefit/) , [ Liúntas Cuardaitheora Poist ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/unemployed-people/jobseekers-allowance/) , [ Íocaíocht Idirthréimhseach do Lucht Cuardaigh Fostaíochta ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/unemployed-people/jobseekers-transitional-payment/) nó [ Cúnamh Feirme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/farming-and-fishing/farm-assist/)
  * [ Scéim dhreasachta post páirtaimseartha ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/social-welfare-payments-and-work/part-time-job-incentive-scheme/) . 

Is féidir le do chéile, do pháirtnéir sibhialta nó do chomhchónaitheoir WFP a
éileamh fad is atá ceann de na híocaíochtaí seo á fháil agat. Ní íocfar Méadú
do Dhuine Fásta Cáilithe a thuilleadh, áfach, agus déanfar d’íocaíocht leasa
shóisialaigh a mheasúnú mar ioncam dá WFP. Cuirfear isteach ar aon Mhéadú do
Leanbh Cáilithe (IQC).

Ar an gcaoi chéanna, má tá ceann de na híocaíochtaí seo á fáil ag do chéile,
do pháirtnéir sibhialta nó do chomhchónaitheoir, féadfaidh tú cáiliú le
haghaidh WFP, ach ní íocfar IQA leo a thuilleadh ar do shon.
