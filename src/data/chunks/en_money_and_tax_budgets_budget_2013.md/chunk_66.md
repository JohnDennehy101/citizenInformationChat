####  Other measures for schools

The overhaul of the Junior Cycle will be supported by an investment of €3
million in 2013, and €8.7 million in 2014.

The Literacy and Numeracy Strategy will continue to be rolled out at a cost of
€6.5 million in 2013.

Approximately 900 jobs will be created for teachers – 450 at primary level and
450 at second level.
