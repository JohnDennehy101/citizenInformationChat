####  Household Charge and NPPR

The Household Charge will cease with effect from 1 January 2013. The Non-
Principal Private Residence (NPPR) Charge will cease with effect from 1
January 2014. However, unpaid arrears together with any interest and penalties
that have accrued will remain a charge on the property to which they relate.
