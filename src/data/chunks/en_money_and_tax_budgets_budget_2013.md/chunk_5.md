###  Other income tax changes

Maternity Benefit will be taxable for all claimants. Universal Social Charge
will not be payable. (1 July 2013)

Top Slicing Relief, which is a reduction of tax due on lump sum payments when
leaving employment, will not be available on lump sums of €200,000 or more. It
will apply to payments made on or after 1 January 2013.

Tax relief on all charitable donations will now be claimed by the charitable
organisation at a single tax rate of 31%. The maximum qualifying amount will
be €1 million.
