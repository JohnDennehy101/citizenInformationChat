##  Introduction

Local Link buses connect towns and villages in rural Ireland. Local Link
services are part of the TFI (Transport for Ireland) Public Transport Network.

Local Link services are expanding, so [ keep an eye out for new routes
](https://www.transportforireland.ie/) .

On this page, you can find out about:

  * Your Local Link services 
  * How much it costs 
  * Ways to pay 
  * Community Car Schemes 
  * Getting to the hospital for an appointment
