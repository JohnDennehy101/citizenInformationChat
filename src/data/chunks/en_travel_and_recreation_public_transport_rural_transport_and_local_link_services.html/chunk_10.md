###  **Leap Card**

You can also save money using a [ Leap Card ](http://www.leapcard.ie/) (pre-
paid travel card), which gives you a discounted ticket.

The [ Young Adult (19-25) or Student Leap Card
](https://about.leapcard.ie/young-adult-and-student-card-
launch#:~:text=You%20can%20get%2050%25%20off,all%2019%2D25%20year%20olds.) are
accepted on Local Link services.
