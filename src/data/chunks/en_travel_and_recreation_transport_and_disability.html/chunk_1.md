#  Transport and disability

  * [ Tax relief for drivers and passengers with disabilities ](/en/travel-and-recreation/transport-and-disability/tax-relief-for-disabled-drivers-and-disabled-passengers/)

The Disabled Drivers and Disabled Passengers Scheme provides a range of tax
reliefs for disabled drivers and disabled passengers.

  * [ Mobility training for people with visual impairments ](/en/travel-and-recreation/transport-and-disability/mobility-training-for-people-with-a-visual-impairment/)

If you have a visual impairment, orientation and mobility training can help
you develop a range of skills that will make it easier to move around safely.
Types of mobility aid and sources of training are described here.

  * [ Motorised Transport Grant ](/en/travel-and-recreation/transport-and-disability/motorised-transport-grant/)

The Motorised Transport Grant is a grant available to people in Ireland with
disabilities who want to buy a car or adapt an existing car. The scheme is
closed to new applicants.

  * [ Mobility Allowance ](/en/travel-and-recreation/transport-and-disability/mobility-allowance/)

The mobility allowance is a payment by the Health Service Executive (HSE) to
people who are unable to walk and who would benefit from a change in
surroundings. The scheme is closed to new applicants.

  * [ Disabled Person's Parking Card ](/en/travel-and-recreation/traffic-and-parking/disabled-persons-parking-card/)

The European Parking Card scheme makes free parking available for disabled
drivers and passengers in Ireland.
