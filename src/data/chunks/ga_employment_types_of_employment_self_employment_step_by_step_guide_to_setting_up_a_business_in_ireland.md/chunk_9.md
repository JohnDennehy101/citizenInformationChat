##  Deontais agus tacaí eile

Tá roinnt gníomhaireachtaí agus eagraíochtaí rialtais tiomnaithe do thacaíocht
a thabhairt do ghnólachtaí nua ar fud na hÉireann.

Léigh faoi:

  * [ Conas maoiniú a fháil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/starting-a-business/#6c48a3)
  * [ Oiliúint agus comhairle gnó a fháil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/starting-a-business/#f10688)
  * [ Deacrachtaí creidmheasa a bhainistiú ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/starting-a-business/#d9e9f0)
  * [ Láithreáin ghréasáin le haghaidh gnó a bhunú ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/finding-information-on-starting-business/#969d1a)
  * [ An comhlacht ionadaíoch a aimsiú do d’earnáil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/finding-information-on-starting-business/#a0bb9a)

Má tá fócas ag do mhúnla gnó ar inbhuanaitheacht, b’fhéidir gur mhaith leat
léamh faoi na [ tacaíochtaí airgeadais atá ar fáil do ghnóthais atá ag dul
glas ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-
employment/self-employment/supports-for-businesses-going-green/) .
