###  Mortgage protection insurance

When taking out a mortgage, you need to think about how it will be paid off if
you die. You will generally be required to take out mortgage protection
insurance. You should also think about how to continue repayments if your
income falls, due to illness, unemployment or other reasons. You can [ find
out more about insurance protection on mortgages ](/en/housing/owning-a-
home/buying-a-home/insurance-protection-on-mortgages/) .
