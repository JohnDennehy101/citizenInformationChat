###  Local authority loan arrears

If you have a loan from a local authority and you are having difficulty making
your repayments, you may be able to make an arrangement with the local
authority. Local authorities have [ guidelines on dealing with mortgage
arrears
](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/108738/2295fd29-65d6-420e-84fb-d33abbcf7099.pdf%23page=null)
.

You can read a short [ guide to resolving your mortgage arrears problem
](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/108741/68254024-78a9-4f10-a30d-3f4eff6cea9c.pdf#page=null)
.
