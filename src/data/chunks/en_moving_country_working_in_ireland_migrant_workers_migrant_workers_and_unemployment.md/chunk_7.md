###  **Jobseeker's Allowance**

If you are not entitled to Jobseeker’s Benefit, you can apply for [
Jobseeker’s Allowance (JA) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/social-welfare-payments/unemployed-people/jobseekers-allowance/) .
Jobseeker’s Allowance is a means-tested payment. To get JA you must be
unemployed, looking for work and pass a means test. You must also be [
habitually resident ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-assistance-payments/habitual-
residence-condition/) to get JA.
