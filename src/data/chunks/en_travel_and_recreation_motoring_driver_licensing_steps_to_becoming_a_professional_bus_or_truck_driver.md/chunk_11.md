##  Step 7: Apply for your driving licence

You can [ apply online ](https://www.ndls.ie/index.php) or in person for a
driving licence when you pass a [ driving test
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-
recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driving-test/) . The cost of a new licence
is €55, or €35 if you’re adding the new category to your category B licence.

Find out in our page about [ applying for a driving licence
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-
licensing/full-driving-licence/) :

  * [ How long is a driving licence valid? ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/full-driving-licence/#894665)
