##  Step 1: Pass the theory test

If you are a new bus or truck driver, you must complete a [ theory test
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-
recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driver-theory-test/#f41842) . The test is
computer based and the format of the test is multiple choice questions. You
should [ prepare for the theory test ](https://theorytest.ie/revision-
material/) . Study the [ Rules of the Road
](https://www.rsa.ie/services/learner-drivers/resources/rules-of-the-road) and
the [ Safe Driving for Work Handbook ](https://drivingforwork.ie/wp-
content/uploads/2017/10/Safe_Driving_for_Work_Handbook_.pdf) .

You can take the test at any [ theory test centre (pdf)
](https://theorytest.ie/wp-content/uploads/theory-test-ireland-test-centre-
location-map-051017.pdf) nationwide. You can [ apply and pay for your driver
theory test online ](http://www.theorytest.ie/driver-theory-test/schedule-
test/) . The test costs â‚¬72. Make sure you bring [ valid identification
](https://theorytest.ie/general-information/id-requirements/) on the day of
your theory test.
