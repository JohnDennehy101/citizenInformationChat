###  **Student grant**

[ Maintenance grant ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/third-
level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/student-grant-
scheme/) rates will increase by €615 for all non-adjacent rates and by 10% for
adjacent rates from September 2024 - a pro-rata increase will be available
from January 2024.

The postgraduate tuition fee grant will increase by €1,000 for students in the
2023/2024 academic year.

There will be a full restoration of maintenance grants under the [ Student
Grant Scheme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/third-level-
education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/student-grant-scheme/)
for postgraduate students from September 2024.
