**Child Benefit**  
---  
**Year** |  **2023** |  **2024**  
**Monthly rate, €** |  140.00 per child  |  140.00 (no change)

for each child.  **All other multiple births:** Child Benefit is paid at
double the normal monthly rate for each child.
