###  **Social and affordable housing**

In 2024, 9,300 new build social homes are expected to be built. Social housing
will also be delivered through the strategic acquisitions programme. 2,130
leased social homes will be delivered through various leasing schemes.

Over 6,400 affordable and cost rental homes will be delivered through the [
First Home Scheme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/owning-a-
home/help-with-buying-a-home/first-home-scheme/) and other schemes that
support local authorities and approved housing bodies to build cost rental
homes.

Funding for the [ Housing Assistance Payment (HAP)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/renting-a-home/help-with-
renting/housing-assistance-payment/) scheme will be €525 million in 2024. This
will provide 8,800 new HAP tenancies and support 58,000 existing HAP
tenancies.

Funding of €111 million for the [ Rental Accommodation Scheme (RAS)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/local-authority-and-social-
housing/rental-accommodation-scheme/) will support 1,400 new RAS tenancies in
2024 and 16,000 households already in the scheme.

Funding of €21 million is allocated in 2024 for the delivery of Traveller-
specific accommodation.
