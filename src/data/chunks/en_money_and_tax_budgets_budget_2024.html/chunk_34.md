###  Leave for parents

[ Parent’s leave
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-
conditions/leave-and-holidays/parents-leave/) and [ Parent’s Benefit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/families-and-children/parents-benefit/) will be extended by 2 weeks
to 9 weeks from August 2024.
