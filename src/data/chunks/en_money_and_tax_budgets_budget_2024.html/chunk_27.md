###  **Carbon tax**

[ Carbon tax ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/motor-
carbon-other-taxes/carbon-tax/) will increase by €7.50 per tonne of carbon
dioxide emitted, from €48.50 to €56.00. This applies from 11 October 2023 for
auto fuels. The increase will apply to all other fuels from 1 May 2024.
