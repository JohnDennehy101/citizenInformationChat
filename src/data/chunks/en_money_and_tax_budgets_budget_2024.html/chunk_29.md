###  **Value Added Tax (VAT)**

The 9% VAT rate on ebooks and audio books will be reduced to zero on 1 January
2024.

The [ reduced 9% VAT rate for gas and electricity will be extended
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/utilities/paying-electricity-
bill/#9efed7) for 12 months to 31 October 2024.
