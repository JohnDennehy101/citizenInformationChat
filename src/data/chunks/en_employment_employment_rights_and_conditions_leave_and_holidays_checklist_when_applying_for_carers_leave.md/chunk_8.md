###  More information

You can find out more about [ carer’s leave ](/en/employment/employment-
rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/carers-leave/) . You can contact
Workplace Relations Commission's [ Information and Customer Service
](https://www.workplacerelations.ie/en/contact_us/) for information about
carer’s leave.

For information about Carer's Benefit, contact your [ local social welfare
office or Intreo centre
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-
offices/?referrer=http://www.gov.ie/en/service/40cf48-find-your-local-intreo-
office/) .
