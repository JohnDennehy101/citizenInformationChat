###  Other payments

If you are getting a social welfare payment you may qualify for additional
financial support because of your illness or disability, for example, under
the [ Supplementary Welfare Allowance Scheme ](/en/social-
welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/) you can apply for [ a Heating
Supplement ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/diet-and-
heating-supplements/) , if you have exceptional heating expenses due to ill-
health or infirmity.

You could be eligible for the [ Long Term Illness Scheme ](/en/health/drugs-
and-medicines/long-term-illness-scheme/) or a [ medical card
](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/medical-card/) or a [ GP Visit
Card ](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/gp-visit-cards/) . Apply to
your Local Health Office in the Health Service Executive. [ Find out more
about health services for people with disabilities
](http://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health_services/health_services_for_people_with_disabilities/)
.

There are [ concessions for disabled drivers and passengers ](/en/travel-and-
recreation/transport-and-disability/) and [ local authority grants to adapt
your home ](/en/housing/housing-grants-and-schemes/housing-supports-for-older-
people-and-people-with-disabilities/housing-adaptation-grant-for-people-with-
disability/) . There are also [ tax benefits available to people that are sick
or have a disability ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/tax-credits-and-reliefs-for-
people-with-disabilities/) .

If someone is providing you with full-time care they may qualify for a [
carer's payment ](/en/social-welfare/carers/payments-to-carers/) .
