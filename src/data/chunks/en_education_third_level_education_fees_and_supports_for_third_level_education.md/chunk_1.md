#  Fees and supports for third-level education

  * [ Third-level student fees and charges ](/en/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/fees/)

Information on the Free Fees Iniative. Explains who doesn't have to pay third-
level fees.

  * [ Tax relief for third-level fees ](/en/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/tax-relief-for-third-level-fees/)

Tax relief is available in respect of tuition fees paid in private third-level
institutions, in institutions abroad and by repeat students and part-time
students. The Student Contribution may also qualify for relief.

  * [ Student Grant Scheme ](/en/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/student-grant-scheme/)

This page explains the Student Grant Scheme (also called the SUSI Grant). Find
out how to apply for a grant and the grant rates.

  * [ Part-time fee scheme for undergraduate courses ](/en/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/part-time-fee-scheme-for-undergraduate-courses/)

The part-time fee scheme is a financial support scheme for students studying
certain part-time undergraduate courses in Ireland and abroad.

  * [ Grants and funds for mature students ](/en/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/grants-for-mature-students/)

Information on grants and funds available for mature students in third-level
education.

  * [ Postgraduate student grants ](/en/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/postgraduate-student-grant/)

If you are starting a postgraduate course, you can qualify for financial
assistance under the Student Grant Scheme.

  * [ Financial supports for students ](/en/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/financial-supports-for-students/)

Information on other financial supports for students in further and higher
education.

  * [ Social welfare payments and the student grant ](/en/social-welfare/back-to-education/social-welfare-payments-and-the-student-grant/)

Information on claiming a social welfare payment, BTEA and the student grant.
