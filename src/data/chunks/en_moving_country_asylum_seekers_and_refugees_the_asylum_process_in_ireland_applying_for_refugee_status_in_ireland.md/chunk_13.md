###  Before your interview

Before your interview, you can:

  * Ask for an interpreter. This is often helpful even if you speak English well as the interpreter can help you if you find it harder to speak in English if you become upset or you are anxious. 
  * Ask to be interviewed by a male or female officer. You will need to explain why you want a female or male interviewer. 
  * Get [ legal advice ](https://www.legalaidboard.ie/en/our-services/legal-aid-services/services-for-international-protection-applicants/services/general-information/legal-services.html)
