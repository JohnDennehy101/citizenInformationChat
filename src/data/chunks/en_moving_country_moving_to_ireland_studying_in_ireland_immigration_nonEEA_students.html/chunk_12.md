###  Short-stay language courses and semesters

There are special arrangements for [ non-EEA students coming to Ireland to do
a short course ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/coming-to-study-in-
ireland/what-are-my-study-options/a-short-term-course/) . The course must be
for less than 90 days.
