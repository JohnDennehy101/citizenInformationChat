##  How to apply

You should contact an SEAI registered One-Stop-Shop to apply for this new
grant. The SEAI will be publishing a list of One-Stop-Shops shortly. In the
meantime, there is information about [ One-Stop Shops on the SEAI website
](https://www.seai.ie/grants/home-energy-grants/one-stop-shop/) , or you can
contact them for further details about the scheme.
