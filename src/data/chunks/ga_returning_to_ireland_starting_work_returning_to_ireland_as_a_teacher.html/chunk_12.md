####  **Character reference**

You can get a blank copy of the Character Reference Form from the first page
of your PRQA-01 online application. This form must be signed by a professional
person in a position of scholastic or academic responsibility who has known
you in a professional capacity for at least 1 academic year in the last 5
years. This could be:

  * A teacher currently registered with the Teaching Council 
  * The head of school placement of the college or university where you completed your Teacher Education Programme 
  * A tutor of the college or university where you completed your Teacher Education Programme 
  * A school principal or director of an Education and Training Board (ETB) 

Your character reference must be fully completed, signed, and dated within 1
year of the date of registration.
