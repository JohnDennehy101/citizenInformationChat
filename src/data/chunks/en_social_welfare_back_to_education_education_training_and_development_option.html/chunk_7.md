##  Rates

You will continue to get your weekly Jobseeker’s Allowance or Jobseeker’s
Benefit at your usual rate and any other secondary benefits.

Secondary benefits may include:

  * [ Fuel Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/fuel-allowance/)
  * [ Rent Supplement ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/rent-supplement/) . However, out of pocket expenses, may affect the amount of Rent Supplement you are getting.
