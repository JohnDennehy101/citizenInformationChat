##  Introduction

Under the Education, Training and Development Option you may continue to get
your [ Jobseeker’s Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-
people/jobseekers-allowance/) or [ Jobseeker’s Benefit ](/en/social-
welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-benefit/) while attending certain short
courses of education, training or development. Your Intreo Centre may make
special signing-on arrangements so that you can attend your chosen course.
