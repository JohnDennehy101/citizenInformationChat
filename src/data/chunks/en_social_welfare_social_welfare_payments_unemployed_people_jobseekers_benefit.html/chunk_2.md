##  What is Jobseeker's Benefit?

Jobseeker's Benefit is a weekly payment from the Department of Social
Protection (DSP) to people who are out of work and are covered by [ social
insurance (PRSI) ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-
insurance-prsi/social-insurance/) . If you don't qualify for Jobseeker's
Benefit you may qualify for [ Jobseeker's Allowance ](/en/social-
welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-allowance/) .
