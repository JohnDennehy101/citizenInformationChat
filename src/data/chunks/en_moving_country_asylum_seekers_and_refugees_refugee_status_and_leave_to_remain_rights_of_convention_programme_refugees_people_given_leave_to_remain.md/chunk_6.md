###  Social housing

Social housing is housing that is provided either by the local authority or an
[ approved housing body ](/en/housing/local-authority-and-social-
housing/approved-housing-bodies/) . To apply for social housing you must
register at the office of your local authority to go on the [ waiting list for
housing ](/en/housing/local-authority-and-social-housing/applying-for-local-
authority-housing/) .
