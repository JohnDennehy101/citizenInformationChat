##  Permanent residence

You are entitled to permanent residence in Ireland if you have lived here
legally for a continuous period of 5 years. You can [ apply for a permanent
residence certificate ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/wp-
content/uploads/2022/03/Form-EUTR2.pdf) , but this is not mandatory.

Your continuous residence is not affected by some temporary absences:

  * Absences of less than 6 months per year 
  * One absence of 12 months for pregnancy and childbirth, serious illness, work, vocational training or if you are posted to another country (by your employer in Ireland) 
  * Absences for compulsory military service 

Your non-EEA family can also [ apply for permanent residence
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/wp-content/uploads/2022/03/Form-EUTR3.pdf)
after 5 years residence in the State.
