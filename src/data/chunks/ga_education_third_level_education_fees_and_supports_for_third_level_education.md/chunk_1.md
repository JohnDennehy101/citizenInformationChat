#  Táillí agus Tacaíocht do mhic léinn tríú leibhéil

  * [ Táillí do scoláirí tríú leibhéal ](/ga/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/fees/)

Tá eolas anseo maidir le cé a bhíonn táillí tríú leibhéal le n-íoc acu.

  * [ Faoiseamh cánach ar tháillí don tríú leibhéal ](/ga/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/tax-relief-for-third-level-fees/)

Cur síos ar an bhfaoiseamh cánach atá le fáil ar tháillí a íoctar le
hinstitiúidí príobháideacha tríú leibhéal, le hinstitiúidí thar lear, ag mic
léinn atá ag athdhéanamh scrúduithe agus ag mic léinn páirt-aimseartha.

  * [ Scéim um Dheontais do Mhic Léinn ](/ga/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/student-grant-scheme/)

Cur síos ar an Scéim um Dheontais do Mhic Léinn. Míniú ar na critéir
intofaíochta agus ar leibhéil na ndeontas.

  * [ Scéim táillí páirtaimseartha do chúrsaí fochéime ](/ga/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/sceim-tailli-pairtaimseartha-do-chursai-focheime/)

Is scéim tacaíochta airgeadais í an scéim táillí páirtaimseartha do mhic léinn
atá ag déanamh staidéir ar chúrsaí fochéime páirtaimseartha áirithe in Éirinn
agus thar lear.

  * [ Grants and funds for mature students ](/ga/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/grants-for-mature-students/)

Information on grants and funds available for mature students in third-level
education.

  * [ Postgraduate student grants ](/ga/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/postgraduate-student-grant/)

If you are starting a postgraduate course, you can qualify for financial
assistance under the Student Grant Scheme.

  * [ Financial supports for students ](/ga/education/third-level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/financial-supports-for-students/)

Information on other financial supports for students in further and higher
education.

  * [ Íocaíochtaí leasa shóisialaigh agus an deontas mac léinn ](/ga/social-welfare/back-to-education/social-welfare-payments-and-the-student-grant/)

Faisnéis faoi íocaíocht leasa shóisialaigh, an BTEA agus an deontas mac léinn
a éileamh.
