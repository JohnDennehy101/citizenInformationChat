###  Getting paid

You can collect your Jobseeker's Benefit payment weekly from your nearest [
post office ](https://www.anpost.com/Store-Locator) .

You must bring valid photo ID with you to collect your payment. The following
is valid photo ID:

  * [ Public Services Card ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/public-services-card/)
  * Driving licence 
  * Passport 
  * [ Irish Residence Permit (IRP) ](/en/moving-country/moving-to-ireland/rights-of-residence-in-ireland/registration-of-non-eea-nationals-in-ireland/) or GNIB card 

Staff working in the post office may ask to see your photo ID before giving
you your payment.
