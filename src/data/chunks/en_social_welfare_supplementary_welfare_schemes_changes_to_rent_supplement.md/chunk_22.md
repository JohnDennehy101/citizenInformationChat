##  Where to apply

You should contact th CWO at your [ local Community Welfare Service
](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/cb07d7-supplementary-welfare-allowance-swa-
list-of-offices-administering-swa/) or contact the Rent Unit for your area
(see [ the central rent unit list)
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/823e83-central-rent-units/) .
