##  How to apply

If your circumstances change (including an increase in your rent), contact the
Department of Social Protection.

If you wish to apply for Rent Supplement for the first time you should read
our [ general document on Rent Supplement ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-
welfare-schemes/rent-supplement/) . You can download the [ application form
from the Department of Social Protection's website (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/42214/d5508c17df7344beb8dde444cc0a02b4.pdf) .
