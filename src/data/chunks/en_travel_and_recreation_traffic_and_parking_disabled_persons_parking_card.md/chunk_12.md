###  Legislation

The law for the disabled parking card scheme is in [ Section 35 of the Road
Traffic Act 1994
](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1994/en/act/pub/0007/sec0035.html#zza7y1994s35)
and [ SI 182/1997 Road Traffic (Traffic and Parking) Regulations 1997
](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1997/en/si/0182.html) .
