###  Credit union deposits

If you are a member of a credit union, you can nominate someone to get your
savings if you die. Up to €27,000 can pass to the nominated person without
going through the usual process for administration of your estate. Amounts
over €27,000 must be administered by your [ personal representative
](/en/death/the-deceaseds-estate/what-happens-the-deceaseds-estate/) .
