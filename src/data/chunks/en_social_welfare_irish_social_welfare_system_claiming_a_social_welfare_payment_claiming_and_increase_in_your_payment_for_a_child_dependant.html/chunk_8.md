##  When is an IQC not paid?

You do not get an Increase for a Qualified Child with the following payments:

  * [ Working Family Payment ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/working-family-payment/)
  * [ Guardian's Payment (Contributory) ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/guardian-and-orphan-payments/)
  * [ Guardian's Payment (Non-Contributory) ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/guardian-and-orphan-payments/)
  * [ Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner's (Non-Contributory) Pension ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-non-contrib-pension/)
  * [ Death Benefit by way of Orphan’s Payment ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/death-benefits-under-oib/)

You do not get an IQC if the gross income of your spouse, civil partner or
cohabitant is over €400, and you get one of the following payments:

  * [ Illness Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/illness-benefit/)
  * [ Injury Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/injury-benefit/)
  * [ Health and Safety Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/health-safety-benefit/)
  * [ Jobseeker's Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-benefit/) or [ Disablement Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/disablement-benefit/) (Incapacity Supplement) 
  * [ Invalidity Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/invalidity-pension/)
  * [ Carer's Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/carers/carers-benefit/)
  * [ State Pension (Contributory) ](/en/social-welfare/older-and-retired-people/state-pension-contributory/)
