####  Changes to your circumstances

If your circumstances change, you may no longer qualify for Rent Supplement or
the amount of your payment may change. Read more about [ Rent Supplement and
changes to your circumstances ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-
schemes/changes-to-rent-supplement/) . You can also read [ FAQs about Rent
Supplement from the Department of Social Protection
](http://www.welfare.ie/en/Pages/rent_faq.aspx) .
