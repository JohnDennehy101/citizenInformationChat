####  Coming from Invalidity Pension

If you are coming from Invalidity Pension, your Partial Capacity Benefit (PCB)
will stop when you are 66. You will transfer to a [ State Pension
(Contributory) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-
welfare-payments/older-and-retired-people/state-pension-contributory/) .

If you want to [ defer your State Pension
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/older-and-retired-people/deferring-your-state-pension-contributory/)
, you must contact the Department of Social Protection (DSP) before you reach
66. You will not get PCB during the deferral period.
