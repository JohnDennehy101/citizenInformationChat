##  How to apply for Blind Pension

To apply for Blind Pension, download and complete [ application form (BP1)
(pdf) ](https://assets.gov.ie/8231/132f44f04ce947efb198924000e6173e.pdf) and
send it to the Department of Social Protection at the address below.

You can get a braille and audio cassette tape version of this application form
from [ Vision Ireland ](http://www.ncbi.ie/) .

You can also get the application form from [ your local Intreo Centre or
Social Welfare Branch Office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) and [ your
local Post Office ](https://www.anpost.com/Store-Locator) .

Staff in your local Intreo office can help you to complete the form and answer
any questions you may have.
