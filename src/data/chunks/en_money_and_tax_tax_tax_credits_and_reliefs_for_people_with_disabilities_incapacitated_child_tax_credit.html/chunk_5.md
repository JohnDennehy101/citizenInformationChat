##  How to apply

You will need to get [ Form ICC2 (pdf) ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-
tax-credits-reliefs-and-exemptions/documents/incapacitated-child-tax-credit-
claim-form-2.pdf) certified by the child's doctor or consultant.

If you are a PAYE taxpayer, you can claim the credit online through Revenue's
[ myAccount service ](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-
web/sign_in.html?execution=e1s1) . If you are self-employed and pay tax under
the self-assessment system, the credit is claimed by completing the
‘Incapacitated Child’ section on your annual tax return online at the [
Revenue Online Service (ROS)
](https://www.ros.ie/oidc/login/noCertsFound?lang=en&client_id=rosint_rp) .

You can also claim the Incapacitated Child Tax Credit by completing [ Form
ICC1 (pdf) ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-credits-reliefs-and-
exemptions/documents/incapacitated-child-tax-credit-claim-form-1.pdf) . [ Find
your Revenue office ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-us/customer-service-
contact/paye-jobs-and-pensions-helpline.aspx) to return the completed forms
to.
