###  Ábhar Gaolmhar

  * [ _Irish citizenship and passports for children born abroad_ ](/ga/returning-to-ireland/residency-and-citizenship/irish-citizenship-and-passports-for-children-born-abroad/)

Outlines the rules about Irish citizenship for children who were born abroad
and have an Irish parent. It also covers the documents you need when applying
for an Irish passport for children born abroad.

  * [ _Ag taisteal thar lear_ ](/ga/travel-and-recreation/travel-abroad/travelling-abroad-during-covid/)

Faisnéis faoi cé atá in ann taisteal thar lear le linn COVID-19, cad atá le
déanamh sula dtéann tú agus conas a bheith cinnte go leanann tú na rialacha
san áit a bhfuil tú ag dul.

  * [ _Taisteal thar lear agus vacsainiú_ ](/ga/travel-and-recreation/travel-abroad/vaccinations-health-tips/)

Tá an vacsaín éigeantach chun dul isteach roinnt tíortha. Eolas ar vacsaíní do
dhaoine a chónaíonn in Éirinn má tá siad ag dul thar lear.
