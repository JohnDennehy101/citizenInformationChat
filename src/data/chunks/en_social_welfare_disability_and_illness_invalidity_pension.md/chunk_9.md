###  Tax and Invalidity Pension

Invalidity Pension is a [ taxable source of income. ](/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-
payment/taxation-of-social-welfare-payments/) The DSP informs [ Revenue
](http://www.revenue.ie/en/contact/lo-call.html) that you are getting
Invalidity Pension and the amount paid to you.
