##  Taxsaver tickets and temporary reduction in fares

There is a 20% average reduction in Taxsaver fares from 1 April 2022 to the
end of 2024. This applies to all monthly and yearly tickets provided by
Iarnród Éireann, Bus Éireann, Dublin Bus, Luas, and Go-Ahead Ireland.

See [ taxsaver.ie ](https://www.taxsaver.ie/) for more information or contact
your employer to order a ticket.
