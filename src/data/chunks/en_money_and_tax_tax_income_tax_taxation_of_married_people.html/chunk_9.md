##  How to apply

**Joint** **assessment:** to nominate or change the assessable spouse, you
must contact Revenue by 31 March of the year you want the change to apply to.
Use [ myAccount ](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-web/home.html) or send a letter
to [ Revenue ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-us/index.aspx) signed by both
of you. You can also fill in an [ Assessable Spouse Election Form (pdf)
](http://www.revenue.ie/en/life-events-and-personal-
circumstances/documents/assessable-spouse-election-form.pdf) or [ Nominated
Civil Partnerâ€™s Election Form (pdf) ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/life-events-
and-personal-circumstances/documents/nominated-civil-partner-election-
form.pdf) and send it to Revenue by 31 March.

**Separate assessment:** to be assessed separately from your spouse, you must
contact Revenue between 1 October and 31 March. For example, to be assessed
separately in 2024, the claim must be made between 1 October 2023 and 31 March
2024. To request separate assessment, use [ myAccount
](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-web/home.html) or write to [ Revenue
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-us/index.aspx) .

Further information about the taxation of married people and civil partners,
with examples showing how income is taxed, is available on [ Revenue's website
](http://www.revenue.ie/en/life-events-and-personal-circumstances/marital-
status/marriage-and-civil-partnerships/index.aspx#) .
