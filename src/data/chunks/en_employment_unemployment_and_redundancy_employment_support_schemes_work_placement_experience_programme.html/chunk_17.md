##  Where to apply

Contact your local [ Intreo Centre or Social Welfare Branch Office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) or read the
detailed [ scheme guidelines
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/98f23-operational-guidelines-work-
placement-experience-programme/) for more information about WPEP.

Register online for WPEP on [ jobsireland.ie ](https://www.jobsireland.ie/) .
Once you are registered, you can apply for WPEP vacancies advertised on [
jobsireland.ie ](https://www.jobsireland.ie/) .

You can find out more about WPEP and the experience of WPEP participants in [
the DSP video ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/95fe1-work-placement-experience-
programme/#case-study-video) .
