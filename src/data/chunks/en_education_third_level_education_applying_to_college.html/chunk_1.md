#  Applying to college

  * [ College application and entrance requirements ](/en/education/third-level-education/applying-to-college/application-procedures-and-entry-requirements/)

Describes the application procedures and entrance requirements for third-level
educational institutions.

  * [ DARE admissions scheme for students with disabilities ](/en/education/third-level-education/applying-to-college/third-level-admissions-scheme-for-students-with-disabilities/)

Supports access to third-level education for students with disabilities.

  * [ HEAR admissions scheme for students from disadvantaged backgrounds ](/en/education/third-level-education/applying-to-college/third-level-admissions-scheme-for-students-from-disadvantaged-backgrounds/)

Higher Education Access Route (HEAR) is a scheme to improve access to college
for school leavers from disadvantaged backgrounds.

  * [ Third-level courses and mature students ](/en/education/third-level-education/applying-to-college/third-level-courses-for-mature-students/)

This page describes the different full-time and part-time educational options
available to adults at third level.

  * [ Returning to education with Springboard+ ](/en/education/third-level-education/applying-to-college/springboard/)

Springboard + provides free, part-time, third-level and retraining places.

  * [ Tertiary education programme ](/en/education/third-level-education/applying-to-college/tertiary-education-programme/)

This is a new pathway to get a degree. There is no need to apply through the
CAO. You start your degree programme in a further education college and finish
it in a higher education (third level) college or university.
