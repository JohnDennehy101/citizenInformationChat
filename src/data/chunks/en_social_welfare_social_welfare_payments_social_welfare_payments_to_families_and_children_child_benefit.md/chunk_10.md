##  How much is Child Benefit?

Child Benefit is €140 a month for each child. It is a ‘universal’ payment.
This means you can get it regardless of your income and social insurance
(PRSI) record.

**Monthly** **Child Benefit rate 2024**

**Family size** |  **Monthly rate of Child Benefit**  
---|---  
1 child  |  €140   
2 children  |  €280   
3 children  |  €420   
4 children  |  €560   
5 children  |  €700   
6 children  |  €840   
7 children  |  €980   
8 children  |  €1,120
