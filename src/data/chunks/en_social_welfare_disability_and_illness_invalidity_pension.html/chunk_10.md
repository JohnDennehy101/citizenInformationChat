###  **Social insurance contributions from abroad and Invalidity Pension**

You may have lived and worked in another country at some stage in your life.
Ireland has social security arrangements with other countries that allow you
to combine social insurance contributions that you have paid in Ireland with
social insurance contributions that you have paid in another country.

Find out [ how social insurance contributions from abroad are calculated for
Invalidity Pension ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/social-
insurance-contributions-from-abroad/#f925f9) .
