##  Getting started as a cyclist in Ireland

Read the [ complete guide to great weekend cycling in Ireland
](https://www.discoverireland.ie/guides/cycling-ireland) from Discover
Ireland.

To ensure your safety, read about the [ laws on cycling
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/cycling/cycling-
offences/) and the rules on using [ bike lights
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-
recreation/cycling/lighting-of-bicycles-in-ireland/) .

Sport Ireland has useful advice about [ getting started in cycling
](https://www.sportireland.ie/outdoors/getting-started-in-
cycling#:~:text=If%20you%20are%20a%20complete,a%20joy%20to%20cycle%20on.) .

You can [ find courses in maintaining your bike with local ETBs
](https://www.fetchcourses.ie/course/finder?sfcw-courseId=425265) .
