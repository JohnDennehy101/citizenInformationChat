###  **Creidmheasanna** **oibrí** **forbartha** **deonach**

Is féidir leat [ creidmheasanna Oibrí Forbartha Deonach
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/moving-country/moving-abroad/working-
abroad/voluntary-development-work-abroad-and-social-insurance/) a fháil as an
am a chaitheann tú mar oibrí deonach forbartha thar lear, suas le 5 bliana ar
a mhéad.
