##  Steps to learning to drive

The steps to learning to drive are:

  1. Pass the [ driver theory test ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driver-theory-test/)
  2. Get a [ learner permit ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/learner-driving-permits-for-cars-and-work-vehicles/)
  3. [ Practise driving ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring-1/driver-licensing/learning-to-drive#l92c6e)
  4. Pass the driving test 

Driver testing in Ireland is carried out by the [ Road Safety Authority (RSA)
](https://www.rsa.ie/en/) . The [ National Driver Licencing Service (NDLS)
](https://www.ndls.ie/index.php) is responsible for issuing learner permits
and driver licences.

Once you have passed your driving test, you can apply for your [ driving
licence ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/full-driving-
licence/) .
