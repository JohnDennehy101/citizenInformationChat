###  **Your bike and safety equipment**

  * Ensure that your motorcycle is always roadworthy. 
  * Check that your tyres are of the [ correct tread depth ](https://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/vehicle-components/tyres/wheel-and-tyre-maintenance#:~:text=What%20is%20the%20minimum%20recommended,new%20tyres%20have%208.0%20mm.) . 
  * Wear an approved helmet and protective clothing.
