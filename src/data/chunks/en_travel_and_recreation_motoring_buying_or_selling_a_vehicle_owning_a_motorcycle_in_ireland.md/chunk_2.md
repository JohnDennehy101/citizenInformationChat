##  Getting started with your motorcycle

If you ride a motorcycle on a public road, you must follow the [ rules of the
road (pdf) ](https://www.rsa.ie/docs/default-source/services/s1.8-learner-
driver-resources/rules-of-the-road-2020.pdf?sfvrsn=6d948b39_2) and carry a
valid driving licence for the [ correct category of motorcycle ](/en/travel-
and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/categories-of-motor-vehicles-and-
minimum-age-of-drivers-in-ireland/) .

You must have [ motor tax ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/motor-tax-and-
insurance/motor-tax-rates/) and [ motorcycle insurance ](/en/travel-and-
recreation/motoring/motor-tax-and-insurance/motor-insurance/) .
