##  What is the Arts and Disability Connect (ADC) fund?

If you are an artist with a disability, you may be eligible for the Arts and
Disability Connect (ADC) fund. The ADC fund can help you to develop your
practice and to connect with art organisations and professionals in Ireland.

Applications to the [ Arts and Disability Connect
](https://www.artscouncil.ie/Funds/Arts-and-Disability-Connect/) fund are
decided by [ Arts and Disability Ireland ](https://adiarts.ie/) .
