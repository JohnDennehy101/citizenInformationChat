##  How to qualify for Free Travel

To get Free Travel you must be living in Ireland and meet one of the following
conditions:

  * You are aged 66 or over 
  * You are getting [ Disability Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/disability-allowance/) , [ Blind Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/blind-persons-pension/) , [ Carer's Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/carers/carers-allowance/) or an [ Invalidity Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/invalidity-pension/)
  * You are blind or visually impaired and meet the medical conditions for Blind Pension 
  * You have been getting [ Incapacity Supplement ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/disablement-benefit/) or Workmen's Compensation with Disablement Pension for at least 12 months 
  * You are a specified carer for a person getting [ Constant Attendance Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/disablement-benefit/) or Prescribed Relative's Allowance 
  * You are getting a social security invalidity payment (or similar payment) from a country covered by [ EU regulations ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/social-insurance-contributions-from-abroad/) or from a country with which Ireland has a bilateral social security agreement for at least 12 months
