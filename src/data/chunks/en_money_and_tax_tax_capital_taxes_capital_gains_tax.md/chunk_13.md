####  Online

You must register for CGT and then pay online using [ Revenue Online Service
(ROS) ](https://www.ros.ie/) or [ myAccount ](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-
web/sign_in.html) .

To register for CGT:

  * If you have a tax registration number, send a request through [ MyEnquiries ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/online-services/services/manage-your-record/myenquiries.aspx)
  * If you have never been registered for tax, complete [ Form TR1 (pdf) ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/self-assessment-and-self-employment/documents/form-tr1.pdf) (if you are non-resident, use [ Form TR1 FT (pdf) ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/self-assessment-and-self-employment/documents/form-tr1-non-resident.pdf) instead)
