###  Assets from a spouse

Transfers of assets between spouses and civil partners are exempt from Capital
Gains Tax.

Transfers of assets between spouses and civil partners who are separated are
exempt from Capital Gains Tax if they are made under a Separation Agreement or
a court order. [ Read more about capital taxes following separation or divorce
](/en/money-and-tax/tax/capital-taxes/capital-taxes-following-separation-or-
divorce/) .
