##  Cé mhéad is féidir a fháil do mo leictreachas?

The amount of money you will get for your excess electricity depends on:

  * Cé mhéad leictreachais a ghineann tú 
  * An ráta atá socraithe ag do sholáthraí leictreachais 

The amount of electricity that you sell to the grid will generally be shown on
your electricity bill. However, some suppliers will send you a separate
statement with the information.
