##  More information on micro-generation

The Commission of Regulation of Utilities (CRU) has a useful [ questions and
answers document on microgeneration (pdf) ](https://cruie-
live-96ca64acab2247eca8a850a7e54b-5b34f62.divio-
media.com/documents/CRU202366_CRU_Microgeneration_QAs__-_V1.0_Updated.pdf) .

Tá [ faisnéis ag an SEAI faoi conas iarratas a dhéanamh ar an deontas
Leictreachais Gréine ](https://www.seai.ie/grants/home-energy-grants/solar-
electricity-grant/) .

Má tá ceisteanna agat ar mhicrea-ghineadóirí, tá [ leathanach Ceisteanna
Coitianta ag ESB Network ](https://www.esbnetworks.ie/help-centre/generator-
connections/connect-a-micro-generator) .

Is féidir leat féachaint ar [ Dhearadh Scéim Deiridh na Scéime Micrea-ghiniúna
(PDF)
](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/244404/1477c2aa-9fbc-4c30-b4f7-454136806d33.pdf#page=null)
ar gov.ie.

Léigh tuilleadh faoi [ dheontais le haghaidh painéil ghréine i do theach
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/housing-grants-and-
schemes/grants-for-home-renovations-and-improvements/grants-for-solar-panels/)
agus [ tithe níos glaise ](/ga/environment/environmental-grants-and-schemes-
for-your-home/greener-homes/) . Is féidir leat faisnéis a fháil faoi [
thacaíochtaí do ghnólachtaí glasa
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-
employment/supports-for-businesses-going-green/) .
