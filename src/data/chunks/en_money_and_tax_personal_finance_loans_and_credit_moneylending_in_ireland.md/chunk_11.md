##  Further information

The Central Bank of Ireland has information for high cost credit providers
including information on [ legislation
](https://www.centralbank.ie/regulation/industry-market-
sectors/moneylenders/legislation) and [ regulatory requirements and guidance
](https://www.centralbank.ie/regulation/industry-market-
sectors/moneylenders/regulatory-requirements-and-guidance) . It also provides
[ Frequently Asked Questions for high cost credit providers
](https://www.centralbank.ie/regulation/industry-market-
sectors/moneylenders/frequently-asked-questions-for-moneylenders) .
