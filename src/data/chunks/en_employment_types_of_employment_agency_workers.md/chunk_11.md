##  Protection against victimisation

You are protected against being [ victimised
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/enforcement-and-
redress/victimisation-at-work/) as an agency worker for reporting breaches of
the Protection of Employees (Temporary Agency Work) Act 2012.

This means the hirer or agency cannot penalise you through dismissal, unfair
treatment or unfavourable changes in your employment conditions.
