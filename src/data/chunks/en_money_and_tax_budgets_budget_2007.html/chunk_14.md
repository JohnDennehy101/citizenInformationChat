###  PRSI  

The threshold for payment of 2% Health Contribution increases from €440 to
€480 per week and the annual threshold increases from €22,880 to €24,960.  
  
The threshold for employee PRSI increases from €300 to €339 per week  
An additional 0.5% Health Contribution on earnings over €100,100 per annum is
being introduced.  
  
The earnings ceiling for PRSI increases by €2,200 from €46,600 to €48,800.  
  
People on Farm Assist are permitted to make PRSI contributions. (January 2007)
