###  **Green cards for Russia and Belarusian cars**

Since 1 June 2023, [ Ireland, and other EEA States have stopped recognising
green cards for Russia and Belarusian cars. ](https://www.mibi.ie/belarus-and-
russia-suspended-from-green-card-system/) This means that green cards will no
longer be accepted as proof of insurance in EEA States. Any Irish cars
travelling to Russia or Belarus will need additional frontier (third party)
insurance on or after 1 June 2023.

The Motor Insurers Bureau of Ireland (MIBI) has [ guidance on Green Cards
](https://www.mibi.ie/brexit/what-are-green-cards/) .
