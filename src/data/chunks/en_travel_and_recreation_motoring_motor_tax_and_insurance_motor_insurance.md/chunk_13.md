##  How to shop for insurance

Before buying motor insurance, you should shop around and get quotes from a
number of different companies to identify the policy that best meets your
needs and budget.

The Competition and Consumer Protection Commission (CPCC) has [ a checklist
(pdf) ](https://www.ccpc.ie/consumers/wp-
content/uploads/sites/2/2020/03/Motor-insurance-shopping-around-checklist.pdf)
you can use to compare the policies offered by different insurers, as well as
[ guidance ](https://www.ccpc.ie/consumers/money/insurance/car-
insurance/#think-about) on practical steps you can take to cut the cost of
your premium.

This includes:

  * Paying your insurance in one go 
  * Getting your full driving licence 
  * Avoiding driving convictions and penalty points 
  * Parking your vehicle in a secure location 
  * Adding experienced named drivers to your policy 
  * Paying a higher excess 
  * Avoiding using your personal vehicle for professional purposes
