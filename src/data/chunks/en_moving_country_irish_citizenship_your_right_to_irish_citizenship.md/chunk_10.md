####  3\. You were born abroad and any of the following apply to you:

  * One (or both of your parents) was born in Ireland and was entitled to Irish citizenship 
  * Your grandparent was born in Ireland and you have entered your birth on the [ Foreign Births Register ](/en/moving-country/irish-citizenship/foreign-births-register/)
  * At the time of your birth, your parent had become an Irish citizen by registering with the Foreign Births Register or by naturalisation. You must register your birth with the Foreign Births Register before obtaining Irish citizenship.
