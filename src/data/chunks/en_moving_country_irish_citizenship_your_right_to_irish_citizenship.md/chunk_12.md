##  Who is entitled to apply for Irish citizenship by naturalisation?

If you are not entitled to citizenship by birth or descent, you can apply to
become an [ Irish citizen by naturalisation ](/en/moving-country/irish-
citizenship/becoming-an-irish-citizen-through-naturalisation/) .

In addition to the below conditions, you must also be of good character.
Citizenship by naturalisation is granted at the discretion of the Minister for
Justice.
