##  Rights of Irish citizens

If you are an Irish citizen, you are entitled to

  * Carry an [ Irish passport ](/en/travel-and-recreation/passports/irish-passports-an-overview/)
  * Live and work in Ireland without restriction 
  * [ Vote in a general election ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-referenda/voting/right-to-vote/) , constitutional referendum or presidential election 
  * Be elected to government in Ireland and in the European Union. 
  * Live, travel and work within the European Union 
  * [ Serve on a jury ](/en/justice/courtroom/jury-service/) , unless you are disqualified or ineligible 
  * Receive [ diplomatic assistance ](/en/moving-country/moving-abroad/leaving-ireland/diplomatic-supports/) abroad from an Irish or EU embassy
