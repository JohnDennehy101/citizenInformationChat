####  **Freight ferry**

If shipping your belongings by freight ferry, you must give the ferry company
your ‘Pre-Boarding Notification (PBN) ID’. You can get your PBN ID from
Revenue – simply request it when sending your completed ‘Transfer of
residence’ form. See ‘Moving to Ireland from outside the EU’ above.

Read more in Revenue’s guide to the [ procedure at importation
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/customs/individuals/transfer-residence/procedure-
at-importation.aspx) .
