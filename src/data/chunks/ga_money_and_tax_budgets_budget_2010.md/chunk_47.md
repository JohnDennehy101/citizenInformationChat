###  Carbon tax

A carbon tax at a rate of â‚¬15 per tonne of carbon is being introduced on
fossil fuels. The tax will apply to petrol and auto-diesel with effect from
midnight, 9 December 2009; and from 1 May 2010 to kerosene, marked gas oil,
Liquid Petroleum Gas (LPG), fuel oil and natural gas.

The application of the tax to coal and commercial peat is subject to a
Commencement Order. Exemption from the tax will apply only to participants in
the EU Emissions Trading Scheme (ETS) in respect of fuels so covered.
