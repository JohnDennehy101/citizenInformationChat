###  People with disabilities

The following disability payments will be reduced by €8.30 per week (from
January 2010):

  * Illness Benefit 
  * Disability Allowance 
  * Blind Pension 
  * Injury Benefit 
  * Invalidity Pension (for people under 65) 

Disablement Pension under the Occupational Injuries Scheme will be reduced by
€8.40 per week (January 2010).

This reduction applies to the full-rate payment. There are proportional
reductions for people getting graduated payments (January 2010).

There will be no change in the monthly rate of Domiciliary Care Allowance.
