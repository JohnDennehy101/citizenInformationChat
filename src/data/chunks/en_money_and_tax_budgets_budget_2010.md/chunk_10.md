###  Families and children

From January 2010, Child Benefit will be reduced by €16 per month (approx
€3.69 per week).

Increase for a Qualified Child will rise to €29.80 (€3.80 increase) per week
(January 2010).

All Family Income Supplement (FIS) weekly income thresholds will increase by
€6 per week per child (January 2010).
