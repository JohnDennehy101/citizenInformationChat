##  What is the HomeCaring Periods Scheme?

The HomeCaring Periods Scheme can help you get a higher rate of [ State
Pension (Contributory) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/older-and-retired-people/state-pension-contributory/) if you spent
time out of work to care full-time for:

  * A child (or children) aged under 12 
  * A child (or children) aged 12 or over and who needed an increased level of care, or 
  * An adult who needed an increased level of care.
