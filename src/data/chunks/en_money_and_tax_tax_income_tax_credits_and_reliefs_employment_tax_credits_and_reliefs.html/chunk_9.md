##  Seafarer's Allowance

If you are employed on board a ship and spend at least 161 days in a calendar
year at sea travelling to or from foreign ports, then you may qualify for
Seafarer's Allowance. The allowance is €6,350 and it is an allowance available
at your highest rate of tax.
