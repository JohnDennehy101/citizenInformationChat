##  Cookies

Citizensinformation.ie uses cookies that are necessary to make the website
work. You can also choose to set optional cookies. These cookies help us
understand how visitors use the website and improve the use and functionality
of the website.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the 'Manage
Cookie Preferences' link in the footer of every page on this website. We
provide detailed information in our [ Cookie notice
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/cookies.en.html) about the cookies we
use and their purpose.
