##  Is your passport in date?

Check that your and your families’ passports are in date before you book.
Passport Online is open to all applicants. You can get information on [ how to
renew your passport ](/en/travel-and-recreation/passports/renewing-an-irish-
passport/) or [ how to apply for your child’s first passport. ](/en/travel-
and-recreation/passports/passports-for-children/)

The [ latest passport turnaround times are available on the Passport Service's
website ](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/passports/turnaround-times/) .
