##  Extra Benefits

If you get a State Pension (Non-Contributory), you may also be entitled to:

  * [ Supplementary Welfare Allowance Schemes ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/)
  * [ Rent Supplement ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/rent-supplement/) to help with the cost of your rent 
  * [ Living Alone Increase ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/living-alone-allowance/) if you live alone 
  * [ Household Benefits Package ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/household-benefits-package/) to help with the cost of electricity or gas, and a free TV licence 
  * [ Free Travel Pass ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/free-travel/) to use public transport for free 
  * [ Fuel Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/fuel-allowance/) to help with the cost of heating your home during winter 
  * [ Island Increase ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/island-increase/) if you live on [ specified islands ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/66ac9a-operational-guidelines-state-pension-non-contributory/#increase-for-living-on-a-specified-island) off the coast of Ireland 
  * [ Centenarian's Payment ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/older-and-retired-people/centenarians-bounty/) when you reach 100 years of age 
  * [ Carer's Support Grant ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/carers/carers-support-grant/) , which is an annual payment for carers who provide full-time care for at least 6 months a year.
