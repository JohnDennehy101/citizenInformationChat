####  **Increase for Living on a Specified Island**

This is an increase in the weekly payment of certain payments from the DSP, or
the EU equivalent, to people who are aged 66 or over and living on a specified
island off the coast of Ireland. The [ Increase for Living on a Specified
Island ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/island-increase/)
**** is to compensate for the additional costs of living on these islands
compared to living on the mainland.
