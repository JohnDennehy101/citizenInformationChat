####  **Widow’s, Widower’s or Surviving Civil Partner's Non-Contributory
Pension**

If you are a widowed person or a surviving civil partner who is not entitled
to a Widow’s, Widower’s or Surviving Civil Partner's Contributory Pension, has
not remarried or entered into a new civil partnership, has no dependent
children and is not cohabiting, you may be eligible for a [ Widow's, Widower's
or Surviving Civil Partner's Non-Contributory Pension ](/en/social-
welfare/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-non-contrib-pension/) . This is
a means-tested payment. At age 66 you transfer to the State Pension (Non-
Contributory).
