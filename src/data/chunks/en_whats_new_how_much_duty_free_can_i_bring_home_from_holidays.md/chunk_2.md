#####  News

  

Are you planning to take duty-free back to Ireland from holidays?

From inside the EU, you can bring:

  * 800 cigarettes 
  * 90 litres of wine 
  * 110 litres of beer 

From outside the EU, you can bring:

  * 200 cigarettes 
  * 4 litres of wine 
  * 16 litres of beer 

Find out more about [ duty-free rules
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/travel-to-
ireland/customs-regulations-for-travellers/) .
