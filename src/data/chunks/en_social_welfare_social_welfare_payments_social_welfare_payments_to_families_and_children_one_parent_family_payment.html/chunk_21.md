##  Rate of the One-Parent Family Payment

**Weekly** **One-Parent Family Payment rate 2024**

**Maximum personal weekly rate** |  **Child dependant**  
---|---  
€232  |  Child under 12: €46  Child aged 12 and over: €54   
  
As long as your youngest child is under the age limit, an Increase for [ a
Qualified Child (IQC) ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/claiming-and-increase-in-your-
payment-for-a-child-dependant/) will be paid for other children in the family
until they reach 18 (or 22 if in full-time education).

One-Parent Family Payment is a [ taxable source of income ](/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-
payment/taxation-of-social-welfare-payments/) .
