##  How income is assessed for the One-Parent Family Payment

One-Parent Family Payment (OFP) is a means-tested payment.

In a means test, the Department of Social Protection (DSP) examines all your
sources of income. To get OFP, your income must be below a certain amount.

You can check the latest version of the [ SW 19 Social Welfare Rates of
Payment booklet ](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/1af6ca-rates-of-payment-
sw19/) for the amount of OFP payable with your means.
