###  Housing tax reliefs

If you rent a room in your home to a private tenant you may claim [ rent a
room relief ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/home-owners/renting-a-room-in-your-
home/) . If you pay a mortgage, you might be entitled to [ tax relief on
mortgage interest ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-home/mortgage-interest-
relief/) .

For an overview of tax reliefs relating to housing, and for information on
taxation when transferring the ownership of a home, see [ housing tax credits
and reliefs ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/housing-
tax-credits-and-reliefs/) .
