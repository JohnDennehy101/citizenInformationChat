##  How to apply for Free Travel

If you are aged 66 or over and are getting a State pension, your Public
Services Card (PSC) will be issued showing that you have Free Travel
automatically.

If you are under 66, you will get Free Travel automatically issued on your PSC
if you get one of the following payments:

  * Invalidity Pension 
  * Blind Pension 
  * Disability Allowance 
  * Carerâ€™s Allowance 

If you are married or in a civil partnership, your PSC will be issued with
FT-S on it. If you are cohabiting, you must fill in Part 1 and 2 of the [ Free
Travel application form FT1 (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/20017/65d2fce4ceab49a59ac4e4a8b60361e6.pdf) .

If you are not getting one of the payments above but you are eligible for Free
Travel, you must fill in the [ Free Travel application form FT1(pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/20017/65d2fce4ceab49a59ac4e4a8b60361e6.pdf) and send
to the Free Travel Section in the Department of Social Protection.
