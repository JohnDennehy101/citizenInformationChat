##  Where can I use Free Travel?

You can use your Free Travel on most [ Iarnród Éireann ](/en/travel-and-
recreation/public-transport/rail-services-in-ireland/) , [ Bus Éireann
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/public-
transport/bus-services-in-ireland/#28fd85) , [ Dublin Bus
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/public-
transport/bus-services-in-ireland/#77a111) , [ DART ](/en/travel-and-
recreation/public-transport/rail-services-in-ireland/) and [ Luas Services
](/en/travel-and-recreation/public-transport/luas/) and on certain [ private
bus and ferry services (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/77461/5103085b-6197-4858-b583-9efcbb5c2620.pdf) .

Free travel is also available on Dublin Bus [ Nitelink
](https://bustimes.org/operators/nitelink-dublin-bus) , [ Go-Ahead Ireland
](https://www.goaheadireland.ie/) and TFI (Transport for Ireland) [ Local Link
services ](https://www.transportforireland.ie/tfi-local-link/) .

Free travel does not include some [ special services such as Dublin Bus
AIRLINK services ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/ba6e26-operational-
guidelines-free-travel-scheme/) .

You can find a [ full list of services where you can use Free Travel (pdf)
](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/77461/5103085b-6197-4858-b583-9efcbb5c2620.pdf#page=null)
on gov.ie.

There are no restrictions on the times you can use your Free Travel.
