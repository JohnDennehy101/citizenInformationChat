###  If you can’t travel alone

If you are [ unable to travel alone
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/ba6e26-operational-guidelines-free-travel-
scheme/#appendix-1-eligibility-for-companion-free-travel) , you can have a
person, over 16 years of age, travel with you for free. Your PSC card must
have FT-C on it. To add a companion to your Free Travel, see ‘How to apply for
Free Travel’ below.
