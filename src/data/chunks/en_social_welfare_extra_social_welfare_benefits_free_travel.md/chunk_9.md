###  Widow or widower or a surviving civil partner

If you are a widow or widower or a surviving civil partner aged 60 or over,
living in Ireland and your late spouse or civil partner had Free Travel, you
can get Free Travel, if you are getting one of the following payments:

  * [ Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Partner's (Contributory) Pension ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-contrib-pension/)
  * [ Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Partner's (Non-Contributory) Pension ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-non-contrib-pension/)
  * [ One-Parent Family Payment ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/one-parent-family-payment/)
  * Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Partner's Pension under the [ Occupational Injuries Benefit Scheme ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/occupational-injuries-benefit-overview/)
  * A similar social security pension or benefit from a country covered by [ EU regulations ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/social-insurance-contributions-from-abroad/) or a country with which Ireland has a bilateral social security agreement 
  * You are getting an ordinary Garda widow's pension
