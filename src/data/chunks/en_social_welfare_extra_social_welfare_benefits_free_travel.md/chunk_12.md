###  **Free travel companion card for children**

A child with a visual impairment can get Free Travel for a companion if they
are under 18 and they either:

  * Satisfy the blindness condition for a Blind Pension, or 
  * Are registered with [ Vision Ireland ](https://vi.ie/) or the National League of the Blind of Ireland 

The person they travel with (companion) must be age 16 or over.
