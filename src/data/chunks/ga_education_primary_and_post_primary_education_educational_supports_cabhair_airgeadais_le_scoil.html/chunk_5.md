###  **Free primary schoolbooks**

Since September 2023, parents of children in primary school and special
schools do not have to pay for [ schoolbooks, workbooks and copybooks (pdf)
](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/250812/c0e12161-a64b-4dce-88d8-c7e6a6ed4a68.pdf#page=null)
.

Soláthraíonn an scoil na leabhair in aisce ar iasacht. Caithfidh tú na
leabhair a thabhairt ar ais ag deireadh na scoilbhliana, nó má fhágann do
leanbh an scoil. Ba chóir duit a chinntiú go gcoimeádtar na leabhair i riocht
maith ionas gur féidir iad a athúsáid.

Má bhíonn ort earraí breise a cheannach do do leanbh, cuirfidh an scoil é sin
in iúl duit.

Léigh tuilleadh faoin [ Scéim na Leabhair Bunscoile In Aisce (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/250686/b1aa4f60-a8d7-4a8c-8257-6f585bb87a10.pdf) .
