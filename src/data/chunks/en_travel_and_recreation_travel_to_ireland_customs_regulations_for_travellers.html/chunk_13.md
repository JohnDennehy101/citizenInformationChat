###  Bringing in meat or dairy products

You cannot import meat or dairy products for your personal consumption from
almost all non-EU countries. Limited quantities of certain other products may
be imported provided their country of origin is an EU-approved country.
Further information is available on the Department of Agriculture, Food and
the Marine website on [ animal products imports for personal consumption
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/03dfd-brief-guide-to-importing-animal-
products-from-third-countries/) .
