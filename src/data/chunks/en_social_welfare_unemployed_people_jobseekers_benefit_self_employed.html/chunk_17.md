##  How to apply for Jobseeker's Benefit (Self-Employed)

You should apply for Jobseeker's Benefit (Self-Employed) the first day you
become unemployed. You can bring your completed [ Jobseeker's Benefit (Self-
Employed) application from (UP1) (pdf)
](https://eur03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fassets.gov.ie%2F42221%2F798cc6889e3249bc88103db93d52cb91.pdf&data=02%7C01%7CPaschal.Keogh%40ciboard.ie%7C5048376afe5e4c1a42e708d7cf14229d%7Cbb4e61ce39a3414abb5d6a0290c95fd9%7C1%7C0%7C637205557689200891&sdata=YUyf5APgFZZF25pScbvLu8UAOv6x5mNYqWc1XsIlE34%3D&reserved=0)

More information is available in our document about [ signing on for the first
time. ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/signing-on/)

If you think you have been wrongly refused Jobseeker's Benefit you can [
appeal the decision ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-
welfare-appeals/) .
