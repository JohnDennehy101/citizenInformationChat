###  Getting paid

Jobseeker’s Benefit (Self-Employed) is paid directly each week into your bank
account.

You can also collect your Jobseeker’s Benefit (Self-Employed) payment each
week from your nearest [ Post Office ](https://www.anpost.com/Store-
Locator?_ga=2.178261216.596433994.1580140892-830786725.1564412890) .

Staff working in the post office may ask to see your photo ID before giving
you your payment. The following is valid photo ID:

  * [ Public Services Card ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/public-services-card/)
  * Driving licence 
  * Passport 
  * [ Irish Residence Permit (IRP) ](/en/moving-country/moving-to-ireland/rights-of-residence-in-ireland/registration-of-non-eea-nationals-in-ireland/) or GNIB card
