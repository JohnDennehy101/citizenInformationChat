##  The BowelScreen test

You will be sent a screening test kit that includes step-by-step instructions
for self-administration of the test at home. The HSE will post the free test
to you.

You then send the completed test back to BowelScreen using the pre-paid
envelope provided. Your test results will be sent to you within 4 weeks. If
you have registered your GP details, they will also receive a copy of your
results.
