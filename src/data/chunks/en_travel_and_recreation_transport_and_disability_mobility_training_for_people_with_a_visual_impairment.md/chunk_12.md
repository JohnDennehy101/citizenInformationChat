###

If you have a trained guide dog from Irish Guide Dogs for the Blind, you can
claim a [ Guide Dog Allowance ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/tax-credits-and-reliefs-
for-people-with-disabilities/tax-reliefs-for-people-with-a-visual-impairment/)
. This allowance is given as a tax credit. You must get a letter from Irish
Guide Dogs for the Blind confirming that you are a registered owner of a guide
dog to claim this relief.

You can get more information about other [ tax reliefs for people with a
visual impairment ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/tax-credits-and-reliefs-for-people-
with-disabilities/tax-reliefs-for-people-with-a-visual-impairment/) .
