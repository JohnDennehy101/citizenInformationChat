###  Vision Ireland

[ Vision Ireland have an online referral process
](https://www.ncbi.ie/supporting-you/make-a-referral/) . Once you have made a
referral Vision Ireland will contact you to carry out an assessment.

They accept referrals from:

  * A Self-referral 
  * A friend or relative 
  * A Professional (Ophthalmologist or Optometrist)
