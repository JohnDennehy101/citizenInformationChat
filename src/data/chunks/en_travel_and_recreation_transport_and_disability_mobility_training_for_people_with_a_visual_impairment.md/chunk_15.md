###  Irish Guide Dogs for the Blind

You can apply [ online ](https://guidedogs.ie/get-support/orientation-
mobility/) for orientation and mobility training with Irish Guide Dogs for the
Blind.

If you need a guide dog, you can apply to Irish Guide Dogs for the Blind
yourself or through your family doctor. You can [ apply online
](https://guidedogs.ie/get-support/guide-dogs/guide-dog-application-form/) at
guidedogs.ie. You will need a medical report from your doctor confirming your
visual impairment. If your application is successful, a qualified instructor
will visit you to discuss the training.
