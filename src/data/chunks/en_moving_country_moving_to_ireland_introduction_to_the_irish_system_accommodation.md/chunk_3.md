###  Buying a house

If you are thinking of [ buying a house
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/owning_a_home/buying_a_home/)
, you need to be aware of the price trends in this country, the process by
which houses are bought and sold and the initial [ costs ](/en/housing/owning-
a-home/buying-a-home/costs-of-buying-a-home/) that are involved.
