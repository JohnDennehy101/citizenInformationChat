###  Taxation of payments under the Occupational Injuries Scheme

Disablement Benefit, Incapacity Supplement and Constant Attendance Allowance
are [ taxable sources of income ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/taxation-of-social-welfare-payments/)
but you are unlikely to pay tax if they are your only income. Disablement
Gratuity is _not_ taxable.
