##  Changing a vehicle’s use

To change a vehicle’s use from commercial use to private use or from private
to commercial, contact your [ local motor tax office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/1fc151-find-your-local-motor-tax-office/) to
see if they will accept your vehicle’s change of classification and find out
what documents they require.

Changing the classification of the vehicle may also change the amount of motor
tax you need to pay. You should contact your [ local motor tax office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/1fc151-find-your-local-motor-tax-office/) for
information on this.
