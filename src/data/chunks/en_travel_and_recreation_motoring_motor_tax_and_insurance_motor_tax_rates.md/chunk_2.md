##  What is motor tax?

You are legally required to have motor tax and display your motor tax disc if
you want to drive your vehicle in a public place. Motor tax is a charge
imposed by the Government on most motor vehicles, and it is collected by local
authorities.

To pay motor tax, you can [ tax your vehicle online
](https://www.motortax.ie/OMT/) , visit your [ local motor tax office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/1fc151-find-your-local-motor-tax-office/) or
request the forms and complete them by post. A motor tax disc is issued for 3,
6 or 12 months and how the [ rate is calculated depends on your type of
vehicle ](https://www.motortax.ie/OMT/motortaxinfotype.do) .

You should get a reminder about a month in advance if you are renewing your
motor tax, and there are no changes in ownership or in the car details.
