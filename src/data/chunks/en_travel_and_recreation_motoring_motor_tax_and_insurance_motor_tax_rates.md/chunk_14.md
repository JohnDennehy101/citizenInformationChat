##  Rates

A motor tax disc is issued for 3, 6 or 12 months and how the rate is
calculated depends on the type of vehicle.

You can [ view the motor tax rates ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/41c9cc-
motor-tax-rates/#motor-tax-rates-2021) on the Department of Transport website.
You can get information on how motor tax rates are calculated (CO2 emissions
etc.) on [ motortax.ie ](https://www.motortax.ie/OMT/omt.do) .

On the Sustainable Energy Authority of Ireland (SEAI) Power of One website,
you can [ check the CO2 emissions levels for different car models
](https://www.seai.ie/data-and-insights/seai-statistics/key-statistics/co2/) .
