##  Fuel Allowance income limit for people over 70

You should read how your income is assessed (above) before reading this
section.

You can have income of up to €512 per week, or €1,024 per week for a couple
and still qualify for Fuel Allowance.

Your capital (savings, property and investments) are also assessed. The first
€50,000 of your capital is not included in the means test.
