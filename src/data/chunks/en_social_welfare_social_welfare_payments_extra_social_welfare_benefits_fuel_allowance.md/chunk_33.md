If you get:

  * Long-term [ Jobseeker's Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-allowance/) (12 months) 
  * [ Farm Assist ](/en/social-welfare/farming-and-fishing/farm-assist/)
  * [ One-Parent Family Payment ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/one-parent-family-payment/) or [ Jobseeker's Transitional payment ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-transitional-payment/)

Send your completed form to your [ local Intreo Centre or Social Welfare
Branch Office ](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-
offices/?referrer=/en/service/40cf48-find-your-local-intreo-office/) .  

If you get long-term [ Supplementary Welfare Allowance ](/en/social-
welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/supplementary-welfare-allow/) (12
months), send your form to your local Community Welfare Officer at your [
local office ](http://www.welfare.ie/en/Pages/otheroffice/community-welfare-
office-contact.aspx) .
