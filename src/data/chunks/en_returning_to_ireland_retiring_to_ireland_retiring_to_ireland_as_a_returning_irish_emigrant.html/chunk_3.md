###  UK pension deadline

If you have worked in the UK and have gaps in your National Insurance record,
you can pay voluntary contributions to help you qualify for the [ UK state
pension ](https://www.gov.uk/state-pension) .

You have until **5 April 2025** to pay voluntary contributions to make up for
gaps since 2006. After 5 April 2025, you will only be able to pay
contributions to cover the previous 6 years.
