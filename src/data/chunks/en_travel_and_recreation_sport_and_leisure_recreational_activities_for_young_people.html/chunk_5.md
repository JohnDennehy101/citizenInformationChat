###  Local Authority Play and Recreation Network

The [ Local Authority Play and Recreation Network (LAPRN)
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/364bef-local-authority-play-and-
recreation-network-laprn/) gives a more coordinated and interagency approach
to achieving the main goals of the play and recreation policies at both the
national and local levels.
