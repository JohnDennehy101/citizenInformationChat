###  **How many PRSI contributions can I make during deferral?**

During deferral, you can continue to make PRSI contributions to increase your
personal rate of payment, or meet the qualifying condition of 520 (10 years)
contributions for [ State Pension (Contributory).
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/e6f908-state-pension-contributory/)

However, you will not be able to increase your contributions past the current
maximum of 2080 (40 years).
