###  **Voluntary contributions**

If you defer your State Pension (Contributory) and are aged under 70, you can
apply to make [ voluntary social insurance contributions
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/social-insurance-prsi/voluntary-prsi-contributions/) if you:

  * Are no longer employed or a self-employed contributor, and 
  * Have at least 520 contributions
