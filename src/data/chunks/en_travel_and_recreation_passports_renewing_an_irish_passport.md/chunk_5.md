##  Renewing by post through Post Passport

[ Post Passport ](https://www.anpost.com/Government-Services/Post-Passport) is
a service provided by An Post. Post Passport is open. [ Updates on services
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/passports/updates/) are available on the
Passport Service's website.

If you are an Irish citizen living in Ireland, you should fill in form APS 1.
You will find this form at Garda stations and any Post Office. It is not
currently possible to download the paper application form.

If your current passport has more than 12 months of validity at the time of
your renewal application, you will have to send the original passport by post.
