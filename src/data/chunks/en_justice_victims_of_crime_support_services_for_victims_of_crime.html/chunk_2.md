##  Introduction

If you or someone close to you has been a victim of crime, you can contact an
organisation for support. You can get emotional and practical support and
information about [ your rights as a victim ](/en/justice/victims-of-
crime/your-rights-as-a-victim-of-crime/) . These organisations also raise
public awareness about victim issues.

This page has information about some of the services that are available.

You can also learn more about your rights as a victim of crime on the [
Victims Charter website ](https://www.victimscharter.ie/) .
