###  **Harassment**

[ Harassment including sexual harassment ](/en/employment/equality-in-
work/harassment-at-work/) that is based on any of the 9 grounds is a form of
discrimination in relation to conditions of employment. [ Bullying at work
](/en/employment/equality-in-work/bullying-at-work/) which is linked to one of
the 9 discriminatory grounds above comes under employment equality
legislation.
