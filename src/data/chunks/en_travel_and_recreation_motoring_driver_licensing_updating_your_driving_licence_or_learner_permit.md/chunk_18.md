###  Apply in person

You can apply in person to one of the [ National Driver Licence Service (NDLS)
centres ](https://www.ndls.ie/where-to-apply.html) \- see ‘How to apply’
above. You must [ book an appointment online ](https://www.ndls.ie/booking-
service.html) . There is no walk-in service available.

More information is available on the [ National Driver Licence Service website
](https://www.ndls.ie/help/faq.html#updating-your-personal-details) .
