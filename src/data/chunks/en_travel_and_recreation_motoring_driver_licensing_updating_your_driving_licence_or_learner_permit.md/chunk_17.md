###  Apply online

You can [ apply online ](https://ndls.rsa.ie/) to update your personal
details. You need to have a [ Public Services Card (PSC) ](/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/personal-public-service-number/) and a [
MyGovID ](https://www.mygovid.ie/en-IE/Home/Index) verified account.
