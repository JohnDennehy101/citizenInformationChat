##  Mortgage Interest Supplement rules

**The Mortgage Interest Supplement (MIS) scheme has been closed to new
entrants since 1 January 2014** . MIS was wound down for existing recipients
over a 4-year period and closed on 31 December 2017.

However, claimants getting MIS payments that were due to end on that date,
continue to get payments under the [ Supplementary Welfare Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-
schemes/supplementary-welfare-allow/) from 1 January 2018.
