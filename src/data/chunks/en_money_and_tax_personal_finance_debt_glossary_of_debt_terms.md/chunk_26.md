###  Mortgage Arrears Resolution Process (MARP)

Under the Central Bank's [ Code of Conduct on Mortgage Arrears (CCMA) (pdf)
](https://www.centralbank.ie/docs/default-source/news-and-media/press-
releases/2015/April/2013-ccma.pdf) mortgage lenders must operate a [ Mortgage
Arrears Resolution Process (MARP) ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/mortgage-
arrears/consumer-protection-codes-and-mortgages/) when dealing with borrowers
in mortgage arrears or in pre-arrears. The 4 steps of the MARP are:
communication; financial information; assessment; and resolution.
