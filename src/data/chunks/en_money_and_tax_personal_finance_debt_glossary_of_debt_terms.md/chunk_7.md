###  Bankruptcy

[ Bankruptcy ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/debt/personal-
insolvency/what-is-bankruptcy/) is a settlement of the debts of someone who is
unable to repay their debts. It deals with both **secured** and **unsecured**
debt.

The purpose of the bankruptcy is to distribute your assets fairly among your
**creditors** and protect you from these **creditors** . The distribution is
done through a court official, the Official Assignee in Bankruptcy.
