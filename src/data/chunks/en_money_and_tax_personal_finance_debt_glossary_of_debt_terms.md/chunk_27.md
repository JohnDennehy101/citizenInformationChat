###  Personal insolvency

There are 3 debt resolution mechanisms for people who cannot afford to pay
their personal debts:

  * [ Debt Relief Notice (DRN) ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/debt/personal-insolvency/debt-relief-notices/)
  * [ Debt Settlement Arrangement (DSA) ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/debt/personal-insolvency/debt-settlement-arrangements/)
  * [ Personal Insolvency Arrangement (PIA) ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/debt/personal-insolvency/personal-insolvency-arrangements/)

These arrangements offer different solutions to people in different
situations. [ Read more about these personal insolvency options
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/personal-
finance/debt/personal-insolvency/personal-insolvency-options/) .
