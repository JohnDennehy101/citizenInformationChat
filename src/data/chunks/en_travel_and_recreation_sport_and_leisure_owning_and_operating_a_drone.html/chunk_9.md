##  More information

[ EU law ](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-
content/EN/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:32019R0945&from=EN) regulates the design,
specifications, manufacture, and marketing of unmanned aircraft systems within
the European market.

It also outlines strict rules about potential add-ons and accessories that
operators can purchase and fit into their drones.

For more information, please see the website of the [ Irish Aviation Authority
](https://www.iaa.ie/general-aviation/drones) .
