##  How do I renew my card?

You must apply to renew your Card as they are not reissued automatically when
they expire. You will need your old EHIC number and your PPS number.

If you have **changed your address or your name,** you will need to contact
your [ Local Health Office
](http://www.hse.ie/eng/services/list/1/schemes/EHIC/contactdetails/) . You
should apply well in advance of the date you plan to travel.

Apply to renew your European Health Insurance Card [ online
](https://www.sspcrs.ie/portal/ehic/) .

  1. Select Renew Current Card at the top of the screen. 
  2. Enter the 10-digit ID number on your expired Card. This number is on the front of the card at the bottom left. 
  3. Confirm the address to which the Card will be sent. It should confirm that your application is successful and that a card is being sent out to you.
