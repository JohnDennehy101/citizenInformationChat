###  **Apply in person**

You can apply for your driving licence in person by [ booking an appointment
](https://www.ndls.ie/booking-service.html) at one of [ 34 NDLS centres
nationwide ](https://www.ndls.ie/how-to-apply/applying-in-person.html#centre-
locations) . There is no walk-in service available.

You will need to bring:

  * Appropriate [ Identification ](https://www.ndls.ie/how-to-apply/applying-in-person.html#photo-id) , 
  * Supporting documentation, and 
  * The [ fee ](https://www.ndls.ie/how-to-apply/fees.html) (see ‘Rates’ above) 

Unless you want to [ make your application in Irish ](https://www.ndls.ie/how-
to-apply/forms-reports.html) , you are no longer required to submit an
application form. However, you do need to prove your identity. Your photograph
and signature will be captured at the NDLS centre.
