##  Further information

The Department also has a User Guide on Directive 2005/36/EC, the main EU
legislation for the recognition of professional qualifications. The guide
explains everything you need to know about the [ recognition of professional
qualifications in Europe
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/d7527-professional-qualifications-
recognition/) .

For further information contact the Department of Further and Higher
Education, Research, Innovation and Science by email [
Qualifications@dfheris.gov.ie ](mailto:Qualifications@dfheris.gov.ie) .

The Teaching Council of Ireland has information about [ registering as a
teacher when you have qualified outside Ireland
](https://www.teachingcouncil.ie/en/Registration/How-do-I-register-/Qualified-
Outside-of-Ireland/) .

The Nursing and Midwifery Board of Ireland (NMBI) has [ registration
information for nurses and midwives who trained outside Ireland
](https://www.nmbi.ie/Registration/Qualified-outside-Ireland) .

The European Commission has [ FAQs on regulated professions
](https://europa.eu/youreurope/citizens/work/professional-
qualifications/regulated-professions/faq/index_en.htm) .
