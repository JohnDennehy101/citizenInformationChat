##  Where to apply for the Back to Work Family Dividend

Send your application for the Back to Work Family Dividend to your [ Intreo
Centre or local Social Welfare Branch Office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-
offices/?referrer=https://www.welfare.ie/en/Pages/List-of-Social-Welfare-
Local-and-Branch-Offices-by-County.aspx) .
