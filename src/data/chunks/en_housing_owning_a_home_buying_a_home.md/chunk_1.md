#  Buying a home

  * Overview 

[ Steps involved in buying a home ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-
home/steps-involved-buying-a-home/)

This document looks at what you need to consider before buying a property and
the processes involved.

  * [ Stamp duty on property ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-home/stamp-duty/)

Stamp duty is paid on the transfer of property

  * [ Property deeds ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-home/property-deeds/)

Each time the ownership of a property changes, a new deed of title is drawn up
to record the change. Tailte Eireann is responsible for 2 systems for
recording property transactions.

  * [ Insurance protection on mortgages ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-home/insurance-protection-on-mortgages/)

Brief description of 2 different types of insurance that you can take out in
respect of a mortgage.

  * [ Costs of buying a home ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-home/costs-of-buying-a-home/)

As well as mortgage payments, the other costs involved in buying a home can be
significant.

  * [ Moving to a new home ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-home/moving-to-a-new-home/)

A summary of steps to take when moving house, including transferring
utilities, redirecting post and notifying schools.

  * [ Mortgage interest tax credit ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-home/mortgage-interest-relief/)

A mortgage interest tax credit or relief is a tax relief based on the amount
of qualifying mortgage interest that you pay in a tax year for your home. A
new temporary mortgage interest tax credit was announced in Budget 2024. The
previous scheme ended on 1 January 2021.

  * [ Regulation of estate agents, auctioneers and management agents ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-home/regulation-of-property-services/)

The Property Services Regulatory Authority (PSRA) is responsible for licensing
and regulating property services providers in Ireland. This document described
their licencing and other functions

This page explains the property rights of cohabiting couples, for example, in
relation to buying a home together, making a cohabitation agreement, applying
for local authority housing, and getting legal advice.
