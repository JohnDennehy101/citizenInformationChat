###  How to get an application form

Download and print the [ application form for an Supplementary Welfare
Allowance (SWA1) (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/24920/f93565af8bed495a817b8d7fbb18482a.pdf) or get an
application form at your [ local Intreo Centre or social welfare branch office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/cb07d7-supplementary-welfare-allowance-swa-
list-of-offices-administering-swa/) .

You can also [ request application forms online
](https://www.eforms.gov.ie/en/forms/5) .

Phone the Community Welfare Service for an application form: 0818 60 70 80
