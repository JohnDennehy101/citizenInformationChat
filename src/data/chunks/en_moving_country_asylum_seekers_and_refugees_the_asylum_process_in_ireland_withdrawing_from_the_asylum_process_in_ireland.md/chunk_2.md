##  Introduction

You can withdraw your [ application for international protection ](/en/moving-
country/asylum-seekers-and-refugees/the-asylum-process-in-ireland/applying-
for-refugee-status-in-ireland/) before the International Protection Office
(IPO) makes its recommendation to the Minister, or while you are waiting for
an appeal on a decision.

Your application can also be deemed to have been withdrawn if you do not
cooperate in the appeals process or fail to attend an oral hearing.
