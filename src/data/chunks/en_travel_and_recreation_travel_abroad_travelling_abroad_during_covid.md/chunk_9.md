##  While abroad

Note the address, telephone number and opening hours of the [ Irish Embassy or
Consulate ](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/embassies/) in the country you are
visiting. In countries with no Irish diplomatic or consular representation,
you may seek emergency assistance from embassies or consulates of other
European Union countries. On its website, the Department of Foreign Affairs
provides advice on how to [ stay safe while abroad
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/overseas-travel/know-before-you-go/safety-and-
security/) and what to do in an emergency.
