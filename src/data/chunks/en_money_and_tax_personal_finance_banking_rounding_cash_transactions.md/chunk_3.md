##  Rules

If a bill total is being paid in cash and the number of cent does not end in
zero or 5, the amount of change paid will be rounded to the nearest 5 or 10
cent:

  * 1 or 2 is rounded down to zero 
  * 3 or 4 is rounded up to 5 
  * 6 or 7 is rounded down to 5 
  * 8 or 9 is rounded up to 10
