####  Examples

A bill total of:

  * €10.21 or €10.22 would be rounded to €10.20 
  * €10.23 up to €10.27 would be rounded to €10.25 
  * €10.28 or €10.29 would be rounded to €10.30 

For 3 individual items priced at €2.99, €4.49 and €8.18, the prices will be
added together and the total bill (€15.66) would be rounded down to €15.65.
