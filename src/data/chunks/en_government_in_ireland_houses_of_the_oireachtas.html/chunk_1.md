#  The Dáil, Seanad and President

  * [ Seanad ](/en/government-in-ireland/houses-of-the-oireachtas/the-seanad/)

The Seanad is the upper house of the Oireachtas (the Irish parliament). This
document describes how it is elected, who may run to become a senator, and its
functions.

  * [ Dáil Éireann ](/en/government-in-ireland/houses-of-the-oireachtas/dail-eireann/)

Dáil Éireann is the lower house of the Oireachtas (the Irish parliament). This
document describes how it operates, who can be elected as a TD, and outlines
what is meant by Dáil privilege, PQs and dissolution of the Dáil.

  * [ President of Ireland ](/en/government-in-ireland/houses-of-the-oireachtas/president-introduction-to-the-president-of-ireland/)

The President of Ireland (Uachtarán na hÉireann) is the head of State. The
President's role and functions are set out in the Constitution.

  * [ Legislation ](/en/government-in-ireland/houses-of-the-oireachtas/legislation/)

This document describes how laws are made by the Oireachtas (the Irish
parliament).

  * [ Private Members' Bills ](/en/government-in-ireland/houses-of-the-oireachtas/private-members-bills/)

Private Members' Bills are proposals for legislation initiated by members of
the Dáil or Seanad.

  * [ President's Award - Gaisce ](/en/government-in-ireland/houses-of-the-oireachtas/presidents-award-gaisce/)

The President's Award is the President's National Challenge Award for young
people in Ireland between the ages of 15 and 25. Find out how to achieve a
Bronze, Silver or Gold Gaisce award.
