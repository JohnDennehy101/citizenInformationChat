##  Replacing a lost tax disc

You can get replacement motor tax and other vehicle documents.

**Replacement documents** **Document** |  **Replacement cost**  
---|---  
Tax disc  |  €6   
Registration book/certificate  |  €12   
Vehicle licensing certificate  |  €12   
  
To get replacement documents, you need to:

  * Complete [ Form RF134 (pdf) ](https://www.motortax.ie/OMT/pdf/RF134_en.pdf)
  * Have the form witnessed by a member of the Garda Síochána at a [ Garda station ](http://www.garda.ie/Stations/Default.aspx)
  * Send your completed form with the appropriate fee to your motor tax office.
