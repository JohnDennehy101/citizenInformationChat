##  Where to apply

You apply for Jobseeker's Benefit or Jobseeker's Allowance to [ your Intreo
Centre or Social Welfare Branch Office ](http://www.welfare.ie/en/Pages/List-
of-Social-Welfare-Local-and-Branch-Offices-by-County.aspx) .
