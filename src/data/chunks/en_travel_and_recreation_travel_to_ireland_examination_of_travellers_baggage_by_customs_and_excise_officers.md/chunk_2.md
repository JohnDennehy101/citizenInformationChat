##  Introduction

This page outlines what you need to know about arriving in customs in Ireland.
It covers Customs Officers, customs searches and where you can go if you wish
to make a complaint.

**Returning to Ireland**

If you are an Irish person living abroad and planning to move home, visit our
new [ Returning to Ireland section ](/en/returning-to-ireland/overview-of-
returning-to-ireland/) where we have the practical information you need to
start a life in Ireland again.
