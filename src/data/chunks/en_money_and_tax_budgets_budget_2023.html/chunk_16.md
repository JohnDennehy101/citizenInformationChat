###  Employment programmes

The [ Community Employment (CE) ](/en/employment/unemployment-and-
redundancy/employment-support-schemes/community-employment-scheme/) , [ Tús
](/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/employment-support-schemes/tus/)
