####  November 2022

  * Double [ Child Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/child-benefit/) payment 
  * Once-off payment of €200 to people getting the [ Living Alone Increase ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/living-alone-allowance/)
  * Once-off payment of €400 to people getting the [ Fuel Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/fuel-allowance/)
  * Once-off payment of €500 to people getting the [ Working Family Payment ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/working-family-payment/)
  * Once-off payment of €500 to people getting [ Disability Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/disability-allowance/) , [ Invalidity Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/invalidity-pension/) , [ Blind Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/blind-persons-pension/) and carers who qualify for the [ Carer’s Support Grant. ](/en/social-welfare/carers/carers-support-grant/) You will get one €500 payment only, even if you qualify for more than one of these payments or you are caring for more than one person. 

People getting [ Partial Capacity Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-
illness/partial-capacity-benefit/) will **not** get the once-off payment of
€500.

The double Child Benefit payment will be paid 1 November 2022. The once-off
payments will be paid the week starting 14 November 2022. The once-off Carer's
Support Grant will be paid Thursday, 24 November 2022.
