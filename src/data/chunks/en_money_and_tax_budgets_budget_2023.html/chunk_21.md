##  Tax

Income tax rates will stay the same (at 20% and 40%), but there will be
increases to tax credits and changes to the income tax bands in 2023.

The [ standard rate income tax band
](../tax/income_tax/how_your_tax_is_calculated.en.html#l2729c) (the amount you
can earn before you start to pay the higher rate of tax) will be increased by
€3,200.

The following [ tax credits ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-
reliefs/introduction-to-income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/) will increase by €75
to €1,775:

  * Personal Tax Credit 
  * Employee Tax Credit 
  * Earned Income Tax Credit 

The Home Carer Tax Credit will increase by €100 to €1,700.
