####  Energy upgrades

In 2023, €337 million will fund over 37,000 **home energy upgrades** including
upgrades under the Warmer Homes Scheme for households in, or at risk of,
energy poverty. €291 million of this funding is being provided from carbon tax
revenue.

The introduction of a **new low-cost loan scheme for residential retrofit** .
Credit institutions will be able to offer loans at reduced interest rates to
private homeowners and non-corporate landlords to make home energy efficiency
upgrades.
