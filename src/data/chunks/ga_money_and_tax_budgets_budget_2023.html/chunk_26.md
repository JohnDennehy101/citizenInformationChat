####  VAT (Value Added Tax)

The VAT rate for newspapers (including digital editions) will be reduced from
9% to zero.

VAT will also be reduced to zero for:

  * Defibrillators 
  * Period products 
  * Hormone replacement therapy (HRT) and nicotine replacement therapy medicines that are not currently zero-rated (such as patches and gels) 

These VAT changes will apply from 1 January 2023.

The reduced VAT rate of 9% for the hospitality and tourism sector will
continue until 28 February 2023.
