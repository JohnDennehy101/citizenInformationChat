##  Register your vehicle with NCTS

When your vehicle arrives in Ireland, you must contact a National Car Testing
Service (NCTS) within 7 days to [ make a VRT appointment
](https://www.ncts.ie/vrt-home/) . Your appointment date should be within 30
days of bringing your vehicle into Ireland.

The NCTS carry out a VRT inspection on behalf of Revenue. You must bring your
VRT exemption letter to this appointment.
