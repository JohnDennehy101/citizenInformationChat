###  Credits for different types of leave

You will automatically be awarded _credits_ when you are getting Maternity
Benefit, Paternity Benefit, Adoptive Benefit, Parent's Benefit or Health and
Safety Benefit.

If you take [ unpaid statutory maternity leave
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-
conditions/leave-and-holidays/maternity-leave/) , your employer must complete
an [ application for maternity leave credits (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/70415/a8216143d57b441fa4068a1ba107685b.pdf) when you
return to work.

If you take [ unpaid statutory adoptive leave
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-
conditions/leave-and-holidays/adoptive-leave/) , your employer must complete
an [ application for adoptive leave credits (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/96970/18854bdf-34b6-49c2-8784-fe29e6a1a737.pdf) when
you return to work.

If you take [ parental leave
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-
conditions/leave-and-holidays/parental-leave/) , your employer must [ contact
the Department of Social Protection
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/80e5ab-prsi-pay-related-social-
insurance/#prsi-for-employers) with the number of weeks and dates of your
parental leave, so that you can get credits for this time.
