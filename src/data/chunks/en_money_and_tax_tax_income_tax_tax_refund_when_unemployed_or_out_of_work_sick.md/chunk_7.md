####  **When to apply**

You should apply 4 weeks after you become unemployed. If [ emergency tax
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/jobs-and-pensions/emergency-tax/index.aspx) was
being deducted by your former employer you can apply immediately.

If you are getting another income which is taxable, you should apply 8 weeks
after you become unemployed. This includes [ taxable social welfare payments
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/taxation-of-social-welfare-payments/)
.
