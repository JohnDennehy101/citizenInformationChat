###  If you are unemployed

To apply for a repayment of tax because you are unemployed, sign in to [
myAccount ](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-web/home.html) and select ‘Claim
unemployment repayment’ under PAYE Services.

If you do not have [ myAccount ](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-web/home.html) ,
you can complete [ form P50 (pdf) ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/jobs-and-
pensions/documents/form-p50.pdf) and send it to [ Revenue
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-us/index.aspx) .

If you are unemployed and due a tax refund, Revenue will refund you by cheque
or to your bank account.
