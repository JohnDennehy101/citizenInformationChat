###  **Resources for specific sectors**

Learn more about [ greenhouse gas emissions by industry
](http://www.epa.ie/ghg/) on the EPA website.

The [ National Waste Prevention Programme ](https://www.epa.ie/waste/nwpp/) ,
led by the Environmental Protection Agency, has resources to help ‘green your
business’.

You can also read about [ ‘greening your office’ and resource efficiency in
the retail sector ](http://epa.ie/waste/nwpp/greenbusiness/) on the EPA
website.
