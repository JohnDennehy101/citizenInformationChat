###  **If you have a carer**

If you need full-time care, the person looking after you may qualify for [
Carer's Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/social-welfare-payments/carers/carers-allowance/) or [ Carer's Benefit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/carers/carers-benefit/) .
