##  Accessing MyWelfare online services

To use MyWelfare online services, you must create a [ MyGovID account
](https://www.mygovid.ie/en-IE/HowDoISignUp) . This is a secure online
identity-verification service.

Your MyGovID account lets you access a range of government services online, as
well as social welfare services.

There are 2 types of MyGovID account:

  * A basic MyGovID account 
  * A verified MyGovID account
