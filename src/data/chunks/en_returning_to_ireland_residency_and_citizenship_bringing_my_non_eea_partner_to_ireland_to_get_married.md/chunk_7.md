##  How to apply for a marriage visa

You must apply for a [ marriage (C) short-stay visa online
](https://www.visas.inis.gov.ie/avats/OnlineHome.aspx) .

After you complete the visa form online, you have 30 days to send the
supporting documents. It’s a good idea to prepare your documents before
completing the form.

You pay a fee of €60 for each application.
