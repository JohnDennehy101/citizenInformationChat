##  More information

If you have questions about your PRSI, you can:

  * Use Revenue’s online [ MyAccount and ROS services ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/online-services/index.aspx)
  * Contact the relevant area using the contact details [ on Revenue’s Contact us page ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-us/Index.aspx) (you may need to enter your [ PPS number ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/personal-public-service-number/) to see the phone number) 
  * [ Make an in-person or virtual appointment ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-us/customer-service-contact/appointment-service.aspx) with Revenue (if you have been unable to resolve your query online or over the phone) 
  * Visit a [ public Revenue office ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-us/customer-service-contact/public-offices.aspx) during opening times (if you have been unable to resolve your query online or over the phone and have not made an appointment) 

You can also [ contact your local Intreo office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) , or [ your
local Citizens Information Centre ](https://centres.citizensinformation.ie/) .
