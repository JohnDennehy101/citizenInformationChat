##  Go for Life programme

Go for Life is the national programme for sport and physical activity for
older people (aged over 50 years). The programme aims to encourage older
people in all aspects of sport and physical activity. It is run by [ Age &
Opportunity ](https://ageandopportunity.ie/) in partnership with the [ Health
Promotion and Improvement Offices ](https://www.healthpromotion.ie/) of the [
Health Service Executive (HSE) ](/en/health/health-system/health-service-
executive/) and the [ Local Sports Partnerships
](https://www.sportireland.ie/participation/lsp-contact-finder) .

This page gives an overview of the Go for Life programme and the National
Grant Scheme.
