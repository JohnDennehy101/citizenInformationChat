##  Taxation of Maternity Benefit, Adoptive Benefit and Paternity Benefit

If you get [ Maternity Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/social-welfare-payments/families-and-children/maternity-benefit/) , [
Adoptive Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/adoptive-benefit/)
, [ Paternity Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/social-welfare-payments/families-and-children/paternity-benefit/) , [
Parentâ€™s Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/parents-benefit/)
and [ Health and Safety Benefit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/families-and-children/health-safety-benefit/) , you are liable to pay
income tax. The actual rate of tax you pay depends on your personal
circumstances and the tax reliefs and tax credits you are claiming.

You do not pay Universal Social Charge (USC) and PRSI.
