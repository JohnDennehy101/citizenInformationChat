##  More information

Read our page about [ taxation of social welfare payments ](/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-
payment/taxation-of-social-welfare-payments/) .

If you have a question about your tax liability, you should [ contact Revenue
](http://www.revenue.ie/en/contact/index.html) . You can also visit Revenue's
website for more information on the [ taxation of social welfare payments
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/jobs-and-pensions/taxation-of-social-welfare-
payments/index.aspx) .

You may also want to [ contact your local Citizens Information Centre
](https://centres.citizensinformation.ie/) , to speak with an Information
Officer.
