##  Getting other payments with Carer’s Allowance

You can get a [ half-rate Carer’s Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/carers/half-rate-
carers-allowance/) paid with other social welfare payments, except the
following payments:

  * [ Basic Supplementary Welfare Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/supplementary-welfare-allow/) (SWA) and SWA Increase for a Qualified Adult 
  * [ Jobseeker's Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-benefit/)
  * [ Benefit for people who retire at 65 ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/older-and-retired-people/payment-for-people-retired-at-65/)
  * [ Jobseeker's Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-allowance/)
  * [ Working Family Payment ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/working-family-payment/)
  * [ Back to Education ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/back-to-education/back-to-education-allowance/)
  * [ Carer’s Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/carers/carers-benefit/)
  * [ Jobseeker's Transitional payment ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/unemployed-people/jobseekers-transitional-payment/)
