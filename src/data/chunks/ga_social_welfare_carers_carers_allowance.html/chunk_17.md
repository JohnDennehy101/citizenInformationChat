###  **Maintenance**

The DSP looks at maintenance paid to you. From 6 June 2024, child maintenance
payments are not included in the means test for Carer's Allowance. Read more
about [ how maintenance is assessed as means
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/means-test-for-social-welfare-payments/maintenance-and-social-welfare-
payments/) .
