###  **Other supports for carers**

If you get Carer's Allowance, you can also get:

  * [ GP visit card ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/gp-visit-cards/)
  * [ Free Travel ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/free-travel/)
  * [ Household Benefits Package ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/household-benefits-package/) (if you live with the person you care for)
