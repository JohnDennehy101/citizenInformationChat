###  Failure to comply with a sex offender order

Under the [ Criminal Law Act 1997
](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1997/en/act/pub/0014/index.html) , the Garda√≠
can arrest a sex offender without an arrest warrant or without having to get
permission from the courts if they have reasonable grounds to believe that the
person is not complying with the terms of a sex offender order.

Anyone found guilty of breaching a sex offender order can be fined or
imprisoned for up to 5 years (or both).
