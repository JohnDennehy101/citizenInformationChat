##  An clár 'Back for Business'

Is clár forbartha saor in aisce é Back for Business chun cabhrú le saoránaigh
Éireannacha atá tar éis filleadh ar Éirinn chun gnó a fhorbairt. Tá an clár
maoinithe tríd an [ gClár Tacaíochta Eisimirceach ](https://www.dfa.ie/global-
irish/support-overseas/emigrant-support-programme/) ag an Roinn Gnóthaí
Eachtracha.

Tabhair cuairt ar ár leathanach faoin gclár [ Back for Business
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/setting-up-a-
business/back-for-business-programme/) .
