##  How to apply

You can apply for Long-Term Carers Contributions online at [ MyWelfare.ie.
](https://services.mywelfare.ie/en/topics/pensions-and-older-people/pension-
recalculation/) You must have a [ verified MyGovID account
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/b6ecfd-sign-up-for-mygovid/) to apply using
MyWelfare.ie.

If you cannot apply online, you can contact Pension Caring Supports in the
Department of Social Protection to ask for a paper application form.
