##  Immigration and registration

If your application is successful and you live outside of Ireland, you still
have to [ apply for an entry visa online
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/visas-for-ireland/) if you are from a country
whose citizens need a visa to enter Ireland. You should include a copy of your
Letter of Approval as part of the documentation for your visa application.

If you will be staying in Ireland for longer than 90 days, you must [ register
with your local immigration officer
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/registering-your-immigration-permission/how-
to-register-your-immigration-permission-for-the-first-time/) .
