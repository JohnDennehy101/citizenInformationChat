##  How to apply

You should apply at age 65 and not before.

You can apply for the _Benefit Payment for 65 Year Olds_ online at [
mywelfare.ie ](https://www.mywelfare.ie/Account/Login) using a verified [
MyGovID account ](https://www.mygovid.ie/en-IE/GettingStarted) .

You can get a paper application form by emailing [ BP65forms@welfare.ie
](mailto:BP65forms@welfare.ie) with your full name, address and form request.
An application form will be posted to you. You can find more about [
requesting an application form ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/442136-request-
a-form/) .
