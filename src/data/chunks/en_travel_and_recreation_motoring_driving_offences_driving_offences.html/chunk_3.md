##  Licence offences

You must have a driving licence to drive in Ireland. You can read more about:

  * [ How to apply for a learner permit to drive a car ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/learner-driving-permits-for-cars-and-work-vehicles/)
  * [ Applying for a driving licence ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/full-driving-licence/)
  * [ Driving tests in Ireland ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driving-test/)

Several driving offences relate to licences and driving with the right
licence.
