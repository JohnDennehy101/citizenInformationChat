###  Motor tax, NCT and insurance

You have to pay [ motor tax ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/motor-tax-
and-insurance/motor-tax-rates/) and the vehicle may have to have a [ National
Car Testing ](/en/travel-and-recreation/vehicle-standards/national-car-test/)
certificate. You must have [ motor insurance ](/en/travel-and-
recreation/motoring/motor-tax-and-insurance/motor-insurance/) to drive in
Ireland.
