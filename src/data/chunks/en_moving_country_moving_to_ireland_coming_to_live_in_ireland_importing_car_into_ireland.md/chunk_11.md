##  Importing a vehicle from the EU

If you are importing a car from the EU, you have to:

  * Register the vehicle 
  * Pay VRT unless you are exempt 
  * Pay VAT unless the vehicle is exempt 
  * Pay [ motor tax ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/motor-tax-and-insurance/motor-tax-rates/)
  * Get [ motor insurance ](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/motor-tax-and-insurance/motor-insurance/)
