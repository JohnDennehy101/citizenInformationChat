###  Courses covered

The Education, Training and Development Option allows you to attend short
courses , typically no longer than 10 weeks. For example, courses may be one-
day courses, half-day courses for a number of weeks or evening courses. You
must be able to show that the course chosen will improve your chances of
getting a job. Courses you can take part in include:

  * Personal development courses 
  * General training courses 
  * Specific job skills courses 
  * Evening courses 

The course must be approved by a Case Officer and should form part of your DSP
Personal Progression Plan. You can arrange an appointment with a Case Officer
by contacting your Intreo Centre- see 'Where to apply' below.
