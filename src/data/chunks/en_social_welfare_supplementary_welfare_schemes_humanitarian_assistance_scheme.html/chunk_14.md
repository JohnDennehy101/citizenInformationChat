##  Further information

You can [ read more about the Humanitarian Assistance Scheme
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/12e880-humanitarian-assistance-scheme-swa/) on
gov.ie.

You can get more information about the scheme at your [ local Community
Welfare Service ](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/cb07d7-supplementary-
welfare-allowance-swa-list-of-offices-administering-swa/) , or you can call
the National Phone Line for Community Welfare Services on 0818 607080.
