##  What is the Back for Business programme?

Back for Business is a free development programme to help Irish citizens who
have returned to Ireland to start or develop a business. The programme is
funded through the [ Emigrant Support Programme ](https://www.dfa.ie/global-
irish/support-overseas/emigrant-support-programme/) by the Department of
Foreign Affairs.

Applications for the current programme are now closed.

If you are interested in participating in the next Back for Business
programme, you can [ register online
](https://www.backforbusiness.com/register-your-interest/) . You will be sent
a link to the application form as soon as they become available
