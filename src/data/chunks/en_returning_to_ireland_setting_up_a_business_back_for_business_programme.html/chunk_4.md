##  Who can apply for Back for Business?

Applications for the current programme are now closed. If you are interested
in participating in the next cycle of the Back for Business programme, you can
register [ **here** ](https://www.backforbusiness.com/register-your-interest/)
and you will be sent a link to the application form as soon as they become
available (November 2024).

If you have any questions, or if there is any difficulty registering online,
call Back for Business on +353 1 8450770 or email [ info@backforbusiness.com
](mailto:info@backforbusiness.com) .
