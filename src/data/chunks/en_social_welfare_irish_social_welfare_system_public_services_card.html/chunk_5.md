##  Registering for a Public Services Card

You can register for a Public Services Card:

  * Using the [ MyGovID app ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/f61b1-mygovid-app/)
  * In-person at a [ Public Services Card (PSC) Centre ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/be29c7-psc-online-registration-centres/)

The MyGovID app allows you to verify your identity using your smartphone.

You can download the [ MyGovID

IE) app on your smartphone, if you:

  * Are resident in Ireland 
  * Hold a current Irish passport 
  * Are aged 16 years and over 
  * Have a [ basic MyGovID ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/government-in-ireland/how-government-works/egovernment/mygovid/#dcd9ee) account 
  * Have not already completed the SAFE Registration process 

Once your online application has been approved, you will get:

  * A Public Services Card (PSC) in the post 
  * A [ verified MyGovID ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/government-in-ireland/how-government-works/egovernment/mygovid/#3de5eb) account 

If the DSP is unable to process your online application, you will be notified
of what information you need to bring with you to complete the SAFE
registration process at a [ PSC Centre
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/be29c7-psc-online-registration-centres/) .
