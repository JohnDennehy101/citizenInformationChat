####  3\. Additional helpful documents

If you have any of the items listed below, you should bring them along with
you as they may also help to confirm your identity.

  * [ Free Travel ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/free-travel/) Pass 
  * [ Medical card ](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/medical-card/)
  * European Health Insurance Card 
  * Credit/debit card 
  * Student card 

The following items are not acceptable as proof of identity for the purpose of
SAFE registration: Baptismal certificate, work ID card, Garda form ML-10,
Garda age card, photocopied certificates or documents and expired documents.
