##  Where to apply

To apply for a social welfare payment you should either:

  * Apply online at [ MyWelfare.ie ](https://www.mywelfare.ie/Account/Login) (only certain payments) 
  * Send a paper application form to the address printed on the form 

You can get information about applying for social welfare payments through [
MyWelfare.ie ](https://services.mywelfare.ie/) . You can also call 0818 405
060.
