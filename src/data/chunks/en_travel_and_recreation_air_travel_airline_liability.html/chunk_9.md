##  Delayed passengers and luggage

An airline is liable to pay for damages of up to 5, 346 SDRs if you are
delayed. If your luggage is delayed, the airline is liable to pay for damages
of up to 1,288 SDRs. The airline is not liable for damages where the airline
took all reasonable measures or it was impossible to take such measure

Complain in writing within 21 days of you receiving the luggage.

See our page on [ compensation for overbooked, cancelled and delayed flights
in the EU ](/en/travel-and-recreation/air-travel/compensation-for-overbooked-
and-delayed-flights/) .
