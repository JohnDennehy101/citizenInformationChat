###  **Gather your medical records**

Before returning to Ireland, ask your doctor for copies of your medical
records, prescriptions for drugs and medicines, and immunisation records for
you and your family.

Read more about [ accessing healthcare on your return to Ireland
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/looking-after-
your-health/accessing-healthcare-on-your-return-to-ireland/) .
