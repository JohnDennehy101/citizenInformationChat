###  **Customs and related charges**

When you arrive, [ customs officers may carry out checks
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/travel-to-
ireland/examination-of-travellers-baggage-by-customs-and-excise-officers/) on
your belongings, including personal baggage. This is to make sure you are not
carrying prohibited or restricted goods.

Check the [ customs regulations
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/travel-to-
ireland/customs-regulations-for-travellers/) to see how much cash, tobacco and
alcohol you can bring into the country. You may have to pay [ import charges
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/planning-your-
journey-home/shipping-your-belongings-back-to-ireland/#11d1b1) , such as
customs duty and Value Added Tax. However, you can [ get relief from import
charges ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/planning-
your-journey-home/shipping-your-belongings-back-to-ireland/#24a38a) on certain
items.
