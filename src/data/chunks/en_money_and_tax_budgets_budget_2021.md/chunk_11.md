###  Carbon tax

The [ carbon tax ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/motor-carbon-other-taxes/carbon-tax/)
on fuel will increase by €7.50 from €26 per tonne to €33.50 per tonne. The
increase will apply to auto fuels from midnight on 13 October 2020 and to
solid fuels from May 2021. See 'Environment' below for more details.
