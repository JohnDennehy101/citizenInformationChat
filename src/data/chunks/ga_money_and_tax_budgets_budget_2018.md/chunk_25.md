####  Supports for homeless people

The current allocation for **homelessness services** is increased by €18
million, to over €116 million.

It is intended that there will be 3,000 exits from homelessness in 2018.

As part of the development of the **Family Hubs programme** , 6 new facilities
are due to become operational by the end of 2017. An additional €18 million is
provided for this programme in 2018.
