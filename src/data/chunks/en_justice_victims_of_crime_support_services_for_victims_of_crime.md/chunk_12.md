##  Migrant victims of crime

**Doras**

Doras runs a [ Migrant Victim Support Project ](https://doras.org/mvs/) for
migrants and refugees who have been victims of crime. You can contact them on
083 008 6391 or by email at [ mvs@doras.org ](mailto:mvs@doras.org) .

**Rape Crisis Centres**

If you need assistance in another language, the National Sexual Violence
Helpline offers support in 240 languages through its [ helpline interpreting
service ](https://www.drcc.ie/services/helpline/interpreting-service/) .
