###  Payment abroad

**EU citizens:** If you are an EU citizen, you can get Parent’s Benefit for
any period of your parent’s leave spent in another EU country.

**Non-EU citizens:** Non-EU citizens can only get Parent’s Benefit for periods
of parent’s leave spent in Ireland.

There are some exceptions, you can find more information about [ getting
Parent’s Benefit abroad
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/28c64c-operational-guidelines-parents-
benefit/#entitlement) .
