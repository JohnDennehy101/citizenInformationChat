###  Cad iad na cionta ar féidir ordú srianta gluaiseachta a ghearradh astu?

Is féidir orduithe srianta gluaiseachta a ghearradh ar dhaoine as na cionta
seo a leanas faoin [ Cacht um Cheartas Coiriúil (Ord Poiblí) 1994
](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1994/en/act/pub/0002/index.html) agus faoin [
Acht um Chionta Neamh-Mharfacha In Aghaidh an Duine 1997
](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1997/en/act/pub/0026/index.html) :

**Acht um Cheartas Coiriúil (Ordú Poiblí) 1994**

  * [ Alt 6: (iompraíocht bhagrach, mhaslach nó tharcaisneach in áit phoiblí) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1994/en/act/pub/0002/sec0006.html)
  * [ Alt 8: (mainneachtain ordachán ó Chomhalta den Gharda Síochána a chomhlíonadh) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1994/en/act/pub/0002/sec0008.html)
  * [ Alt 11: (dul isteach i bhfoirgneamh srl. le hintinn cion a dhéanamh) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1994/en/act/pub/0002/sec0011.html)
  * [ Alt 13: (Foghail a dhéanamh ar fhoirgneamh, srl) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1994/en/act/pub/0002/sec0013.html)
  * [ Alt 16: (gráscar) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1994/en/act/pub/0002/sec0016.html)
  * [ Alt 19: (ionsaí nó bac ar oifigeach síochána) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1994/en/act/pub/0002/sec0019.html)

**Acht um Chionta Neamh-Mharfacha in aghaidh an Duine, 1997**

  * [ Alt 2: (ionsaí) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1997/en/act/pub/0026/sec0002.html)
  * [ Alt 3: (ionsaí is cúis le díobháil a dhéanamh) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1997/en/act/pub/0026/sec0003.html)
  * [ Alt 9: (comhéigean) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1997/en/act/pub/0026/sec0009.html)
  * [ Alt 10: (ciapadh) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1997/en/act/pub/0026/sec0010.html)

_**Dáta an Leasaithe Deireanaigh:** 16 Meitheamh 2015 _
