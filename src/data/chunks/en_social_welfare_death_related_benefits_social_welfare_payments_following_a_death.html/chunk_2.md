##  Introduction

Some social welfare payments can continue after someone dies. Here we list the
payments that can continue to be paid following a death and when they are
paid. The Department of Social Protection has [ guidelines on payments after
the death of a claimant or a dependant
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/847761-recently-bereaved/#if-the-deceased-
was-in-receipt-of-a-pension-or-payment-from-the-department) .
