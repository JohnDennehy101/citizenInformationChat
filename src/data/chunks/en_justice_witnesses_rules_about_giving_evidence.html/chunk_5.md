###  Perjury

It is a criminal offence to give evidence which you know is false ( _perjury_
). This applies to evidence given while in court or any evidence which is
included in an affidavit (a sworn written statement from you) or anything in a
statutory declaration.

It is also a criminal offence to force, persuade or induce someone to commit
perjury.
