###  Short-term work or training

The DSP has a fast-tracking system for people who sign off JA to take up work
for a short period (of up to 12 weeks) or to go on a short training course
(lasting up to 12 weeks).

You will get your payment again without delay.

You must inform your Intreo Centre or local Social Welfare branch office
before you start work or training.

Your [ Rent Supplement ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-
schemes/rent-supplement/) can also be suspended for up to 12 weeks.
