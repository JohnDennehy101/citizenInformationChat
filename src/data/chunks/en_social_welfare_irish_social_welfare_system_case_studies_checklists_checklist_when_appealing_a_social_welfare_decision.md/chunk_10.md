####  How to submit your appeal

You may be able to [ make your appeal online with MyWelfare
](https://services.mywelfare.ie/en/topics/appeals/make-an-appeal/) , if you
have a verified [ MyGovID account. ](https://www.mygovid.ie/) You can also
post your appeal to the [ Social Welfare Appeals Office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/dd7b8-contact-the-social-welfare-appeals-
office/) .
