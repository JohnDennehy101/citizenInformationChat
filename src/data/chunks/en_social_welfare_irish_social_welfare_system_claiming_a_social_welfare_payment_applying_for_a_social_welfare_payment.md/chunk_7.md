####  **Late claims**

It is important that you make your claim as soon as you know you are entitled
to the payment. All payments must be claimed within a specific period of time.
If you don't claim on time you may lose out. Find out more about [ making a
late claim ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-
welfare-payment/making-a-late-claim/) .
