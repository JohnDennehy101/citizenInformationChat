####  Step 2: Consider the marginal rate allowance

If you have any [ tax allowances at the marginal rate
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/how-your-
tax-is-calculated/#d43041) , you can calculate their effect by deducting them
from your taxable pay before calculating the percentages.

The way that your employer may apply marginal-rate allowances is by [
adjusting your standard rate cut-off point
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/how-your-
tax-is-calculated/#61fb30) .
