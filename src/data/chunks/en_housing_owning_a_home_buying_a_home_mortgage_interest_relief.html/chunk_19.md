###  **How do I apply for the Homeowner’s Once-Off Payment?**

Complete the [ application form
](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/303144/031b86d2-f61b-4335-9d53-2313a96c70f7.pdf%22%20\\l%20%22page=null)
. Bring the completed form and any supporting documents to the Community
Welfare Officer at your local [ Intreo Centre
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-
offices/?referrer=https://www.gov.ie/en/service/40cf48-find-your-local-intreo-
office/) , or send them to:

**Community Welfare Services**

Department of Social Protection  
National Rent Supplement Section  
PO Box 12188  
Freepost FDN 7609  
Dublin 2

If you need help filling in the application form, you can:

  * Phone the National Phone Line for Community Welfare Services on 0818 607 080 
  * Contact your [ local Intreo Centre ](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/?referrer=https://www.gov.ie/en/service/40cf48-find-your-local-intreo-office/)
  * Contact your [ local Citizen Information Centre ](https://centres.citizensinformation.ie/)

**You can apply for the Homeowner’s Once-Off Payment between 1 September 2024
and 31 March 2025.**
