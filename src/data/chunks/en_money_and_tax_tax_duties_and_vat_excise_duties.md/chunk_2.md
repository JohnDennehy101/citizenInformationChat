##  Introduction

Excise duty is charged on certain goods such as alcohol, tobacco and [ mineral
oils ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/companies-and-charities/excise-and-
licences/excise-duty-rates/mineral-oil-tax.aspx) (for example petrol, diesel
and kerosene).

The Revenue website has [ information about the different types of excise duty
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/companies-and-charities/excise-and-
licences/index.aspx) , including an [ overview of the current rates of excise
duty ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/companies-and-charities/excise-and-
licences/excise-duty-rates/index.aspx) .
