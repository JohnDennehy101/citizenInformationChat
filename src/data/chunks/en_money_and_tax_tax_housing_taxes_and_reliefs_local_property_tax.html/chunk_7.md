##  How to submit a Local Property Tax return

If your property has never been registered for stamp duty or LPT, you first
need to [ register the property with Revenue
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/online-services/services/property/register-
property-lpt.aspx) .

To view your LPT record and make your LPT return online, you can use your
existing login details for [ myAccount ](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-
web/home.html) or [ Revenue Online Service (ROS)
](http://www.revenue.ie/en/online/ros/index.html) . Alternatively, you can log
in directly to the [ LPT online service ](https://lpt.revenue.ie/lpt-
web/views/login.html?execution=e1s1) .

To log in to the LPT online service, you will need your Property ID and PIN
which you can find on any LPT correspondence you have received from Revenue.
You will also need your Personal Public Service Number (PPSN) or Tax Reference
Number.

If you do not have your Property ID and PIN, you can use the [ LPT online
service ](https://lpt.revenue.ie/lpt-web/views/login.html?execution=e1s1) to
request them.

Revenue has a [ short video that explains how to submit your LPT return online
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/property/local-property-tax/submitting-your-local-
property-tax-return/submitting-lpt-return-online.aspx) .

You can contact the [ LPT helpline ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-
us/customer-service-contact/lpt-vht.aspx) for help with the Local Property
Tax. You can also send queries to Revenue through [ MyEnquiries
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/online-services/services/manage-your-
record/myenquiries.aspx) .
