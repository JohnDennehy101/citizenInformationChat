##  When do I have to pay the Residential Zoned Land Tax?

It was announced in [ Budget 2024
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/budgets/budget-2024/)
that the introduction of the Residential Zoned Land Tax (RZLT) is deferred by
one year. This means that the first payment date will be in 2025 for property
that is liable on 1 February 2025
