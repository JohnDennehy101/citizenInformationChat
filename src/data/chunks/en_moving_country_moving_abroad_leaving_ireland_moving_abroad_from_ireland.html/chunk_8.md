##  Social security

If you leave Ireland to live or work in another country or if you are
returning to your own country having worked in Ireland you may be entitled to
receive [ social security benefits in the country you are moving to
](/en/moving-country/moving-abroad/leaving-ireland/leaving-ireland-and-your-
social-security-entitlements/) .

You can find information about [ social security arrangements between Ireland
and other countries ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-
a-social-welfare-payment/social-insurance-contributions-from-abroad/) .

If you leave your employment to work as a [ volunteer development worker
](/en/moving-country/moving-abroad/working-abroad/voluntary-development-work-
abroad-and-social-insurance/) you may be entitled to credited social security
contributions.
