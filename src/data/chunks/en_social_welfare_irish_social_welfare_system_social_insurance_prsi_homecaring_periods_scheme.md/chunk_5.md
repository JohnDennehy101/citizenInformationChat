###  **Rules on working and the** **HomeCaring Periods Scheme**

You cannot be employed for more than 18.5 hours a week.

You must make sure that adequate provision is in place to provide care to the
person that you are caring for while you are working.

You must have a limited income from work. You may get a HomeCaring Period
added to your social insurance record for a week that you were employed:
