###  Group A

The Group A threshold applies to gifts or inheritances a child gets from their
parent. ‘Child’ includes the adult child of a parent. It also includes:

  * A stepchild 
  * An adopted child 

Group A applies if you are the child of:

  * The person who gives you the benefit 
  * Their civil partner 

It can also apply [ if you are a foster child
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/capital-
taxes/capital-acquisitions-tax-exemptions-and-reliefs/#238a55) .

If you die and you or your civil partner have a child under the age of 18, the
child can get a benefit under Group A from:

  * Your parent 
  * Your parent's civil partner
