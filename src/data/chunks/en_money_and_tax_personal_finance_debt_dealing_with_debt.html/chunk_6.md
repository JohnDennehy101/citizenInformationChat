##  Understanding words used aboutdebt

There are different types of debt, including mortgages, loans, overdrafts,
credit cards and hire purchase agreements. Some debts, for example, mortgages,
are _secured_ . This means that goods or property, usually your house, is used
as security in case the debt is not paid. Find out more about types of debt
and the words used about debt in our [ glossary of debt terms ](/en/money-and-
tax/personal-finance/debt/glossary-of-debt-terms/) .
