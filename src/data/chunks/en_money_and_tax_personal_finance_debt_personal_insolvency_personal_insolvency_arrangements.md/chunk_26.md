##  How to apply

Your application for a Personal Insolvency Arrangement must be made through a
Personal Insolvency Practitioner (PIP). You can choose one from the [ Register
of Personal Insolvency Practitioners ](https://backontrack.ie/get-help/find-a-
personal-insolvency-practitioner/) that is published by the ISI.

Further information is available from the ISI website [ isi.gov.ie
](http://www.isi.gov.ie/en/ISI/Pages/Home) , as well as its website [
backontrack.ie ](http://backontrack.ie/) for people who are struggling with
debt.
