###  Voluntary maintenance payments

If you get voluntary maintenance payments, you do not pay:

  * [ Income Tax ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/how-your-tax-is-calculated/)
  * [ Universal Social Charge ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/universal-social-charge/) (USC) 
  * [ Pay Related Social Insurance ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-insurance-prsi/) (PRSI) 

You may have to pay [ Capital Acquisitions Tax ](/en/money-and-
tax/tax/capital-taxes/capital-acquisitions-tax/) if the total amount of the
payments is [ over a certain amount
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/capital-
taxes/capital-acquisitions-tax/#2d5ff5) .
