##  Pay Related Social Insurance (PRSI)

You pay PRSI on your income from employment or self-employment. You do not pay
PRSI on your occupational or social welfare pension.

You are not liable to pay PRSI if you:

  * Are aged over 70 
  * Were aged over 66 by 1 January 2024 
  * Were getting a State Pension (Contributory) by 1 January 2024
