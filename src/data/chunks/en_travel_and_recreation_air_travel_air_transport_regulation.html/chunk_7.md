##  Air accidents and incidents

The Air Accident Investigation Unit is part of the Department of Transport. It
has responsibility for investigating all plane accidents and incidents within
Ireland. You can download [ reports of all air accidents, incidents, and
investigations within Ireland ](http://www.aaiu.ie/reports/aaiu-investigation-
reports) .
