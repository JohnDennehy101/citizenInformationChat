###  Pass a means test

Jobseeker’s Transitional Payment is a means-tested payment. This means your
income, [ savings and capital are assessed ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-
welfare-system/means-test-for-social-welfare-payments/how-to-assess-your-
means-from-capital-for-social-welfare-payments/) to see if you qualify for the
full payment, a reduced payment, or nothing.

Capital includes:

  * Property (that you don’t live in) 
  * Land 
  * Company shares 
  * Other types of investment 

You can rent out a room in the home that you live in and earn up to €14,000 a
year [ without it affecting your JST
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/means-test-for-social-welfare-payments/means-test/#deeee4) .
