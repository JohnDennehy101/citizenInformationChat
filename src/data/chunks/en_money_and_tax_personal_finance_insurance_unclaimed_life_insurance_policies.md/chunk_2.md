##  Introduction

A life insurance policy provides money for dependants if you die. You may need
life insurance if you have family or others who rely on you. If you are
thinking about taking out insurance, you can find [ information about the
different types of life insurance
](https://www.ccpc.ie/consumers/money/insurance/) on ccpc.ie.

Insurance companies must try to get in touch with policy owners they have lost
contact with.

If the insurance company cannot find the owners, they classify the policy as
unclaimed.
