###  **Leabhair scoile saor in aisce do dhaltaí na tSraith Shóisearach**

Ó Mheán Fómhair 2024 ar aghaidh, [ cuirfear leabhair scoile agus acmhainní
ranga saor in aisce, amhail cóipleabhair, irisí, áireamháin agus foclóirí
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-
education/educational-supports/financial-help-with-school/#c267ad) , ar fáil
do dhaltaí na Sraithe Sóisearaí i Scéim an tSaoroideachais. Léigh tuilleadh
faoi [ leabhair scoile saor in aisce do dhaltaí na tSraith Shóisearach
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/5652a-schoolbooks-scheme-for-junior-cycle-
in-post-primary-schools/) ar Gov.ie.
