#####  News

From 1 March 2024, the [ Residential Tenancies Board (RTB)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/renting-a-home/resolving-
disputes/residential-tenancies-board/) will resume charging late fees for
tenancies that are not registered on time.

If you are a landlord, you will have to pay late fees for each month that your
RTB registration is late.

Late fees will apply to new tenancies and tenancies with an annual renewal
date on or after 1 March 2024.

The basic fees for registering are different depending on the type of property
you rent.

Find out more about [ tenancy registration fees and late fees
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/renting-a-home/landlords-
rights-and-responsibilities/registering-a-tenancy/#434d31) .
