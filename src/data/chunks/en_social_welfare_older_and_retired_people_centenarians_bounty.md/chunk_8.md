###  People not living in Ireland

You should forward information about Irish citizens born in the island of
Ireland and resident outside the State, using the [ application form for
Centenarian Bounty (pdf)
](https://www.dfa.ie/media/dfa/alldfawebsitemedia/ourrolesandpolicies/theirishabroad/CENTENARIAN-
BOUNTY-FORM_2016.pdf) to the person's nearest Irish diplomatic mission or the
Department of Foreign Affairs (see 'Where to apply' below). Once it has been
verified that the person is eligible, the award will be made to them.
