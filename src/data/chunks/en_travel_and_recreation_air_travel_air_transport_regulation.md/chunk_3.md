###  **Legislation**

The [ Air Navigation and Transport Act 2022
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2022/act/40/enacted/en/html#:~:text=An%20Act%20to%20provide%20for,on%20it%20of%20certain%20functions)
:

  * Set up a new commercial semi-state body [ AirNav Ireland ](https://www.airnav.ie/home)
  * Made the [ Irish Aviation Authority ](https://www.iaa.ie/) the single aviation regulator for Ireland as it was merged with the Commission for Aviation Regulation
