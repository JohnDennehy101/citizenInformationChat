##  Airports

There are 9 airports in Ireland.

[ Dublin ](http://www.dublinairport.com/home.aspx) , [ Cork
](https://www.corkairport.com/) and [ Shannon
](http://www.shannonairport.ie/gns/Passengers/Home.aspx) are owned by the
Irish government and operated on its behalf by separate airport authorities.
The [ other Irish airports ](https://www.gov.ie/en/policy-
information/bb3245-airports/) are at Waterford, Knock, Galway, Sligo, Donegal,
and Kerry.

[ Passengers with reduced mobility ](/en/travel-and-recreation/air-travel/air-
passenger-rights-in-the-eu#lc7b94) have the right to assistance free of
charge.
