##  Irish Aviation Authority

The [ Irish Aviation Authority (IAA) ](https://www.iaa.ie/) is a commercial
semi-state agency. It is the national aviation regulator. It is responsible
for safety, security, and consumer protection functions. The IAA has taken on
[ functions previously held by the Commission for Aviation Regulation
](https://www.iaa.ie/about-us/about-the-irish-aviation-authority) .
