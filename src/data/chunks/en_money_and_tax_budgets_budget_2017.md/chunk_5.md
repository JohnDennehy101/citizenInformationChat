###  Universal Social Charge (USC)

Incomes of €13,000 or less will continue to be exempt from USC in 2017. Once
your income is over this limit, you will pay the relevant rate of USC on all
of your income as follows:

  * €0 to €12,012 @ 0.5% 
  * €12,012.01 to €18,772 @ 2.5% 
  * €18,772.01 to €70,044 @ 5% 
  * €70,044.01 to €100,000 @ 8% 
  * PAYE income in excess of €100,000 @ 8% 
  * Self-employed income in excess of €100,000 @ 11% 

Medical card holders and individuals aged 70 years and over whose aggregate
income does not exceed €60,000 will pay a maximum USC rate of 2.5%.
