##  Losing your Irish passport in Ireland

If you are an Irish citizen living in Ireland and you have lost your Irish
passport, or it has been stolen, you must:

  1. Let the Passport Office know immediately 
  2. Report the loss or theft to the [ Garda Síochána ](https://www.garda.ie/en/contact-us/station-directory/)

**Three ways you can renew your Irish passport**

  * Online, through [ Passport Online ](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/passports/passport-online/)
  * By post, through [ Post Passport ](https://www.anpost.com/Government-Services/Passport-express) with An Post 
  * In person, through the [ Passport Office’s counter ](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/passports/urgent-appointment/) service in Dublin and Cork. We explain the [ circumstances ](/en/travel-and-recreation/passports/renewing-an-irish-passport/) in which you can do this. 

**Emergency travel documents from Ireland**

If you don’t have a passport and need to travel abroad due to an emergency,
you should [ contact the Passport Service by webchat
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/passports/contact-us/) or by phone 01 671
1633.

Examples of such emergencies are:

  * A death or funeral of immediate family members abroad 
  * Urgent medical attention abroad
