##  Local Property Tax

When buying a home you may have to pay some or all of the [ Local Property Tax
(LPT) ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/housing-taxes-and-reliefs/local-property-tax/)
depending on when you buy. LPT is charged on all residential properties in
Ireland and is due on 1 November each year. The LPT charge for your home
varies depending on the value of your property. Your solicitor should [ check
the LPT information for your new home before completing the sale,
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/property/local-property-tax/selling-buying-or-
transferring-a-property/you-are-buying-a-property.aspx) including checking if
there are any outstanding payments or charges due on the property.
