####  **Find out what you can afford**

Review your budget to determine how much you can afford in monthly mortgage
repayments. You should make sure you have enough to cover all the [ costs
involved in buying a home
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-
home/costs-of-buying-a-home/) , for example:

  * Mortgage costs 
  * Legal fees 
  * Insurance 
  * Stamp duty 

The Competition and Consumer Protection Commission (CCPC) has a [ budget
planner ](https://www.ccpc.ie/consumers/tools-and-calculators/budget-planner/)
you can use to see how much you can afford each month.
