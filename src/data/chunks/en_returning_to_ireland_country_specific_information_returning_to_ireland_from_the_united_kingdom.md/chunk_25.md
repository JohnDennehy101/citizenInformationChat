####  **More about NARIC**

NARIC has published a guide on [ how to get your foreign qualification
recognised (pdf) ](http://www.cao.ie/) . You can also find [ guides in
different languages on the website ](https://www.qqi.ie/what-we-do/the-
qualifications-system/national-academic-recognition-information-centre) .

NARIC is part of Quality and Qualifications Ireland (QQI), which has a [ guide
for comparing UK qualifications (pdf)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-
education/going-to-post-primary-school/types-of-post-primary-school/) .
