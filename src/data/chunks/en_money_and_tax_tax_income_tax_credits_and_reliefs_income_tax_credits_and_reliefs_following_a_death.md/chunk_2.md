##  Introduction

[ Tax credits ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-
tax/tax/income-tax/how-your-tax-is-calculated/#6cc0a0) reduce the amount of
tax you have to pay. This page explains the tax credits that apply to your
income in the year of your death. It also explains the tax credits and reliefs
you get if you are a widowed person or surviving civil partner.

You can also read about other [ money matters after death
](/en/death/financial-concerns-following-a-death/) .
