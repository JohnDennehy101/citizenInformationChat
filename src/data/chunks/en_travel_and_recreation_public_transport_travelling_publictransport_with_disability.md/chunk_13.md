###  Where can I use it?

You can use the badge on Dublin Bus, Bus Éireann, Iarnród Éireann—Irish Rail,
Luas, Go-Ahead Ireland, and TFI Local Link services.

Find out more at the [ Transport for Ireland
](https://www.transportforireland.ie/news/tfi-launches-badge-and-card-for-
passengers-with-invisible-disabilities/) website.
