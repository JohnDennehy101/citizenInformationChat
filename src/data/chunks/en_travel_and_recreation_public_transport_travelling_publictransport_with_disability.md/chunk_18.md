###  If you have a hearing impairment

Dublin bus provide Real Time Passenger Information with on street signs at
approximately 500 bus stops. Bus Éireann have real time information on their
website. There is a [ TFI real time app
](https://www.transportforireland.ie/live-travel-info-service-updates/real-
time-app/) which combines all real time information Bus Éireann, Dublin Bus,
GO-Ahead Ireland and Iarnród Éireann and Luas.
