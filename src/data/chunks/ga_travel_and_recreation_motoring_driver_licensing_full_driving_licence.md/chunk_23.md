##  More information

More information is available from the [ National Driver Licence Service
website ](https://www.ndls.ie/how-to-apply.html) . You can [ download and read
the rules of the road (pdf) ](https://www.rsa.ie/docs/default-source/road-
safety/rules-of-the-road.pdf?sfvrsn=e0334acb_22) from the Road Safety
Authority website.

You can find information on:

  * [ Learning to drive ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/learning-to-drive/)
  * [ Renewing your driving licence ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/renewing-a-full-driving-licence/)
  * [ Updating your personal details on your driving licence or learner permit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/updating-your-driving-licence-or-learner-permit/)
  * [ Replacing a lost, stolen, or damaged driving licence or learner permit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/replacing-a-lost-or-stolen-irish-driving-licence/)
  * [ Foreign driving licences in Ireland ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/exchanging-foreign-driving-permit/)
