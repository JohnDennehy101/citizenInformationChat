##  Introduction

Customs officers are allowed to carry out [ selective checks on all travellers
](/en/travel-and-recreation/travel-to-ireland/examination-of-travellers-
baggage-by-customs-and-excise-officers/) at all points of entry to Ireland
(air and sea). These checks ensure that you are not carrying prohibited or
restricted goods and combat smuggling.

**Returning to Ireland**

If you are an Irish person living abroad and planning to move home, visit our
[ Returning to Ireland section ](/en/returning-to-ireland/overview-of-
returning-to-ireland/) , where we have the practical information you need to
start a life in Ireland again.
