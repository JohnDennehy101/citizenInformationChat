###  Making an appeal

If you are convicted of a driving offence and disqualified from driving, you
generally have 14 days to appeal the decision to [ the Circuit Court
](/en/justice/courts-system/circuit-court/) .

To [ initiate an appeal ](https://courts.ie/appeals-circuit-court) , you
should lodge a notice of appeal together with a statutory declaration of
service with [ the District Court Clerk in the court area
](https://courts.ie/content/find-us) where the decision to disqualify you was
made. If you decide to appeal the conviction, your disqualification will be
suspended pending the outcome of your appeal.
