###  Taxation of payments to guardians

Social welfare payments made for the benefit of an orphan are [ taxable
](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-
payment/taxation-of-social-welfare-payments/) . However, these payments are
regarded as the beneficial property of the orphan and are therefore assessable
against _their_ income (if any), _not_ against the income of the guardian.
