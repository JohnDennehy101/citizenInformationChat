###  **Illness-related payments**

If you are getting [ Invalidity Pension
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/disability-and-
illness/invalidity-pension/) or [ Illness Benefit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/disability-and-
illness/disability-benefit/) , you must get a medical certificate to show that
you can provide the required care.
