##  What is the Homemaker's Scheme?

The Homemaker's Scheme can help you qualify for the [ State Pension
(Contributory) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/older-
and-retired-people/state-pension-contributory/) if you are a man or woman who
cares full-time for:

  * A child under age 12, or 
  * An ill or disabled person aged 12 or over.
