###  **Carer’s payments**

If you get [ Carer’s Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/carers/carers-allowance/) and are not entitled to credits under that
scheme, you may be entitled to have homemaking years taken into account for
the [ State Pension (Contributory)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/older-and-retired-
people/state-pension-contributory/) .

If you get the [ Carer’s Support Grant
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/carers/carers-support-
grant/) , you may be entitled to have homemaking years taken into account for
the State Pension (Contributory).
