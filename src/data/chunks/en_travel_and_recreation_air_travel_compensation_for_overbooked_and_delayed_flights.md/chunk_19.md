###  Compensation when getting a refund

Where a refund of the cost of the ticket is chosen and you are also entitled
to compensation, the following compensation must be paid:

**Compensation when getting a refund** **Type of flight** |  **Compensation**  
---|---  
Flights of 1,500 km or less  |  €250   
Flights of over 1,500 km within the EU and  
other flights between 1,500 and 3,500 km  |  €400   
All other flights  |  €600
