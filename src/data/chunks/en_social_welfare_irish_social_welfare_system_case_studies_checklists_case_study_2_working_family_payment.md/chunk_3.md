###  **This is an example showing how income is assessed for Working Family
Payment in 2024**

John is living with his partner Mary and their 4 children. He has been working
for a company for some time but his hours are irregular, varying from 30 to 48
hours per week. As a result, he can earn between €330 and €528 gross per week.

Mary is getting [ Carer’s Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/carers/carers-allowance/) of €248 and also works part-time as a home-
help for the Health Service Executive (HSE) earning €96 per week.

Their daughter is 15 years old and has a part-time job at the weekend, earning
€45 per week.

John and Mary get [ Child Benefit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/families-and-children/child-benefit/) of €560 per month for their 4
children.
