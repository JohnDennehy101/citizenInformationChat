###  Telephone Support Allowance

The [ Telephone Support Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-
benefits/telephone-support-allowance/) is an extra payment that helps people
living alone with the cost of communications or a home alert system.

You will qualify for the Telephone Support Allowance if you are getting both
the Living Alone Increase and the [ Fuel Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-
benefits/fuel-allowance/) , with [ certain social welfare payments
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-
benefits/telephone-support-allowance/#bb11a8) .
