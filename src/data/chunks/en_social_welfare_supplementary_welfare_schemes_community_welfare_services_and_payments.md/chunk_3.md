##  What support can I get from the Community Welfare Service?

Community Welfare Officers (CWOs) are responsible for the day-to-day running
of the [ Supplementary Welfare Allowance Scheme ](/en/social-
welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/supplementary-welfare-allow/) .

The payments made under this scheme include:

  * A [ weekly Supplementary Welfare Allowance (SWA) ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/supplementary-welfare-allow/)
  * SWA Supplements, for example, [ Diet and Heating Supplements ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/diet-and-heating-supplements/)
  * [ Rent Supplement ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/rent-supplement/)
  * [ Additional Needs Payments ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/additional-needs-payment/)
