###  What you need to provide when you apply

You will need some of the following to apply:

  * [ PPS number ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/personal-public-service-number/) for you and people in your family that are dependent on you such as your partner or children 
  * Proof of your identity such as [ Public Services Card ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/public-services-card/) , Driving Licence or Passport 
  * Proof of address such as a household bill 
  * Proof of residency such as an Irish Residence Permit (IRP) or EU/EEA passport or national ID card 
  * Proof of income and financial situation such as payslips and bank statements
