####  **Leanaí altrama**

Ní cháilíonn [ leanaí altrama ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-
family-relationships/adoption-and-fostering/fostering/) don LÉCSN. Tá sé seo
amhlaidh toisc go bhfaigheann tuismitheoirí altrama [ Liúntas Cúraim Altrama
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/adoption-
and-fostering/fostering/#60a83b) , lena n-áirítear cabhair le costas éadaí
agus coisbhirt don leanbh.
