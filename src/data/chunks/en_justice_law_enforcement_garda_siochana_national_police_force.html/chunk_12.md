###  Oversight of the Gardaí

An independent [ Policing Authority ](http://www.policingauthority.ie/)
oversees the performance of the Garda Síochána. The Authority members approve
a 3 year strategy statement and give advice to the Minster for Justice. They
oversee the Gardaí’s compliance with their [ Code of Ethics for the Garda
Síochána (pdf) ](https://www.garda.ie/en/about-us/publications/policy-
documents/code-of-ethics-2020.pdf) . The Policing Authority is established
under the [ Garda Síochána (Policing Authority and Miscellaneous Provisions)
Act 2015 (pdf)
](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/pdf/2015/en.act.2015.0049.pdf) .
