###  Energy efficiency grants

You could qualify for a [ grant to upgrade your home energy system
](/en/housing/housing-grants-and-schemes/grants-for-home-renovations-and-
improvements/grants-for-a-home-energy-upgrade/) and improve the BER rating of
your home. This will lower the amount of electricity or gas that you use.
There are also [ grants available for solar panels ](/en/housing/housing-
grants-and-schemes/grants-for-home-renovations-and-improvements/grants-for-
solar-panels/) .
