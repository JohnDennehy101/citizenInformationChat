###  Rent increases

If you live in a [ Rent Pressure Zone ](/en/housing/renting-a-home/rent-
increases#lcfdd0) , your landlord cannot increase the rent by more than 2% per
year. [ Outside of Rent Pressure Zones ](/en/housing/renting-a-home/rent-
increases#l4c97b) , your landlord can increase rent in line with the current
market value for similar properties in the area.

Your landlord cannot [ increase the rent ](/en/housing/renting-a-
home/landlords-rights-and-responsibilities/rent-increases-in-private-rented-
housing/) more than once per year. Disputes about rent increases can be
referred to the Residential Tenancies Board.
