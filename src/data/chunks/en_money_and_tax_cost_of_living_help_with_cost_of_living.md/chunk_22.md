###  Youth Travel Card

A [ Young Adult Leap Card ](https://about.leapcard.ie/young-adult-and-student-
card-launch) is available to all 19 to 23 year olds (up to their 24th
birthday). Fares are reduced by up to 50%. Existing Student Leap cardholders
can also get the same discounted rates, without needing to change their card.
