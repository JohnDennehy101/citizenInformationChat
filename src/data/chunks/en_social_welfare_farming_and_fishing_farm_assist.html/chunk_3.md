##  How to qualify for Farm Assist

To qualify for Farm Assist, you must:

  * Be a farmer 
  * Be farming land in Ireland 
  * Be aged between 18 and 66 
  * Pass a means test – see ‘how your income is assessed for Farm Assist’ below. 

You are considered a farmer, if you farm land that you own or lease and that
you use for the purpose of _husbandry_ . Husbandry means working the land with
the aim of taking produce from the land.
