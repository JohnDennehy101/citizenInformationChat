##  Other supports for people with disabilities

You can read the [ Guide to entitlements for people with disabilities
](https://www.citizensinformationboard.ie/downloads/guides/guide_to_entitlements_for_people_with_disabilities_2022.pdf)
(pdf) for information on the range of supports available to people with
disabilities, in health and other areas.
