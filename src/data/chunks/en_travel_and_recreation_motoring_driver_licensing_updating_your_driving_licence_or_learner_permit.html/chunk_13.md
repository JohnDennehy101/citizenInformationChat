###  **Change of name using the Irish version of your name**

You must submit evidence that you are using the Irish version of your name
i.e. 2 official documents that show you've been using your new name for 2
years on the date of application at the NDLS centre. Alternatively, you can
present an acceptable [ photographic I.D ](https://www.ndls.ie/identity-
validation.html#photographic-i-d-) issued in your new name. See the [ NDLS
website ](https://www.ndls.ie/licensed-driver/update-my-personal-
details.html#to-apply-to-update-your-name-to-the-irish-language-version-of-
your-name) for more information.
