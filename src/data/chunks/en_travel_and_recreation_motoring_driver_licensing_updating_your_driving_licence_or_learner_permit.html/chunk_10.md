###  **Change of name upon marriage or civil partnership**

You must provide your original civil marriage or civil partnership
certificate. Certified translations must be submitted with the original
marriage or civil partnership certificate if they are not in English or Irish.
Alternatively, you can present an acceptable form of [ photographic ID
](https://www.ndls.ie/identity-validation.html#photographic-i-d-) issued in
your new name (see Photographic ID below). See the [ NDLS website
](https://www.ndls.ie/licensed-driver/update-my-personal-details.html#to-
apply-in-person-at-an-ndls-centre-to-update-your-surname-following-marriage-
or-civil-partnership) for more information.
