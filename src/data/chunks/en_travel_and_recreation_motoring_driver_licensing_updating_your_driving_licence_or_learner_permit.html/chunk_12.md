###  **Change of name following deed poll**

You must provide an original [ Irish deed poll document ](/en/birth-family-
relationships/problems-in-marriages-and-other-relationships/changing-your-
name/) which has been enrolled in the Irish High Court. Alternatively, you can
present an acceptable [ photographic I.D ](https://www.ndls.ie/identity-
validation.html#photographic-i-d-) issued in your new name. See the [ NDLS
website ](https://www.ndls.ie/licensed-driver/update-my-personal-
details.html#to-apply-in-person-at-an-ndls-centre-to-change-your-name-
following-a-change-of-name-by-deed-poll) for more information.
