##  COVID-19 enhanced Illness Benefit

The [ enhanced Illness Benefit for COVID-19 absences ](/en/social-
welfare/disability-and-illness/covid19-enhanced-illness-benefit/) ended on
Friday, 30 September 2022. After 30 September 2022, if you are advised to
self-isolate due to COVID-19 and have a medical certificate from your GP, you
can apply for [ Illness Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-
illness/illness-benefit/) .
