####  Gather your documentation

The switching pack will tell you the list of documents your new bank needs to
open your new account. Get your [ photo ID and proof of address ](/en/money-
and-tax/personal-finance/banking/financial-institutions-and-identification/) ,
such as an electricity or gas bill.

You may need to have a copy of previous bank statements if you want to apply
for credit in the future. This can be important if you are applying for a
mortgage. Before closing your account with your old bank, ask for a copy of
your old statements either online or through your local branch.
