###  Special Drawing Rights (SDR)

Under the Montreal Convention, the liability limits are set in [ Special
Drawing Rights (SDR) ](http://www.imf.org/external/np/exr/facts/sdr.htm) .
These are a mix of currency values set up by the International Monetary Fund
(IMF). The [ current value of an SDR in Euro is available on the IMF's website
](http://www.imf.org/external/np/fin/data/rms_mth.aspx?reportType=CVSDR) . The
liability limits are reviewed every 5 years.
