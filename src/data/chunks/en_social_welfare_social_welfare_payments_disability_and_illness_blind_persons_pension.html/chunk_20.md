###  **If you are under 18**

If you are under 18 and you are blind or have low vision, you should apply for
Blind Pension 3 months before your 18th birthday.

While you can't get Blind Pension until you are 18, you may qualify for [
Disability Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/disability-and-illness/disability-allowance/) if you are 16 or over.
