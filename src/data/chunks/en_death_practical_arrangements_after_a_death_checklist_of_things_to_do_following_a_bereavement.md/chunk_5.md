##  Miscellaneous

  * Notify the deceased's employer if an employee or the school/college if in education 
  * [ Contact the Local Health Office ](http://www.hse.ie/eng/services/list/1/LHO/) of the deceased or voluntary organisation if they were getting [ community care health services ](/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-community/community-care-services/)
  * Contact the doctor/hospital if the deceased was awaiting any appointments 
  * Contact the travel agency, hotel, airline etc., if the deceased had made reservations, to enquire about any refunds 
  * The deceased's passport can be [ returned to the passport office for cancellation ](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/passports/faqs/)
  * Notify the deceased's local authority to update the register of electors by submitting a [ third party request ](https://www.checktheregister.ie/en-IE/forms)
