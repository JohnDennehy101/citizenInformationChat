##  Further information

The National Learners Database has information on entry requirements for all
third-level courses in Ireland on [ Qualifax.ie ](https://www.qualifax.ie/) .

The European Commission has an online tool that allows you to [ compare and
contrast qualifications within Europe
](https://ec.europa.eu/ploteus/search/site?f%5b0%5d=im_field_entity_type%3A97)
.

Quality and Qualifications Ireland (QQI) has specific guides on how to:

  * [ Compare UK qualifications (pdf) ](https://www.qqi.ie/sites/default/files/2023-08/Qualifications%20Can%20Cross%20Boundaries-Aug-2023_0.pdf)
  * [ Compare qualifications in Ireland and Hong Kong (pdf) ](https://www.qqi.ie/Downloads/36563-IRELAND-HONG%20KONG%20Booklet_FINAL.pdf#search=hong%20kong%2A)
  * [ Compare qualifications between Ireland and New Zealand (pdf) ](https://www.qqi.ie/Downloads/NZQA%20New%20Zealand%20Webx.pdf)

Read our page on [ recognition of professional qualifications in Ireland
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/starting-
work/Recognition-of-professional-qualifications-in-ireland/) .
