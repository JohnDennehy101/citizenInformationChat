###  **Electric vehicle grants for taxis, hackneys, and limousines (SPSV)**

If you drive a small public service vehicle (SPSV), you may get a grant from
the National Transport Authority (NTA). Grants of up to €20,000 or €25,000 for
wheelchair accessible EV’s are available. The amount of the grant you get
depends on the car’s age, specifications, and mileage.

The application closing date is 13 December 2024 or earlier if notified by the
NTA or if no more funding is available. You can find out more information on
the [ National Transport Authority website
](https://www.nationaltransport.ie/taxi/forms-and-guides/) .
