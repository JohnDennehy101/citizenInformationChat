###  The role of Ministers

Under the Constitution, [ Departments of State ](/en/government-in-
ireland/how-government-works/national-government/departments-of-state/) are
administered by members of the Government (Ministers) and the Minister is in
charge of the Department. This means that final responsibility rests with the
Minister but many of the functions of the Department and responsibility for
various actions are assigned to [ civil servants ](/en/government-in-
ireland/how-government-works/national-government/the-civil-service/) .
