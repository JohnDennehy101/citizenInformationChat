###  Ábhar Gaolmhar

  * [ _Cearta comharbais tar éis idirscartham colscartha nó scaoilte_ ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/separation-and-divorce/succession/)

Baineann do chearta comharbais le d'oidhreacht, agus is féidir leis na cearta
seo athrú tar éis idirscartha nó colscartha.

  * [ _Capital Acquisitions Tax exemptions and reliefs_ ](/ga/money-and-tax/tax/capital-taxes/capital-acquisitions-tax-exemptions-and-reliefs/)

Some types of gifts and inheritances are exempt from Capital Acquisitions Tax
(CAT). Others qualify for reliefs that reduce the amount of CAT you have to
pay.

  * [ _How pensions are assessed during a separation, divorce and dissolution_ ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/separation-and-divorce/sep-divorce-pensions/)

When you end a relationship by judicial separation, divorce or dissolution,
the court can decide to share your pension with your former partner and (or)
any dependent children. This is called a pension adjustment order.
