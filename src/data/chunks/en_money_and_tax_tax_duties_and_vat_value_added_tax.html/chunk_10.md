##  Claiming back VAT

**People not registered for VAT  
** Under certain circumstances, people can claim back VAT. People with
disabilities can claim a [ VAT refund on certain aids and appliances
](/en/money-and-tax/tax/tax-credits-and-reliefs-for-people-with-
disabilities/vat-refunds-on-aids-and-appliances-used-by-people-with-
disabilities/) .

**People registered for VAT  
** A business person must register for VAT to fulfil tax obligations to the
government. Registering for VAT also allows a business to reclaim VAT on
purchases.
