###  Programme refugees and Convention refugees

A Programme refugee is a person who has been invited to Ireland under a
Government decision in response to a humanitarian request, usually from the [
United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees ](http://www.unhcr.org/cgi-
bin/texis/vtx/home) (UNHCR), either for the purposes of temporary protection
or resettlement.

A Convention refugee is a person who fulfils the requirements of the
definition of a refugee under the terms of the [ Geneva Convention
](http://www.unhcr.org/pages/49da0e466.html) (as described above) and is
granted refugee status.
