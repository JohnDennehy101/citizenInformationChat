###  **Job Clubs**

Job Clubs are run by the Department of Social Protection, to support
jobseekers through a drop-in service and formal workshops.

At Job Club, you can:

  * Get help preparing your CV and cover letters 
  * Get help preparing for interview 
  * Develop an individual ‘employment action plan’ 
  * Access phone and internet services to help you with job-seeking 
  * Get up-to-date information on the local economy and job opportunities 
  * Speak with potential employers. 

You should [ contact your local Intreo Centre
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) for more
information on Job Clubs.
