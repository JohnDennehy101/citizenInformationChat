##  How long is Back to Work Family Dividend paid?

BTWFD will last for up to 2 years, if you remain in work.

If you claim a primary social welfare payment at any time within the 2-year
period, the BTWFD payment will stop. If your spouse, civil partner or
cohabitant claims a payment, the BTWFD payment will also stop.
