##  Where to apply for the Local Authority Purchase and Renovation Loan

You apply to [ your local authority
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/942f74-local-authorities/) for the Local
Authority Purchase and Renovation Loan.

_**Dáta an Leasaithe Deireanaigh:** 24 Iúil 2024 _
