##  How to apply for the Local Authority Purchase and Renovation Loan

To apply for the Local Authority Purchase and Renovation Loan, complete the [
application form ](https://purchaseandrenovationloan.ie/application-form/) .

You submit your application and supporting documents to the housing section of
the [ local authority ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/942f74-local-
authorities/) in the area where you want to buy and renovate your home. You
can submit your application by post or in person. Contact your local authority
to check how they would like you to submit your application.

You will get a decision in writing about your application.
