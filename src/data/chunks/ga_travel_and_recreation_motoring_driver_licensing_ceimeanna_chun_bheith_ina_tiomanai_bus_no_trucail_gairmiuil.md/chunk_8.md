##  Céim 5: Pas a fháil sa tástáil tiomána

Ní mór duit tástáil tiomána a dhéanamh chun ceadúnas tiomána trucaile nó bus a
fháil. Déanann an [ Údarás um Shábháilteacht ar Bhóithre (RSA)
](https://www.rsa.ie/) tástáil tiománaithe in Éirinn. Is é €120 an táille don
triail.

Faigh amach inár leathanach faoi [ tástálacha tiomána
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-
recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driving-test/) :

  * [ Cad is gá duit a ghlacadh le tástáil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driving-test/#371883)
  * [ Ag cur isteach ar thástáil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driving-test/#492027)
  * [ Ag ullmhú le haghaidh tástála ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driving-test/#601889)
  * [ Cad a dhéanfar tástáil ort ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driving-test/#601889)
  * [ Achomharc a dhéanamh ar thoradh tástála ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driving-test/#07db8d)
