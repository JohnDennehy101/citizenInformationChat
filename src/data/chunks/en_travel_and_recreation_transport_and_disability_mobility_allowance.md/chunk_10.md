##  Rates

The allowance is paid monthly. The lower rate is payable to those who are
availing of the [ Disabled Drivers and Disabled Passengers (Tax Concessions)
Scheme ](/en/travel-and-recreation/transport-and-disability/tax-relief-for-
disabled-drivers-and-disabled-passengers/) . The rates are:

**High rate** |  **Low rate**  
---|---  
€208.50 per month  |  €104.25 per month
