##  Tuilleadh faisnéise

Tá tuilleadh faisnéise ag ComReg maidir le [ gearán a dhéanamh faoi
leathanbhanda, fón baile, fón póca, seirbhísí ardráta agus seirbhísí poist
(pdf) ](https://www.comreg.ie/media/dlm_uploads/2019/10/ComReg-Complaints-
Guide-En.pdf) .

Is féidir leat léamh faoi [ rialú teileafóin, idirlín agus teilifíse
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/phone-internet-tv-and-postal-
services/regulation-of-postal-services-phone-internet-radion-and-tv/) .

Is féidir leat faisnéis a fháil freisin faoi [ chonarthaí teileafóin
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/phone-internet-tv-and-postal-
services/phone-contracts/) agus faoi [ chonarthaí idirlín
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/phone-internet-tv-and-postal-
services/internet-contracts/) .
