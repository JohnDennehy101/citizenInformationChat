####  Leanaí

Sa chás go bhfuil leanaí ann, is dóigh go dtiocfaidh saincheisteanna chun cinn
maidir le  coimeád  , [ caomhnóireacht ](/ga/birth-family-
relationships/cohabiting-couples/guardianship-and-cohabiting-couples/) agus [
rochtain ar na leanaí ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/cohabiting-
couples/access-to-children-cohabiting-couples/) . Ina theannta sin, cé nach
bhfuil freagracht airgeadais ag tuismitheoirí neamhphósta a chéile a chothú,
tá an bheirt tuismitheoirí freagrach as [ cothabháil a gcuid leanaí
](/ga/birth-family-relationships/parenting-alone/maintenance-lone-parents/) .
