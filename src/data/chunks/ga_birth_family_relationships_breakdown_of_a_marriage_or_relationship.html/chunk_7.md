##  Caidrimh chomhchónaithe

Níl [ cearta lánúineacha comhchónaithe ](/ga/birth-family-
relationships/cohabiting-couples/rights-of-cohabiting-couples/) aitheanta i
ndlí na hÉireann go ginearálta. Má chliseann an caidreamh, tá impleachtaí
tábhachta ann do leithéidí rudaí ar nós [ úinéireacht réadmhaoine ](/ga/birth-
family-relationships/problems-in-marriages-and-other-relationships/property-
rights-and-the-breakdown-of-a-cohabiting-relationship/) . Tá [ scéim shásaimh
do lánúineacha comhchónaithe ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/problems-in-
marriages-and-other-relationships/redress-scheme-for-cohabiting-couples/) a
bhí i gcaidreamh fadtéarmach nó a raibh leanaí acu.
