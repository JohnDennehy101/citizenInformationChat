###  **Child dependant**

A child dependant is referred to as a _qualified child_ .

A qualified child must:

  * Live in the State (be resident) 
  * Not be detained in a reformatory or industrial school 
  * Satisfy the age conditions 

Children under 18 are regarded as qualified children. Payments for child
dependants are made for children over 18 in some circumstances.
