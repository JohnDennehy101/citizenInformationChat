##  Updating your details

You can update your details online at [ checktheregister.ie
](https://www.checktheregister.ie/en-IE/forms) .

If you are homeless, you can also use the [ paper form ERF 2
](https://checktheregister.ie/en-IE/forms) to update your details.

Your PPS number and date of birth will not be published on the electoral
register.
