##  Tacaíochtaí airgeadais

Ag brath ar do chás, d’fhéadfá a bheith i dteideal tacaíochtaí airgeadais chun
filleadh ar an oideachas.

Áirítear ar an raon tacaíochtaí féideartha:

  * [ Clár um Fhilleadh ar an Oideachas ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/back-to-education/back-to-education-programme/)
  * [ Liúntas chun Filleadh ar an Oideachas ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/back-to-education/back-to-education-allowance/)
  * [ Liúntais oiliúna ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/further-education-and-training/training-allowances/)

Is féidir leat dul i dteagmháil le [ d'Ionad Faisnéise do Shaoránaigh
](https://centres.citizensinformation.ie/) áitiúil chun faisnéis a fháil faoi
na tacaíochtaí airgeadais atá ar fáil duit.

Is féidir leat léamh freisin faoi na [ tacaí airgeadais don oiliúint agus don
oideachas ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/58c68-get-financial-support-for-
training-or-education/) ar Gov.ie.
