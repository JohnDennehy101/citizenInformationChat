###  **Má tá míchumas nó riachtanais speisialta ag do leanbh**

In Éirinn, féadfaidh leanaí a bhfuil riachtanais speisialta oideachais acu
freastal ar:

  * Ranganna i mbunscoileanna príomhshrutha 
  * Ranganna speisialta i scoileanna príomhshrutha 
  * Scoileanna speisialta, má tá cineál áirithe míchumais nó riachtanais speisialta acu 

D’fhéadfadh do leanbh cúram breise agus tacaíocht oideachais a fháil ó
chúntóirí riachtanas speisialta (CRSanna).

Faigh tuilleadh eolais faoi [ na cineálacha oideachais speisialta in Éirinn
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/the-irish-education-
system/special-education/) .

B'fhéidir gur mhaith leat léamh freisin:

  * [ Oideachas riachtanas speisialta: iar-bhunscoil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-post-primary-school/post-primary-education-for-students-with-special-needs/)
  * [ Iompar scoile do leanaí le riachtanais speisialta ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-primary-school/school-transport-for-children-with-special-needs/)
