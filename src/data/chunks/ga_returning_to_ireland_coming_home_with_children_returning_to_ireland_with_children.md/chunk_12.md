##  Cearta fostaíochta do thuismitheoirí in Éirinn

Tá tuismitheoirí atá fostaithe in Éirinn i dteideal cineálacha éagsúla saoire
reachtúla ón obair. Is ionann saoire reachtúil agus an t-íosmhéid saoire a
bhfuil tú i dteideal a fháil, mar sin féadfaidh d’fhostóir níos mó ná seo a
thabhairt duit.

I measc na gcineálacha saoire reachtúil do thuismitheoirí i bhfostaíocht tá:

  * 26 seachtain de [ shaoire mháithreachais ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/maternity-leave/) (agus suas le 16 seachtaine gan phá) 
  * 24 seachtain de [ shaoire uchtaíoch ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/adoptive-leave/) (agus suas le 16 seachtaine gan phá) 
  * 2 sheachtain [ saoire atharthachta ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/paternity-leave/)
  * 7 seachtaine de [ shaoire tuismitheora ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/parents-leave/)
  * 26 seachtain de [ shaoire tuismitheora ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/parental-leave/) gan phá 
  * 5 lá saoire in aon tréimhse 12 mhí le haghaidh [ cúram leighis tromchúiseach ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/unpaid-leave-for-medical-care/)

Léigh tuilleadh faoi [ Shaoire do thuismitheoirí
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-
conditions/leave-and-holidays/leave-for-parents/) .
