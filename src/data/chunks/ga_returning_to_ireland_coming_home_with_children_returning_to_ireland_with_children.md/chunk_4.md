###  **Cúram leanaí**

Tá raon roghanna [ cúram leanaí
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/pre-school-education-and-
childcare/your-childcare-options/) ann do leanaí réamhscoile.

Má tá do leanbh os cionn 2 bhliain agus 8 mí nuair a fhilleann tú, d’fhéadfadh
sé/sí a bheith incháilithe d’uaireanta réamhscoile saor in aisce a chuirtear
ar fáil tríd an [ Scéim um Chúram agus Oideachas na Luath-Óige (SCOL)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/pre-school-education-and-
childcare/early-childhood-care-and-education-scheme/) .
