###  **Scoil**

Má tá leanaí in aois scoile agat, seans go mbeidh ort do leanbh a [ chlárú sa
bhunscoil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-
ireland/education-and-schooling/enrolling-your-child-in-a-primary-school-
after-returning-to-ireland/) nó [ sa mheánscoil (iar-bhunscoil)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/education-and-
schooling/enrolling-your-child-in-a-secondary-school-after-returning-to-
ireland/) .

Léigh faoi [ bhunscoil a roghnú
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-
education/going-to-primary-school/choosing-a-primary-school/) agus [ faoin
scéim iompair bunscoile
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-
education/going-to-primary-school/primary-school-transport-scheme/) .

Is féidir leat cuairt a thabhairt freisin ar ár leathanaigh ar [ roghnú
meánscoil (iar-bhunscoil)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-
education/going-to-post-primary-school/types-of-post-primary-school/) agus [
Iompar scoile do dhaltaí iar-bhunscoile
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-
education/going-to-post-primary-school/post-primary-school-transport-scheme/)
.

Mar mhalairt air sin, is féidir leat smaoineamh ar [ scolaíocht bhaile in
Éirinn ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/the-irish-education-
system/home-education/) .
