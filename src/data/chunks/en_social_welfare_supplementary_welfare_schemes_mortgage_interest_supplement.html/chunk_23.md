###  **Appealing a decision**

If you are not satisfied with a decision made in relation to Mortgage Interest
Supplement, you should request the reasons for the decision, in writing, from
the CWO. You should provide any extra documentation to back up your case.

If the decision is not changed, you can [ appeal to the Social Welfare Appeals
Office ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-
welfare-system/social-welfare-appeals/) .
