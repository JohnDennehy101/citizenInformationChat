##  Tax allowances

Tax allowances also reduce the amount of tax you have to pay. The amount a tax
allowance reduces your tax by depends on how much income tax you pay at the
highest rate of tax. This is because the allowance is subtracted from your
income before tax is calculated.

See an [ explanation and example of how tax allowances are calculated
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/how-your-
tax-is-calculated/#d43041) .
