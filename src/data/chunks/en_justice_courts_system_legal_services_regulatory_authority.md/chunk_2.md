##  Introduction

The Legal Services Regulatory Authority (LSRA) is responsible for the
regulation of legal practitioners (solicitors and barristers) in Ireland. It
was established in 2016 under [ the Legal Services Regulation Act 2015
](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2015/act/65/enacted/en/html) . The LSRA
is independent in the performance of its functions.

practitioners and to ensure high standards. It investigates complaints against
