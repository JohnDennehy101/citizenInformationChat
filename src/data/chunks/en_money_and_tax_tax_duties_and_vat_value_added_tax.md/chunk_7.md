###  Gifts from outside the EU

You can receive a gift from outside the EU up to the value of €45 without
paying import charges. However, the gift must be correctly declared. More
information is available in Revenue’s [ guide to relief available for imports
from non-EU countries ](http://www.revenue.ie/en/importing-vehicles-duty-free-
allowances/gifts-and-consignments-of-low-value/rules-for-duty-relief-on-gift-
consignments.aspx) .
