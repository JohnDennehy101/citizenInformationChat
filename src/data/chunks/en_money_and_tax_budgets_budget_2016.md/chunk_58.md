####  Education

**Pupil/teacher ratios** will reduce to 27:1 in primary schools and 18.7:1 in
secondary schools.

**New education posts** will be created. There will be 2,260 new classroom
teachers, including 600 new resource teachers.

Funding for the **School Meals Programme** will increase by €3 million.

€3 million will be allocated to support disadvantaged students including the
**Student Assistance Fund** in higher education.
