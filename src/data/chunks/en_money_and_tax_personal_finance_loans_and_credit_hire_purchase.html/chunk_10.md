##  Making a complaint about a hire purchase agreement

For independent information or help in understanding any of the terms and
conditions of your hire purchase (or any other loan) contract, you can [
contact the CCPC ](https://www.ccpc.ie/consumers/contact/) .

For free and confidential advice about HP debt, [ contact MABS
](https://www.mabs.ie/en/contact-us/) .

If you want to [ make a complaint ](/en/consumer/how-to-complain/complain-
about-financial-services/) about a HP agreement, you should make your
complaint to the finance company. If you are not satisfied with their
response, you can make a formal complaint to the [ Financial Services and
Pensions Ombudsman (FSPO) ](https://www.fspo.ie/) . The FSPO can award you
compensation where your rights have been breached, or where there is evidence
of unfair treatment.

You do not have to accept the solution recommended by the Ombudsman. You can
look for redress by taking legal action against the finance company.
