###  Class A tapered credit

A weekly tapered PRSI credit of €12 is available for employees insured at
Class A whose earnings are between €352.01 and €424 in a week. The maximum
PRSI credit of €12 per week applies to gross weekly earnings of €352.01. A
person earning €352.01 pays €14.08 PRSI (4%). After the €12 credit is deducted
they will pay PRSI of €2.08. For people earning between €352.01 and €424, the
credit of €12 is reduced by one-sixth of earnings over €352.01. There is no
PRSI credit once earnings exceed €424.
