##  Further information

More information about the Assessment of Need is available on the [ HSE
website ](https://www.hse.ie/eng/services/list/4/disability/disability-
assessment/application-for-assessment-of-need-2017.pdf) .

You can read the [ Standards for the Assessment of Need (pdf)
](https://www.hse.ie/eng/services/list/4/disability/disability-
assessment/standards-for-need-assessment.pdf) .

Each Local Health Office has an Assessment Officer that can help you with your
application. You can contact your Local Health Office by:

  * Phoning the HSE information line on 1800 700 700 
  * Emailing [ info@hse.ie ](mailto:info@hse.ie)
  * Visiting the [ HSE website ](https://www.hse.ie/eng/services/list/4/disability/disability-assessment/assessment-officers.html)
