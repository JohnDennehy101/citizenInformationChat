###  **Taxi fare increases**

From 1 December 2024 [ , taxi fares will increase on average by 9%
](https://www.nationaltransport.ie/news/nta-board-approves-9-increase-in-
national-maximum-taxi-fare/) **.**

The Special Rate previously applied during Christmas and New Year will now
extend to weekend nights from midnight to 4:00 AM, Friday to Sunday, to
encourage nighttime service. The pre-booking fee will increase from €2 to €3.
