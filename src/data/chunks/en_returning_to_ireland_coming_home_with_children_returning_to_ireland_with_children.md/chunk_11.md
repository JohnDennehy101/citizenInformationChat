###  **Other social welfare supports**

If you have enough PRSI contributions, you may qualify for [ Maternity Benefit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/families-and-children/maternity-benefit/) following the birth of your
baby, or [ Adoptive Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/social-welfare-payments/families-and-children/adoptive-benefit/)
following adoption of a child.

[ Paternity Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/social-welfare-payments/families-and-children/paternity-benefit/) is
paid to new parents (other than the mother of the child) during [ paternity
leave ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-
and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/paternity-leave/) .

Read more about the different types of [ social welfare payments for families
and children ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-
welfare-payments/families-and-children/payments-to-families-and-children/) .
