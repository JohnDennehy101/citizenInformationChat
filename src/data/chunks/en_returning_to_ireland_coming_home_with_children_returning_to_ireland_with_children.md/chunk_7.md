###  **Third-level education**

If your children are going to third-level education, they may qualify for [
free third-level fees ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/third-
level-education/fees-and-supports-for-third-level-education/fees/) . Your
child must have been resident in Ireland or another EU country for 3 out of
the previous 5 years.

They may also be eligible for the [ Student Grant Scheme
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/third-level-education/fees-
and-supports-for-third-level-education/student-grant-scheme/) (also known as
the SUSI grant). The student grant may be used to cover your childâ€™s living
costs, their [ Student Contribution
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/third-level-education/fees-
and-supports-for-third-level-education/fees/#ld8ce9) , the cost of essential
field trips, or tuition fees if they do not qualify for the Free Fees Scheme.

Read more about [ Accessing the education system on your return to Ireland
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/education-and-
schooling/accessing-the-education-system-on-your-return-to-ireland/) .
