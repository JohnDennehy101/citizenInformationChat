##  How do I pay Vacant Homes Tax?

When you make your VHT return, you can choose to pay VHT:

  * In a single debit card or credit card payment by 1 January 2024 
  * In a single annual debit instruction on 21 March 2024 
  * In a number of direct debit payments during 2024
