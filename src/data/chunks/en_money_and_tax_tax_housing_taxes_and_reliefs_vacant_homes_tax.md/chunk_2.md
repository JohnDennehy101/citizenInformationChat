##  What is Vacant Homes Tax?

Vacant Homes Tax (VHT) is a new tax introduced in 2023. It is an annual tax
that will apply on 1 November each year.

VHT applies to property that:

  * Can be lived in and 
  * Has been stayed in for less than 30 days in the previous 12 months 

The previous 12-month period is called the _chargeable period_ . For 2023 VHT,
the chargeable period is 1 November 2022 to 31 October 2023.
