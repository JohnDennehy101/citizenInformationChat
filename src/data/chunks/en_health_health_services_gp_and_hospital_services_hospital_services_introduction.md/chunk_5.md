##  Private patients

If you choose to be a private patient in a public hospital, you become the
private patient of the consultant treating you and you must pay for all the
treatments and services you get from them. There is also a set [ daily charge
for private patients in public hospitals. ](/en/health/health-services/gp-and-
hospital-services/hospital-charges/)

Private hospitals can set their own charges for patients. [ Private health
insurance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-
system/private-health-insurance/) may cover some or all of the cost.
