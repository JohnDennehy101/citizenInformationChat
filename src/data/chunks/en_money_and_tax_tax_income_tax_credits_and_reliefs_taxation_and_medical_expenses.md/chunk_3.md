##  What healthcare expenses can I claim tax back on?

You can claim tax relief on:

  * Doctor and consultant fees 
  * Maintenance or treatment in a hospital, treatment facility (such as a clinic) or a [ nursing home ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-credits-reliefs-and-exemptions/health-and-age/health-expenses/nursing-home-and-additional-nursing-care-expenses.aspx)
  * [ Transport by ambulance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-system/emergency-health-services-in-ireland/)
  * Employing a qualified nurse at home 
  * Treatment from a psychologist or psychotherapist 
  * Acupuncture 
  * [ Kidney patients' expenses ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-credits-reliefs-and-exemptions/health-and-age/health-expenses/additional-expenses-for-a-kidney-patient.aspx)
  * Specialised dental treatment 
  * Maternity care 
  * [ In-vitro fertilisation (IVF) ](/en/birth-family-relationships/before-your-baby-is-born/fertility-treatments-and-dahr/)
  * [ Speech and language therapy ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-community/speech-and-language-therapy-services/) for a child under 18 or in full-time education 
  * Educational psychological assessments for a child under 18 or in full-time education
