###  Home nursing

You can claim relief on the cost of employing a qualified nurse at home at the
standard rate of 20%.

See the Revenue website for [ more information on employing a qualified nurse
in the home ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-credits-reliefs-and-
exemptions/health-and-age/employing-a-carer/health-expenses-for-home-
nursing.aspx) .
