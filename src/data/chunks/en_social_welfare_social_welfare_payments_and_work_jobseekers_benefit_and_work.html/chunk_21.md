##  How to apply

If your working hours are reduced to 3 days or less per week you can apply for
Short Time Work Support under the Jobseeker’s Benefit scheme.

To apply you must fill in an application **form for Jobseeker's Benefit** and
an application form for [ Short Time Work Support (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/80601/58d1561d-1359-4bbe-93f0-03c0e46f8774.pdf) .

You can get forms posted to you by emailing your request with your name and
address to [ forms@welfare.ie ](mailto:forms@welfare.ie) .

You can also apply for support when applying for Jobseeker’s Benefit on [
MyWelfare.ie ](http://services.mywelfare.ie/) . You will need a basic [
MyGovID ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-
welfare-payment/applying-for-social-welfare-payments-and-services-online/) to
apply online using [ MyWelfare.ie. ](http://services.mywelfare.ie/)

You can also apply at your local [ Intreo Centre or Social Welfare Branch
Office ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/40cf48-find-your-local-intreo-office/)
.
