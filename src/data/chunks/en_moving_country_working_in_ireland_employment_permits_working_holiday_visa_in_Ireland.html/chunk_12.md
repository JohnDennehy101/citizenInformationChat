###  Taiwan

The Working Holiday Programme allows up to 400 citizens of Taiwan between the
ages of 18 and 30 to live and work in Ireland for up to 12 months. You may
also study for up to 6 months. Applications from citizens of Taiwan are dealt
with by the [ Immigration Service Delivery (ISD)
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/coming-to-work-in-ireland/what-are-my-
options-for-working-in-ireland/coming-to-work-for-more-than-90-days/working-
holidays-in-ireland/) .
