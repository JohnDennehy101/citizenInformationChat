###  Argentina

Argentinian citizens between the ages of 18 and 35 can apply for a Working
Holiday Visa. There are 200 places available each year. If you are successful,
you may stay in Ireland for a period up to 12 months. You can leave and re-
enter Ireland during your stay and you can work, as long as it is not a
permanent job. You may also study if you wish. You can find the application
form on the [ Embassy of Ireland web page.
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/argentina/buenosaires/)
