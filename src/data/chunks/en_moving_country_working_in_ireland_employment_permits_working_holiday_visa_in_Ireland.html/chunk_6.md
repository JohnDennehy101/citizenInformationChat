###  Canada

Canadian citizens between the ages of 18 and 35 can apply for a Working
Holiday Authorisation. This allows you to live and work in Ireland for up to 2
years. You can apply directly to the [ Embassy
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/canada/ottawa/) or through [ SWAP Working Holidays
](https://www.swap.ca/) . There is a fee of $150 CAD.
