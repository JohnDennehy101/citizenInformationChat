##  Introduction

The child age limit for the [ One-Parent Family Payment ](/en/social-
welfare/families-and-children/one-parent-family-payment/) (OFP) is set at 7
years of age. There are exemptions to the age limit for people parenting alone
who are getting a Domiciliary Care Allowance or Blind Pension, who are caring
or who have been recently bereaved.

This document gives an overview of the options open to you. You can also get
more information and advice at [ your Intreo Centre or Social Welfare Branch
Office ](http://www.welfare.ie/en/Pages/List-of-Social-Welfare-Local-and-
Branch-Offices-by-County.aspx) or your nearest [ Citizens Information Centre
](http://centres.citizensinformation.ie/) .
