##  Where to apply

If you have lost or if your Irish passport is stolen here in Ireland, [ notify
the Passport Office immediately
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/passports/contact-us/) . You can report your
passport as lost or stolen when you apply online for your new passport.

If you have lost your Irish passport abroad, notify the local police and then
your [ nearest Irish embassy or consulate
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/embassies/) .

If you have an emergency, [ contact the Passport Service via webchat
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/passports/contact-us/) .
