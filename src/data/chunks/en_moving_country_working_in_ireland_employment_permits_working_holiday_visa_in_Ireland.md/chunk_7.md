###  Chile

There are 100 places available for Chileans between the ages of 18 and 30. If
selected you may stay in Ireland for up to one year and you may leave and
return during your stay. You may work as long as it is not a permanent job and
you may study or train. You can find the application form on the [ Embassy of
Ireland webpage ](https://www.ireland.ie/en/chile/santiago/) . There is an
application fee of 60,000 CLP.
