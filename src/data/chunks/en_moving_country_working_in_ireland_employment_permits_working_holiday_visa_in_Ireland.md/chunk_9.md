###  Japan

The Working Holiday Programme is open to Japanese citizens between the ages of
18 and 25. If you have been in full time education or employment, the age
limit can be raised to 30. The Embassy of Ireland has certain periods of time
when they accept applications. These are [ listed on their website
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/japan/tokyo/) . Once the application period has
started you can download the application, gather the required documents, and
submit your application by mail to the Embassy of Ireland in Tokyo.
