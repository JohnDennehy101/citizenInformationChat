###  New Zealand

The Working Holiday Authorisation is open to applicants between the ages of 18
and 30. You will be issued a document that will let you engage in casual or
temporary work. The Authorisation is valid for 12 months after you enter
Ireland. You may not work for a single employer for more than 6 months. [
Applications ](https://www.ireland.ie/en/new-
zealand/wellington/services/visas/) must be submitted to the Embassy of
Ireland in Wellington or through the Embassy of Ireland in London if you are
resident in the UK. There is an application fee of $95 AZD.
