###  Australia

The Working Holiday Authorisation is open to Australian citizens between the
ages of 18 and 35. You can get a document that will let you engage in casual
or temporary work. You may not work for a single employer for more than 6
months. The Authorisation is valid for 12 months after you enter Ireland. You
can find the application form on the [ Embassy of Ireland webpage
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/australia/canberra/) . There is a processing fee
of $95 AUD.
