Sular féidir le cúirt colscaradh a dheonú, ní mór coinníollacha speisialta a

pósadh. Caithfear conníollacha speisialta a chomhlíonadh, áfach, sular féidir
le cúirt díscaoileadh a bhronnadh.

agus toradh an riail seo ar chearta páistí do oidhreacht agus cothú.

  * [ Cearta comharbais tar éis idirscartham colscartha nó scaoilte ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/separation-and-divorce/succession/)

Baineann do chearta comharbais le d'oidhreacht, agus is féidir leis na cearta
seo athrú tar éis idirscartha nó colscartha.

  * [ Orduithe cothabhála agus comhaontuithe ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/separation-and-divorce/orduithe-cothabhala-agus-comhaontuithe/)

Cad is cothabháil ann agus cé a chaithfidh í a íoc? Mura féidir leat teacht ar
chomhaontú, conas iarratas a dhéanamh ar ordú cothabhála.

  * [ How pensions are assessed during a separation, divorce and dissolution ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/separation-and-divorce/sep-divorce-pensions/)

When you end a relationship by judicial separation, divorce or dissolution,
the court can decide to share your pension with your former partner and (or)
any dependent children. This is called a pension adjustment order.
