##  How to apply for Farm Assist

To apply for Farm Assist, [ fill in form Farm 1 (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/37078/b024b52a38a1463cbc12877f89f01a43.pdf) and return
it to your local Intreo Centre or Social Welfare Branch Office.

You can also get this form at your [ local Intreo Centre or Social Welfare
Branch Office ](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-
offices/?referrer=http://www.gov.ie/INTREOoffices/) or Citizens Information
Centre.

If you are not happy with a decision on your application, you can [ appeal
](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-welfare-appeals/) .
You must appeal within 21 days of the decision.
