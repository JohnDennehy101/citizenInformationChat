##  Getting your result

The test is scored automatically and results are available to you immediately.
If you are successful, you will receive a pass certificate for your theory
test at the test centre. You can then [ apply for a learner permit
](/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring-1/driver-licensing/learner-driving-
permits-for-cars-and-work-vehicles#l1702f) .

Regardless of whether you pass or fail, you will also be provided with a score
report listing the categories recommended for further study, based on your
performance.
