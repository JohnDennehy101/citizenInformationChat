##  Introduction

Learning to drive and passing your test is an important personal achievement.
This page aims to provide practical guidance and advice on learning to drive
in Ireland. It includes information on the 4 key steps you must complete to
get a full Irish driving licence.

The steps to learning to drive are:

  1. Pass the driver theory test 
  2. Get a learner permit 
  3. Practice driving 
  4. Pass the driving test 

Driver testing in Ireland is carried out by the [ Road Safety Authority (RSA)
](https://www.rsa.ie/en/) . The [ National Driver Licencing Service (NDLS)
](https://www.ndls.ie/index.php) is the body responsible for issuing learner
permits and driver licences.
