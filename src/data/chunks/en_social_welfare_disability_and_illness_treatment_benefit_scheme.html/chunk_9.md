##  Hearing aids

Hearing aids may be provided by [ suppliers who have a contract with the DSP
](http://www.welfare.ie/en/Pages/Medical-Appliance-Suppliers.aspx) . The DSP
pays the full cost of a hearing aid up to a maximum of €500 (€1,000 for a
pair) once every 4 years. It also pays the full cost of repairs to aids, up to
a maximum of €100, once every 4 years.
