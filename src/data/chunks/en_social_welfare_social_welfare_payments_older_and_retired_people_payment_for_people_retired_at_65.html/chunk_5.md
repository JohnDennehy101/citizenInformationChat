###  Subsidiary employment

You can continue in subsidiary employment. Subsidiary employment is work that
could be done while you were in full-time employment and outside your normal
working hours. Not all work is considered subsidiary employment and there is a
limit on how much you can earn. Find out more about the [ rules for subsidiary
employment ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/65561-benefit-payment-
for-65-year-olds/#subsidiary-employment) .
