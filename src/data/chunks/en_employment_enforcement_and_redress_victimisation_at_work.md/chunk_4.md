##  What to do if you are being victimised

If you have been victimised in work, you can [ make a complaint to the
Workplace Relations Commission ](/en/employment/enforcement-and-
redress/enforcing-your-employment-rights/) .

You should use the [ online complaint form
](https://www.workplacerelations.ie/en/Complaints_Disputes/Refer_a_Dispute_Make_a_Complaint/)
available on workplacerelations.ie. You must make your complaint within 6
months of the last act of victimisation or the date of dismissal. This time
limit can be increased to 12 months but only if there is reasonable cause
which prevented you from making the complaint within the normal time limit.

Read more about [ how to make a complaint ](/en/employment/enforcement-and-
redress/adjudication-employment-rights-disputes-and-complaints/) , including
details of the WRC adjudication process.
