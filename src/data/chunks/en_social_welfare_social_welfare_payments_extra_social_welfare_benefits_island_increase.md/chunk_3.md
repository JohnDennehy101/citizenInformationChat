##  Rules

To qualify for an increase in the rate of your social welfare payment you must
ordinarily live on a [ specified island off the coast of Ireland
](http://www.welfare.ie/en/pages/800_increase-for-living-on-a-specified-
island.aspx) , be 66 years of age or over and getting one of the following
payments:

  * [ State Pension (Non-Contributory) ](/en/social-welfare/older-and-retired-people/state-pension-non-contributory/)
  * [ State Pension (Contributory) ](/en/social-welfare/older-and-retired-people/state-pension-contributory/)
  * [ Blind Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/blind-persons-pension/)
  * [ Invalidity Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/invalidity-pension/)
  * [ Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner's (Contributory) Pension ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-contrib-pension/)
  * [ Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner's (Non-Contributory) Pension ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-non-contrib-pension/)
  * [ Carer’s Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/carers/carers-allowance/)
  * [ One-Parent Family Payment ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/one-parent-family-payment/)
  * Widow’s/Widower’s Benefit under the [ Occupational Injuries Benefit Scheme ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/occupational-injuries-benefit-overview/)

If you are 66 years of age or over and getting a comparable pension to those
listed above from another EU State, you are also entitled to the increase.

If you live on a specified island and are under 66 years of age you will
qualify for an increase in your social welfare payment if you are getting one
of the following social welfare payments:

  * [ Invalidity Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/invalidity-pension/)
  * [ Disability Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/disability-allowance/)
  * Incapacity Supplement under the Occupational Injuries Benefit Scheme 
  * Blind Pension 

If you are under 66 years of age and getting a comparable pension to those
listed above from another EU State, you are also entitled to the increase.
