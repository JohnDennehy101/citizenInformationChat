###  Seirbhísí do dhaoine fásta

Is féidir leat bunchúram altranais, comhairle agus cúnamh a fháil ó altra
sláinte poiblí. Is féidir leo tú a atreorú chuig [ seirbhísí cúraim pobail
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-
community/community-care-services/#l7b1e0) eile ar nós [ fisiteiripe
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-
community/physiotherapy-services/) , [ cosliacht
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-
community/chiropody-services/) agus [ cúram faoisimh
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/health-
services-for-people-with-disabilities/respite-care/) .

I gceantair áirithe, coimeádann altraí sláinte poiblí clár de dhaoine
scothaosta agus tugann siad cuairt orthu mar chuid dá gcuid oibre. Uaireanta,
cuirtear é seo ar fáil freisin do dhaoine faoi mhíchumas.
