###  **Apply for a State Pension (Contributory)**

You cannot apply for the State Pension (Contributory) online.

You must complete a [ State Pension application form (SPC1) (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/26952/c008566575c14e958b0597dfd5b3be91.pdf) .

You can also get an application form from:

  * Your local post office 
  * [ Your Intreo Centre or Social Welfare Branch Office ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/e6f908-state-pension-contributory/)
  * [ Gov.ie ](https://www.gov.ie/en/form/9b3ec-state-pension-contributory-application-form-spc1/) (download and print)
