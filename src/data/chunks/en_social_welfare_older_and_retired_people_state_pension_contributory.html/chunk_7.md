###  **1\. You have paid PRSI contributions before a certain age**

To get a State Pension (Contributory) at 66, you must have started paying PRSI
before the age of 56. If you are [ deferring your pension ](/en/social-
welfare/older-and-retired-people/deferring-your-state-pension-contributory/) ,
you must have started paying PRSI at least 10 years before you drawdown your
pension.
