###  Is it legal to cycle two abreast in Ireland?

Cyclists can cycle two abreast but under [ Article 47 of the Road Traffic
(Traffic and Parking) 1997 Regulations
](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/1997/si/182/made/en/print#article47) (as
substituted by the 2012 Regulations), you must not cycle more than two
abreast, except when overtaking and it does not endanger or obstruct other
traffic.
