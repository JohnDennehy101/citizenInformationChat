##  How to apply

You can claim the Home Carer Tax Credit by using Revenue's [ myAccount
](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-web/home.html) . You can also apply by
completing a [ Home Carer Tax Credit claim form (pdf)
](http://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-credits-reliefs-and-
exemptions/documents/home-carers-credit-claim-form.pdf) and returning the form
to your [ Revenue office ](https://revenue.ie/en/contact-us/index.aspx) .

If you pay tax under [ the self-assessed system
](https://revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-credits-reliefs-and-exemptions/health-
and-age/home-carer-credit/claiming-home-carer-tax-credit.aspx) , you can claim
the credit by completing the Home Carer section on your [ annual tax return
](https://revenue.ie/en/self-assessment-and-self-employment/filing-your-tax-
return/index.aspx) .
