###  **Example: If you are self-employed and getting a social welfare
pension**

If your other source of income is not taxed through the PAYE system, (for
example, if you are self-employed, have an [ occupational pension
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/personal-
finance/pensions/occupational-pensions/) from abroad, or have investment
income), then you are classed as a self-employed person and your tax is
payable annually by 31 October each year.
