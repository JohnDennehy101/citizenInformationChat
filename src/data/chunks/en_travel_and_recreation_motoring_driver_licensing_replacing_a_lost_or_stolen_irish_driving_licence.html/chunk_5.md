##  Apply in person

You must [ book an appointment ](https://www.ndls.ie/booking-service.html) .
There is no walk-in service available.

You must bring the following documentation:

  * A completed [ Garda Declaration Form ](https://www.ndls.ie/images/Documents/169620_RSA_NDLS_Garda_Declaration_Form_Web_HR.pdf)
  * Documentation to [ prove your identity ](https://www.ndls.ie/how-to-apply/applying-in-person.html#photo-id)
  * Proof of your [ personal public services number ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/personal-public-service-number/) (PPSN) 
  * [ Proof of address ](https://www.ndls.ie/how-to-apply/applying-in-person.html#evidence-of-address) dated within the last 6 months if your address is different to the one you provided when you got your Public Services Card or if your current address is not recorded on your licence 
  * A [ medical report form ](https://www.ndls.ie/images/Documents/Forms/171315_NDLS_Medical_Form_JAN_2022_WEB_HR.pdf) dated within one month, if there has been a change in your medical details since you made your last application for your driving licence.
