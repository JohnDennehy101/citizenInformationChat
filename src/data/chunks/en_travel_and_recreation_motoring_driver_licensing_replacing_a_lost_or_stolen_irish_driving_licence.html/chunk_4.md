##  Apply online

You can apply online which is the quickest way.

You will need the following to apply online:

  * A [ Public Services Card ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/public-services-card/) and verified [ MyGovID ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/government-in-ireland/how-government-works/egovernment/mygovid/)
  * A completed [ Garda Declaration Form ](https://www.ndls.ie/images/Documents/169620_RSA_NDLS_Garda_Declaration_Form_Web_HR.pdf)
  * [ Proof of your address ](https://www.ndls.ie/how-to-apply/applying-in-person.html#evidence-of-address) dated within the last 6 months if your address is different to the one you provided when you got your Public Services Card or if your current address is not recorded on your licence 
  * A [ medical report form ](https://www.ndls.ie/images/Documents/Forms/171315_NDLS_Medical_Form_JAN_2022_WEB_HR.pdf) dated within one month, if there has been a change in your medical details since you made your last application for your driving licence.
