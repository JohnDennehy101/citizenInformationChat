###  **What if I find a job?**

The JobPath activation programme can last for a year (52 weeks).

If you find a job, the JobPath company will continue working with you while
you are in employment for up to a year. It will give you particular support
during the first few weeks of employment.

Read more about [ JobPath ](https://www.gov.ie/en/policy-
information/9e575c-jobpath/) on gov.ie.
