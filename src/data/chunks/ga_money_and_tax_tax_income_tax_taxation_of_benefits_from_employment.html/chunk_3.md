##  Conas a ghearrtar cáin ar shochair

Suimítear formhór na sochar le do phá agus gearrtar cáin orthu ar an mbealach
céanna. Asbhaineann d’fhostóir cáin ioncaim, Muirear Sóisialta Uilíoch (MSU)
agus Árachas Sóisialach Pá-Choibhneasa (ÁSPC) ar luach an tsochair.

Is éagsúil do na rialacha a bhaineann le sochair chomhchineáil. Go ginearálta,
is é luach an tsochair an luach is airde den mhéid seo a leanas:

  * An costas ar an bhfostóir 
  * Luach margaidh an tsochair 

Má ranníocann an fostaí suim i dtreo an tsochair, laghdaítear luach inchánach
an tsochair faoin suim sin.

Baineann rialacha speisialta leis na sochair chomhchineáil seo a leanas:

  * [ Gluaisteán a úsáid ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/employing-people/benefit-in-kind-for-employers/private-use-company-cars/index.aspx)
  * [ Veain a úsáid ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/employing-people/benefit-in-kind-for-employers/private-use-of-company-vans/index.aspx)
  * [ Sócmhainní eile a úsáid ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/employing-people/benefit-in-kind-for-employers/free-use-of-assets/index.aspx)
  * [ Iasachtaí fabhracha ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/employing-people/benefit-in-kind-for-employers/other-benefits/preferential-loans.aspx)
  * [ Cóiríocht ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/employing-people/benefit-in-kind-for-employers/free-or-subsidised-accommodation/index.aspx)
