###  Picking a course

The Central Applications Office (CAO), which is based in Galway, operates a
centralised applications procedure for all third-level colleges. The CAO
website has a [ listing of all of the third-level courses
](http://www.cao.ie/courses.php) on offer through their system. You can also
check the [ Qualifax website for lists of higher education courses.
](http://www.qualifax.ie/?Mainsec=courses&Subsec=search_courses) If you have
identified some courses that are of particular interest, you can obtain more
detailed information about them from the relevant colleges.
