###  Maternity and Adoptive Benefit

The reckonable weekly earnings threshold increases from €332 to €350, for
determination of entitlement to earnings-related Maternity and Adoptive
Benefit. (January 2007)  
  
There is an increase of €25.20 in minimum rate to €207.80 per week  
(January 2007)  
  
There will be an extra 4 weeks Maternity Benefit which increases to 26 weeks.
(March 2007)
