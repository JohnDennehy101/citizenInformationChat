###  Social and affordable housing

Capital funding of €2.3 billion has been allocated for social and affordable
housing in 2023. The funding is expected to deliver 9,100 new build social
homes and over 5,550 affordable and cost rental homes.

Funding for the **Housing Assistance Payment (HAP)** scheme will be €544
million in 2023. This will provide 8,800 new HAP tenancies and continue to
support 58,400 existing HAP tenancies.

Funding of €113 million for the **Rental Accommodation Scheme (RAS)** will
support 800 new RAS tenancies in 2023, and continue to support 16,500
households already in the scheme.

Funding of €20 million is allocated in 2023 for the delivery of **Traveller-
specific accommodation** .
