###  Carers

Carers who qualify for the [ Carer’s Support Grant ](/ga/social-
welfare/carers/carers-support-grant/) will get a once-off payment of €500
(November 2022).

[ Domiciliary Care Allowance (DCA) ](/ga/social-welfare/disability-and-
illness/domiciliary-care-allowance/) will increase by €20.50 from €309.50 to
€330 (January 2023). DCA will be available for babies who remain in an acute
hospital after birth for a period of 6 months (January 2023).
