###  Women's health

Women’s health funding of €32.2 million includes:

  * Extending the age range for free [ contraception ](/ga/health/health-services/reproductive_health/contraception/) to include women aged 16 to 30 (from 1 September 2023) 
  * Funding for [ IVF treatment ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/before-your-baby-is-born/fertility-treatments-and-dahr/) in the public health system (from September 2023) 
  * Provide repayments for morning sickness medicines 
  * Funding to set up a Breast Implant Registry 
  * Additional Women’s Health Hubs (July 2023)
