###  Fuel Allowance

People getting [ Fuel Allowance ](/ga/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-
benefits/fuel-allowance/) will get a once-off payment of €400 (November 2022).

The Fuel Allowance income threshold will increase from €120 to €200 above the
applicable State Pension (Contributory) rate for people aged under 70 (January
2023).

People aged 70 and over can qualify for Fuel Allowance as long as their weekly
means (which includes their State Pension) is not above €500 per week for a
single person and €1,000 per week for a couple (January 2023).

[ Disablement Benefit ](/ga/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/disablement-
benefit/) and half-rate [ Carer’s Allowance ](/ga/social-
welfare/carers/carers-allowance/) will be disregarded in the means test for
Fuel Allowance (January 2023).
