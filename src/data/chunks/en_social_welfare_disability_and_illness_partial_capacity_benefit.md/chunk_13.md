##  How to apply for Partial Capacity Benefit

To apply, fill in a [ Partial Capacity Benefit application form (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/225838/81532a8e-8f06-4ac9-a707-00acb54c3522.pdf) . The
Partial Capacity Benefit Section can post you an application form - see 'Where
to apply' below for their contact details.

A Medical Assessor will assess the restriction on your capacity for work. This
may require you to attend a medical assessment. You should include all
appropriate medical evidence with your application. If you qualify for Partial
Capacity Benefit, you are not required to send in medical certificates.
