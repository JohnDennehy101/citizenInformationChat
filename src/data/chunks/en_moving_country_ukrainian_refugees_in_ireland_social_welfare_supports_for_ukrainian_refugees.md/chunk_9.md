##  Applying for a social welfare payment

If you need help applying for a social welfare payment, you can visit your [
local Intreo centre or DSP branch office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-
offices/?referrer=http://www.gov.ie/en/service/40cf48-find-your-local-intreo-
office/) .

Your local [ Citizens Information Centre
](https://centres.citizensinformation.ie/) can also give you help and advice
on social welfare payments.

When you apply for a social welfare payment, you should provide information
about any [ children ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/claiming-and-increase-in-your-
payment-for-a-child-dependant/) or [ adult dependents ](/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-
payment/claiming-an-increase-in-your-social-welfare-payment-for-an-adult-
dependant/) you may have. You could get an increase in your payment for them.
