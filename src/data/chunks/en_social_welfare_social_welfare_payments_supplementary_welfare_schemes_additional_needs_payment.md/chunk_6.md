##  How to apply for an Additional Needs Payment

You can make a paper application or apply online.

When you apply for an Additional Needs Payment, your application will be
assessed by a Community Welfare Officer (CWO).

**Online application**

You can apply online through [ MyWelfare.ie ](https://services.mywelfare.ie/)
. You will need a [ verified MyGovID account ](/en/government-in-ireland/how-
government-works/egovernment/mygovid/) .

**Paper Application**

You can post your application for an Additional Needs Payment, or make an
appointment with your local CWO, if you prefer.

If you post your application, the CWO may issue a payment or arrange an
appointment to meet with you.
