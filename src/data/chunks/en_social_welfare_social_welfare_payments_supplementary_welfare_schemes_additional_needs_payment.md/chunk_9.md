##  More information

You can [ read more about the Additional Needs Payment
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/4eb45-additional-needs-
payment/?referrer=https://www.gov.ie/en/service/80b24c-exceptional-needs-
payments-swa/) on gov.ie.

You can also get information about the payment at your [ local Community
Welfare Service ](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/cb07d7-supplementary-
welfare-allowance-swa-list-of-offices-administering-swa/) , or you can call
the National Phone Line for Community Welfare Services on 0818 607080.
