##  Assessing the means of a couple

If you apply for a [ social assistance or means-tested
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/social-assistance-payments/social-assistance-in-ireland/) payment and
you are married, in a civil partnership or [ cohabiting
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/cohabiting-
couples/rights-of-cohabiting-couples/#e6e823) , the DSP takes the means of
your spouse, civil partner or cohabitant into account in the means test.

Read more in our page, [ Assessing the means of a couple for social assistance
payments ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-
welfare-system/means-test-for-social-welfare-payments/assessing-the-means-of-
a-couple/) .
