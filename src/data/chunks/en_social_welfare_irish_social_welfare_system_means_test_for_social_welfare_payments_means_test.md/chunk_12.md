####  **Income tax and USC**

Usually, income tax or [ the Universal Social Charge
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-
tax/universal-social-charge/) (USC) are not deducted from your income from
employment. Exceptions include assessments for:

  * [ Working Family Payment ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/families-and-children/working-family-payment/)
  * [ Benefit and privilege for Jobseeker's Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/means-test-for-social-welfare-payments/how-parents-income-can-affect-jobseekers-allowance/)
  * [ Benefit and privilege for Supplementary Welfare Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/means-test-for-social-welfare-payments/how-parents-income-can-affect-supplementary-welfare-allowance/) .
