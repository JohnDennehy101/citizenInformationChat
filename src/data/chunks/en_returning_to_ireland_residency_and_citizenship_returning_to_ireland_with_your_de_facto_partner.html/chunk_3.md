##  What is a de facto partner?

A [ de facto partner ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/my-situation-has-
changed-since-i-arrived-in-ireland/de-facto-partner-of-an-irish-or-non-eea-
national/#what-is-de-facto) is a partner, including a same-sex partner, who
you are not married to or in a civil partnership with. Your relationship must
meet all of the following criteria:

  * You and your partner are in a mutual and committed relationship to the exclusion of all others like a marriage or civil partnership in practice, but not in law 
  * You have been living together for at least 2 years 
  * Your relationship is genuine and continuing 
  * You are not related to each other by family
