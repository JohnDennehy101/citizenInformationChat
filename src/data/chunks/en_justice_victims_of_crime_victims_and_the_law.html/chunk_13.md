###  Criminal Law (Human Trafficking) (Amendment) Act 2013

This [ amendment to the Criminal Law (Human Trafficking) Act 2008
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2013/act/24/enacted/en/index.html) makes
it an offence to traffic in adults or children for the purpose of forcing them
to engage in criminality, labour, enslavement and begging.
