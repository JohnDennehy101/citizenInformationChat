###  Criminal Law (Human Trafficking) Act 2008

The [ Criminal Law (Human Trafficking) Act 2008
](https://https/www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2008/act/8/www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2008/act/8)
makes it an offence to [ traffic in adults or children ](/en/justice/crime-
and-crime-prevention/human-trafficking/) for the purpose of their sexual or
labour exploitation, or for the removal of their organs.

It also makes it an offence to sell or purchase, or offer to sell or purchase,
any person for any purpose. It is also an offence to solicit a trafficked
person for prostitution.
