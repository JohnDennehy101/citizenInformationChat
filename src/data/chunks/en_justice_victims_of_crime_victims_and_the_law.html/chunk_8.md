###  Civil Legal Aid Act 1995

Under [ Civil Legal Aid Act 1995
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/1995/act/32/enacted/en/html) , the [
Legal Aid Board ](/en/justice/legal-aid-and-advice/legal-aid-board/) provides
legal aid or advice to a _complainant_ in [ certain criminal cases
](https://www.legalaidboard.ie/en/our-services/legal-aid-services/common-
legal-problems/rape-and-sexual-assault/) involving prosecution for a range of
sexual offences, including rape, aggravated sexual assault and incest. A
complainant is the person making the complaint.

The Board also provides legal advice in relation to criminal matters to
alleged victims of [ human trafficking ](https://www.legalaidboard.ie/en/our-
services/legal-aid-services/common-legal-problems/human-trafficking/) .
