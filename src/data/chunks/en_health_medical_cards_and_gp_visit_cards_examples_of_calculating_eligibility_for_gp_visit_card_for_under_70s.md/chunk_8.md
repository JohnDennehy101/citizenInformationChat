##  More information

You can read more information, including how to apply for a [ GP visit card
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-
cards/gp-visit-cards/) or the [ GP visit card for children aged under 8
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-
cards/gp-visit-cards-for-under-8s/) . Check if you are eligible for a [
medical card ](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/medical-card/) .

If you are aged **under 70** find out about the [ means test for a medical
card or GP visit card ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-
cards-and-gp-visit-cards/medical-card-means-test-under-70s/) for under 70s.

You can find more information on the [ medical card
](https://www2.hse.ie/services/schemes-allowances/medical-cards/) and [ GP
visit card ](https://www2.hse.ie/services/schemes-allowances/gp-visit-cards/)
on the HSE website. You can find further detail in the [ HSE Assessment
Guidelines for medical cards and GP visit cards
](https://www.hse.ie/eng/staff/pcrs/medical-card-and-gp-visit-card-assessment-
guidelines.pdf) .

The HSE has information on [ financial assessment.
](https://www2.hse.ie/services/medical-cards/medical-card-application-
process/the-medical-card-assessment.html)
