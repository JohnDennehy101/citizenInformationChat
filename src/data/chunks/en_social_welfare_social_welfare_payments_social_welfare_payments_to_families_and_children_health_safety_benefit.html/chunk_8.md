##  Rates

**Weekly** **Health and Safety Benefit rate 2024**

**Earnings per week** |  **Personal rate** |  **Qualified adult rate**  
---|---|---  
Less than €150  |  €104.10  |  €99.70   
€150 - €219.99  |  €149.60  |  €99.70   
€220 - €299.99  |  €181.70  |  €99.70   
€300 or more  |  €232  |  €154   
  
Half-rate Health and Safety Benefit can be paid if you are getting [ certain
social welfare payments ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/claiming-two-social-welfare-
payments/) .
