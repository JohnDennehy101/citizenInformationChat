##  How to apply

To get this benefit you must fill in a [ Health and Safety Benefit application
form (pdf) ](https://assets.gov.ie/21783/a8cbe2cf88f544568b78dcb9a1c85bb1.pdf)
and send it to the Health and Safety Benefit Section (see 'Where to apply'
below).

You must complete parts 1, 2, 3, 5, 6 and 7.

Your employer must fill in, sign and stamp part 4.

Your GP must fill in, sign and stamp part 8.
