####  **Parenting alone**

If you are parenting alone, you may be entitled to WFP in addition to your [
One-Parent Family Payment ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/one-
parent-family-payment/) , [ Deserted Wife's Benefit ](/en/social-
welfare/families-and-children/deserted-wife-s-payments/) or [ Widow’s,
Widower’s or Surviving Civil Partner’s (Contributory) Pension ](/en/social-
welfare/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-contrib-pension/) .
