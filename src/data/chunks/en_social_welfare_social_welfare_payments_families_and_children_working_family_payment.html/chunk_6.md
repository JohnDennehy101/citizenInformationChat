###  Changes to your personal situation

Your claim can be reassessed and you may get an increase in your weekly rate
of WFP in the following 2 situations:

  * If you start to care for an additional child 
  * If you were getting a [ One-Parent Family Payment (OFP) ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/one-parent-family-payment/) and your payment has stopped because your youngest child has reached the OFP age limit
