####  **New Pay-Related Benefit scheme**

The [ Social Welfare (Miscellaneous Provisions) Act 2024
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2024/act/24/enacted/en/print) provides
for the introduction of [ a new Pay-Related Benefit system
](https://www.gov.ie/en/press-release/1806c-landmark-legislation-to-introduce-
pay-related-benefit-in-ireland-passed-by-the-oireachtas/) . It will be
available for people who become fully unemployed after the commencement of the
scheme.
