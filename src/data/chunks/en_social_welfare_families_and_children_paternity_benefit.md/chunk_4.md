##  How to qualify for Paternity Benefit

To get Paternity Benefit, you must have:

  * Enough PRSI (social insurance) contributions - see below 
  * Certified paternity leave – see ‘Leave certification from work’ below 

You should apply for Paternity Benefit at least 4 weeks before the date you
intend to start your paternity leave. If you are self-employed you should
apply 12 weeks before. You must register for a [ Public Services Card
](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/public-services-card/) (if
you don't already have one) and register with [ MyWelfare.ie
](https://www.mywelfare.ie/Account/Login) (see 'How to apply' below for
details of how to do this).
