###  PRSI contribution week

Your working week may not be the same as the PRSI contribution week. The PRSI
contribution week starts on 1 January each year. Week one is the period from 1
January to 7 January, week two is from 8 January to 14 January and so on. For
2024, it started on Monday and ended on Sunday. [ The contribution week can
affect the social insurance cover of part-time workers and people job-sharing
](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-insurance-prsi/part-
time-work-and-social-insurance-prsi/) .

**COVID-19 payments**

If you got COVID-19 payments – either the [ COVID-19 Pandemic Unemployment
Payment ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/covid19-pandemic-unemployment-
payment/) or Revenue’s wage subsidy schemes (including the [ Temporary Wage
Subsidy Scheme ](/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/covid19-temporary-
wage-subsidy-scheme/) , the Employment Wage Subsidy Scheme and the Employer
COVID-19 Refund Scheme) – you will be awarded PRSI contributions at the class
you were paying immediately before you were laid off, while you got the
COVID-19 payment or scheme.
