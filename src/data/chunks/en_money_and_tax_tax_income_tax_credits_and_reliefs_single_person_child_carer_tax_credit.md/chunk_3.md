##  Rules

You cannot claim the SPCCC if you are:

  * Jointly assessed for tax as a married person or civil partner or 
  * Married or in a civil partnership (unless separated) or 
  * Cohabiting 

If you are cohabiting, you cannot claim the SPCCC for the year in which you
stop living together.

You cannot claim the SPCCC for the year in which you become widowed or a
surviving civil partner (the year of bereavement).

You can claim the SPCCC in subsequent years if you qualify.
