##  What age can I get a State Pension (Contributory)?

You must be at least 66 to qualify for a State pension.

You can choose to [ defer claiming your State Pension (Contributory) up to age
70 ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/older-and-retired-people/deferring-your-state-pension-contributory/)
.
