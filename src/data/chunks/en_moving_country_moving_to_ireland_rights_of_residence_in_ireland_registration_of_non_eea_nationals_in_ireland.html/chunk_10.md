##  How to renew your Irish Residence Permit

Before your Irish Residence Permit (IRP) expires you should go to [ your
immigration registration office ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/registering-
your-immigration-permission/how-to-renew-your-current-permission/) to renew
it. You will need to bring your passport, your current card and evidence to
support your residence permission, such as an employment permit.
