###  People aged over 65

You can get your deposit interest paid without the deduction of DIRT, or you
can claim a DIRT refund, if you are over 65 and:

  * Your income is less than the [ low income exemption limits for people aged over 65 ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/older-peoples-tax-credits-and-reliefs/) **or**
  * Your tax credits for the year (including your spouse's) amount to more than the tax that would be due on your (and your spouse's) income for the year 

Joint accounts can qualify if the account holders are spouses or civil
partners.
