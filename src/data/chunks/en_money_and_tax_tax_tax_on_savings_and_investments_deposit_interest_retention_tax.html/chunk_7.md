###  People with disabilities

You can get your deposit interest paid without the deduction of DIRT, or get a
refund, if:

  * You are permanently incapacitated from maintaining yourself **and**
  * Your tax credits for the year (including your spouse's) amount to more than the tax that would be due on your (and your spouse's) income for the year 

[ You can get more information on the DIRT exemption for permanently
incapacitated individuals from Revenue ](http://www.revenue.ie/en/additional-
incomes/dirt/who-is-exempt-from-dirt.aspx) .
