##  More information

Affordable Homes has developed an [ FAQ about the scheme
](https://affordablehomes.ie/rent/faq/) . They also have a [ step-by-step
guide on how to buy an affordable home
](https://affordablehomes.ie/buy/stepbystep/) .

The qualifying criteria for this scheme is set-out in the [ Affordable Housing
Act 2021
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2021/act/25/enacted/en/print#sec10) and
the [ Affordable Housing Regulations 2023
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2023/si/20/made/en/print) . Each local
authority will use these regulations and their own [ scheme of priority
](https://affordablehomes.ie/buy/faq/#question-8) when assessing if you
qualify for the scheme.
