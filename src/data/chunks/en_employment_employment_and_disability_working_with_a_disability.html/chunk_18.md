##  More information

For more resources and support:

  * [ **Work and Access** ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/47db8-work-and-access-supports/?referrer=https://www.gov.ie/workandaccess/) **:** an Employment Programme offering support to help people with a disability get a job or stay in work. 
  * [ **AHEAD** ](http://www.ahead.ie/graduate) : information for employees and employers, including reasonable accommodations and work placements for graduates with disabilities. 
  * [ **Employers for Change** ](https://www.employersforchange.ie/Contact-Us) : guidance on disability, inclusive recruitment, and workplace management. 
  * [ **Health and Safety Authority** ](https://www.hsa.ie/eng/publications_and_forms/publications/safety_and_health_management/employees_with__disabilities.html) : guide to inclusive health and safety practices for employees with disabilities. 
  * **Eastern Vocational Enterprises (E.V.E.)** : [ 'Just Ask - a Handbook for Employers and Employees' (pdf) ](https://www.eve.ie/download/518/Just%20Ask_English%20Booklet_2009.pdf) for mental health support. 
  * [ **Irish Human Rights and Equality Commission** ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/justice/law-and-rights/irish-human-rights-and-equality-commission/) : information on equality and discrimination issues in the workplace.
