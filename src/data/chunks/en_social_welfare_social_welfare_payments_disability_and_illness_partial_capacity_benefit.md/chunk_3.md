###  **Voluntary work**

If you are getting [ Illness Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-
illness/illness-benefit/) and [ want to do volunteer work ](/en/social-
welfare/voluntary-work-and-social-welfare-payments/) , you must apply directly
to the Partial Capacity Benefit section for permission (see ‘Where to apply
for Partial Capacity Benefit’ below).

If you are getting the Invalidity Pension and want to do volunteer work, you
must apply directly to the [ Invalidity Pension section
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/disability-and-illness/invalidity-pension/#lef21f) for permission.
