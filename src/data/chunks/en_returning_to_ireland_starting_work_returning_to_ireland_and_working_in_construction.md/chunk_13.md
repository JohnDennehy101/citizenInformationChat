###  **Your wages**

There are rules (called a [ ‘Sectoral Employment Order’ (SEO)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-
conditions/industrial-relations-and-trade-unions/employment-agreements-and-
orders/#578fa2) ) that set minimum pay rates for certain workersin the
construction sector, including apprentices.

The SEO for the construction sector also sets out other conditions like sick
pay and pension entitlements. In addition, it defines the experience and
qualifications that place you in a particular employment category.

You can [ read more about the SEO for the construction sector
](https://www.workplacerelations.ie/en/what_you_should_know/hours-and-
wages/sectoral%20employment%20orders/construction-sector/) on the Workplace
Relations Commission (WRC) website.
