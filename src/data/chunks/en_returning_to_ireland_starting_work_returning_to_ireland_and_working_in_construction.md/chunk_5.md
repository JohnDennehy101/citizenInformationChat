##  Safepass

You will need to complete safety awareness training and get a [ Safe Pass
registration card
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-
conditions/health-and-safety/safe-pass/) before you can work on construction
sites. This includes apprentices, trainees, and on-site security workers.

SOLAS is the organisation in Ireland that manages Safe Pass. They have a
database you can use to [ search for an Approved Training Organisation (ATO)
](https://www.constructioncourses.ie/Home/PublishedCourses) that provides the
Safe Pass training near you.
