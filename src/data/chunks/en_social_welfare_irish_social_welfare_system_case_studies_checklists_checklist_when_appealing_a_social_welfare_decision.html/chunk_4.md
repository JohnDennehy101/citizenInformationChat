####  **Is the payment or scheme you wish to appeal covered by the Social
Welfare Appeals Office?**

You can find a list of payments [ covered by the Social Welfare Appeals Office
(pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/42571/5e0eb903e82c432482943283fd667a16.pdf#page=1) .

If the payment or scheme you wish to appeal is [ not covered by the Social
Welfare Appeals Office ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-welfare-appeals/#8c4f18) , you can
ask for a review of the decision by the section of the Department of Social
Protection that dealt with your application, but you cannot bring it to a
formal appeal.
