##  What is a Personal Public Service (PPS) number?

Your Personal Public Service (PPS) number is a unique reference number that
helps you access social welfare benefits, public services and information in
Ireland.

A PPS Number is always 7 numbers followed by either one or 2 letters. It is
sometimes called a PPSN.

You have a PPS number if:

  * You were born in Ireland in or after 1971 
  * You started work in Ireland after 1979 
  * You are getting a social welfare payment 
  * You are taking part in the [ Drugs Payment Scheme ](/en/health/drugs-and-medicines/drugs-payment-scheme/)
