##  Taxis

Licenced and regulated by the National Transport Authority, [ taxis, hackneys
and limousines ](/en/travel-and-recreation/public-transport/regulation-of-
taxis-and-small-public-service-vehicles/) are available across Ireland.

You can check if your driver is licenced using the [ TFI Driver Check App
](https://www.transportforireland.ie/getting-around/by-taxi/driver-check-app/)
.

Use the [ Taxi Fare Estimator ](https://www.transportforireland.ie/fares/taxi-
fare-estimator/) to estimate your taxi fare. The fares calculated are
approximate and depend on traffic.
