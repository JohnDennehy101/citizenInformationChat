####  **Credits and benefit payment for 65 year olds**

If you are getting the [ benefit payment for people aged 65
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/older-and-retired-
people/payment-for-people-retired-at-65/) , you will get credits if you are
entitled to credits when the claim starts.

If you transfer from [ Jobseeker’s Benefit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/unemployed-
people/jobseekers-benefit/) to the benefit payment for people aged 65, you
should qualify for credits.

However, if you transfer from [ Jobseeker’s Benefit (Self-Employed) (JBSE)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/unemployed-
people/jobseekers-benefit-self-employed/) to the benefit payment for people
aged 65, you won’t get credits unless you met the conditions for credits on
your JBSE claim.
