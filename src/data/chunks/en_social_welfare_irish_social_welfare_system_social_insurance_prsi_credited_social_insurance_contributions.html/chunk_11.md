####  Disability allowance

You can get credits while on [ Disability Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/disability-and-illness/disability-allowance/) , if you have paid or
credited contributions in the last 2 years. If you are getting Disability
Allowance because you have used up your entitlement to Illness Benefit, you
will get credits.
