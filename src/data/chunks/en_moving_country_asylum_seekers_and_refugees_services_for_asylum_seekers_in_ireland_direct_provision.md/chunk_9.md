####  Other social welfare payments

You cannot get other social welfare payments such as [ Disability Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/disability-and-
illness/disability-allowance/) , [ One-Parent Family Payment
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-
children/one-parent-family-payment/) and [ Jobseekerâ€™s Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/unemployed-
people/jobseekers-allowance/) . However, if you have permission to work and
are in employment, you may qualify for [ Working Family Payment.
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/08bb21-working-family-payment/)

You can apply for an [ Additional Needs Payment
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-
schemes/additional-needs-payment/) if you have essential needs that you cannot
pay for from your weekly payment. For example, you might get some help to buy
school uniforms for your children.
