##  Are quad bikes legal on public roads in Ireland?

Yes. A quad bike is treated the same as any other vehicle on the road. You
must have tax, insurance, a driving licence, and your [ quad should be
registered with Revenue ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/vrt/vehicle-registration-
tax/index.aspx) . You must display number plates. Contact Revenue for
information on the documents you need.

You must be 16 to drive a light quad of 350kg or less with a maximum design
speed of not more than 45 km/h. To drive a heavier quad, you must be at least
17 years old.
