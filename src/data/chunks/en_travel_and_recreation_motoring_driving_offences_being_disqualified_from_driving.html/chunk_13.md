##  How to apply for the restoration of your licence

If you have been disqualified from driving, [ you can apply for your driving
licence to be restored ](https://courts.ie/restoring-driving-licence) .

You make the application [ to the District Court
](https://courts.ie/content/find-us) where the order to disqualify you was
made. An application costs â‚¬55. You must be more than halfway through your
disqualification period before you can make an application.

Your driving licence will not be restored if:

  * The disqualification is for 2 years or less 
  * You have been given another disqualification in the past 10 years 

The court can reduce the period of disqualification to two-thirds of the
original period of disqualification or to 2 years, whichever is the greater.

When a court is considering an application for the restoration of a driving
licence it will look at the nature of the offence, the character of the
applicant and the conduct of the applicant after conviction.

You can get further information and the forms you need from the District Court
clerk.
