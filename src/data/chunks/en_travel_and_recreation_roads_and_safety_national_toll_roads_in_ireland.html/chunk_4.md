##  Exemptions from toll charges

Certain vehicles are exempt from toll charges, including vehicles belonging to
and used for official purposes by the [ Defence Forces ](/en/government-in-
ireland/security-and-emergency-services/defence-forces/) , vehicles used by [
An Garda Síochána ](/en/justice/law-enforcement/garda-siochana-national-
police-force/) , [ fire brigade ](/en/government-in-ireland/security-and-
emergency-services/fire-services/) vehicles and [ ambulances.
](/en/health/health-system/emergency-health-services-in-ireland/)
