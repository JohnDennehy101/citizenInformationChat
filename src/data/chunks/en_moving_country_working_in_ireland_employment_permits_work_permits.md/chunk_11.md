##  How to apply

Either you or the employer can apply for a General Employment Permit to the [
Employment Permits Section ](https://dbei.gov.ie/en/What-We-Do/Workplace-and-
Skills/Employment-Permits/Contact/) of the Department of Enterprise, Trade and
Employment.

You apply online with the required documentation, using the [ Employment
Permits Online System (EPOS) ](https://epos.enterprise.gov.ie/#/app/welcome) .
You can use the [ General Employment Permit checklist
](https://enterprise.gov.ie/en/Publications/Publication-files/General-
Employment-Permits-Checklist.pdf) to help you apply.
