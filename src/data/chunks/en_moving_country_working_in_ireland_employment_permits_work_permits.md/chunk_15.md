##  Applying for a Stamp 4 after 57 months

General Employment Permits are issued for a maximum period of 2 years. If you
renew your permit, you can get a permit for a maximum period of 3 years.

You renew the permit using the [ Employment Permit Online System (EPOS)
](https://epos.enterprise.gov.ie/#/app/welcome) . You should apply within 16
weeks before your current permit is due to expire.

When you have [ completed 57 months on a General Employment Permit
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/update-to-eligibility-requirements-for-
stamp-4-upgrades/) you can apply to ISD for a Stamp 4 permission. Stamp 4
holders can work without an employment permit. After 5 years residence, you
may also apply for [ citizenship by naturalisation. ](/en/moving-
country/irish-citizenship/becoming-an-irish-citizen-through-naturalisation/)
