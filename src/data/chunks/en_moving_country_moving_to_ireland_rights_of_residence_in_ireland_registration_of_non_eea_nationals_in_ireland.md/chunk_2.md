##  Introduction

If you are not a citizen of the [ European Economic Area (EEA)
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/eu-eea-country-or-switzerland/) , the [
United Kingdom ](/en/government-in-ireland/ireland-and-the-uk/residence-rules-
UK-citizens/) or Switzerland, you need [ permission to enter ](/en/moving-
country/visas-for-ireland/permission-to-land-in-ireland/) Ireland. If you want
to stay for more than 3 months you must have [ permission to remain
](/en/moving-country/moving-to-ireland/rights-of-residence-in-
ireland/residence-rights-of-non-eea-nationals-in-ireland/) and register your
presence in Ireland with Immigration Service Delivery (ISD). This is how you
get an Irish Residence Permit (IRP).
