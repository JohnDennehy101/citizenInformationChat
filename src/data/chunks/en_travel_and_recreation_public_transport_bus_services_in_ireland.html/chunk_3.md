###  **Laws**

Public bus services are [ regulated by the National Transport Authority
](https://www.nationaltransport.ie/bus-licensing/) under the [ Public
Transport Regulation Act 2009
](http://revisedacts.lawreform.ie/eli/2009/act/37/revised/en/html) .

Operators need [ licensing ](https://www.nationaltransport.ie/bus-
licensing/licensing/licensing-process/) under a [ Public Services Obligation
Contract ](https://www.nationaltransport.ie/public-transport-services/pso-
contracts/) .

[ Córas Iompair Éireann ](http://www.cie.ie/) (CIÉ) is a state-owned company
that operates most of the bus services in Ireland.
