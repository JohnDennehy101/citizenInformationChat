###  Prepaid tickets

You can also pay your fare before you travel using a prepaid ticket. You can
buy one ahead of time at the bus station or download it to your phone from the
[ TFI app ](https://www.transportforireland.ie/tfi-go-app/) .

On Bus Eireann services, you also have an option to [ order your ticket online
](http://www.buseireann.ie/) .
