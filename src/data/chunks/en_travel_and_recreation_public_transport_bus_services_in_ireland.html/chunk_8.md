##  How much does a bus fare cost?

Most reduced fares of [ 20% will continue on public transport
](https://www.gov.ie/en/press-release/5c270-budget-2024-to-deliver-further-
reductions-in-fares-and-introduce-new-services-for-families-and-commuters/) in
2024 as well as a 50% reduction on the Young Adult Leap Card. See the [
National Transport Authority Fare (NTA) Plan 2024
](https://www.nationaltransport.ie/news/nta-publishes-fares-plan/) for more
details.

Since [ 23 June 2024 there are fare increases
](https://www.nationaltransport.ie/news/first-phase-of-2024-fares-
determination-to-take-effect-from-23rd-june/) on some services.
