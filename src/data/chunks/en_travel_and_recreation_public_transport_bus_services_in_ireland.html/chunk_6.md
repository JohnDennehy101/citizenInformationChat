##  Dublin Bus

[ Dublin Bus ](http://www.dublinbus.ie/) is part of CIÃ‰, providing [ bus
services ](https://www.dublinbus.ie/journey-information) in Dublin.

These services include:

  * **City Bus Services** : Regular bus routes throughout Dublin. 
  * [ **Airport Services** ](https://airlinkexpress.ie/faqs/times-routes-timetables) **(Airlink)** : Direct bus routes to and from Dublin Airport. 
  * [ **Night Services** ](https://www.dublinbus.ie/nitelink-timetables) **(Nitelink)** : Late-night bus services. 
  * [ **Express Routes** ](https://www.expressway.ie/routes-and-timetables) **(Xpresso)** : Faster bus services with fewer stops. 
  * **Rail Connection:** Bus services connecting [ Connolly ](https://www.irishrail.ie/station/dublin-connolly) and [ Heuston ](https://www.irishrail.ie/en-ie/station/dublin-heuston) Railway Stations. 
  * **School Services:** Managed through private contractors, with all coaches displaying the Dublin Bus logo.
