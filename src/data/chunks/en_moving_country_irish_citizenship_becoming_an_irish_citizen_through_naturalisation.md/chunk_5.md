###  Age

You must be 18 years or older.

You can **apply on behalf of a child** if:

  * The child was born in Ireland after 1 January 2005 and did not qualify for citizenship by birth, but has 3 years reckonable residence 
  * The child has at least one parent who is already an Irish citizen by naturalisation 
  * The child is of Irish descent or Irish associations 

A child is someone aged under 18 who is not married at the time of
application. A child cannot make the application by themselves. The
application must be made by their parent, legal guardian or a person acting on
the child’s behalf ‘in loco parentis’.
