##  Returning to Ireland after release from prison or being deported

Contact the [ Irish Council for Prisoners Overseas (ICPO)
](https://www.icpo.ie/) before you are released and return to Ireland. The
ICPO is a charitable organisation that supports Irish prisoners in prisons
overseas and when they return.

If you are in a prison overseas and want to apply to transfer to a prison in
Ireland, read the ICPO’s information on [ repatriation
](https://www.icpo.ie/repatriation/) .

You can download the ICPO’s [ step-by-step guide on returning to Ireland after
being released from prison (pdf) ](https://www.migrantproject.ie/wp-
content/uploads/2019/10/Crosscare-ICPO-Returning-to-Ireland-
leaflet-10.2019.pdf) . You can also download a copy of their ‘ [ Resettlement
handbook (pdf) ](https://www.icpo.ie/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/ICPO-
Resettlement-Handbook-Final.pdf) ’.
