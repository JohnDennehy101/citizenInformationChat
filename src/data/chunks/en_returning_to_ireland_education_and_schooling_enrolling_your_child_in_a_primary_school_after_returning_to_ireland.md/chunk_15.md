##  How to appeal a decision

If a school refuses to enrol your child, [ you can appeal the decision
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/the-irish-education-
system/admissions-policies-in-primary-and-secondary-schools#lc788f) .

You can read:

  * [ FAQs for parents if your child is refused admission because the school is oversubscribed (pdf) ](https://assets.gov.ie/89207/d4ccb93e-3119-4d4d-b629-b14e02296f30.pdf)
  * [ Information for parents if your child is refused admission for another reason (pdf) ](https://assets.gov.ie/89208/3f9ddca8-0230-4fc1-b3ab-b9fdde1581d2.pdf)
