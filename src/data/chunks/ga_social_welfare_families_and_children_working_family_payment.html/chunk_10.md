####  **Díbhinn** **Teaghlaigh um Fhilleadh** **ar an Obair**

Is féidir an [ Díbhinn Teaghlaigh um Fhilleadh ar Obair (BTWFD)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/families-and-children/back-to-work-family-dividend/) agus WFP a íoc
le chéile. Ní chuirtear an BTWFD san áireamh sa tástáil ioncaim don WFP.
