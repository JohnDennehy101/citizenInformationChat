####  **Household Benefits Package**

The [ Household Benefits Package ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-
benefits/household-benefits-package/) includes the Electricity or Natural Gas
Allowance and a Free TV Licence. If you are aged 70 or over, you qualify
regardless of your income or who lives with you. The scheme may also be
available to people aged 66 and over who get certain social welfare payments
or whose income is below certain limits. Apply to the Department of Social
Protection.
