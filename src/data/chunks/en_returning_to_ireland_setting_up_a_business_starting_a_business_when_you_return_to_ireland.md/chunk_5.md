##  Financial considerations

You must consider a range of financial issues before you start a business. For
example, your tax responsibilities will change. You might also need a start-up
grant or want to hire employees.

  * [ Understanding your tax as a business owner ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/understanding-your-tax-as-a-business-owner/)
  * [ Employing people in your business ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/employing-people-in-your-business/)
