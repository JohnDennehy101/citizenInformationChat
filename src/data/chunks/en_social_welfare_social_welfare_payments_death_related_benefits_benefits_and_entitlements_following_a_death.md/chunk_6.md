###  Further information

To check your entitlements, contact your nearest [ Intreo centre or Social
Welfare Branch Office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) or your
nearest [ Citizens Information Centre
](http://centres.citizensinformation.ie/) . You can also phone the Citizens
Information Phone Service on **0818 07 4000** .
