##  How to apply

You can get an application form for the heating supplement at your [ local
Community Welfare Service
](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/cb07d7-supplementary-welfare-allowance-swa-
list-of-offices-administering-swa/) . The Community Welfare Officer can help
you fill in the form.

The diet supplement was discontinued for new applicants from 1 February 2014.
