###  **Mortgage interest tax credit**

A new temporary [ mortgage interest tax credit ](/en/housing/owning-a-
home/buying-a-home/mortgage-interest-relief/) was announced in Budget 2024 for
homeowners who had an outstanding mortgage balance of between €80,000 and
€500,000 on their primary home on 31 December 2022.

The Mortgage Interest Tax Relief is available on the increased interest you
pay on your mortgage in 2023 when compared with the amount you paid in 2022.
The tax relief on the increase is 20%, which is the standard income tax rate.
The relief is capped at €1,250.

This tax credit only applies to tax assessments for 2023.
