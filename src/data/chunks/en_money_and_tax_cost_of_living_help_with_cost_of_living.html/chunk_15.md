###  School transport

The fee for the School Transport Scheme has been reduced to €50 per [ primary
school child ](/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-post-
primary-school/post-primary-school-transport-scheme/) and €75 per [ secondary
school child ](/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-post-
primary-school/post-primary-school-transport-scheme/) , up to a limit of €125
per family.
