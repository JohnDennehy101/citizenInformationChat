###  **Once-off double week in January 2024**

A once-off double week for all weekly qualifying social welfare payments was
paid in the week beginning 29 January 2024. Payments that qualify for the [
Christmas Bonus ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-
social-welfare-system/christmas-bonus/#f6befb) qualified for the once-off
double week.
