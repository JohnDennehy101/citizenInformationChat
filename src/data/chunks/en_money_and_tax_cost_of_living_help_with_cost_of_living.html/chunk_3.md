##  Claiming social welfare

You may be able to get social welfare assistance even if you work full-time or
have never claimed a payment before. There are many [ types of social welfare
payment
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social_welfare/social_welfare_payments/)
and you can find the rules of each payment in our [ social welfare section
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social_welfare/social_welfare_payments/)
.
