###  **Road Safety Transition Year programme**

The Road Safety Authority has designed a [ new 10-week programme about road
safety and learning to drive ](https://www.rsa.ie/road-
safety/education/secondary/transition-year) for Transition Year students.
‘Road Safety Matters’ covers different topics, such as the components of a car
and the dangers of alcohol, drugs and distractions while driving.

Read more about [ learning to drive
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-
licensing/learning-to-drive/) .
