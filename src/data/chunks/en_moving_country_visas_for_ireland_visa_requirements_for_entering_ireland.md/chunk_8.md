###  Short stay visas

If you want to come to Ireland for less than 3 months, you should apply for a
[ short stay ‘C’ visa ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/short-stay-visas-
policy/) . You should apply for this type of visa if you want to come to
Ireland as a tourist or to visit someone, for a business meeting, or to attend
a short course.

You cannot stay for longer than 3 months on a ‘C’ visa. You must leave Ireland
and apply for another visa if you want to return.
