##  How to apply for a visa orpreclearance

You must [ apply for an entry visa or preclearance online
](https://www.visas.inis.gov.ie/avats/OnlineHome.aspx) .

There is information on [ how to complete an online application
](http://www.inis.gov.ie/en/INIS/Information to Assist you in Completing your
Online Visa Application \(2\)_EN.pdf/Files/Information to Assist you in
Completing your Online Visa Application \(2\)_EN.pdf) in English (pdf) as well
as in [ Arabic (pdf) ](http://www.inis.gov.ie/en/INIS/Information to Assist
you in Completing your Online Visa Application\(2\)_AR.pdf/Files/Information
to Assist you in Completing your Online Visa Application\(2\)_AR.pdf) , [
Chinese (pdf) ](http://www.inis.gov.ie/en/INIS/Information on Completing
Online Application - Chinese FINAL.pdf/Files/Information on Completing Online
Application - Chinese FINAL.pdf) , [ Russian (pdf)
](http://www.inis.gov.ie/en/INIS/Information to Assist you in Completing your
Online Visa Application\(2\)_RU.pdf/Files/Information to Assist you in
Completing your Online Visa Application\(2\)_RU.pdf) , [ Hindi (pdf)
](http://www.inis.gov.ie/en/INIS/Information to Assist you in Completing your
Online Visa Application\(2\)_HI.pdf/Files/Information to Assist you in
Completing your Online Visa Application\(2\)_HI.pdf) , [ French (pdf)
](http://www.inis.gov.ie/en/INIS/Information to Assist you in Completing your
Online Visa Application\(2\)_FR.pdf/Files/Information to Assist you in
Completing your Online Visa Application\(2\)_FR.pdf) , [ Turkish (pdf)
](http://www.inis.gov.ie/en/INIS/Information to Assist you in Completing your
Online Visa Application\(2\)_TU.pdf/Files/Information to Assist you in
Completing your Online Visa Application\(2\)_TU.pdf) and [ Urdu (pdf)
](http://www.inis.gov.ie/en/INIS/Information to Assist you in Completing your
Online Visa Application\(2\)_UR.pdf/Files/Information to Assist you in
Completing your Online Visa Application\(2\)_UR.pdf) .

You should apply at least 8 weeks before you plan to come to Ireland. Details
of the [ photographic requirements
](http://www.inis.gov.ie/en/INIS/Pages/Photograph Requirements) and [ current
processing times
](http://www.inis.gov.ie/en/INIS/Pages/Current_Processing_Times) are on the
ISD website.

**Biometric data** : All visa applicants residing in Nigeria must provide [
biometric data ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/biometrics/) . Applicants
residing in Pakistan, India and China must provide fingerprints.

**Appeals:** If you are refused a visa you can [ appeal the decision
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/appeal-a-negative-decision/) by writing to
the [ Irish Immigration Service Visa office
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/visa-offices/) .
