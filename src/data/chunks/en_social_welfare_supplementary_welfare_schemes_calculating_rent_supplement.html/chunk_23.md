##  How to apply for Rent Supplement

You can donwload and fill in the [ application form for Rent Supplement (pdf)
](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/42214/d5508c17df7344beb8dde444cc0a02b4.pdf#page=null)
and send to your [ local Community Welfare Service
](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/cb07d7-supplementary-welfare-allowance-swa-
list-of-offices-administering-swa/) .

Contact [ your local Intreo Centre or social welfare branch office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) , or your [
local Citizens Information Centre ](https://centres.citizensinformation.ie/)
for help completing this form.
