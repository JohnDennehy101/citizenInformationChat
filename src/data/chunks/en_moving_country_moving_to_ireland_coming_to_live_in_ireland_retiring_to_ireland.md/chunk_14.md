###  Driving, cars and transport

Full driving licences from all other EU member states (and some other
countries) are recognised for use in Ireland. You may be able to [ convert
your driving licence to an Irish one ](/en/travel-and-
recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/exchanging-foreign-driving-permit/) . If
you want to bring your car to Ireland you need to know about [ importing a car
](/en/moving-country/moving-to-ireland/coming-to-live-in-ireland/importing-
car-into-ireland/) and implications for Vehicle Registration Tax (VRT).

If you are aged over 66 and living permanently in Ireland you are entitled to
[ free travel on public bus and rail transport ](/en/social-welfare/extra-
social-welfare-benefits/free-travel/) .
