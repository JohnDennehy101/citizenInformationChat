###  Housing

You may find that [ housing ](/en/moving-country/moving-to-
ireland/introduction-to-the-irish-system/accommodation/) is expensive in
Ireland. If you want to [ buy a home in Ireland ](/en/housing/owning-a-
home/buying-a-home/) you need to be aware of the prices, the process by which
houses are bought and sold and the initial costs that are involved. There are
no restrictions on the purchase or rent of residential property or land. [
Rented accommodation ](/en/housing/renting-a-home/looking-for-a-flat-or-house-
to-rent/) comes in many forms including houses, flats and apartments.
