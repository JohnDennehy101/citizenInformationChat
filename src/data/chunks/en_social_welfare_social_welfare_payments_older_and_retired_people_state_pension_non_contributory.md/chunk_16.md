###  **Increase for an adult dependant**

If you get an [ increase in your State Pension (Non-Contributory) for an adult
dependent ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-
welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/claiming-an-increase-in-your-
social-welfare-payment-for-an-adult-dependant/) , this increase will be paid
directly to your spouse, civil partner or cohabitant. **This only applies if
you claimed for an adult dependant after 27 September 2007** .
