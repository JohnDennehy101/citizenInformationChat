####  **Reporting the crime**

You are required to [ report the crime ](/en/justice/law-
enforcement/reporting-a-crime/) without delay to [ An Garda Síochána (the
Irish police force) ](/en/justice/law-enforcement/garda-siochana-national-
police-force/) . In any case where the crime is alleged to have been carried
out by a member of An Garda Síochána, you are required to report details of
the incident to the [ Garda Síochána Ombudsman Commission (GSOC)
](/en/justice/law-enforcement/garda-siochana-ombudsman-commission/) . You also
must co-operate fully with the investigation into the criminal incident by the
relevant authorities.
