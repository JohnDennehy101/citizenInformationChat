####  **If you were injured between 30 June 2005 and 20 April 2021**

From 31 January 2024 until 30 January 2025, you can submit a late application
if you were criminally injured between 30 June 2005 and 20 April 2021. The
Tribunal can accept such applications if there are [ exceptional circumstances
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/6edbc-criminal-injuries-compensation-
scheme-your-questions-answered/#when-an-application-must-be-made-and-whether-
there-is-a-time-limit) that justify a late application. If you are making a
late application, you must insert reasons on the application form to explain
the delay.

From 30 January 2025, applications relating to incidents which took place
between 30 June 2005 and 20 April 2021 will no longer be accepted.
