###  Time limits for making an application

Applications for compensation should be made as soon as possible and must be
made in writing within 3 months from the date of the incident causing the
injury. However, the Tribunal can accept late applications up to a maximum of
2 years following the incident in [ exceptional circumstances
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/6edbc-criminal-injuries-compensation-
scheme-your-questions-answered/#when-an-application-must-be-made-and-whether-
there-is-a-time-limit) .
