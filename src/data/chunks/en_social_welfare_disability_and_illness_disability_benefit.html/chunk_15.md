###  Taxation of Illness Benefit

Illness Benefit, both the personal rate and Increase for a Qualified Adult [
is taxable ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-
welfare-payment/taxation-of-social-welfare-payments/) . Any increases for
child dependants is not taxed.

Illness Benefit is paid directly to you without any tax deducted. It is taxed
by reducing your tax credits and rate band. Whether you are employed or
unemployed, Revenue will take account of the amount of Illness Benefit paid to
you when they adjust your tax credits or review the tax of your spouse or
civil partner.
