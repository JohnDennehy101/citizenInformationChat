##  Rates

**Weekly half-rate payment rates 2024**

**Social insurance payment** |  **Half-rate**  
---|---  
Maternity Benefit (standard rate €274)  |  €137   
Paternity Benefit (standard rate €274)  |  €137   
Adoptive Benefit (standard rate €274)  |  €137   
Health and Safety Benefit (maximum rate €232)  |  €116   
  
If you are working and on a reduced social welfare payment, you may get more
than half-rate Maternity, Paternity, Adoptive or Health and Safety Benefit
when you stop working.
