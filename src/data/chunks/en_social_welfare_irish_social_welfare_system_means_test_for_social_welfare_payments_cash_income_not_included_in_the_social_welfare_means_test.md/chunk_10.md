###  **Income related to old age, illness or disability**

  * Mobility Allowance or [ Blind Welfare Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/health-services-for-people-with-disabilities/blind-welfare-allowance/) from the HSE 
  * [ Consumer Directed Home Support (CDHS) ](https://control.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/health-services-for-older-people/home-support-service/) from the HSE 
  * Any repayments under the Nursing Home Repayments Scheme to the spouse or civil partner of the person overcharged
