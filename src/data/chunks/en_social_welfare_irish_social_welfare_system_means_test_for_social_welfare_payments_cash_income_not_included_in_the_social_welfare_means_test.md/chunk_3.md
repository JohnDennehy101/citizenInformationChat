##  Income from rent

You can get up to [ €14,000 per year for renting out a room in your own home
](https://www.gov.ie/en/press-release/4bf45-minister-humphreys-announces-
measures-to-remove-barriers-facing-social-welfare-customers-seeking-to-take-
in-tenants/) without it affecting your means-tested:

  * [ Weekly social assistance payment ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-assistance-payments/social-assistance-in-ireland/)
  * [ Supplementary Welfare Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/supplementary-welfare-allow/)
  * [ Working Family Payment ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/working-family-payment/) . 

You should check if renting a room in your home will affect any other payments
you’re getting, such as [ Fuel Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-
benefits/fuel-allowance/) .
