##  More information

Read our page on the [ means test
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/means-test-for-social-welfare-payments/means-test/) for more
information about how your means are assessed, and what income can be
disregarded (not taken into account).

You can also read about [ cash income not taken into account in the means test
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/11f23e-means-assessment-guidelines/) on
gov.ie.

Or, you can [ contact your local Citizens Information Centre
](https://centres.citizensinformation.ie/) to speak with an information
officer.
