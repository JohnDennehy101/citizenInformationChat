###  Inheritance by others

If the ARF funds are inherited by any other person (who is not your surviving
spouse or child) income tax at the marginal rate and CAT are both payable.

You can read more about [ pension benefits payable on death
](https://www.pensionsauthority.ie/en/LifeCycle/Benefits_payable_on_death/) .
