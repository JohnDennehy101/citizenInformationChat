##  Options on retirement

When you retire, you can take a [ tax-free lump sum
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/personal-
finance/pensions/tax-relief-on-pensions/#8bc76d) . You can also transfer some
or all of your retirement fund into an annuity or other approved scheme that
will pay you a pension.

For personal pension plans, the options available on retirement include:

  * Purchasing an annuity 
  * Investing in an Approved Retirement Fund (ARF)
