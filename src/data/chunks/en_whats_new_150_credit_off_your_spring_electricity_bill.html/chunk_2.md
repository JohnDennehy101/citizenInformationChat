#####  Site updates

Are your electricity bills high?

This Spring you will get a €150 [ energy credit off your electricity bill
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/utilities/electricity-
account-credit/#9b3c59) .

This credit will apply automatically to your electricity bill, between 1 March
and 30 April 2024. You do not need to apply for it.

The exact date you get your credit depends on the date that your supplier
normally sends you your bill.

It was announced in [ Budget 2024
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/budgets/budget-2024/)
that domestic electricity customers will get €450 off their electricity bills.
The energy credit is paid in 3 instalments of €150. This is the third
instalment.

Find out if you are eligible for the [ energy credit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/utilities/electricity-
account-credit/) and how it is paid.
