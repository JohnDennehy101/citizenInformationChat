###  Employment-related tax reliefs

Contributions to a [ pension ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/pensions/)
are eligible for tax relief at your highest rate of tax.

[ Find out more about employment-related tax reliefs ](/en/money-and-
tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/employment-tax-credits-and-reliefs/) ,
including reliefs for workers at sea and workers who commute to work in a
different country. You can also read about taxation of benefits from
employment.
