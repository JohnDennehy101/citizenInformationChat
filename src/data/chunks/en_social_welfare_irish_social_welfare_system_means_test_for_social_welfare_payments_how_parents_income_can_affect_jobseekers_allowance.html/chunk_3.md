##  Rules

To be assessed for benefit and privilege you must:

  * Be 24 years of age or under 
  * Be living with a parent or step-parent in the family home 
  * Have applied for [ Jobseeker’s Allowance (JA) ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-allowance/) or [ Supplementary Welfare Allowance (SWA) ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/supplementary-welfare-allow/) . 

**If you are living with your spouse, civil partner or cohabitant and living
with your parents you will not be assessed for benefit and privilege, even if
you are under 25 years of age.**

**If you have lived elsewhere in Ireland (or abroad) independent of your
parents for at least 3 years, benefit and privilege is assessed as €7.**
