##  Rent Tax Credit

You can claim an income tax credit for rent you have paid.

For 2024, the amount of the credit is 20% of your rent payments in the year,
up to a maximum of:

  * €750 for an individual 
  * €1,500 for a couple who are [ jointly assessed for tax ](../income_tax/taxation_of_married_people.en.html#l83f01)
