##  Fisher Tax Credit

If you have fished on a registered fishing vessel for at least 80 days in a
tax year you can claim an income tax credit of â‚¬1,270 per annum.

It is not available to fish farmers. If you claim this credit you cannot also
avail of the Seafarer's Allowance in the same tax year.
