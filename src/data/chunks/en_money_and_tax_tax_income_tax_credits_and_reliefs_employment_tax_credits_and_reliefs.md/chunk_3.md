##  Employee Tax Credit

If you are in employment, tax on your income is deducted by your employer on
behalf of the Revenue Commissioners. This system of deduction is known as the
Pay As You Earn (PAYE) system. All PAYE taxpayers are entitled to a tax credit
known as the [ Employee Tax Credit ](http://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-
credits-reliefs-and-exemptions/income-and-employment/employee-tax-
credit/index.aspx) (formerly known as the PAYE tax credit). This is worth
€1,875 in 2024 (€1,775 in 2023). If you are [ married and taxed under joint
assessment ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/taxation-of-married-people/) ,
then you and your spouse may both claim an Employee Tax Credit.
