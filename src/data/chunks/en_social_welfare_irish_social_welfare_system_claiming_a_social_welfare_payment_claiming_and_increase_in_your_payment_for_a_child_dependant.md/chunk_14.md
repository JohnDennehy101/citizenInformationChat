##  How to apply

To get an Increase for a Qualified Child, you must give details about your
child dependant on your claim form when you apply for a social welfare
payment.

If you did not claim for your child dependant at the time you made your claim,
you should contact [ the section in the Department of Social Protection which
pays your social welfare payment ](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/173b5-dsp-
phone-numbers/) , or your [ local Intreo or Branch office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-
offices/?referrer=https://www.gov.ie/en/service/40cf48-find-your-local-intreo-
office/) .
