###  Bilateral agreements

If you have returned from a country with which Ireland has a [ bilateral
social security agreement ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/social-insurance-contributions-from-
abroad/) , for example the USA, your pension rights from the other country are
protected when you move to Ireland. It is possible to have a State pension
from Ireland and another country.
