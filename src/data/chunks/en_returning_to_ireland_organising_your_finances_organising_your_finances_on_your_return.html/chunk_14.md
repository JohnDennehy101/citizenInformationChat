###  Private pensions

If you return to Ireland and want to transfer your private pension to Ireland
from abroad, you can transfer to an [ approved occupational pension scheme
](https://www.pensionsauthority.ie/en/lifecycle/private_pensions/private_sector_occupational_pension_schemes/)
, [ Personal Retirement Savings Account (PRSA)
](https://www.pensionsauthority.ie/en/lifecycle/private_pensions/personal_retirement_savings_accounts/)
or [ Buy-out bond (BOB)
](https://www.pensionsauthority.ie/en/lifecycle/benefits_payable_on_leaving/transfers_of_benefit/)
.

Your foreign pension must meet [ certain criteria to qualify
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/9ee188-transfer-of-private-pensions-to-
ireland-for-returning-irish-emigrant/) . You should contact the pension
administrator in the country where you have pension savings.
