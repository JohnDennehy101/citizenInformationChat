##  Buying motor insurance

There are 3 types of motor insurance available in Ireland:

  * Third party 
  * Third party fire and theft 
  * Comprehensive insurance 

The motor insurance you choose will depend on the level of cover you need and
how much you are willing to pay.

You can find more [ information on these different types of motor insurance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/motor-
tax-and-insurance/motor-insurance/#2509c3) .
