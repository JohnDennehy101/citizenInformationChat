####  Jobseeker’s Transitional payment

The earnings disregard for Jobseeker’s Transitional payment will increase from
€60 to €90 per week for existing and new recipients. All earnings above €90
will be assessed at 50% rather than the current assessment of 60% (January
2016). This measure will cost €8 million a year and will benefit 5,900 people
in 2016.
