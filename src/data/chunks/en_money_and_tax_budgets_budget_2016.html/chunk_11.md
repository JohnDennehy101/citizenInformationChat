###  Stamp duty

The current €2.50 per annum **charge on ATM cards** and €5 on combined (ATM
and debit) cards is being abolished from 1 January 2016.

A **new 12 cent fee per ATM withdrawal** is being introduced from 1 January
2016. The total fee paid per annum per card will be capped at €2.50 on ATM
cards and €5 on combined (ATM and debit) cards.
