###  Comhairle dlí

You can get [ legal advice and representation ](/ga/moving-country/asylum-
seekers-and-refugees/services-for-asylum-seekers-in-ireland/legal-aid-for-
asylum-seekers-in-ireland/) though the [ Legal Aid Board
](https://www.legalaidboard.ie/) . The Board can assist with applications for
international protection to the International Protection Office and appeals to
the International Protection Appeals Tribunal (IPAT). You must inform the IPO
of the details of your legal representative.

Solathraíonn an [ tIonad Dlí Neamhspleách Chomhairle Dídeanaithe na hÉireann
](https://www.irishrefugeecouncil.ie/Listing/Category/law-centre) comhairle
dlí agus ionadaíocht do dhaoine sna céimeanna tosaigh den phróiseas tearmainn.

Tá tú i dteideal dul i gcomhairle le [ hArd-Choimisinéir na Náisiún Aontaithe
le haghaidh Dídeanaithe ](https://www.unhcr.ie/) (UNHCR) a bhfuil oifig aige i
mBaile Átha Cliath.
