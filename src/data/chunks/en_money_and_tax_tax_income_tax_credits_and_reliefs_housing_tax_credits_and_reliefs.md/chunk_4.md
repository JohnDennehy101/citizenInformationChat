##  Mortgage interest tax credit

A new mortgage interest tax relief was announced in Budget 2024.

To qualify, you must have had an outstanding mortgage balance of between
€80,000 and €500,000 on 31 December 2022.

The tax relief is 20% of the increased interest you paid on your mortgage in
2023 when compared with the amount you paid in 2022. The relief is capped at
€1,250.
