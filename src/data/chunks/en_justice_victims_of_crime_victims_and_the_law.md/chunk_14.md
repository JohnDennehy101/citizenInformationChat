###  **Criminal Law (Sexual Offences and Human Trafficking) Act 2024**

When the relevant sections of the [ Criminal Law (Sexual Offences and Human
Trafficking) Act 2024
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2024/act/28/enacted/en/html) are
commenced it will put the new [ National Referral Mechanism
](https://www.gov.ie/en/press-release/27823-government-approves-proposal-for-
a-revised-national-referral-mechanism-to-make-it-easier-for-human-trafficking-
victims-to-come-forward-and-receive-supports/) (NRM) for human trafficking
victims on a statutory footing.

Under the new scheme, several state and non-governmental organisations are to
join with An Garda Síochána in identifying victims of human trafficking and
providing them with access to the range of support services available through
the NRM.

Read more about [ human trafficking
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/justice/crime-and-crime-
prevention/human-trafficking/) on our website.
