###  Criminal Justice Act 1999

The [ Criminal Justice Act 1999
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/1999/act/10/enacted/en/html) deals with
the protection of witnesses, including victims, who may have to give evidence
in court.

It allows witnesses who are in fear or subject to intimidation to give
evidence by a live video link.

15 years imprisonment.
