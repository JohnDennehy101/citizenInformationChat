####  Further information or documents

If you are asked to provide information reasonably required by a social
welfare inspector when investigating your claim, you must provide the
statements, information or documents within 21 days. The same time period
applies to your spouse, civil partner, cohabitant, employer and certain other
people (for example, landlords).

If you are refused a social welfare payment or get a lesser amount than you
expected, you have 21 days to [ appeal the decision of the Department of
Social Protection ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-
welfare-appeals/) .

There is more information about how the Department makes decisions on claims
in 'More information' below.
