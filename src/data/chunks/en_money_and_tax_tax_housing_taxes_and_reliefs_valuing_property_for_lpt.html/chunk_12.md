##  Properties affected by pyrite or defective concrete

If your property has pyrite or mica damage, you can take this into account
when self-assessing the market value of your property as at 1 November 2021.

You may also qualify for:

  * [ Exemption from LPT for properties with pyrite damage ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/property/local-property-tax/lpt-exemptions/pyritic-damage.aspx) or 
  * [ Exemption from LPT for properties constructed using defective concrete blocks ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/property/local-property-tax/lpt-exemptions/defective-concrete-blocks.aspx)
