###  Revenue valuation tool

Revenue provides an [ online valuation tool ](https://lpt.revenue.ie/lpt-
web/valuation-guide/index.htm) that will give you the average values for
property in your area. You need to take into account the specific features of
your property compared to others to decide whether it is within the average
valuation band given for your area. For example, your property could be
smaller or bigger than the average in your area.
