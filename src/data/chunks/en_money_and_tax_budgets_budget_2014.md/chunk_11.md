###  Returning to education or training

From 14 January 2014 people aged 25 will get an age-reduced rate of
Jobseeker’s Allowance of €144. If they take part in the Back to Education
Scheme (BTEA) they will get a standard maximum rate of €160 per week.

All new BTEA participants aged under 26 who were getting a reduced age-related
Jobseeker’s Allowance payment, will get a maximum BTEA rate of €160 per week
(any means participants have will be deducted from this rate).

People aged 25 who were getting an age-related reduced rate of Jobseeker’s
Allowance will also get a standard weekly allowance of €160 if they take part
in a FÁS course, the Vocational Training Opportunities Scheme (VTOS) or a
Youthreach course.
