###  **Can I use an electric bike in Ireland?**

Electric bicycles or e-bikes are growing in popularity and can be bought under
the [ Cycle to Work Scheme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-
recreation/cycling/cycle-to-work-scheme/) . You can find out more about [
e-bikes ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-
recreation/e-scooters-e-bikes-and-e-mopeds/e-bikes/) .
