###  **Cásanna tromchúiseacha bulaíochta**

Ní mór cásanna tromchúiseacha bulaíochta, nuair a mheastar go bhféadfadh an
t-iompar a bheith maslach, a thuairisciú do [ Tusla
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/services-
and-supports-for-children/tusla-the-child-and-family-agency/) nó [ An Garda
Síochána ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/justice/law-
enforcement/garda-siochana-national-police-force/) .
