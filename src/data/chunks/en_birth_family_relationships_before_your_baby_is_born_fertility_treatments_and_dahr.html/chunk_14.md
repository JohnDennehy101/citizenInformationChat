##  More information

The Health Services Executive (HSE) has information on [ types of fertility
problems ](https://www2.hse.ie/conditions/fertility/) and what you should do
if you are worried.

The [ National Infertility Information and Support Group (NISIG)
](https://nisig.com/) provide information and support for anyone experiencing
fertility problems. It operates a 24-hour helpline and holds support group
meetings for both members and non-members.

LGBT Ireland and FLAC have published detailed [ FAQs on family rights for
LGBTI+ parents and those planning parenthood (pdf) ](https://lgbt.ie/wp-
content/uploads/2020/04/2020_CFRA_FAQ-1.pdf) in relation to the Children and
Family Relationships Act 2015.
