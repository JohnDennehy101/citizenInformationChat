###  **Enrol children in school**

If you’re returning with children, bring their recent school reports and test
results to help their new school determine what class suits their needs.

You can book your child’s school places in advance. See our pages on:

  * [ Enrolling your child in primary school ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/education-and-schooling/enrolling-your-child-in-a-primary-school-after-returning-to-ireland/)
  * [ Enrolling your child in secondary school ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/education-and-schooling/enrolling-your-child-in-a-secondary-school-after-returning-to-ireland/)

You and your children may also be eligible for [ free tuition
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/third-level-education/fees-
and-supports-for-third-level-education/fees/) on a publicly funded [ third-
level
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/third_level_education/)
course. Read our information on the [ Irish education system
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/the_irish_education_system/)
for more information.
