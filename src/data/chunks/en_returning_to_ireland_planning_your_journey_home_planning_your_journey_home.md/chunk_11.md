##  Shipping your personal belongings home

If you’re returning to Ireland for an extended period of time, you will need
to [ ship your belongings home
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/planning-your-
journey-home/shipping-your-belongings-back-to-ireland/) .

When you’re deciding what to ship, be aware that the cost will depend on size,
weight and shape. If possible, get sales receipts or invoices for all the
items you’re shipping.

Depending on the type of goods you’re shipping, and where they’re coming from,
[ import charges ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-
ireland/planning-your-journey-home/shipping-your-belongings-back-to-
ireland#l683b4) such as customs duty and [ Value Added Tax
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/duties-and-
vat/value-added-tax/) may apply.

You may be able to claim [ tax relief
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/planning-your-
journey-home/shipping-your-belongings-back-to-ireland#l683b4) for certain
items. Revenue has a full list of rules on [ relief from Customs Duty and VAT
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/customs/individuals/transfer-residence/rules.aspx)
.
