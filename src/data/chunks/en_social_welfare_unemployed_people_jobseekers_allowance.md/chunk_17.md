##  Where to apply for Jobseeker's Allowance

You can apply for JA:

  * Online at [ MyWelfare.ie ](https://services.mywelfare.ie/)
  * In person at your [ Intreo Centre or Social Welfare Branch Office. ](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/)

Applications should be made either online or by post where possible.
