###  **Pensions in construction**

If you work in construction, there are pension schemes available. [ The
Construction Workers' Pension Scheme (CWPS)
](https://www.cwps.ie/content/AboutUs/2) , for example, is one of the largest.

An [ Auto-enrolment pension savings scheme
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/auto-enrolment/) is expected to be introduced in 2025. Your employer
may enroll you in this scheme if they don’t have another one in place.

You can watch the Department of Social Protection’s [ video on auto-enrolment
](https://youtu.be/2_f2_8AP2l8) or visit [ the auto-enrolment information hub
](https://www.gov.ie/en/campaigns/0ab04-automatic-enrolment-for-pensions-hub/)
.

You can also read [ frequently asked questions about auto-enrolment
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/01568-auto-enrolment-your-questions-
answered-rol-draft/?) .
