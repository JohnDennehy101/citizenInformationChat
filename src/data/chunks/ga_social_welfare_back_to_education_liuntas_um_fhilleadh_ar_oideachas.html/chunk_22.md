###  **An féidir LFO a fháil agus mé ar scéim leasa shóisialaigh eile?**

Má fhaigheann tú Liúntas um Fhilleadh ar Oideachas (LFO), ní féidir leat
oibriú go lánaimseartha ná a bheith páirteach sna scéimeanna seo a leanas:

  * [ Fostaíocht Phobail ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/employment-support-schemes/community-employment-scheme/)
  * [ Tús ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/employment-support-schemes/tus/)
  * [ Scéim Shóisialta Thuaithe ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/farming-and-fishing/rural-social-scheme/)
  * [ Scéim Dreasachta Poist Pháirtaimseartha ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments-and-work/part-time-job-incentive-scheme/)
  * [ JobsPlus ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/employment-support-schemes/jobsplus/)
  * [ Scéim Fóirdheontas Pá (WSS) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-and-disability/wage-subsidy-scheme-for-people-with-disabilities/)
  * [ Clár Taithí Socrúcháin Oibre (WPEP) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/employment-support-schemes/work-placement-experience-programme/)
  * [ Scéim Deiseanna Oiliúna Gairmoideachais (SDOG) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/further-education-and-training/vocational-training-opportunities-scheme/)
  * Cláir oiliúna BOO 
  * [ Youthreach ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/further-education-and-training/youthreach/)
  * Aon fhostaíocht, scéim nó clár oiliúna eile a fheidhmíonn an Líonra Foghlama Náisiúnta 

Má fhaigheann tú [ Íocaíocht do Theaghlach Oibre (WFP)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-
children/working-family-payment/) , níl tú incháilithe don LFO.
