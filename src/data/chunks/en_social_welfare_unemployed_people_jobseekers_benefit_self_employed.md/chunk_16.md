###  Extra benefits

If you are getting Jobseeker's Benefit (Self-Employed), you may be entitled
to:

  * [ Rent Supplement ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-schemes/rent-supplement/) \- a payment under the Supplementary Welfare Allowance scheme. 
  * [ Back to School Clothing and Footwear Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/back-to-school-clothing-and-footwear-allowance/) \- an allowance to help with the cost of uniforms and footwear for children who are attending school. 
  * [ Medical card ](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/medical-card/) \- if your income is below a certain level, you may get a medical card. 

You do not qualify for the [ Household Benefits Package ](/en/social-
welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/household-benefits-package/) or [ Free
Travel ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/free-travel/) with
