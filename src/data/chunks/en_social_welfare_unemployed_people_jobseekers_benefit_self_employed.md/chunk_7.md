###  How long can I get paid Jobseeker's Benefit (Self-Employed)?

Jobseeker's Benefit (Self-Employed) is paid for 9 months (39 weeks) for people
with 260 or more Class S PRSI contributions. It is paid for 6 months (26
weeks) for people with fewer than 260 Class S PRSI contributions.
