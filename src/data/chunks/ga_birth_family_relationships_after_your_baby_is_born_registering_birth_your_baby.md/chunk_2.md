##  Réamhrá

Cláraíonn an [ tSeirbhís um Chlárú Sibhialta FSS
](https://www2.hse.ie/services/births-deaths-and-marriages/find-a-civil-
registration-service/) gach breith, bás agus pósadh in Éirinn.

**Ba cheart duit** breith do linbh a chlárú laistigh de 3 mhí óna bhreith, más
féidir. Mar sin féin, tá suas le 12 mhí agat chun an bhreith a chlárú. Más gá
duit breithe a chlárú tar éis 12 mhí, déan teagmháil leis an [ tSeirbhís um
Chlárú Sibhialta chun comhairle a fháil ](https://www2.hse.ie/services/births-
deaths-and-marriages/find-a-civil-registration-service/) .

Ní gá duit do leanbh a thabhairt chuig an oifig chlárúcháin agus a bhreith á
chlárú.

Nuair a bheidh an bhreith cláraithe, tabharfar [ uimhir PSP
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/personal-public-service-number/) do do leanbh agus is féidir leat [
teastas breithe ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-
relationships/getting-birth-marriage-or-death-certificate/) a fháil (a bheidh
ag teastáil uait chun [ do leanbh a chlárú ar scoil
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-
education/going-to-primary-school/starting-primary-school/) , chun [ iarratas
a dhéanamh ar phas ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-
recreation/passports/applying-for-or-renewing-an-irish-passport/) agus chun
críocha go leor eile).
