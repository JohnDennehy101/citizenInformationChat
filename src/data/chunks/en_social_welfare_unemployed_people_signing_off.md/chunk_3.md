##  Close your jobseeker’s payment online

You can close your Jobseeker's Allowance or Jobseeker’s Benefit payment on
MyWelfare.ie. To do this you will need a [ MyGovID ](/en/government-in-
ireland/how-government-works/egovernment/mygovid/) account.
