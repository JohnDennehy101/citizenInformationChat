##  Employment rights

Workers in Ireland have a number of important rights. These include:

  * [ Minimum wage ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/pay-and-employment/minimum-wage/)
  * [ Rest periods and breaks ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/hours-of-work/work-breaks-and-rest-periods/)
  * [ Annual leave ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/annual-leave/)
  * [ Health and safety in the workplace ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/health-and-safety/health-safety-work/)
  * [ Written terms of employment ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/contracts-of-employment/contract-of-employment/)
  * [ Equality in the workplace ](/en/employment/equality-in-work/equality-in-the-workplace/)
  * [ Public holidays ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/public-holidays/)
  * [ Unfair dismissal laws ](/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/dismissal/unfair-dismissal/)
  * [ Redundancy ](/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/redundancy/what-is-redundancy/)
  * Family leave, including [ maternity ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/maternity-leave/) and [ paternity leave ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/leave-and-holidays/paternity-leave/)
