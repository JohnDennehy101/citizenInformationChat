##  Health and Safety Benefit and tax

Health and Safety Benefit is taxable for all claimants. It is taxed in the
same way as Maternity Benefit. Universal Social Charge and PRSI are not
payable. You can read about how [ Maternity Benefit is taxed ](/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-
payment/taxation-of-social-welfare-payments/) .

You can also get more information from Revenue on [ taxation of Maternity
Benefit ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/jobs-and-pensions/taxation-of-social-
welfare-payments/maternity-adoptive-health-safety-benefits.aspx) .
