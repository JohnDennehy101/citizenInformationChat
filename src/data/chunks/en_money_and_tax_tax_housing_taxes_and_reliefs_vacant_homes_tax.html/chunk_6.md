##  How much is Vacant Homes Tax?

The rate of VHT on a property is 3 times the [ basic rate of Local Property
Tax ](local_property_tax.en.html#l20ad0) for the property.

The basic rate of Local Property Tax depends on the value of the property.

It was announced in [ Budget 2024 ](/en/money-and-tax/budgets/budget-2024/)
that the rate of VHT will increase to 5 times the basic rate of Local Property
Tax for the chargeable period 1 November 2023 to 31 October 2024.
