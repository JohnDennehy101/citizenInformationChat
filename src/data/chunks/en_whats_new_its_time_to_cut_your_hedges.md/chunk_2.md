#####  News

  

Did you know? It is illegal to cut hedges between 1 March and 31 August. 🚫

The ban gives species like birds the chance to nest safely and raise their
chicks. 🐣

Since 1 September, you can start cutting your hedges again.

See more information about 👉 [ hedge cutting
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/environment/land/hedge-cutting/) .
