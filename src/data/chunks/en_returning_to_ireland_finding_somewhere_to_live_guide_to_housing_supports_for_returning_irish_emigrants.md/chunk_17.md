###  Older people and people with disabilities

If you already own a property in Ireland and meet certain eligibility
criteria, you can [ apply for the Mobility Aids Grant Scheme
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/housing-grants-and-
schemes/housing-supports-for-older-people-and-people-with-
disabilities/mobility-aids-grant-scheme/) (for example, to add a stairlift or
an accessible shower).

You may also qualify for the [ Housing Aid for Older People Grant
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/housing-grants-and-
schemes/housing-supports-for-older-people-and-people-with-
disabilities/housing-aid-for-older-people-grant/) (for example, to upgrade
electrical wiring or provide central heating).

See more [ grants for older people and people with disabilities
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/housing-grants-and-
schemes/housing-supports-for-older-people-and-people-with-
disabilities/mobility-aids-grant-scheme/) .

You can also visit the [ Age Friendly Homes website
](https://agefriendlyhomes.ie/) for information on adapting an existing home,
new accessible homes, housing grants, housing design, housing policy and
research.
