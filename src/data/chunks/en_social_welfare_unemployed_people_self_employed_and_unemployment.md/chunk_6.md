##  Other benefits

If your income is below a certain amount you may get a [ medical card
](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/medical-card/) or a [ GP visit
card ](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/gp-visit-cards/) .

You may get [ help with paying your rent ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-
welfare-schemes/rent-supplement/) . If you are in local authority housing, you
should contact your local authority and ask for a review of your rent because
of the reduction in your income.

You may get help with the cost of uniforms and footwear for children attending
school. The [ Back to School Clothing and Footwear Allowance Scheme
](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/back-to-school-clothing-and-
footwear-allowance/) operates from 1 June to 30 September each year.

If you have an additional need, for example, you need help with a funeral
bill, a range of [ discretionary payments are provided by the Department of
Social Protection's representative ](/en/social-welfare/supplementary-welfare-
schemes/additional-needs-payment/) in your [ Intreo Centre or Social Welfare
Branch Office ](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-
offices/) .
