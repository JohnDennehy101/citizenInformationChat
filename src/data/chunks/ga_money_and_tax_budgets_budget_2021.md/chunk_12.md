###  Vehicle Registration Tax (VRT) and motor tax

New rates tables for [ VRT ](/ga/moving-country/moving-to-ireland/coming-to-
live-in-ireland/importing-car-into-ireland/) and [ motor tax ](/ga/travel-and-
recreation/motoring/motor-tax-and-insurance/ratai-motarchanach/) will be
introduced. The CO2-based VRT and motor tax regimes will be replaced with the
**new Worldwide Harmonised Light Vehicle Test Procedure (WLTP) emissions
system** from January 2021. See 'Transport' below for details of changes to
VRT and motor taxes.
