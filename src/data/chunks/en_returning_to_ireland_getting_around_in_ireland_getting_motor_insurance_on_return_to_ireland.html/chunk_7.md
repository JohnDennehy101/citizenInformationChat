###  **If you have been abroad for less than 2 years**

If you have been abroad for less than 2 years, your no claims bonus will still
apply. Your no claims bonus is based on your previous driving experience in
Ireland in your own name. Insurance Ireland has published information on [
using a no claims discount when returning to Ireland (pdf)
](https://www.insuranceireland.eu/media/documents/Policyholders%20with%20a%20No%20Claims%20Discount%20Moving%20Abroad.pdf)
.
