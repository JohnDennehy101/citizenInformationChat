###  **Quarries Skills Certification Scheme (QSCS)**

The Quarries Skills Certification Scheme (QSCS) programmes are for non-craft
operatives in the quarrying sector and are divided into [ three categories,
and each requires a different QSCS registration card
](https://www.solas.ie/construction-lp/course-information/#qscs) . You do not
need a Safe Pass card to enroll.

You can use the SOLAS database to [ search for Approved Training Organisations
(ATO) ](https://www.constructioncourses.ie/Home/PublishedCourses) that provide
the QSCS-required training for activities you will do in your job. The ATO
will charge you a fee for participating in a CSCS programme.

QSCS Experienced Operator Registration Cards are issued for 5 years. After
that, you can [ renew your card online.
](https://cardrenewals.solas.ie/#/qscs/renew)
