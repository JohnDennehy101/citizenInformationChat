###  Duration

The pension remains payable while you remain widowed or a surviving civil
partner. If you re-marry or start to cohabit, it is no longer payable.

You may get [ increases for qualified children ](/en/social-welfare/irish-
social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/claiming-and-increase-
in-your-payment-for-a-child-dependant/) with your pension - these remain
payable while the child is aged under 18 and they may then be continued until
age 22 if the child is in full-time education.
