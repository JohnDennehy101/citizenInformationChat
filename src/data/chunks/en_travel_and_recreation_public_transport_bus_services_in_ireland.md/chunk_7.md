##  Bus Éireann

[ Bus Éireann ](http://www.buseireann.ie/) is a subsidiary of CIÉ and provides
[ bus services ](https://www.buseireann.ie/our-services) throughout Ireland
except for Dublin City.

Bus Éireann services include:

  * [ **Expressway Inter-Urban Coaches** ](https://www.expressway.ie/) **:** Long-distance coach services between cities. 
  * **Town and City Services:** Bus services within various towns and cities. 
  * **Commuter Buses:** Services to and from Dublin, Cork, Limerick, Galway, and Waterford. 
  * [ **Airport Services** ](https://www.buseireann.ie/groups/airport-services) **:** Bus routes connecting airports to multiple destinations. 
  * [ **Local Town-to-Town and Rural Services** ](https://www.buseireann.ie/inner.php?id=245) **:** Bus services between smaller towns and rural areas. 
  * [ **School Bus Services** ](https://www.buseireann.ie/schools) **:** Operated on behalf of the Department of Education, including student services. 
  * [ **Eurolines** ](https://www.expressway.ie/eurolines) **:** Coach services to Britain and Europe.
