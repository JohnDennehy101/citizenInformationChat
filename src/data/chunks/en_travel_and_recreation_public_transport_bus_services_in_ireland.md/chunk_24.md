##  Passengers with disabilities

Public transport companies offer a range of accessibility services.

You can find information on the [ Dublin Bus website about bye-laws
](https://www.dublinbus.ie/policys-and-procedures/dublin-bus-bye-laws) and [
information for people with disabilities
](https://www.dublinbus.ie/accessibilityhome) .

Read more about [ travelling on public transport with a disability or reduced
mobility ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/public-
transport/travelling-publictransport-with-disability/) .
