###  **Passenger rights**

Under [ EU Regulation 181/2011 ](https://eur-
lex.europa.eu/LexUriServ/LexUriServ.do?uri=CELEX:32011R0181:EN:HTML) , bus and
coach passengers have [ minimum rights
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/travel/your-rights-when-you-
travel-by-bus-or-coach/) when travelling in the European Union.
