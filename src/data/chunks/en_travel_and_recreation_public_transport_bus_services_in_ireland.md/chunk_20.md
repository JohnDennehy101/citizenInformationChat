###  **Complain about a Bus Éireann service**

If you have a complaint about a Bus Éireann service, you can:

  * Phone 0818 836 611 
  * [ Fill out the contact form ](https://www.buseireann.ie/inner.php?id=290)
  * Email customercare@buseireann.ie 

You can also read the [ Bus Éireann customer charter
](https://www.buseireann.ie/customer-charter) on its website.
