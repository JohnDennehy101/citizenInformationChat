##  Stamp Duty

Stamp duty is tax you pay when you transfer property. .

Transfers of property between spouses or civil partners are exempt from stamp
duty.

If spouses or civil partners are separated or divorced, transfers of property
which are ordered by a court are exempt from stamp duty.
