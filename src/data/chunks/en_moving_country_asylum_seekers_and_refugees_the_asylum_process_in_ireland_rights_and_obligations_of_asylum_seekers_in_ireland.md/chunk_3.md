###  Staying in Ireland and accommodation

You can stay in Ireland while your application is being processed.

The International Protection Accommodation Services (IPAS) is responsible for
providing you with [ accommodation while you are waiting for your application
to be finalised ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/moving-country/asylum-
seekers-and-refugees/services-for-asylum-seekers-in-ireland/direct-provision/)
.

Since January 2023, applicants for international protection who do not have
children are not prioritised for accommodation. You can read more about [ how
international protection applicants are prioritised for accommodation
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/e202e-statistics-on-international-
protection-applicants-not-offered-accommodation/) . When you apply for
international protection, you will give your contact details. When
accommodation is available, IPAS will contact you.

The Irish Refugee Council has useful information on [ supports for homeless
international protection applicants.
](https://www.irishrefugeecouncil.ie/info-for-homeless-international-
protection-applicants)
