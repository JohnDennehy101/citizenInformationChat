##  Rate of the Accommodation Recognition Payment

The Accommodation Recognition Payment (ARP) is a set monthly rate of €800 per
property from 1 December 2022. It was previously €400 per month.

If there are other costs and you are experiencing financial difficulty, you
can apply for the [ Additional Needs Payment
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/4eb45-additional-needs-payment/) . The
Additional Needs Payment is decided on a case-by-case basis.
