##  How to get your Irish social insurance record

If you want to bring your U1 with you, you can [ request your Irish social
insurance record for U1 (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/69370/10649c2dcdb84da4a44721dcd917aeb2.pdf) before
leaving. Processing your application can take some time as the DSP may have to
contact your recent employers before U1 forms can be issued.

You should send your completed application form to the [ International Records
Section ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/cf633a-claiming-benefits-abroad/) of
the Department of Social Protection.
