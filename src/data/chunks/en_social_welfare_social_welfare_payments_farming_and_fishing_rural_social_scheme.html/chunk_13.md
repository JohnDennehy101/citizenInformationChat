##  How to apply for the Rural Social Scheme

You should contact your [ local RSS implementing body
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/eb8f4e-rural-social-scheme-list-of-
implementing-bodies/) for further information or an application form.

You can also read the Department of Social Protection's [ guidelines on the
Rural Social Scheme ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/e8c048-operational-
guidelines-rural-social-scheme/) .
