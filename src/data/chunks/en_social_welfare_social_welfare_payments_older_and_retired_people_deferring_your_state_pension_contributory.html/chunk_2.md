##  What is deferring the State Pension (Contributory)?

The State Pension (Contributory) is paid to people from the age of 66 who have
enough [ (PRSI) contributions ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-insurance-prsi/social-insurance/) .
From January 2024, you can choose to start claiming your State Pension
(Contributory) anytime between the ages of 66 and 70.

Choosing a later start date is called deferring your pension. You can defer
your start date up until you turn 70.
