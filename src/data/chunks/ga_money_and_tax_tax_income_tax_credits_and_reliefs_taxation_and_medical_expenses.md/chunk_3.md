##  Cad iad na speansais cúram sláinte ar féidir liom cáin a éileamh ar ais
orthu?

Féadfaidh tú faoiseamh cánach a éileamh ar:

  * Táillí dochtúir agus comhairleoirí 
  * Cothabháil nó cóireáil in ospidéal, saoráid chóireála (mar chlinic) nó i [ dteach altranais ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-credits-reliefs-and-exemptions/health-and-age/health-expenses/nursing-home-and-additional-nursing-care-expenses.aspx)
  * [ Iompar in otharcharr ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-system/emergency-health-services-in-ireland/)
  * Altra cáilithe a fhostú sa bhaile 
  * Cóireáil ó shíceolaí nó síceiteiripeoir 
  * Snáthaidpholladh 
  * [ Costais othar duán ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-credits-reliefs-and-exemptions/health-and-age/health-expenses/additional-expenses-for-a-kidney-patient.aspx)
  * [ Cóireáil fiaclóireachta speisialaithe ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/taxation-and-medical-expenses/#5f907b)
  * Cúram máithreachais 
  * [ Toirchiú in vitro (TIV) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/before-your-baby-is-born/fertility-treatments-and-dahr/)
  * [ Teiripe urlabhra agus teanga ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-community/speech-and-language-therapy-services/) do leanbh faoi 18 nó in oideachas lánaimseartha 
  * Measúnuithe síceolaíochta oideachais do leanbh faoi 18 nó in oideachas lánaimseartha
