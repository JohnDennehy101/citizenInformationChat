##  Further information

There are strict regulations about [ importing pets from abroad ](/en/moving-
country/moving-to-ireland/coming-to-live-in-ireland/bringing-pets-to-ireland/)
and there are [ customs regulations about the importation of prohibited or
restricted goods ](/en/travel-and-recreation/travel-to-ireland/customs-
regulations-for-travellers/) .

Irish citizens living in Ireland have the right to vote in all elections and
referenda. If you are living in Ireland but are not an Irish citizen you may [
vote in some elections ](/en/government-in-ireland/elections-and-
referenda/voting/right-to-vote/) . You can read about the [ political system
at national level ](/en/moving-country/moving-to-ireland/introduction-to-the-
irish-system/political-system/) and [ the political system at local level
](/en/government-in-ireland/how-government-works/local-and-regional-
government/functions-of-local-authorities/) .

A [ range of organisations ](/en/employment/retirement/retirement-from-
work/active-retirement/) promote education and leisure activities for retired
people.
