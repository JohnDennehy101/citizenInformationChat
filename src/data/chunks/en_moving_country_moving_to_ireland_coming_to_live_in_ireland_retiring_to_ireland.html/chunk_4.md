###  Nationals who can enter and live in Ireland with no restrictions

Irish citizens and UK citizens can enter Ireland and live here without any
conditions. But if you have family who are citizens of other countries and
want to join you here, you may have to show that they can support themselves
or that you can support them.

You can read more about [ Residence rights of UK citizens ](/en/government-in-
ireland/ireland-and-the-uk/residence-rules-UK-citizens/) and [ Retiring to
Ireland as a returning Irish emigrant ](/en/returning-to-ireland/retiring-to-
ireland/retiring-to-ireland-as-a-returning-irish-emigrant/) .
