##  Introduction

If you are planning on retiring to Ireland, this page can help you with some
of the practical steps involved. You will also find links to further
information available on citizensinformation.ie and other websites.

If you are an Irish citizen, you can read about [ returning home to Ireland to
retire ](/en/returning-to-ireland/retiring-to-ireland/retiring-to-ireland-as-
