###  Lump sum payments

In 2024/2025, the total Fuel Allowance payment over 28 weeks is €924.

It can be paid to you in 2 instalments (lump sums) - September 2024 and
January 2025.

To change how your Fuel Allowance is paid, fill in the [ Change the Payment
Frequency form (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/26443/817ed1f255c04cd3b08a283db1a98831.pdf) at least
one month before the first instalment is due.

This instalment option is not available to people getting payments under the
Rural Social Scheme, Tús, Gateway, Community Employment or Supplementary
Welfare Allowance.
