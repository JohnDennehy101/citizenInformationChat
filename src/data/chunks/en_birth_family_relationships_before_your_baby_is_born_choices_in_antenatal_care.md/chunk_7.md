##  How to apply

Your GP or the [ Health Service Executive (HSE) ](/en/health/health-
system/health-service-executive/) will advise you of the nearest maternity
hospital(s), and will supply you with a letter of referral for the hospital if
needed. You can telephone the hospital directly for a first appointment.
