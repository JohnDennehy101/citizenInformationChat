####  Child Benefit

Child Benefit rates will be reduced to €130 per month for the 1st, 2nd and 3rd
child. (January 2013)

Child Benefit rates will be €140 per month for the 4th and subsequent children
(announced in Budget 2012). (January 2013)

From the following year, the monthly rate for the 4th and subsequent children
will be €130. (January 2014)
