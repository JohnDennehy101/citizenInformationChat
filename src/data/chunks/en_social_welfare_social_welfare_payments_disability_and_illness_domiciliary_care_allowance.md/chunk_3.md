###  **DCA medical card scheme**

All children getting DCA can get a [ medical card
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-
cards/medical-card/) without a means test.

If your child does not already have a medical card or [ GP visit card
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-
cards/gp-visit-cards/) , you can:

  * [ Register online ](https://www.sspcrs.ie/portal/free-gp-care-dca-web/pub) using the HSE website, or 
  * [ Download the HSE’s paper registration form (pdf) ](http://www.hse.ie/eng/services/list/1/schemes/mc/dca/Final%20Version%20DCA%20Form.pdf)

If your child already has a medical card or GP visit card, you don’t need to
register.
