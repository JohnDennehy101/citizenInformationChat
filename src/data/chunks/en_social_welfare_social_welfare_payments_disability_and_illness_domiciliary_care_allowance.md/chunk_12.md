###  **Other payments**

You can get [ Child Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/families-and-children/child-benefit/) with Domiciliary Care Allowance.

You can also get the annual [ Carer's Support Grant
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/carers/carers-support-
grant/) , which is paid automatically in June.

You may qualify for [ Carer's Benefit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/carers/carers-benefit/)
or [ Carer's Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/carers/carers-allowance/) if you meet the criteria for these payments.
