##  How to apply for a driver theory test

You can [ apply and pay for your driver theory test online
](http://www.theorytest.ie/driver-theory-test/schedule-test/) . Applying
online is the quickest way to book a theory test. The [ application form (pdf)
](https://theorytest.ie/wp-content/uploads/theory-test-ireland-dtt-
application-form-cars-bikes-110918.pdf) is also available from the testing
service and NDLS centres. You can also [ book a test by phone or by post
](https://theorytest.ie/general-information/contact-us/) .

When your application has been received, you will receive notification of the
date, location, and time of your test in advance.

It is possible to [ re-schedule or cancel a theory test online
](http://www.theorytest.ie/driver-theory-test/reschedule-or-cancel-your-test/)
or you can [ contact the call centre ](https://theorytest.ie/general-
information/contact-us/) to do so. If you do not arrive at the Test Centre in
time for your test appointment or cancel your test in less than 5 business
days ( including 3 full business days) before your test date, you will lose
your test fee.
