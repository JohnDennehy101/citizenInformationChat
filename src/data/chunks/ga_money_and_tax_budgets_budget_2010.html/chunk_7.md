###  Older people

There will be no change to the maximum rate of the following pensions (from
January 2010):

  * State Pension (Contributory) 
  * State Pension (Transition) 
  * State Pension (Non-Contributory) 
  * Invalidity Pension (for people who are 65) 
  * Widow’s/Widower’s (Contributory) Pension (over 66) 
  * Death Benefit Pension (over 66) 
  * Deserted Wife’s Benefit (over 66) 
  * Carer's Allowance (over 66) 

There will be no change to the Increase for a Qualified Adult for these
payments.
