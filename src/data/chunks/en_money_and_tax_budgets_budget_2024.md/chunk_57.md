###  **Help-to-Buy Scheme**

The [ Help to Buy Scheme (HTB)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/owning-a-home/help-with-
buying-a-home/help-to-buy-scheme/) is extended to the end of 2025.

All applicants buying a home using the [ Local Authority Affordable Purchase
Scheme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/owning-a-home/help-
with-buying-a-home/local-authority-affordable-purchase-scheme/) will now
qualify for HTB. This will come into effect from 11 October 2023.
