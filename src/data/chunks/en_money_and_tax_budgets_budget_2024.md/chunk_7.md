###  Social welfare weekly payments

The maximum rate of all weekly social welfare payments will increase by €12
with proportional increases for qualified adults and people on reduced rates
of payment (January 2024).

The weekly rate for a qualified child will increase by €4. It will increase
from €42 to €46, for children under 12 years of age. It will increase from €50
to €54 for children aged 12 years and over (January 2024).
