###  **Once-off payments in winter 2023**

  * Once-off payment of €300 to people getting [ Fuel Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/extra-social-welfare-benefits/fuel-allowance/) in the week beginning 20 November 2023 
  * Once-off payment of €400 to people getting the [ Working Family Payment ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/families-and-children/working-family-payment/) on 23 November 2023 
  * Double payment of [ Child Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/families-and-children/child-benefit/) for each child on 5 December 2023 
  * Once-off payment of €100 to people who get an [ increase for a qualified child ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/claiming-and-increase-in-your-payment-for-a-child-dependant/) \- you will get one payment per qualified child in the week beginning 27 November 2023 (you may qualify for this payment if you are getting [ Illness Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/illness-benefit/) for a year and have an IQC) 
  * Once-off payment of €200 to people getting the [ Living Alone Increase ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/extra-social-welfare-benefits/living-alone-allowance/) between 29 November 2023 and 1 December 2023 (you will get the once-off payment on the same day you get your primary payment) 
  * Once-off payment of €400 to people getting [ Disability Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/disability-and-illness/disability-allowance/) , [ Invalidity Pension ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/disability-and-illness/invalidity-pension/) and [ Blind Pension ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/disability-and-illness/blind-persons-pension/) between 22 November 2023 and 24 November 2023 (you will get the once-off payment on the same day you get your primary payment) 
  * Once-off payment of €400 to people getting the [ Carer’s Support Grant ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/carers/carers-support-grant/) or [ Domiciliary Care Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/disability-and-illness/domiciliary-care-allowance/) on 30 November 2023 

If you get Disability Allowance, Invalidity Pension, Blind Pension, Carer's
Support Grant or Domiciliary Care Allowance, you'll get one payment of €400,
even if you qualify for more than one of these payments or you are taking care
of more than one person.
