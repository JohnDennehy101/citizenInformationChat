###  **Electric vehicles**

The **VRT relief for battery electric vehicles** (BEVs) with a value of up to
€50,000 is extended to 2025.

The current **benefit in kind deduction for electric vehicles** of €35,000 has
been extended until the end of 2025. This will be reduced to €20,000 in 2026
and €10,000 in 2027.
