###  Income tax

Income tax rates will stay the same (at 20% and 40%), but there will be
increases to tax credits and changes to the income tax bands in 2024.

The [ standard rate tax band ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-
and-tax/tax/income-tax/how-your-tax-is-calculated/#354b71) (the amount you can
earn before you start to pay the higher rate of tax) will be increased by
€2,000 to €42,000 for a single person, with proportionate increases for
married couples and civil partners.

The following tax credits will increase by €100 to €1,875:

  * Personal Tax Credit 
  * Employee Tax Credit 
  * Earned Income Tax Credit 

The [ Home Carer Tax Credit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-
tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/home-carers-tax-credit/) will increase
by €100 to €1,800.

The [ Single Person Child Carer Credit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax-credits-
and-reliefs/single-person-child-carer-tax-credit/) will increase by €100 to
€1,750.

The [ Incapacitated Child Tax Credit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/tax-credits-and-
reliefs-for-people-with-disabilities/incapacitated-child-tax-credit/) will
increase by €200 to €3,500.
