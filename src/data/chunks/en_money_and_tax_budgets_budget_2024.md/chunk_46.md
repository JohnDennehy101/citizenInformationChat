###  **Foster Care Allowance**

In January 2024, the statutory [ Foster Care Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/adoption-
and-fostering/fostering/#l078f5) rate will increase by €25 per week. In
November 2024, the Foster Care Allowance for children under 12 will increase
to €400 per week, and the Foster Care Allowance for children aged 12 and over
will increase to €425 per week.

A once-off double payment of the Foster Care Allowance will also be made this
year (2023).
