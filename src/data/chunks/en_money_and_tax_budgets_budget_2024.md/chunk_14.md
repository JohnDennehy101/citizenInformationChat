###  Employment programmes

People on [ Community Employment (CE)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-
redundancy/employment-support-schemes/community-employment-scheme/) , [ Tús
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-
redundancy/employment-support-schemes/tus/) , [ Rural Social Scheme
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/farming-and-fishing/rural-social-scheme/) or the Work Placement
Experience Programme will get an increase of €12 per week from January 2024.
