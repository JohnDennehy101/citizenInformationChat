###  **Supports for unemployed people**

[ Community Employment (CE)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-
redundancy/employment-support-schemes/community-employment-scheme/) , [ Tús
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-
redundancy/employment-support-schemes/tus/) and [ Work Placement Experience
Programme (WPEP)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/unemployment-and-
redundancy/employment-support-schemes/work-placement-experience-programme/)
weekly payments will increase by €12 from 1 January 2024.

A new **Pay-Related Jobseeker’s Benefit Scheme** is expected to be introduced
in December 2024. Under the scheme, a person can get a jobseeker’s payment
rate linked to their previous employment income.
