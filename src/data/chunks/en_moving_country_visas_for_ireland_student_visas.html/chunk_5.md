###  Student visa/permission renewals

You can renew your permission with the [ local immigration officer
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/registering-your-immigration-permission/how-
to-renew-your-current-permission/) for the district in which you live. If you
live in Dublin, Cork, Kildare, Limerick, Meath or Wicklow you can [ renew your
student permission online ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/registering-your-
immigration-permission/how-to-renew-your-current-permission/registration-
renewal-dublin-kildare-meath-or-wicklow/) . Your Irish Residence Permit will
be sent to you by post.

If your registration is refused and you believe that you have exceptional
circumstances, you can [ apply for an extension of your student permission by
post ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/introduction-of-application-form-for-
extension-of-student-permission/) only.

**Summer 2024: If you are waiting for a course to start**

English Language students who have successfully completed a second English
language course on or after 1 July 2024, or a third English language course,
and have enrolled for a Higher Education Programme (HEP) which starts by the
end of October 2024, may apply for a [ short term Stamp 2 immigration
permission ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/english-language-students-
stamp-2-bridging-permission-2024-update/) to stay in Ireland until 30
September 2024.

You must renew your immigration permission before your short-term permission
ends on 30 September 2024.
