####  **Income related to families**

  * [ Child Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/child-benefit/) or corresponding payment from an EU member state 
  * Foster care payments from the Health Service Executive 
  * Payments for accommodating children under the Child Care Act 
  * Child maintenance (other maintenance is taken into account) 
  * [ Guardian's Payment (Contributory) and Guardian's Payment (Non-Contributory) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/guardian-and-orphan-payments/)
  * [ Back to Work Family Dividend ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/back-to-work-family-dividend/)
