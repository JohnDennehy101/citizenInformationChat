##  How much is State Pension (Non-Contributory)?

**Weekly** **State Pension (Non-Contributory) rate 2024**

**Your age** |  **Personal rate (maximum)** |  **Increase for an adult dependant under 66** |  **Increase for a child dependant**  
---|---|---|---  
66 and under 80  |  €266  |  €175.70  |  Child under 12: €46 (full rate), €23 (half rate)  Child aged 12 and over: €54 (full rate), €27 (half rate)   
80 and over  |  €276   
  
Your first €30 per week of means does not affect your rate of State Pension
(Non-Contributory). But after €30, your pension is reduced by €2.50 for every
€2.50 of means.

You can check your rate of State Pension (Non-Contributory) based on your
means using [ the SW19 (Rates of Payment) booklet
](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/1af6ca-rates-of-payment-sw19/) .
