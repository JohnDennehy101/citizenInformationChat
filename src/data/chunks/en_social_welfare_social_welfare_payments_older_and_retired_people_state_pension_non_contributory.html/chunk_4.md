##  Means test for a State Pension (Non-Contributory)

The State Pension (Non-Contributory) is a means-tested payment.

In a means test, the Department of Social Protection (DSP) looks at all your
sources of income. They also look at your spouse, civil partner, or
cohabitantâ€™s income.

To get a State Pension (Non-Contributory), your combined weekly income must be
below a certain amount.

The main items included in the means test are cash income and capital.
