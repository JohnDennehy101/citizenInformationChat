##  Borrowing money

When you return home, you may need to [ borrow money ](/en/money-and-
tax/personal-finance/loans-and-credit/borrowing-money-in-ireland/) . Before
you do, make sure you are dealing with a lender that is [ authorised by the
Central Bank ](http://registers.centralbank.ie/) .

If you plan on buying a home in Ireland, you should read about [ taking out a
mortgage ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/help-with-buying-a-home/taking-out-a-
mortgage/) and all the [ steps involved ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/buying-a-
home/steps-involved-buying-a-home/) .

Whether you’re applying for a loan or a mortgage, your [ credit history
](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/loans-and-credit/your-credit-history/)
will be taken into consideration by the lender. If you’re returning to Ireland
from abroad, make sure to bring all relevant financial documentation home with
you.
