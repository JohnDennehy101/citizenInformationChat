##  Social welfare

If you need financial support when you return to Ireland, you can apply for a
[ social welfare payment ](/en/returning-to-ireland/applying-for-social-
welfare/accessing-social-welfare-as-a-returning-Irish-emigrant/) . You need to
be [ habitually resident ](/en/returning-to-ireland/applying-for-social-
welfare/habitual-residence-condition/) to be eligible for some social welfare
payments. You may also need to satisfy a means test.

If you have children, you may be able to get [ Child Benefit ](/en/social-
welfare/families-and-children/child-benefit/) .
