###  Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) Scheme

The [ Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) Scheme
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/pre-school-education-and-
childcare/early-childhood-care-and-education-scheme/) provides free early care
and education for children of pre-school age. In general, this is for 3 hours
per day, 5 days a week, over a certain number of weeks for children enrolled
in participating playschools.

The ECCE scheme is administered by the [ Department of Children, Equality,
Disability, Integration and Youth (DCEDIY)
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/2459ee-early-childhood-care-and-education-
programme-ecce/) .
