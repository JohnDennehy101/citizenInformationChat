###  Adult diet supplement

The rate for a single person and lone parent is calculated as follows: The
diet cost as set by the Department of Social Protection, less one-third of
your social welfare payment at the **2007** rate = the diet supplement to be
paid to you. [ You can check the rates for 2007 in SW19 (pdf).
](https://assets.gov.ie/11117/6beb1ad2f51346f4ad6f27db1c473e59.pdf)
