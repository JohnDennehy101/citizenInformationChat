###  Ábhar Gaolmhar

  * [ _Oideachas, deontais agus tacaíochtaí feirmeoireachta_ ](/ga/environment/land/oideachas-deontais-agus-tacaiochtai-feirmeoireachta/)

Tá roinnt roghanna éagsúla ar fáil duit más mian leat oideachas a fháil san
fheirmeoireacht nó má tá tú ag iarraidh breisoiliúint a fháil i réimse ar
leith. Lena chois sin, tá roinnt deontas ar fáil chun cabhrú le costais na
gcúrsaí oideachais a chlúdach.

  * [ _Work and Access - In-Work Support Grant_ ](/ga/employment/employment-and-disability/work-and-access-in-work-support-grant/)

The In-Work Support Grantis part of the new Work and Access employment
programme, designed to help people with disabilities to find or keep a job by
removing or reducing workplace barriers.
