##  How much is Back to School Clothing and Footwear Allowance?

See the table below for the current rates of Back to School Clothing and
Footwear Allowance (BSCFA):

**Rates of BSCFA 2024** **Child’s age on 30 September 2024** |  **Rate of payment**  
---|---  
Age 4-11  |  €160   
Age 12-22  |  €285*   
  
*Children aged between 18-22 must be returning to full-time second-level education in a recognised school in the autumn of 2024.
