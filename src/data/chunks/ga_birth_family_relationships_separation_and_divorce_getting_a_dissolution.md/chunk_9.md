  * [ _Páirtnéireacht shibhialta agus oidhreachtúlacht_ ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/civil-partnerships/civil-partnership-and-inheritance/)

Cuireann sé síos ar na saincheisteanna i ndáil le hoidhreachtúlacht i
bpáirtnéireachtaí sibhialta.

  * [ _How pensions are assessed during a separation, divorce and dissolution_ ](/ga/birth-family-relationships/separation-and-divorce/sep-divorce-pensions/)

When you end a relationship by judicial separation, divorce or dissolution,
the court can decide to share your pension with your former partner and (or)
any dependent children. This is called a pension adjustment order.
