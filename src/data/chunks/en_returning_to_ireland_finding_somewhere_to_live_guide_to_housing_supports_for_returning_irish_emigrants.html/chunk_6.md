####  **Your eligibility**

Your income must be under a certain amount to qualify for social housing.
Currently, this ranges from €30,000 to €40,000, depending on where you want to
live.

You must also show that you do not have suitable alternative accommodation,
such as:

  * A property in another area or country 
  * Accommodation belonging to a family member that you could reasonably be expected to live in
