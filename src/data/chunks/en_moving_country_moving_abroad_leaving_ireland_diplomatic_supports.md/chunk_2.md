##  Introduction

The Department of Foreign Affairs offers [ consular assistance
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/overseas-travel/assistance-abroad/consular-
assistance-charter/) to Irish citizens abroad. If you need help while you are
abroad, you can contact the [ Irish embassy or consulate
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/embassies/) in that country.

If you are in a country where there is no embassy or consulate, you can
contact embassies or consulates of other European Union countries for
assistance.
