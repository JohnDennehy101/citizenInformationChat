##  Ag tabhairt aire do leanbh faoi mhíchumas

Tá leanaí faoi mhíchumas i dteideal na seirbhísí agus na sochar teaghlaigh
céanna le gach leanbh eile. Tá tacaíochtaí agus seirbhísí breise ar fáil do
thuismitheoirí agus cúramóirí leanaí faoi mhíchumas. Bíonn feidhm acu cibé acu
ar rugadh an leanbh faoi mhíchumas nó ar éirigh leo é a fháil níos déanaí.

I measc na seirbhísí cúraim phobail tá:

  * [ Altraí sláinte poiblí ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-community/public-health-nurses/)
  * [ Oibrithe sóisialta ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-community/social-work-services/)
  * [ Teiripeoirí saothair ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-community/occupational-therapy-services/)
  * [ Seirbhísí cosliachta ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-community/chiropody-services/)
  * [ Seirbhísí teiripe urlabhra agus teanga ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/care-in-your-community/speech-and-language-therapy-services/)
  * [ Cúram faoisimh ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/health-services-for-people-with-disabilities/respite-care/)
