##  Sláinte do leanaí

Cuirtear roinnt seirbhísí sláinte leanaí ar fáil saor in aisce. Go ginearálta,
tá na seirbhísí seo mar chuid de [ sheirbhísí máithreachais agus leasa naíonán
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-
services/reproductive_health/maternity-and-infant-welfare-services/) , [
seirbhísí sláinte do leanaí réamhscoile
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/children-s-
health/health-services-for-preschool-children/) agus [ seirbhísí sláinte
scoile ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-
services/children-s-health/school-health-services/) .

Tá leanaí in Éirinn i dteideal [ seirbhísí vacsaínithe agus imdhíonta
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/children-s-
health/vaccinations-for-children/) saor in aisce freisin.

Ní gá duit [ táillí ospidéil
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/children-s-
health/child-health-services/#73349e) a íoc in aon ospidéal poiblí má tá do
leanbh faoi 16 bliana d’aois.

Tá gach leanbh faoi 8 mbliana d'aois i dteideal cuairteanna DG saor in aisce
le [ cárta cuairte DG ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-
cards-and-gp-visit-cards/gp-visit-cards-for-under-8s/) . Clúdaíonn an cárta
cuairte DG do leanaí faoi 8 mbliana measúnuithe sonracha ag aois 2 agus 5
freisin. Clúdaíonn sé freisin cúram do leanaí a bhfuil asma orthu.

Má tá d’ioncam faoi leibhéal áirithe, féadfaidh tú féin agus do leanaí a
bheith i dteideal [ cárta leighis
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-
cards/medical-card/) freisin.

Léigh tuilleadh faoi [ sheirbhísí sláinte do leanaí in Éirinn
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/children-s-
health/child-health-services/) .
