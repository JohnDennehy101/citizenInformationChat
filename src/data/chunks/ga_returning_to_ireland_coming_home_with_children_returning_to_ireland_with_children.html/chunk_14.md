###  **Tacaíocht airgeadais**

Má tá tú ag tabhairt aire do leanbh faoi mhíchumas d’fhéadfá cáiliú le
haghaidh tacaíochta airgeadais. Mar shampla, d’fhéadfá a bheith incháilithe le
haghaidh:

  * [ Sochar Cúramóra ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/carers/carers-benefit/)
  * [ Liúntas Cúramóra ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/carers/carers-allowance/)
  * [ Deontas Tacaíochta do Chúramóirí ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/carers/carers-support-grant/)
  * [ Liúntas Cúraim Baile ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/disability-and-illness/domiciliary-care-allowance/)

Chun cáiliú le haghaidh Liúntas Cúramóra nó Liúntas Cúraim Baile, ní mór duit
an [ ‘coinníoll gnáthchónaithe’
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/applying-for-
social-welfare/habitual-residence-condition/) a shásamh.

Léigh tuilleadh faoi [ chúram a thabhairt do leanbh faoi mhíchumas
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/children-s-
health/caring-for-a-child-with-a-disability/) .
