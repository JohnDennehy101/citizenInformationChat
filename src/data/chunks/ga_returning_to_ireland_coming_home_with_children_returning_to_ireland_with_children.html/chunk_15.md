##  Tacaíochtaí do dhaoine a thugann tuismitheoir aonair dóibh

Tá tacaíochtaí ar fáil [ do dhaoine a bhíonn ag tuismitheoireacht leo féin
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/parenting-
alone/) . Ina measc seo tá an [ Creidmheas Cúramóra Linbh do Dhuine Singil
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax-credits-
and-reliefs/single-person-child-carer-tax-credit/) (CCCLDS) agus an [
Íocaíocht Teaghlach Aontuismitheora
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-
payments/families-and-children/one-parent-family-payment/) (ÍTA) do dhaoine ar
ioncam íseal.

Mura bhfuil tú pósta nó má tá tú i do thuismitheoir aonair, b’fhéidir gur
mhaith leat eolas a fháil faoi [ chaomhnóireacht
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/cohabiting-
couples/custody-of-children-cohabiting-couples/) , [ rochtain
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/cohabiting-
couples/access-to-children-cohabiting-couples/) , [ oidhreacht
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/cohabiting-
couples/inheritance-rights-cohabiting-couples/) agus [ cothabháil
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/cohabiting-
couples/inheritance-rights-cohabiting-couples/) . B’fhéidir gur mhaith leat
léamh faoi [ chaomhnóireacht dhlíthiúil
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/birth-family-relationships/cohabiting-
couples/guardianship-and-cohabiting-couples/) freisin.
