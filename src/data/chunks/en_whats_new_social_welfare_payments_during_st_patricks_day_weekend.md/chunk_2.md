#####  News

  

Are you expecting a  social welfare payment  on Monday, 18 March 2024?

Monday, 18 March is a [ public holiday ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-
conditions/leave-and-holidays/public-holidays/) for St Patrick’s Day. If you
are due to get a payment on Monday, you will be paid on Friday, 15 March 2024
instead.

Payments will be paid as normal on Tuesday, 19 March 2024.

All Intreo centres and local branch offices will be closed on Bank Holiday
Monday, 18 March 2024. [ Intreo centres and local branch offices
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) will reopen
on Tuesday, 19 March 2024.

Check gov.ie for more updates [ about St Patrick’s Day bank holiday payments
](https://www.gov.ie/en/news/67e0b4-customer-notices/#latest-updates) .

Find out [ how social welfare payments are paid
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/how-social-welfare-payments-are-
paid/) .
