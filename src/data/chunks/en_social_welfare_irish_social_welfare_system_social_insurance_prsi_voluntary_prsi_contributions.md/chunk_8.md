##  How to apply

To become a [ voluntary contributor you must download and complete application
form VC 1 (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/38579/05744c80b36743c7a5c7e420fa5f7a13.pdf) .

You can also get this form from your [ Intreo centre or social welfare local
office ](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) .
