##  More information

The Health Service Executive (HSE) website has a wide range of [ information
and resources for anyone planning to breastfeed ](https://www2.hse.ie/babies-
and-toddlers/breastfeeding/) . It has advice, tips and information for parents
interested in breastfeeding and details of [ support groups
](https://www2.hse.ie/services/breastfeeding-support-search/) throughout the
country.
