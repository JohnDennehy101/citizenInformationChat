###  Public servants

Comhlámh also administers the Public Service Pension Scheme for Volunteer
Development Workers on behalf of Irish Aid. This scheme allows public servants
to get their pension contributions paid while serving overseas for six
consecutive months or more and up to a maximum of two years. You can contact
Comhlámh for [ more information about the scheme for public sector workers
](https://comhlamh.org/social-services-and-pensions/) .
