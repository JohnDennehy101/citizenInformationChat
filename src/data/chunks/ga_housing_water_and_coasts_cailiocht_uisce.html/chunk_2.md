##  Réamhrá

Tugtar ‘uisce óil’ ar uisce a chuirtear ar fáil duit – ól, ullmhú bia, oighir
a dhéanamh, deochanna a dhéanamh le huisce agus fiacla a scuabadh.

Is féidir leat uisce óil a fháil ó dhá fhoinse éagsúla. De ghnáth ní mór uisce
ó lochanna, aibhneacha agus sruthanna (uisce dromchla) a chóireáil chun é a
dhéanamh sábháilte le hól. D’fhéadfadh go mbeadh gá le huisce ó spriongaí agus
ó thollphoill (screamhuisce), ag brath ar cháilíocht a fhoinse.

You can read information from the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) [ on
drinking water quality ](http://www.epa.ie/water/dw/) and the Health Service
Executive (HSE) also has [ information about drinking water and health issues
](http://www.hse.ie/eng/health/hl/water/drinkingwater/) . [ Uisce Éireann
](https://www.water.ie/) has published [ FAQs on water quality
](https://www.water.ie/help/water-quality/faqs/) .

Is féidir leat faisnéis a fháil ó Údarás Sábháilteachta Bia na hÉireann ar [
uisce i mbuidéil ](https://www.fsai.ie/enforcement-and-
legislation/legislation/food-legislation/water/bottled-waters) . Foilsíonn an
GCC [ faisnéis ar uisce snámha. ](https://www.fsai.ie/enforcement-and-
legislation/legislation/food-legislation/water/bottled-waters)
