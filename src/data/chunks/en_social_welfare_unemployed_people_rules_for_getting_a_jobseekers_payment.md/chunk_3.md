##  Rules for getting a jobseeker's payment

To qualify for a jobseeker’s payment, you must be 18 years or over and under
66 years of age.

You must meet certain PRSI conditions to get [ Jobseeker's Benefit (JB)
](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-benefit/) . You must pass a
means test and habitual residency conditions for [ Jobseeker's Allowance (JA)
](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-allowance/) .

For JA and JB, you must also be:

  * Unemployed (you must be fully unemployed or unemployed for at least 4 days out of 7) 
  * Capable of work 
  * Available for work 
  * Genuinely seeking work 

While you are getting a jobseeker’s payment, you must attend meetings and take
part in appropriate employment schemes, training, or work experience if
requested by the Department of Social Protection (DSP). See ‘Sanctions for not
meeting the jobseeker’s payment rules’ below.
