##  More information

You can apply for Jobseeker's Benefit or Jobseeker's Allowance online, or
through [ your local Intreo Centre or Social Welfare Branch Office
](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) .

Visit our pages on [ Jobseeker's Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-
people/jobseekers-benefit/) or [ Jobseeker's Allowance ](/en/social-
welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-allowance/) for more information on how
to apply.
