###  Medical card

If your income is below a certain level, you might be eligible for a [ medical
card ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-
visit-cards/medical-card/) . With a medical card, you can get [ certain health
services (including GP appointments)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-services/gp-and-
hospital-services/gp-services-to-medical-card-holders/#cbc6db) free of charge.

The HSE will do a ‘means test’ to check your income, assets and savings.

Some people can [ qualify for a medical card without a means test
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-
cards/medical-card/#552e17) , including people with [ an EU entitlement
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-system/entitlement-to-
public-health-services/#18c223) .
