##  Further information

You can find out more in our pages on [ safety advice for motorcyclists
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/buying-
or-selling-a-vehicle/owning-a-motorcycle-in-ireland/#l864e0) , [ seat belts
and the law ](/en/travel-and-recreation/roads-and-safety/seatbelts-when-
motoring-in-ireland/) and the national speed limits in Ireland. You can also [
find road safety information for motorists, pedestrians and cyclists on the
website of An Garda Síochána ](https://www.garda.ie/en/roads-policing/road-
safety/) . The [ Road Safety Authority (RSA) ](https://www.rsa.ie/en/) is
another resource.
