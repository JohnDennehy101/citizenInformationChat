###  **If you transfer from Jobseeker's Allowance**

If you transfer from a long-term [ Jobseeker's Allowance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/unemployed-
people/jobseekers-allowance/) (paid for at least 390 days) at the maximum rate
to Illness Benefit, you can get the maximum rate of Illness Benefit. Your
Jobseeker's Allowance and Illness Benefit claim cannot be separated by more
than 3 days and you must have a minimum of 260 qualifying contributions.
