#  Cineálacha Fostaíochta

  * [ Féinfhostaíocht ](/ga/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/)
  * [ Cineálacha difriúla fostaíochta ](/ga/employment/types-of-employment/different-types-of-workers/)

Tá cur síos anseo ar chineálacha difriúla fostaíochta agus ar an éifeacht atá
acu ar do chearta fostaíochta

  * [ Cearta fostaíochta oibrithe tí ](/ga/employment/types-of-employment/domestic-workers-employment-rights/)

Cad is oibrí tí ann? Sracthuairisc ar chearta fostaíochta daoine a oibríonn i
dtithe príobháideacha.

  * [ Oibrithe gníomhaireachta ](/ga/employment/types-of-employment/agency-workers/)

Tá cearta ag oibrithe gníomhaireacht faoi reachtaíocht fostaíochta áirithe.
Faigh amach tuilleadh fúthu seo agus faoin Acht um Chosaint Fostaithe (Obair
Ghníomhaireachta Shealadach) 2012.

  * [ Cearta oibrithe páirtaimseartha ](/ga/employment/types-of-employment/rights-of-part-time-workers/)

Tá cearta fostaíochta áirithe ag oibrithe páirtaimseartha faoin tAcht um
Chosaint Fostaithe (Obair Pháirtaimseartha) 2001.

  * [ Do stádas fostaíochta a thuiscint ](/ga/employment/types-of-employment/understanding-your-employment-status/)

Usually it is clear whether you are employed or self-employed. If it is not
obvious, this page will help you understand the important differences and
where to go for help in getting a determination on your employment status.
