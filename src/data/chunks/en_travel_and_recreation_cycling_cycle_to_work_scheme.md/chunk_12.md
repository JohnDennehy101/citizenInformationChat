###  **Step 1: Check with the employer**

Ask your employer if they run the scheme and check their requirements. They
may allow you to select the bike and equipment from any shop or only certain
bikes from specific shops.

If you are a civil or public servant, the cycle shop must be on the [
suppliers' list (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/205289/f971c984-969b-41f3-9fc5-d2e6f4a6b141.pdf) .
