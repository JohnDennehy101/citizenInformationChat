#####  News

If your dog is over 4 months old, you need a dog licence.

You can get:

🐶 An individual licence for 1 dog for 1 year

🐶 A 'lifetime of dog' licence, for your dog’s lifetime

🐶 A general licence for 1 year covering multiple dogs at one location

Find out more about 👉 [ dog licences and ownership
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/environment/pets-and-wildlife/control-
of-dogs/#le8d37)
