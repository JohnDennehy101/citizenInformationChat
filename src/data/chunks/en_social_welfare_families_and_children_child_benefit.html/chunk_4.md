###  **Rules to get Child Benefit**

To get Child Benefit, you must be living in Ireland and meet the [ Habitual
Residence Condition (HRC) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-assistance-payments/habitual-
residence-condition/) .

Your child must be under 16, or:

  * Aged 16, 17 or 18 and in full-time education or training 
  * Aged 16, 17 or 18, have a disability, and be unable to support themselves. 

You cannot claim Child Benefit for a child in [ a detention facility
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/justice/children-and-young-
offenders/detention-of-children/) .
