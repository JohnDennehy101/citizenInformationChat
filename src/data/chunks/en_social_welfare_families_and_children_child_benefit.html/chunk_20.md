####  **Child born abroad, or not registered within 3 months**

If your child was not born in Ireland, or if you did not register their birth
within 3 months, you must complete [ Child Benefit form CB1 (pdf)
](https://assets.gov.ie/27239/42f7099b15a3431b9ef0a46e23948ae9.pdf) and send
it to the Child Benefit Section. See contact details under ‘More information’
below.
