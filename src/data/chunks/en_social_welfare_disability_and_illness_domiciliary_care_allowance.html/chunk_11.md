###  **Hospital stays**

You can continue getting DCA for **up to 26 weeks, in a period of** **12
months** , if your child is in hospital getting medical treatment (or other
treatment) of a temporary nature.

If your baby stays in hospital for medical treatment immediately after their
birth, you can get DCA for **up to 18 months** while they are in hospital.
This applies to babies born on or after 1 January 2023 only.
