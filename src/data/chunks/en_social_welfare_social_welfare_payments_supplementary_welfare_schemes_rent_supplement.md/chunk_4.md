##  Domestic violence and Rent Supplement

If you are a victim of domestic violence and have been referred from [ Tusla-
funded services ](https://www.tusla.ie/services/domestic-sexual-gender-based-
violence/) , you can get immediate [ access to Rent Supplement
](https://www.gov.ie/en/press-release/963f3-minister-humphreys-announces-
access-to-rent-supplement-for-victims-of-domestic-violence-to-become-
permanent/) . The means test for Rent Supplement will not apply for 3 months.

Rent Supplement can be extended for a further 3 months, but the means test
will apply.

After this 6 month period, if you have a long-term housing need, you can apply
to your local housing authority for social housing supports and access the [
Housing Assistance Payment (HAP)
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/renting-a-home/help-with-
renting/housing-assistance-payment/) , if eligible.
