##  Introduction

The [ Social Welfare Scheme for Professional Artists on Jobseeker’s Allowance
(JA) ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/b5474f-professional-artists-on-
jobseekers-allowance/) allows professional artists who are getting JA to focus
on their artistic work and to develop their portfolio for the first year that
they are out of work.

Under the Scheme, professional artists getting Jobseeker’s Allowance can be
exempt from [ labour market activation ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-
people/employment-supports/) for the first year that they are out of work.

Art forms covered under the Scheme include visual arts, theatre, literature,
music, dance, opera, film and circus. The Department of Social Protection
provides a [ full list of professional art forms covered by the Scheme (pdf)
](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/27311/cbcb43e3c0144fa8bb145fe997f2324b.pdf#page=1)
.
