#####  News

  

For the years following your marriage or civil partnership, there are three
options for taxation:

  * Assessment as a single person (you are both still taxed as single people) 
  * Separate assessment 
  * Joint assessment 

The deadline to [ request separate assessment
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/life-events-and-personal-circumstances/marital-
status/marriage-and-civil-partnerships/separate-assessment.aspx) or to [
nominate an assessable spouse ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/life-events-and-
personal-circumstances/marital-status/marriage-and-civil-partnerships/joint-
assessment.aspx) or civil partner for 2024 is 31 March 2024.

Read more about the [ taxation of married people and civil partners
](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/taxation-of-married-people/) .
