###  **Jobseeker’s Benefit**

You do not pay tax on the first €13 per week of [ Jobseeker's Benefit
](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-benefit/) and [ Jobseeker's
Benefit (Self-Employed) ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-
benefit-self-employed/) . If you are a systematic short-time worker, you do
not pay tax on Jobseeker's Benefit.
