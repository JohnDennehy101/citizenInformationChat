##  Introduction

The Back to Education Programme can support you to take part in educational
and personal development courses that will help you to access employment.
There are 3 different options under the Back to Education Programme:

  1. Back to Education Allowance (which includes the Second-Level Option, the Third-Level Option and the Post-Graduate Option) 
  2. Part-Time Education Option 
  3. Education, Training and Development Option
