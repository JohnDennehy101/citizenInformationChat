###  Your family

Your EU, EEA, UK or Swiss citizen family members can reside with you in
Ireland.

If you have [ family members ](/en/moving-country/moving-to-ireland/rights-of-
residence-in-ireland/residence-rights-eu-national/) that are from outside the
EU, EEA, UK or Switzerland, they must [ apply for residence
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/coming-to-join-family-in-ireland/joining-an-
eea-or-swiss-national/eu-treaty-rights/) . If they are from a country whose
citizens need a [ visa to enter Ireland ](/en/moving-country/visas-for-
ireland/visa-requirements-for-entering-ireland/) , they must apply for a visa
before travelling to Ireland. If the family member has a residence card issued
by another EEA country under EU law, they can enter Ireland without a visa.
