##  Rates

You need a state licence to fish for salmon or sea trout. You can buy a
licence for a period of one day or up to a year. Information on licences and
their cost is available on the Inland Fisheries Ireland (IFI) angling website,
where you can also [ purchase the licence online
](https://store.fishinginireland.info/) .

Having a licence to fish for salmon and sea trout does not entitle you to
fish. You may also need to get a fishing permit or permission from the
fisheries owner. In general, a fishing permit costs from €36 to €50 a day.
Some of the more exclusive fisheries can be more expensive.
