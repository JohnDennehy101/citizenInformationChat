###  **How to claim tax relief**

You must complete a ‘ [ Transfer of Residence C and E 1076' form (pdf)
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/customs/individuals/customs/c-and-e-1076.pdf) to
claim the relief. See ‘ [ What documents do I need?
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/planning-your-
journey-home/shipping-your-belongings-back-to-ireland#le2327) ’ below for more
information.

You can visit Revenue’s website for more information on [ relief from Customs
Duty and VAT ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/customs/individuals/transfer-
residence/rules.aspx) . You can also check if your goods are banned or need a
licence in Revenue’s [ guide on prohibited and restricted items (pdf)
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/customs/documents/prohibitions-restrictions.pdf) .
