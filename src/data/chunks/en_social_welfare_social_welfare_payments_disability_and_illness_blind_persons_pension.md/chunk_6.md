##  Means test for Blind Pension

Blind Pension is a means-tested payment.

In a means test, the Department of Social Protection (DSP) looks at all your
sources of income. They also look at your spouse, civil partner, or
cohabitant’s income.

To qualify for Blind Pension, your weekly means must be €235.10 or less. Your
rate of Blind Pension may be reduced depending on your weekly means (see ‘Rate
of Blind Pension’ below).
