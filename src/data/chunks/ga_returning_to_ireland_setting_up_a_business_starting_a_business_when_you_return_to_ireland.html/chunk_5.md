##  Beithnithe airgeadais

Ní mó duit raon saincheisteanna airgeadais a mheas sula dtosaíonn tú ar ghnó.
Mar shampla, athrófar do fhreagrachtaí cánach. D'fheadfadh go mbeadh deontas
tosaithe uait freisin nó gur mhaith leat fostaithe a fhostú.

Léigh ár bhfaisnéis ar:

  * [ Conas maoiniú a fháil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/starting-a-business/#6c48a3)
  * [ Cad atá le déanamh má tá deacrachtaí creidmheasa agat ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/starting-a-business/#d9e9f0)
  * [ Do cháin mar úinéir gnó a thuiscint ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/understanding-your-tax-as-a-business-owner/)
  * [ Daoine a fhostú i do ghnó ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/employing-people-in-your-business/)
  * [ Árachas nó cead pleanála a fháil ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-employment/self-employment/becoming-self-employed/#54b49d)
