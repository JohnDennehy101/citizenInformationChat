####  Property with a significant level of pyrite damage

There is an exemption for property certified as having a significant level of
pyrite damage. This exemption is no longer available for new applicants since
22 July 2023.

You can read the [ detailed guidelines on LPT exemption for pyrite (pdf)
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/property/documents/lpt/lpt-exemption-pyrite-
damage.pdf) .
