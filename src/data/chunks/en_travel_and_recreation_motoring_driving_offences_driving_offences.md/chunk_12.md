###  Dangerous driving

It is an [ offence to drive dangerously
](https://www.lawreform.ie/_fileupload/RevisedActs/WithAnnotations/HTML/EN_ACT_1961_0024.htm#SEC52)
in a public place. If you are convicted of dangerous driving, you could be
fined up to €5000, or be sent to prison for up to 6 months, or both. If you
are convicted of **dangerous driving causing death or serious bodily harm** ,
you could be fined up to €20,000 and imprisoned for up to 10 years, or both.
