###  **Cash income**

His wife's Carer's Allowance and Child Benefit are not taken into account in
the assessment of means. As she is getting her own social welfare personal
payment (Carer's Allowance), he cannot claim a qualified adult increase for
her.

You can read more about [ cash income and social welfare payments
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/means-test-for-social-welfare-payments/cash-income-not-included-in-the-
social-welfare-means-test/) .
