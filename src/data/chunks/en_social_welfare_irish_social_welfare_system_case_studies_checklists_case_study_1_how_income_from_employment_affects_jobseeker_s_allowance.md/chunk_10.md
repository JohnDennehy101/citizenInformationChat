###  **Weekly means**

His means of €42 is halved because his wife is getting a social welfare
payment.

You can read more about the [ means test
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/means-test-for-social-welfare-payments/means-test/) for social welfare
payments.

**Therefore, his total means is assessed as €21.**
