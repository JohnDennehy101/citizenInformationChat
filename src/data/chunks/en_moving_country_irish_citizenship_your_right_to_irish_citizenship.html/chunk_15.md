###  Children

You can apply on behalf of your child, who was born in Ireland, but who is not
entitled to citizenship by birth if:

  * Your child has lived in Ireland for 3 years before application including a continuous 12 month period before their application 

You can also apply on behalf of your child if you have become a naturalised
Irish citizen and:

  * Your child has resided in Ireland for at least 3 years before their application
