###  Residence for 5 out of the last 9 years

You can apply if you have 5 years' _reckonable residence_ in the 9 year period
ending on the day before your application and:

  * You have resided in Ireland legally for the 12 month period before your application 
  * You are over 18 

Legal residence (or _reckonable residence_ ) does not include time spent on a
student visa or time spent while waiting for a decision on International
Protection.
