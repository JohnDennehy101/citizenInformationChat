##  Penalties for drink driving offences

Penalties on conviction for drink driving vary depending on the amount of
alcohol that has been detected in your system. The court will also take into
account whether this is your first offence or otherwise. Under the penalties
introduced by the Road Traffic Act 2006 all convictions for drink driving
result in a mandatory [ disqualification from driving ](/en/travel-and-
recreation/motoring/driving-offences/being-disqualified-from-driving/) .
