###  Education, Training and Development Option

If you are getting Jobseeker's Allowance or Jobseeker's Benefit, the
Education, Training and Development Option allows you to keep your social
welfare payment and attend a course not covered by the standard Back to
Education Allowance such as a personal development course, a basic education
course (for example, literacy classes) a general training course or specific
job skills. You continue to get your jobseeker's payment at your usual rate.

You must meet certain criteria to qualify for the [ Education, Training and
Development Option ](/en/social-welfare/back-to-education/education-training-
and-development-option/) .
