###  When can candidates put up election posters?

Candidates can put up posters 30 days before the polling date, or from the
date that the ministerial polling day order, whichever is the shortest period.

On polling day, you [ cannot display posters or campaign for a candidate or
party
](https://eur03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.irishstatutebook.ie%2Feli%2F1992%2Fact%2F23%2Fenacted%2Fen%2Fprint%23sec147&data=05%7C02%7CGwen.Gunning%40ciboard.ie%7C84492ea70fa945d6297908dc9f428467%7Cbb4e61ce39a3414abb5d6a0290c95fd9%7C0%7C0%7C638560355239511867%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=6XT9R8vyD1thpsWt1NS19gXV3xn%2BNWGTUvAEwHQ%2B3W4%3D&reserved=0)
within [ 50 metres of a polling station
](https://eur03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.irishstatutebook.ie%2Feli%2F2001%2Fact%2F38%2Fsection%2F33%2Fenacted%2Fen%2Fhtml%23sec33&data=05%7C02%7CGwen.Gunning%40ciboard.ie%7C84492ea70fa945d6297908dc9f428467%7Cbb4e61ce39a3414abb5d6a0290c95fd9%7C0%7C0%7C638560355239517840%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=XuTsfUzS9U5IgtQifnpNvmenSGktyNPCFK3FW9sqYw4%3D&reserved=0)
.

Failure to remove election posters within 7 days after the polling date is an
offence.
