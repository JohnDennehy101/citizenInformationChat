##  Who can complain to the FSPO?

You can complain if you are:

  * A member of a pension scheme, including external and former members 
  * A contributor to a PRSA 
  * A person with an entitlement under a scheme 

If a pension member or contributor has died, you can make a complaint if you
are their:

  * Dependant 
  * Personal representative 
  * Widow, widower or surviving spouse or civil partner
