###  Invalidity Pension and work

You cannot do paid work and get Invalidity Pension.

If you want to return to work or self-employment, you may be able to transfer
to [ Partial Capacity Benefit (PCB)
](/en/social_welfare/social_welfare_payments/disability_and_illness/partial_capacity_benefit.html)
. You will qualify for Partial Capacity Benefit if the restriction on your
capacity for work is assessed as moderate, severe, or profound.
