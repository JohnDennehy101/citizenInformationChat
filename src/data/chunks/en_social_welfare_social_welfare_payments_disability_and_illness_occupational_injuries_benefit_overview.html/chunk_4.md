###  Civil law claims for damages

The Recovery of Certain Benefits and Assistance (RBA) Scheme enables the
Department of Social Protection to recover the **value** of certain illness-
related social welfare payments as a consequence of personal injuries claims.
They are recovered from the compensator and not from the injured person. [
Read more about the scheme. ](http://www.welfare.ie/en/Pages/Recovery-of-
Benefits-and-Assistance.aspx)
