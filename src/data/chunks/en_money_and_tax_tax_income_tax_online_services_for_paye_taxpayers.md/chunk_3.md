##  PAYE services

If you are a PAYE taxpayer, you can access PAYE Services via myAccount. When
you [ register for myAccount ](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-
web/register.html?execution=e2s1) , you can:

  * View your tax record 
  * Claim a range of [ tax credits ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/)
  * Apply for refunds of tax including health expenses 
  * Declare additional income 
  * Request a review of tax liability for previous years 
  * Re-allocate credits between [ yourself and your spouse or civil partner ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/taxation-of-married-people/)
  * Apply for the [ Help to Buy ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/help-with-buying-a-home/help-to-buy-scheme/) scheme 
  * File or pay your [ Local Property Tax ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/housing-taxes-and-reliefs/local-property-tax/)
  * Estimate the cost of Vehicle Registration Tax 
  * Track correspondence submitted to Revenue 
  * View and print your [ Employment Detail Summary ](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/pay-and-employment/employment-detail-summary/)
  * View COVID-19 support payments you got in 2020: [ ERS ](https://www.revenue.ie/en/news/articles/employer-covid-19-refund-scheme.aspx) , [ TWSS ](/en/employment/unemployment-and-redundancy/covid19-temporary-wage-subsidy-scheme/) or [ PUP ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/covid19-pandemic-unemployment-payment/)
  * Review your [ Preliminary End-Of-Year Statement ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/how-to-review-your-paye-tax/)
  * Record and manage receipts for your expenses 

You do not have to submit paper claims when you submit a transaction through
myAccount. However, you must keep receipts for 6 years as Revenue may ask to
view them at a later stage. You can keep an electronic record of your receipts
on myAccount using [ Revenueâ€™s receipts tracker service
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/online-services/services/common/manage-your-
receipts-with-receipts-tracker.aspx) .

When a transaction is submitted using myAccount, it will be immediately
acknowledged and a permanent record of the transaction will be recorded.

PAYE Services are available for the current year and the previous 4 years.
