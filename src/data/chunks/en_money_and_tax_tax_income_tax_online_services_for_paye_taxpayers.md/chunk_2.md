##  Introduction

If you have tax deducted from your income by your employer, you are a PAYE
taxpayer. You can use Revenue's myAccount service to access PAYE Online
Services. You can also register a first job or private pension online.

Revenue's [ myAccount ](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-web/home.html) service is
a single access point for secure online services.

For self-employed people, businesses and tax professionals, Revenue has an
online service called [ ROS (Revenue Online Service) ](https://www.ros.ie/ros-
registration-web/ros-registration) .
