##  Introduction

You can [ renew your driver licence online ](https://ndls.rsa.ie/) . You must
have a [ Public Services Card ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/public-services-card/) and a [ MyGovID
](https://www.mygovid.ie/en-IE/Home/Index) verified account. You renew your
licence in person at an NDLS centre to. You must book an appointment to attend
an NDLS centre.

This page explains how to renew your driving licence. You find out what to do
if your [ licence is lost, stolen, or damaged
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-
licensing/replacing-a-lost-or-stolen-irish-driving-licence/) .

The Road Safety Authority has responsibility for driver licensing. The
National Driver Licence Service (NDLS) manages licence applications and
renewals.
