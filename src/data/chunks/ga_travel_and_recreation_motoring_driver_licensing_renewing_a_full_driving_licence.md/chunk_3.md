##  Rules

You can apply to renew your driving licence if is expiring within 3 months or
it expired less than 10 years ago.

If your licence expired more than 10 years ago, you must complete a [ Driver
Theory Test ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-
recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driver-theory-test/) and then apply for a
learner driver permit.
