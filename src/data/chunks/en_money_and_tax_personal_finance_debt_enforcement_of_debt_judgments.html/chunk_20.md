###  Bankruptcy

If you are unable to pay your debts, you can apply to the High Court to be
declared bankrupt. Alternatively, your creditor may apply to have you declared
bankrupt if certain conditions are met. [ Read more about bankruptcy
](/en/money-and-tax/personal-finance/debt/personal-insolvency/what-is-
bankruptcy/) .
