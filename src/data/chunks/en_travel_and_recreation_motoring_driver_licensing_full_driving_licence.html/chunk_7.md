##  How to apply for a driving licence

There are 2 ways that you can apply for your driving licence.

  1. Online at [ NDLS.ie ](https://www.ndls.ie/index.php) , or 
  2. In person by [ booking an appointment ](https://www.ndls.ie/booking-service.html) at an [ NDLS centre ](https://www.ndls.ie/how-to-apply/applying-in-person.html#centre-locations) .
