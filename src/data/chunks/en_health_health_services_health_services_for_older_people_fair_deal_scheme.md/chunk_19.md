####  How to apply **to keep income from renting your home**

Step1: Fill out the [ Principal Private Residence Rental Income application
form (pdf)
](https://assets.hse.ie/media/documents/HSE_Principal_residence_rental_income_application_form.pdf)
.

Step 2: Send copies of the following to [ your local Nursing Homes Support
Office ](https://www2.hse.ie/services/schemes-allowances/fair-deal-
scheme/contact/) :

  * Residential Tenancies Board (RTB) registration approval letter 
  * Rental agreement showing the rental amount 
  * Latest notice of assessment from Revenue 

The tenancy in your principal residence must be registered with the RTB unless
it is a [ type of rental agreement or rented property that is exempt from
having to register with the RTB
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/renting-a-home/landlords-
rights-and-responsibilities/registering-a-tenancy/#77c7cc) .

If you have not registered with the RTB, you may still qualify for the reduced
rate on home rental income. Contact the [ Residential Tenancies Board
](https://www.rtb.ie/) for advice on this.
