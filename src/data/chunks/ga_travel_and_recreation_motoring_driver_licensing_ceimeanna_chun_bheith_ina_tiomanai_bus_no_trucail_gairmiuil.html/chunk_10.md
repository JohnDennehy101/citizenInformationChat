##  Céim 7: Déan iarratas ar do cheadúnas tiomána

Is féidir leat [ iarratas a dhéanamh ar líne ](https://www.ndls.ie/index.php)
nó go pearsanta ar cheadúnas tiomána nuair a éiríonn leat [ triail tiomána
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-
recreation/motoring/driving-tests/driving-test/) . Cosnaíonn ceadúnas nua €55,
nó €35 má tá tú ag cur an chatagóir nua le do cheadúnas chatagóir B.

Faigh amach inár leathanach faoi [ iarratas a dhéanamh ar cheadúnas tiomána
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-
licensing/full-driving-licence/) :

  * [ Conas cur isteach ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/full-driving-licence/#c76aec)
  * [ Cé chomh fada is atá ceadúnas tiomána bailí? ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/full-driving-licence/#894665)
  * [ Cé mhéad a chosnaíonn ceadúnas tiomána? ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/full-driving-licence/#f05909)
  * [ Cad iad na srianta ar thiománaithe nua? ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-licensing/full-driving-licence/#43f97f)
