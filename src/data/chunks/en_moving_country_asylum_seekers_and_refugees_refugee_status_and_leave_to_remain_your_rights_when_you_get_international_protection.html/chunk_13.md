##  Education

All children and young people are entitled to free [ primary
](/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-education/going-to-primary-
school/choosing-a-primary-school/) and [ post-primary ](/en/education/primary-
and-post-primary-education/going-to-post-primary-school/types-of-post-primary-
school/) education. If you apply to third level (colleges and universities),
you may be eligible for the Student Grant.

If you are unemployed, there are different [ education and training
opportunities ](/en/employment/starting-work-and-changing-job/training-and-
looking-for-work/learn-new-skills-or-retrain/) that include courses
specifically for unemployed people.
