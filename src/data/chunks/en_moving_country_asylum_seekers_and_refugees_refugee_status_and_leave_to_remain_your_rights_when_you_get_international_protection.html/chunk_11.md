##  Employment

You have the right to work and there is help available for [ looking for work
and getting a job ](/en/employment/starting-work-and-changing-job/training-
and-looking-for-work/finding-and-getting-a-job/) .

When you start working, you can get information about your [ employment rights
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment_rights_and_conditions/)
, and there are procedures to assist you to [ enforce your employment rights
](/en/employment/enforcement-and-redress/enforcing-your-employment-rights/) .
