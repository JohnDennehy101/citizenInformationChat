###  **Brexit and your pension**

On [ 31 January 2020 the UK exited the EU.
](https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/press/press-releases/2020/01/30/brexit-
council-adopts-decision-to-conclude-the-withdrawal-agreement/) However, you
will still get your Irish State Pension (Contributory) or UK State Pension, as
before. Irish and UK citizens living in Ireland can still benefit from social
insurance contributions made when working in the UK.
