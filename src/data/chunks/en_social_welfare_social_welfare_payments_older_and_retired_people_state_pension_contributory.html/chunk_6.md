##  How to qualify for a State Pension (Contributory)

To qualify for a State Pension (Contributory), you must be aged 66 or over,
and have enough Class A, E, F, G, H, N, or S [ social insurance contributions
(PRSI) ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-
welfare-system/social-insurance-prsi/social-insurance-classes/) . These are
also called full-rate PRSI contributions.
