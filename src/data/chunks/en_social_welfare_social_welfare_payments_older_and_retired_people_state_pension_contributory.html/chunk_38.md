###  Pensions Commisson

The [ Pensions Commission ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/02859-about-the-
pensions-commission/) was set up under the [ Programme for Government 2020
](https://www.finegael.ie/app/uploads/2020/06/ProgrammeforGovernment_Final_16.06.20-1.pdf)
to examine sustainability and eligibility issues with State pensions and the
Social Insurance Fund.
