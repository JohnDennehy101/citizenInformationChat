#####  Site updates

People getting long-term social welfare payments get an extra week’s payment
at Christmas. This [ Christmas Bonus
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/christmas-bonus/) will be paid this week.

People getting [ Child Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/social-welfare-payments/families-and-children/child-benefit/) you will
get a once-off double-payment for each child on 5 December 2023.

The first instalment of the [ Electricity Account Credit
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/utilities/electricity-
account-credit/) will be paid between 1 December and 31 December 2023. The
payment of €150 is automatic and you do not need to apply for it.

If you are a teacher who [ qualified in Ukraine
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/primary-and-post-primary-
education/teachers-and-schools/teacher-qualifications/) and have been granted
[ EU Temporary Protection ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/moving-
country/asylum-seekers-and-refugees/the-asylum-process-in-ireland/temporary-
protection-directive/) , you can [ apply to register and teach in Ireland
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/returning-to-ireland/starting-
work/Recognition-of-professional-qualifications-in-ireland/#2473e9) .

On 30 November 2023, Shannon Local Electoral Area (LEA) and Westmeath County
Council were designated [ Rent Pressure Zones
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/renting-a-home/landlords-
rights-and-responsibilities/rent-increases-in-private-rented-housing/#a18063)
.

If you are unsure [ how to avoid scams
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/buying-digital-content-and-
services/scams-and-fraud/) when online shopping, you can [ use CheckMyLink
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/consumer/buying-digital-content-and-
services/scams-and-fraud/) to verify the authenticity and safety of the
website. This website is managed by Cyber Skills, in partnership with
ScamAdviser and An Garda Síochána.

From 4 December 2023, all children aged 2-17 can get a free [ flu vaccine
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/health-
services/vaccinations/flu-vaccination/) . This was previously only available
to children aged 2-12.
