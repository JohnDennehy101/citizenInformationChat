##  More information about late social welfare applications

If you need help applying for a payment, you can contact your local:

  * [ Intreo Centre or local Social Welfare Branch Office ](https://www.gov.ie/en/directory/category/e1f4b5-intreo-offices/) , or 
  * [ Citizens Information Centre ](http://centres.citizensinformation.ie/) . 

You can also read about [ late applications for social welfare payments
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/723465-operational-guidelines-claims-and-
late-claims/#claiming-within-the-prescribed-time) on gov.ie.
