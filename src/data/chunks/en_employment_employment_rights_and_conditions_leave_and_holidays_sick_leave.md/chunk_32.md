##  More information

The law on sick leave and sick pay is set out in the [ Sick Leave Act 2022
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2022/act/24/enacted/en/html) (as
amended), the [ Commencement Order for the Sick Leave Act 2022
](https://enterprise.gov.ie/en/legislation/legislation-files/si-
no-606-of-2022-sick-leave-act-2022-commencement-order-2022.pdf) and the [ Sick
Leave Act 2022 (Prescribed daily rate of payment) Regulations 2022
](https://enterprise.gov.ie/en/legislation/legislation-files/si-
no-607-of-2022.pdf) .

The WRC also has information on [ sick leave
](https://www.workplacerelations.ie/en/what_you_should_know/leave/sick-leave/)
.

You can read more about [ accidents in the workplace
](/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/health-and-safety/accidents-
in-the-workplace/) .
