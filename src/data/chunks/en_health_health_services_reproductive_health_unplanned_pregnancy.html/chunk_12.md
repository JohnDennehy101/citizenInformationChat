####  3-day wait

After you get this certification, you must wait at least 3 days before you
have an abortion. For example, if you are certified on a Tuesday, the earliest
you can have an abortion is Friday.

The HSE has information on [ where to go for an abortion in Ireland, which
hospitals provide abortion services and how long the procedure takes.
](https://www2.hse.ie/conditions/abortion/how-to-get/where-to-go/)
