##  Step 8: Apply for your first Driver CPC card

You should [ add the bus or truck category to your driving licence
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-
licensing/how-do-i-add-a-category-to-my-irish-driving-licence/) first.
Otherwise, your application for your [ CPC card
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/driver-
licensing/learner-driving-permits-for-cars-and-work-vehicles/#7f5f86) may be
rejected.

[ Download a Driver CPC application form (pdf)
](https://www.rsa.ie/docs/default-source/services/s3.1-cpc/cpc-driver-
qualification-declaration-form.pdf?Status=Master&sfvrsn=8595d21c_13/CPC-
Driver-Qualification-Declaration-form.pdf) , complete it and post it. There is
no fee. You should get a card within 20 working days.

You can only drive professionally when you get this card. There are [ some
limited exceptions ](https://www.rsa.ie/services/professional-
drivers/cpc/what-is-
cpc#:~:text=Who%20doesn%27t%20need,driver%E2%80%99s%20main%20activity.) . For
example, you do not need a Driver CPC card for:

  * Vehicles that carry materials or equipment used by you as part of your work, provided driving isn’t your main activity. 
  * Drivers of vehicles for non-commercial purposes, such as unpaid volunteers or emergency or rescue vehicle drivers. 

See the RSA website for a [ full list of exceptions
](https://www.rsa.ie/services/professional-drivers/cpc/what-is-
cpc#:~:text=Who%20doesn%27t%20need,driver%E2%80%99s%20main%20activity.) .

[ Strict penalties ](https://www.rsa.ie/services/professional-
drivers/cpc/regulations-for-driver-cpc) apply if you drive without a Driver
CPC. The law is enforced by An Garda Síochána and Road Safety Authority
Transport Officers.

To remain CPC certified, [ you must maintain your CPC
](https://www.rsa.ie/services/professional-drivers/cpc/become-a-professional-
cpc-driver/10.-staying-qualified) over 5 years. The [ RSA website
](https://www.rsa.ie/services/professional-drivers/cpc/become-a-professional-
cpc-driver/9.-driver-cpc-card) has more information about the CPC card.
