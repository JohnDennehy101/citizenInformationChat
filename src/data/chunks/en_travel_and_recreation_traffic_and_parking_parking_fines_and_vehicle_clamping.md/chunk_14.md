###  Rates in statutory areas (public road, airport, or harbour)

If you commit a parking offence in a statutory area, you may get a fine, have
your vehicle clamped, or have your vehicle impounded. There is a system of [
fixed charge fines ](https://www.rsa.ie/services/licensed-drivers/penalty-
points/types-of-offences) for illegal parking and other related offences.
