##  Parking laws

There are a number of Acts and regulations that govern parking and clamping
including:

  * The underlying legislation in relation to road traffic offences is generally the [ Road Traffic Acts 1961 (as amended) ](https://revisedacts.lawreform.ie/eli/1961/act/24/revised/en/html) . 
  * [ Section 101B of the Road Traffic Act, 1961 ](https://revisedacts.lawreform.ie/eli/1961/act/24/section/101B/revised/en/html) : provides for vehicle clamping. 
  * [ The Road Traffic Act, 1994 ](https://revisedacts.lawreform.ie/eli/1994/act/7/front/revised/en/html) : which gives local authorities the power to make bye-laws governing the type of paid parking controls in their areas, for example, disc parking or pay-and-display parking. 
  * [ The Road Traffic Act 2010 ](https://revisedacts.lawreform.ie/eli/2010/act/25/front/revised/en/html) : provides for the system of fixed-charge traffic and parking offences. 
  * [ The Vehicle Clamping Act, 2015 ](https://revisedacts.lawreform.ie/eli/2015/act/13/revised/en/html) : makes the National Transport Authority responsible for the regulation of clamping. 
  * [ The Road Traffic (Traffic and Parking) Regulations, 1997 as amended ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/1997/si/182/made/en/print) : provide for the general regulation and control of traffic, pedestrians and parking. 
  * [ The Road Traffic (Signs) Regulations 2024 (pdf) ](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2024/si/200/made/en/pdf) : give local authorities responsibility for authorising regulatory traffic signs and designating areas where parking is restricted or prohibited. 
  * [ The Vehicle Clamping and Signing Regulations, 2017 (pdf) ](http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2017/si/421/made/en/pdf) : set the maximum fees for clamping, re-locating and impounding vehicles in certain areas. 
  * [ Road Traffic Act 2010 (Part 3) (Fixed Charge Offences) Regulations 2024 (pdf) ](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2024/si/227/made/en/pdf) : sets out the amount of certain fixed charge parking offences.
