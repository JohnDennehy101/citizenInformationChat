##  More information

You can get more information from the [ RSA website
](https://www.rsa.ie/services) .

You can get information on [ motorcycle safety gear ](https://www.rsa.ie/road-
safety/road-users/motorcyclists/safety-gear) , [ steps to getting qualified as
a driver ](https://www.rsa.ie/services/learner-drivers/get-qualified) and [
preparing your motorcycle for your driving test
](https://www.rsa.ie/services/learner-drivers/the-driving-test/driving-test-
vehicles/motorcycle) .

You can [ apply for your driving test online ](https://ndls.rsa.ie/) .
