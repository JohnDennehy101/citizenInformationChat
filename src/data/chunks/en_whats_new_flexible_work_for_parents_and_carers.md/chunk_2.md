#####  Site updates

Are you a parent or carer? 👩‍👩‍👧‍👦

Since 7 March 2024, you have a new right to request a [ flexible working
arrangement ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-
rights-and-conditions/hours-of-work/right-to-request-flexible-working/) .

Flexible working is where your normal working hours are adjusted, for example:

👷‍♀️ Part-time work

👨‍🏫 Term-time work

👨‍🏭 Job-sharing

👷‍♂️ Flexitime

👩‍⚕️ Compressed working hours

👨‍💻 Remote working

You can request flexible working from your first day in a new job, but you
must be working at least 6 months with your employer before the arrangement
can start. 🗓️

Check our page for who qualifies and how to apply for a [ flexible work
arrangement ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-
rights-and-conditions/hours-of-work/right-to-request-flexible-working/) .
