####  Stage 1: Submit your receipts

You can use the [ Revenue's receipts tracker
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/online-services/services/common/manage-your-
receipts-with-receipts-tracker.aspx) to store your receipt details online.

You can continue to add receipts until you reach the cap on expenditure of
€625, or €1,250 for a jointly-assessed married couple.
