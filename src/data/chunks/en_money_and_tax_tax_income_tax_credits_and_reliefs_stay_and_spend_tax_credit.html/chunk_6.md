####  Food and non-alcoholic drink

Food and drinks served in a café, restaurant, hotel or pub can also qualify
for relief.

The following expenses **do not apply** :

  * Takeaway food 
  * Alcoholic drinks 
  * Drinks (either alcoholic or non-alcoholic) served without food 
  * Amounts below €25 

You can check that your accommodation or food and drink provider is
participating in the scheme using [ Revenue’s list of qualifying service
providers ](https://www.ros.ie/stay-spend-web/rev/sas) .
