##  How do I qualify for a social welfare payment?

There are [ different types of social welfare payments
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/social-welfare-system-in-ireland/#24bf40) available. Some depend on
your income, and others depend on whether or not you have paid enough [ social
insurance contributions ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-
welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-insurance-prsi/social-insurance/) .
