##  Other routes to information

Citizens Information is available

  * By phone through the Citizens Information Phone Service: 0818 07 4000, Monday to Friday, 9am-8pm 
  * In person by calling to a [ Citizens Information Centre near you ](http://centres.citizensinformation.ie)

**A note on 0818 telephone numbers**

Calls to [ 0818 non-geographic telephone numbers (NGNs)
](https://www.comreg.ie/industry/licensing/numbering/ngn-review/) cost no more
than calling a landline number and will be included in your bundle of call
minutes if your bundle includes landline calls.
