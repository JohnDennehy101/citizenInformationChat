###  **Changes to the Free Travel Scheme in 2024**

From 29 July 2024, you will qualify for the Free Travel Scheme if you are aged
over 17 and under 66, and:

  * Have never been able to drive due to a disability 
  * Have a driving licence and are medically certified as ‘not fit to drive’ for at least 12 months. 

While the change won’t take effect until July, you can download and fill in an
[ application form for Free Travel for people medically certified unfit to
drive (FTM) (pdf)
](https://www.gov.ie/pdf/?file=https://assets.gov.ie/292514/e5291b34-d4e7-43b2-aea3-ea5a832849ad.pdf#page=null)
now.

In this case, your spouse, civil partner, or cohabitant **cannot** accompany
you free of charge when travelling.
