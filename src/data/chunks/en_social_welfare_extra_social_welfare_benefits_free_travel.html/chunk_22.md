###  **Brexit and the Senior SmartPass**

On [ 31 January 2020 the UK exited the EU
](https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/press/press-releases/2020/01/30/brexit-
council-adopts-decision-to-conclude-the-withdrawal-agreement/) . [ Senior
SmartPass ](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/9bba61-free-travel-scheme/) holders
can continue to travel in Northern Ireland on the bus and train.
