##  More information

Read [ more information about the medical card
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-
cards/medical-card/) , including how to apply. Find a copy of the [
application form for a medical card for over 70s
](https://assets.hse.ie/media/documents/Medical_Card_and_GP_Visit_Card_Application_Form_-
_People_Aged_70_Years_or_Older.pdf) .

You can read more information about [ medical cards
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-
cards/medical-card/) and [ GP visit cards
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-
cards/gp-visit-cards/) , including how to apply. If you are under 70, check if
you are eligible under the [ means test for people aged under 70.
](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/medical-card-means-test-
under-70s/)

You can also read about [ prescription charges for medical card holders
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/health/drugs-and-
medicines/prescription-charges-for-medical-card-holders/) .

Find more information on the [ medical card
](https://www2.hse.ie/services/schemes-allowances/medical-cards/) and [ GP
visit card ](https://www2.hse.ie/services/schemes-allowances/gp-visit-cards/)
on the HSE website. You can also find more detail in the [ HSE Assessment
Guidelines for medical cards and GP visit cards
](https://www.hse.ie/eng/staff/pcrs/medical-card-and-gp-visit-card-assessment-
guidelines.pdf) .

The HSE has information on [ financial assessment.
](https://www2.hse.ie/services/medical-cards/medical-card-application-
process/the-medical-card-assessment.html)
