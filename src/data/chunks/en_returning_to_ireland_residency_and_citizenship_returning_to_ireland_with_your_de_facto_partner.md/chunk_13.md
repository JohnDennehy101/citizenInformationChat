####  **Registering**

If your de facto partner is [ living in Cork, Dublin, Kildare, Limerick,
Meath, or Wicklow ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/registering-your-
immigration-permission/how-to-register-your-immigration-permission-for-the-
first-time/information-on-registering-if-living-in-dublin-kildare-meath-or-
wicklow/) , they need to call 1800 800 630 to [ make an appointment
](https://burghquayregistrationoffice.inis.gov.ie/) to register at the [ Burgh
Quay Registration Office ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/burgh-quay-
appointments/) . This Freephone number is open from 9am to 5pm, Monday to
Friday. To make an appointment your partner will need their passport details
and a valid email address.

If your de facto partner is [ living outside Cork, Dublin, Kildare, Limerick,
Meath, or Wicklow ](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/registering-your-
immigration-permission/how-to-register-your-immigration-permission-for-the-
first-time/information-on-registering-if-living-outside-of-dublin-kildare-
meath-and-wicklow/) , call or email your [ local registration office
](http://www.garda.ie/stations/default.aspx) for an appointment.
