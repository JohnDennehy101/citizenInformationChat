####  **How do I apply for my children?**

Children (under the age of 18 or up to 23 years if in full time education)
apply in a similar way to adults using the online form. The parent or legal
guardian of the child will need to sign the form once completed and printed.
ISD has [ information on how to apply for child dependents
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/coming-to-join-family-in-ireland/joining-an-
irish-national/de-facto-partner-of-an-irish-national/#apply-children) .
