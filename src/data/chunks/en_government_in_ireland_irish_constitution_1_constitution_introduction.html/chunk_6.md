##  Further information

You can access a [ full copy of the Constitution
](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/cons/en/html) on irishstatutebook.ie.
You may also order a copy from the [ Government Publications Office
](https://www.opw.ie/en/governmentpublications/) and it is available in
bookshops nationwide.

If you feel that your constitutional rights are being violated, you may wish
to engage a solicitor. The Law Society of Ireland has a [ list of all
solicitors who hold a current practising certificate
](https://www.lawsociety.ie/Find-a-Solicitor/Solicitor-Firm-Search/) .
