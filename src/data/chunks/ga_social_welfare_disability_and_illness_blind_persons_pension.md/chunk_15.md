##  Can I get other payments with Blind Pension?

If you are aged under 66 and you meet the relevant conditions, you may also
qualify for:

  * [ Adoptive Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/adoptive-benefit/)
  * [ Half-rate Carer’s Allowance ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/carers/half-rate-carers-allowance/)
  * [ Health and Safety Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/health-safety-benefit/)
  * [ Illness Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/disability-benefit/)
  * [ Injury Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/injury-benefit/)
  * [ Jobseeker’s Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/unemployed-people/jobseekers-benefit/)
  * [ Maternity Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/maternity-benefit/)
  * [ One-Parent Family Payment ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/one-parent-family-payment/) (see below) 
  * [ Partial Capacity Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/disability-and-illness/partial-capacity-benefit/) (see below) 
  * [ Paternity Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/paternity-benefit/)
  * [ Parent’s Benefit ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/parents-benefit/)
  * [ Occupational Injuries Benefits ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/social-welfare-payments/disability-and-illness/occupational-injuries-benefit-overview/) including Injury Benefit, Disablement Benefit and Death Benefits 
  * [ Widow’s, Widower’s or Surviving Civil Partner’s Contributory Pension ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-contrib-pension/)
  * [ Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner’s (Non-Contributory) Non-Contributory Pension ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-non-contrib-pension/)
