##  Working abroad

Whether you are planning to [ work within the European Union (EU)
](/en/moving-country/moving-abroad/working-abroad/working-in-the-eu/) or [
work in a country outside the EU ](/en/moving-country/moving-abroad/working-
abroad/working-outside-the-eu/) , it can be helpful to look for work before
you leave Ireland.

You can find out about [ work and retirement in the EU
](http://europa.eu/youreurope/citizens/work/index_en.htm) on europa.eu. It has
information on a range of topics including [ residence formalities
](http://europa.eu/youreurope/citizens/residence/index_en.htm) and [ travel
](http://europa.eu/youreurope/citizens/travel/index_en.htm) . If you are
planning to work in the EU you should also check with your [ local employment
services ](https://www.gov.ie/en/collection/d6f636-employment-services-
offices/) office or [ Intreo centre ](http://www.welfare.ie/en/Pages/Intreo---
Contact-Information.aspx) . Each office has a [ EURES noticeboard
](http://ec.europa.eu/eures/home.jsp?lang=en) advertising vacancies from a
range of European countries.

You can read about [ working holidays ](/en/moving-country/moving-
abroad/working-abroad/working-holidays-for-irish-citizens/) abroad.
