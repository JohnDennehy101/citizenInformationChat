####  Self-employed income over €100,000

A surcharge of 3% applies to people who have income from self-employment above
€100,000, regardless of age.

**Note** : Bonuses paid to employees of the 5 financial institutions that have
received financial support from the State are chargeable to the USC at the
rate of 45% on the full amount if the bonus is more than €20,000 in a single
tax year.
