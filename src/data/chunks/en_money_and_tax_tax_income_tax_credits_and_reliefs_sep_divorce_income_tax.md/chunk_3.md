##  Taxation in the year of separation

How you are taxed in the year you separate depends on how you were taxed as a
couple.

You may have been taxed under:

  * Joint assessment 
  * Separate assessment 
  * Single assessment 

For more information on these options, read about the [ taxation of married
people and civil partners ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/taxation-of-
married-people/) .
