##  Other announcements

**An Garda Síochána**

Funding is provided to recruit up to 700 additional trainee Gardaí.

**Judicial Council**

€1 million is provided to establish the Judicial Council in 2020, to promote
and maintain high standards of conduct by judges and provide a complaints
process for misconduct.

**Rainy Day Fund**

The Government will not make the planned contribution of €500 million to the
Rainy Day Fund, due to the possibility of a no-deal Brexit.
