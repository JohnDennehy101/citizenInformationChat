##  How to apply for a public health nurse?

You may be referred to your local public health nurse by your GP. You or your
family can also contact the service yourself through your local [ health
centre ](http://www.hse.ie/eng/services/list/2/healthcentres/) or your [ Local
Health Office ](http://www.hse.ie/eng/services/list/1/LHO/) .

You can find a [ leaflet about the Public Health Nurse service in different
languages ](https://www.hse.ie/eng/services/list/2/primarycare/national-phn-
service/) on the HSE website.
