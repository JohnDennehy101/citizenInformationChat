###  Disability and illness payments

The number of waiting days for [ Illness Benefit ](/ga/social-
welfare/disability-and-illness/illness-benefit/) will be reduced from 6 days
to 3 days on new claims (from the end of February 2021).

The earnings disregard for [ Disability Allowance ](/ga/social-
welfare/disability-and-illness/disability-allowance/) will increase by €20 per
week from €120 to €140 per week (from June 2021).

A grant of up to €500 to buy hearing aids and up to €100 towards repairs will
be provided under the [ Treatment Benefit Scheme ](/ga/social-
welfare/disability-and-illness/treatment-benefit-scheme/) without requiring a
matching payment by the claimant (from April 2021).
