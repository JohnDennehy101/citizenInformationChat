###  Income limit for a dependent relative

If your dependent relative’s income is more than €17,404 in 2024, you will not
get the tax credit for that year. In previous years, the income limit was
€16,780 in 2023, €16,156 in 2022, €15,740 in 2021 and €15,060 in 2020.

All of your dependent relative's income is included for the income limit. This
includes social welfare payments, pensions, and deposit interest.
