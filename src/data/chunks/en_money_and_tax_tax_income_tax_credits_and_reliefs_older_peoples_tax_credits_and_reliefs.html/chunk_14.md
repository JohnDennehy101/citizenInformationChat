##  Deeds of covenant

A deed of covenant is a written legal agreement for one person to pay another
person an amount without getting any benefit in return.

If you pay tax at the [ higher rate of income tax
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-tax/tax/income-tax/how-your-
tax-is-calculated/#354b71) , you can benefit from tax relief on a deed of
covenant.

To qualify for tax relief, it must be a deed of covenant to:

  * Someone who is over 65 (this relief is limited to 5% of your total income) 
  * An adult who is permanently incapacitated 
  * A minor who is permanently incapacitated but is not your child 

Read more about the rules for [ tax relief on deeds of covenant ](/en/money-
and-tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/deeds-of-covenant/) .
