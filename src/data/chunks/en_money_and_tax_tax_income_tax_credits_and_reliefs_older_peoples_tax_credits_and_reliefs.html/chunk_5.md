##  Deposit Interest Retention Tax (DIRT) exemption

[ Deposit Interest Retention Tax (DIRT) ](/en/money-and-tax/tax/tax-on-
savings-and-investments/deposit-interest-retention-tax/) is deducted from
interest paid on deposit or savings accounts.

If you or your spouse or civil partner are over 65, you do not have to pay
DIRT if your income is below the  income exemption limit  .
