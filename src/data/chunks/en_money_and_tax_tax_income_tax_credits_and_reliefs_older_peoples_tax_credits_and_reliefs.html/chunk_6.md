####  How to apply

To apply to have your deposit interest paid to you without DIRT being
deducted, send a completed [ Form DE1 (pdf)
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/additional-incomes/documents/dirt/form-de1.pdf) to
your bank or credit union.

If DIRT is already deducted from your deposit interest, you can apply for a
refund by sending a completed [ Form 54 (pdf)
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/additional-
incomes/documents/dirt/form54-claims.pdf) to Revenue.
