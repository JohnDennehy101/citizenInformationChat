##  Medical expenses

You can claim income tax back on many medical expenses. Tax relief for most
expenses is at the standard rate of tax.

You can claim tax relief on medical expenses you pay for yourself or for
someone else.

Read about the [ medical expenses that qualify for tax relief ](/en/money-and-
tax/tax/income-tax-credits-and-reliefs/taxation-and-medical-expenses/) and how
to claim the tax relief.
