##  Help with finding work

If you are unemployed when you return to Ireland, you can access [ free local
employment services for jobseekers ](/en/social-welfare/unemployed-
people/employment-supports/) . Your local employment service (LES) or your
local Job Club can help you with writing a CV and cover letter. They can also
help with filling in job application forms and training in interview skills.
