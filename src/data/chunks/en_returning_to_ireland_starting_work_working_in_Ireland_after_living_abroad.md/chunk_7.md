##  Childcare options in Ireland

If you have children and you need someone to take care of them while you are
at work, you have different [ childcare options
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/pre-school-education-and-
childcare/your-childcare-options/) , including:

  * Full day care 
  * Sessional services (such as Montessori or playschool) 
  * Childminders 

You may be able to get help with paying for childcare through the [ National
Childcare Scheme ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/pre-school-
education-and-childcare/national-childcare-scheme/) and the [ Early Childhood
Care and Education (ECCE) Scheme
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/education/pre-school-education-and-
childcare/early-childhood-care-and-education-scheme/) .
