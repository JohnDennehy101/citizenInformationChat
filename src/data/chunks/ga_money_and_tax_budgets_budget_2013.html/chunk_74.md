####  Motor Tax

Motor Tax rates across all categories apart from electric cars will increase
with effect from 1 January 2013. There is a revised structure for vehicles
taxed on the basis of CO2 emissions. Increases are being applied to motor tax
rates for all other categories of vehicle also. There is a higher rate of
increase in motor tax for cars taxed on the basis of CO2.
