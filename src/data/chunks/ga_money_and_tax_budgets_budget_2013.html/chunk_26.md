The duration of Jobseeker's Benefit will be reduced by 3 months with effect
from 3 April 2013. For people with 260 or more PRSI contributions paid, it
will reduce from 12 months to 9 months. For people with fewer than 260 PRSI
contributions paid, it will reduce from 9 months to 6 months.

Claimants getting Jobseeker's Benefit for 6 months or more on 3 April 2013 (or
3 months for people with fewer than 260 contributions) will not be affected.
