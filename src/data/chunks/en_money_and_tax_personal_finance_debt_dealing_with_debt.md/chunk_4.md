##  Personal insolvency options

There are 3 [ personal insolvency options ](/en/money-and-tax/personal-
finance/debt/personal-insolvency/personal-insolvency-options/) for people who
cannot afford to pay their personal and mortgage debts.

The Insolvency Service of Ireland (ISI) administers these debt resolution
processes.
