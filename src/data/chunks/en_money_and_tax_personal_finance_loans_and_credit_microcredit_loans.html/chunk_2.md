##  What is the 'It Makes Sense'loan?

The _It Makes Sense_ loan (also known as the Personal Microcredit Scheme) is a
small credit union loan at a low interest rate for people who are getting a
social welfare payment. The scheme aims to reduce dependence on moneylenders
that charge very high interest rates.

If you are not a member of your [ local credit union ](/en/money-and-
tax/personal-finance/banking/credit-unions/) , you can join and apply
immediately for an _It Makes Sense_ loan.
