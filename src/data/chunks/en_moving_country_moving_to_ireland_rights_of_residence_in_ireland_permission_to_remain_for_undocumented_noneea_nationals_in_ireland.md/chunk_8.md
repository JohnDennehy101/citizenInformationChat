##  Documents you need

You must be able to prove your identity and that you have lived in Ireland for
the required period.

You do not need to prove that you have a particular level of income or
savings.

The [ list of documents is now available
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/required-documents-guide-regularisation-of-
long-term-undocumented-migrants-scheme/) for undocumented applicants and for [
international protection applicants ](http://www.ipo.gov.ie/en/IPO/IPRSU-
Application-Form-FAQ.pdf/Files/IPRSU-Application-Form-FAQ.pdf) .
