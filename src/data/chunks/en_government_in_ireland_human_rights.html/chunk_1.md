#  Human rights and equality

  * [ Public Sector Equality and Human Rights Duty ](/en/government-in-ireland/human-rights/public-sector-equality-and-human-rights-duty/)

All public bodies in Ireland have a legal obligation to promote equality,
prevent discrimination and protect human rights under the Public Sector
Equality and Human Rights Duty.

  * [ The right to protest ](/en/government-in-ireland/human-rights/right-to-protest/)

People in Ireland have a right to protest peacefully in public places, as long
as they do not break the law.

  * [ Charter of Fundamental Rights ](/en/government-in-ireland/european-government/eu-law/charter-of-fundamental-rights/)

The civil, political, social and economic rights recognised by the European
Union are listed in the Charter of Fundamental Rights.

**This document is in:** [ EU Law ](/en/government-in-ireland/european-
government/eu-law/)

  * [ Disputes about equality and discrimination ](/en/employment/enforcement-and-redress/disputes-about-equality-and-discrimination/)

The Workplace Relations Commission deals with all complaints of discrimination
in employment and access to goods and services which come under equality
legislation.

**This document is in:** [ Enforcement and redress
](/en/employment/enforcement-and-redress/)

  * [ Equal pay ](/en/employment/equality-in-work/equal-pay/)

Under employment equality legislation you must get equal pay for like work.
Find out about your rights and how to make a claim for equal pay.

**This document is in:** [ Equality in work ](/en/employment/equality-in-
work/)

  * [ Fundamental rights under the Irish Constitution ](/en/government-in-ireland/irish-constitution-1/constitution-fundamental-rights/)

The Irish Constitution recognises and declares that you have certain
fundamental personal rights. These are confirmed and protected by the
Constitution.

**This document is in:** [ Irish Constitution ](/en/government-in-
ireland/irish-constitution-1/)

  * [ Irish Human Rights and Equality Commission ](/en/justice/law-and-rights/irish-human-rights-and-equality-commission/)

The Commission promotes and protects human rights and equality. In some cases,
it can assist you to further human rights issues by assisting with legal
proceedings.

**This document is in:** [ Law and rights ](/en/justice/law-and-rights/)

  * [ UN Convention on the Rights of the Child ](/en/birth-family-relationships/children-s-rights-and-policy/un-convention-on-the-rights-of-the-child/)

The UN Convention on the Rights of the Child sets out the basic human rights
to which every child is entitled. Ireland ratified the Convention on 28
September 1992. Find out more.

**This document is in:** [ Children's rights and policy ](/en/birth-family-
relationships/children-s-rights-and-policy/)
