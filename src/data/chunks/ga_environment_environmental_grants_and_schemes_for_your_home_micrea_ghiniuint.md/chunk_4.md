###  Deontais le haghaidh painéil gréine

If you own your own home you can apply to the SEAI for a [ grant towards the
cost of installing solar panels ](/ga/housing/housing-grants-and-
schemes/grants-for-home-renovations-and-improvements/grants-for-solar-panels/)
.

The SEAI is considering extending their grant support to include other forms
of renewable energy, such as:

  * Micrea-gaoithe 
  * Micrea-hidrea 
  * CHP micrea-inathnuaite 

Léigh tuilleadh faoi [ dheontais do phainéil ghréine i do theach
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/housing/housing-grants-and-
schemes/grants-for-home-renovations-and-improvements/grants-for-solar-panels/)
agus is féidir leat faisnéis a fháil faoi [ dheontais do phainéil ghréine i do
ghnó ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/types-of-
employment/self-employment/supports-for-businesses-going-green/) .
