##  How to qualify for Adoptive Benefit

To get Adoptive Benefit, you must:

  * Have enough PRSI (social insurance) contributions 
  * Have certified adoptive leave from work if you are an employee – see ‘How to apply’ below 
  * Provide a Certificate of Placement (for [ domestic adoption ](/en/birth-family-relationships/adoption-and-fostering/domestic-adoption/) ) or Declaration of Suitability (for [ intercountry adoption ](/en/birth-family-relationships/adoption-and-fostering/intercountry-adoption/) ) – see ‘How to apply’ below
