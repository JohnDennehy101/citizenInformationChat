##  Internship Employment Permit

If you are a full-time student in a college outside of the EEA, you can apply
for an [ Internship Employment Permit ](https://enterprise.gov.ie/en/What-We-
Do/Workplace-and-Skills/Employment-Permits/Permit-Types/Internship-Employment-
Permit/) to get work experience in Ireland.

The following rules apply:

  * The work experience must be a requirement of your course 
  * The minimum pay rate is the [ national minimum wage ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/employment/employment-rights-and-conditions/pay-and-employment/minimum-wage/)
  * The internship must be in an occupation on the [ Critical Skills Occupations List ](https://enterprise.gov.ie/en/What-We-Do/Workplace-and-Skills/Employment-Permits/Employment-Permit-Eligibility/Highly-Skilled-Eligible-Occupations-List/)
  * The Permit is for a maximum period of 12 months. When the Permit is finished, you must leave Ireland.
