##  Exchange Agreement Employment Permit

You can apply for an [ Exchange Agreement Employment Permit
](https://enterprise.gov.ie/en/What-We-Do/Workplace-and-Skills/Employment-
Permits/Permit-Types/Exchange-Agreement-Employment-Permit/) if you are coming
to Ireland through one of the following programmes:

  * [ AIESEC ](https://aiesec.org/)
  * The [ International Association for the Exchange of Students for Technical Experience (IAESTE) ](https://iaeste.org/)
  * The [ Fulbright Programme ](https://www.fulbright.ie/)

  * [ Vulcanus in Europe Programme ](https://www.eu-japan.eu/events/vulcanus-europe) (Ireland) 

Permits are for a maximum period of 12 months.
