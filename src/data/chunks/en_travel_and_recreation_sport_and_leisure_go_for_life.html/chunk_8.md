##  Further information

[ Sport Ireland ](https://www.sportireland.ie/keepwell) and the Local Sports
Partnerships have a range of initiatives for all ages and abilities to help
keep you active and well. They have a home [ exercise plan
](https://www.sportireland.ie/participation/older-adult-home-exercise-plan)
that you can follow.

Find out about your nearest walking trail on our page on [ walking and
rambling ](/en/travel-and-recreation/sport-and-leisure/walking-and-rambling-
in-ireland/) .

There are [ general tips on how to keep active
](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/16d3c-keeping-active/#activity-for-older-
people) on gov.ie. Visit [ HealthyIreland
](https://www.gov.ie/en/campaigns/healthy-ireland/) for health and wellness
information. [ Social Connections
](https://www.gov.ie/en/campaigns/6c10f-social-connections/) has information
and advice on building and keeping social connections.
