###  **Moving from within the EU**

If you are bringing your vehicle from another EU country into Ireland, you are
liable to pay:

  * [ Motor tax ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/motor-tax-and-insurance/motor-tax-rates/)
  * Value Added Tax (VAT) 
  * Vehicle Registration Tax (VRT)
