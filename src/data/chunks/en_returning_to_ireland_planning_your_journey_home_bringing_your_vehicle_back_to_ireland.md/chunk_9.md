###  **Who qualifies for VRT relief?**

To claim an exemption from VRT, you must have had your normal residence
outside Ireland at least 185 days per year before moving to Ireland.

You must also:

  * Own the vehicle 
  * Have owned and used the vehicle outside of Ireland for at least 6 months before moving to Ireland 
  * Make sure the required tax and duty is paid on the vehicle 
  * Import your vehicle within one year of moving to Ireland 

You must import your vehicle within one year of moving to Ireland to claim the
relief.

You are not eligible for VRT relief if you were granted similar relief for a
vehicle in the last 5 years.
