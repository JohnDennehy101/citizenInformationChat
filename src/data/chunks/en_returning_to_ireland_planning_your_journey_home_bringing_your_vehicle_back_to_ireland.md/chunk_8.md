####  **How to claim VAT relief**

To claim the VAT exemption, you must provide [ documents that prove the above
conditions and that you have lived abroad
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/customs/individuals/transfer-
residence/documents.aspx) . These documents should accompany your completed [
VRT Transfer of Residence from Within the EU Form (pdf)
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/vrt/documents/vrt/form-vrt-tor.pdf) .
