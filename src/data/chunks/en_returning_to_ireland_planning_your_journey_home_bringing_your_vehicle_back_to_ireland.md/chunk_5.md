###  **Moving from outside the EU**

If you are bringing your vehicle from outside the EU to Ireland, you are
liable to pay:

  * Customs duty 
  * [ Motor tax ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/motor-tax-and-insurance/motor-tax-rates/)
  * Value Added Tax (VAT) 
  * Vehicle Registration Tax (VRT) 

You will also need to [ register the vehicle
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/vrt/vehicle-registration-tax/general-information-
on-registration.aspx) and get [ motor insurance
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-recreation/motoring/motor-
tax-and-insurance/motor-insurance/) .
