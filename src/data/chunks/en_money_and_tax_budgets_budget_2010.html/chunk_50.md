###  Energy efficiency measures

Allocation of about €130m for energy efficiency measures will include a new
multi-annual National Retrofit Programme in 2010.

€50m of the carbon tax yield will be used to fund measures such as help for
households at risk of fuel poverty to make their homes warmer.

The local authorities will receive additional funding to retrofit the social
housing stock.

Extension of the existing capital allowances scheme for energy-efficient
equipment purchased by companies from seven categories of eligible equipment
to ten.
