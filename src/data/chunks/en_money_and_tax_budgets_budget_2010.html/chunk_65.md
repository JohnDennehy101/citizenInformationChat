###  Home heating

The carbon tax will apply from 1 May 2010 to kerosene, marked gas oil, Liquid
Petroleum Gas (LPG), fuel oil and natural gas.

The application of the tax to coal and commercial peat will be subject to a
Commencement Order. A vouched fuel allowance scheme will be developed to
offset the increases for low-income families dependant on such fuels.

Electricity is not subject to the carbon tax.
