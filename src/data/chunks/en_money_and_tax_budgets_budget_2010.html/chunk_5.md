###  Qualified adult

The qualified adult rate for the following payments will be reduced by €5.50
per week (from January 2010):

  * Jobseeker’s Benefit 
  * Jobseeker’s Allowance 
  * Supplementary Welfare Allowance 
  * Pre-Retirement Allowance 
  * Farm Assist 
  * Health and Safety Benefit 
  * Illness Benefit 
  * Disability Allowance 
  * Blind Pension 
  * Injury Benefit 

This reduction applies to the full-rate payment. There are proportional
reductions for people getting graduated payments.
