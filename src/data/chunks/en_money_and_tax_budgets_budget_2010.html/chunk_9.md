###  Carers

Carer’s Allowance will be reduced by €8.50 for claimants under 66 years of
age. Half-rate Carer's Allowance will be reduced by €4.25 for claimants under
66 years of age. (January 2010).

This reduction applies to the full-rate payment. There are proportional
reductions for people getting graduated payments.

There will be no reduction in Carer’s Allowance for people aged 66 and over.

Carer’s Benefit and Constant Attendance Allowance will be reduced by €8.20 per
week (January 2010).

There is no change to the Respite Care Grant or the Domiciliary Care
Allowance.
