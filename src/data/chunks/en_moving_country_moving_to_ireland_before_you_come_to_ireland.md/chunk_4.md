##  Coming to Ireland to work

You may need an employment permit to work in Ireland, or permission to open a
business, if you are not in one of the categories below:

**UK citizens** can live and work in Ireland without restriction. If you have
family members who are from outside the UK, EEA or Switzerland, they must
apply to join you in Ireland.

**EEA and Swiss citizens** have the right to work or set up a business in
Ireland **.** You are entitled to be treated in the same way as Irish workers.
You can have [ family members ](/en/moving-country/moving-to-ireland/rights-
of-residence-in-ireland/residence-rights-eu-national/) come with you to
Ireland.

Younger people from certain countries outside the UK, EEA or Switzerland can
apply to come to Ireland on a [ working holiday ](/en/moving-country/working-
in-ireland/employment-permits/working-holiday-visa-in-Ireland/) .
