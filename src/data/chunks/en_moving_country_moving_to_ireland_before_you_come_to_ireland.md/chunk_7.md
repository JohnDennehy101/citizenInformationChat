###  Further information

You can get further information from your nearest [ Irish embassy or consulate
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/embassies/) or the [ embassy or consulate of
your country in Ireland ](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/embassies-in-ireland/)
.
