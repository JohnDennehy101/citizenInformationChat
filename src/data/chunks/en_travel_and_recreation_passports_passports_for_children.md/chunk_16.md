##  Useful contacts

You can [ order birth, marriage, civil partnership and adoption certificates
online ](https://www2.hse.ie/services/births-deaths-and-marriages/get-
certificates/ordering-certificates.html) from the HSE.

You can [ order a certificate of entry in the Register of Intercountry
Adoptions ](https://aai.gov.ie/en/who-we-are/inter-country/rica.html) from the
Adoption Authority.

Information on [ how to get a foreign birth registration certificate
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/citizenship/) is available on the Department
of Foreign Affairs website.

Treoir provides [ information on passports for children of unmarried parents
](http://www.treoir.ie/information-passports.php) on its website.

You can [ contact the Passport Service by phone or webchat
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/passports/contact-us/) .
