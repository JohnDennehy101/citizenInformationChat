##  How to apply for a verified MyGovID account

If you have a basic MyGovID account, you can upgrade it to a verified MyGovID
account.

You can [ apply online for a verified MyGovID account
](https://www.mygovid.ie/en-IE/HowDoISignUp) if you have a:

  * [ Public Services Card (PSC) ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/public-services-card/) and 
  * [ Personal Public Service (PPS) number ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/personal-public-service-number/)

You can also use the app and in-person options below.

**If you do not have a PSC** , you can apply:

  * Using the [ MyGovID app ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/f61b1-mygovid-app/)
  * [ In-person ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/fd52ab-public-services-card/#your-safe-registration-appointment) at a [ PSC Centre ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/be29c7-psc-online-registration-centres/)

**If you do not have a PPS number** , find out [ how to apply for a PPS number
](https://eur03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.citizensinformation.ie%2Fen%2Fsocial-
welfare%2Firish-social-welfare-system%2Fpersonal-public-service-
number%2F%23fffeb3&data=05%7C02%7CKen.Dempsey%40ciboard.ie%7Cda61fbc04cbe47c6d40d08dca0d629cb%7Cbb4e61ce39a3414abb5d6a0290c95fd9%7C0%7C0%7C638562088920744201%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=XKZa83CMBZtXd8BIw6g0X7mv8wx%2BF6yM%2Fwmn0PySh2M%3D&reserved=0)
.
