###  Countries outside the EU

Ireland has bilateral or ‘reciprocal’ agreements with [ some countries outside
of the EU ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-
welfare-system/claiming-a-social-welfare-payment/social-insurance-
contributions-from-abroad/#766531) . If you are sent to work temporarily in
one of these countries, you will continue to pay PRSI in Ireland for a certain
period of time.
