#  Farming and fishing

  * [ Farm Assist ](/en/social-welfare/farming-and-fishing/farm-assist/)

Farm Assist is an income support scheme for farmers. Find out about the means
test for this payment and the rules about who can qualify.

  * [ Rural Social Scheme ](/en/social-welfare/farming-and-fishing/rural-social-scheme/)

The Rural Social Scheme (RSS) provides income support for farmers and
fishermen/women who are getting certain social welfare payments.
