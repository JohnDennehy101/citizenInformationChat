###  Health services for people with disabilities  

Home support and personal assistance services are to be expanded. Additional
respite, day services and residential places are to be provided.  
  
Additional multi-disciplinary support to adults and children with
intellectual, physical and sensory disabilities (including those with autism)
are to be provided. There will be a focus on enhancing support and assessment
services for children with disabilities, in line with the Disability Act 2005.
