##  Renewing your passport in person

The counter service at the Passport Office is mainly designed to help people
who need their passports urgently, and donâ€™t have enough time to apply in
other ways. In these cases, you should book an appointment with the Passport
Office in Dublin or Cork. Information on the Urgent Appointment Service is
below.

**If you are living abroad** and unable to use Passport Online, you can apply
for an Irish passport in person, or using ordinary or registered post to your
[ nearest Irish embassy or consulate
](https://www.ireland.ie/en/dfa/embassies/) . You use form APS 2.

You cannot download the application form. You can get the application form
from the Irish embassy or consulate in the country where you live (or the
closest embassy or consulate).
