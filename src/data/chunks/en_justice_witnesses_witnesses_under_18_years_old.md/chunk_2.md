##  Introduction

Children and young people can be called to give evidence in cases at the [
District Court ](/en/justice/courts-system/district-court/) , the [ Circuit
Court ](/en/justice/courts-system/circuit-court/) and the [ Central Criminal
Court ](/en/justice/courts-system/high-court/) .

If you have been called to give evidence you may feel worried or anxious about
what the experience will be like. It may be helpful to understand the steps
involved in taking a case to court. The [ Courts Service
](https://www.courts.ie/) has published an [ information booklet (pdf)
](https://www.courts.ie/acc/alfresco/cda752dc-563f-44e6-9c50-75a051f3fcc6/Going
to Court Booklet - March 2012.pdf/pdf#view=fitH) and [ video
](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I1NKZZLAnL8) for young witnesses about going
to court.
