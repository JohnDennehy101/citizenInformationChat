##  How to apply

Following the death of a spouse, you should [ contact Revenue
](https://www.revenue.ie/en/contact-us/index.aspx) to get the appropriate tax
credits.  

You can claim tax credits online using Revenue's PAYE Services which is
accessed through Revenue's [ myAccount ](https://www.ros.ie/myaccount-
web/home.html) .
