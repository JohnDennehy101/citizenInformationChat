###  **Getting a payment before 2003**

If you were getting one of the payments listed above (except Working Family
Payment) before 2003, you may qualify to have your maintenance assessed using
the old rules.

See more detailed information about these rules and the [ assessment of
maintenance as means ](https://www.gov.ie/en/publication/11f23e-means-
assessment-guidelines/#assessment-of-maintenance) on gov.ie.
