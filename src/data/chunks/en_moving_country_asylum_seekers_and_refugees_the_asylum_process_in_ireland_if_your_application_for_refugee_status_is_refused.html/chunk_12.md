##  How to appeal

You should get legal advice before you appeal any of the recommendations or
decisions described above. You can get free [ legal aid ](/en/moving-
country/asylum-seekers-and-refugees/services-for-asylum-seekers-in-
ireland/legal-aid-for-asylum-seekers-in-ireland/) for making an appeal. You
should be aware of the time limits for appeals, set out below. You appeal to
IPAT on the relevant form, including all the information and documentation you
want to be considered as part of your appeal.

The table below tells you about the time limits and the form to use for each
of the appeal types you can make to IPAT.
