##  Tax relief on pension contributions

When you pay into your pension, your pension contributions get [ income tax
relief at the marginal rate ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/money-and-
tax/tax/income-tax/how-your-tax-is-calculated/#d43041) .

You do not get relief on the [ Universal Social Charge (USC) ](/en/money-and-
tax/tax/income-tax/universal-social-charge/) or [ Pay Related Social Insurance
(PRSI) ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-system/social-insurance-
prsi/) .
