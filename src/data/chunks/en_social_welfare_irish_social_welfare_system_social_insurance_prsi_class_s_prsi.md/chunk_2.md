##  What is Class S PRSI?

[ Pay Related Social Insurance (PRSI) ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-
welfare-system/social-insurance-prsi/social-insurance/) contributions are
deducted from your income and used to fund social welfare benefits and
pensions.

Your [ rate of PRSI depends on your social insurance class
](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/social-insurance-prsi/social-insurance-classes/#165581) .

If you are self-employed, you pay Class S PRSI. PRSI for self-employed people
was introduced in 1988.
