##  Mortgage arrears

[ If you are having difficulty paying your mortgage ](/en/housing/owning-a-
home/mortgage-arrears/overview-of-mortgage-debt/) , MABS can advise you on
your options.

MABS provides [ information on dealing with mortgage debt
](https://mabs.ie/mortgage-arrears/) .

MABS has [ Dedicated Mortgage Arrears (DMA) advisers
](https://www.mabs.ie/en/abhaile/financial-supports-and-advice/) to give
independent expert advice. DMA advisers can help you find out what type of
financial, insolvency or legal services you need and help you to access them.

MABS is also involved in providing free mortgage arrears support as part of
the [ Abhaile ](/en/housing/owning-a-home/mortgage-arrears/abhaile-mortgage-
arrears-support/) scheme. Abhaile offers [ financial and legal support and
advice ](https://mabs.ie/abhaile/about-abhaile/) .
