##  Visa fees

Standard non-refundable visa application processing fees are:

Single-journey visa: €60

Multiple-journey visa: €100

[ Certain applicants are not required to pay a fee
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/preclearance-and-entry-visas-fees/) . They
include non-EU Spouses of EU citizens. In addition, applicants from some
countries are not required to pay a fee. As this can change from time to time,
you should check with your local Irish embassy or consulate, or with the Visa
Office - see 'Where to apply for a visa'.

Nationals of the countries covered by the [ Short-stay Visa Waiver Programme
](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/coming-to-study-in-ireland/what-are-my-
study-visa-options/how-to-apply-for-a-short-c-visit-study-visa/) who are long-
term legal residents of the UK or the Schengen area will still require a visa
but will not have to pay the visa fee.
