###  Ábhar Gaolmhar

  * [ _Irish citizenship and passports for children born abroad_ ](/ga/returning-to-ireland/residency-and-citizenship/irish-citizenship-and-passports-for-children-born-abroad/)

Outlines the rules about Irish citizenship for children who were born abroad
and have an Irish parent. It also covers the documents you need when applying
for an Irish passport for children born abroad.

  * [ _Saoránacht d'Éirinn ó bhreith nó ó ghinealach_ ](/ga/moving-country/irish-citizenship/irish-citizenship-through-birth-or-descent/)

Ní hé an gnáthchúrsa é gur saoránach Éireannach tú má saolaíodh in oileán na
hÉireann tú. D'fhéadfá a bheith i dteideal saoránacht Éireannach dá
mb'Éireannaigh iad do thuismitheoir(í) nó do sheantuismitheoirí ach d'fhéadfá
an bhreith a chlárú.

  * [ _Coimisiún na hÉireann um Chearta an Duine agus Comhionannas_ ](/ga/justice/law-and-rights/irish-human-rights-and-equality-commission/)

Déanann an Coimisiún cearta an duine agus comhionannas a chur chun cinn agus a
chosaint. I roinnt cásanna, féadfaidh an Coimisiún cabhrú leat chun
saincheisteanna chearta an duine a chur chun cinn nó trí chabhrú leat le
himeachtaí dlí.
