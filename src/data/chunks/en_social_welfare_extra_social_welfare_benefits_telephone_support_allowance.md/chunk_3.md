##  How to qualify for Telephone Support Allowance

To qualify, you must be getting both the [ Living Alone Increase ](/en/social-
welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/living-alone-allowance/) and the [ Fuel
Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/extra-social-welfare-benefits/fuel-allowance/)
along with one of the following payments:

  * [ State Pension (Contributory) ](/en/social-welfare/older-and-retired-people/state-pension-contributory/)
  * [ State Pension (Non-Contributory) ](/en/social-welfare/older-and-retired-people/state-pension-non-contributory/)
  * [ Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner's (Contributory) Pension ](/en/social-welfare/death-related-benefits/widowed-persons-contrib-pension/)
  * Widow's, Widower's or Surviving Civil Partner's Pension under the [ Occupational Injuries Benefit Scheme ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/occupational-injuries-benefit-overview/)
  * [ Invalidity Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/invalidity-pension/)
  * [ Disability Allowance ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/disability-allowance/)
  * [ Blind Pension ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/blind-persons-pension/)
  * [ Disablement Pension (Incapacity Supplement) ](/en/social-welfare/disability-and-illness/disablement-benefit/)
  * [ Deserted Wife's Benefit ](/en/social-welfare/families-and-children/deserted-wife-s-payments/)

You can read more about the [ Telephone Support Allowance
](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/4d2773-telephone-support-
allowance/?referrer=https://www.welfare.ie/en/Pages/Telephone-Support-
Allowance.aspx) on Gov.ie.
