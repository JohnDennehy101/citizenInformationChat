####  Countries not covered by EU regulations or bilateral agreements

You must continue to make Irish PRSI contributions for up to 12 months if your
employer sends you to work temporarily in a country that:

  * Is not covered by EU Regulations, or 
  * Does not have a bilateral security agreement with Ireland 

If your employment abroad lasts longer than 12 months, you may be able to stay
in the Irish PRSI system for longer than 12 months.

If you are working abroad and are not in the Irish PRSI system, you can choose
to pay [ voluntary contributions ](/en/social-welfare/irish-social-welfare-
system/social-insurance-prsi/voluntary-prsi-contributions/) .
