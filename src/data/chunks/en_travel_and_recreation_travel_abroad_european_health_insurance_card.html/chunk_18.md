###  If you are living in Ireland

  * If you are an Irish citizen, an Irish pensioner or an EU citizen living in Ireland you can apply for an EHIC: 
  * In person at your [ local health office ](https://www2.hse.ie/services/ehic/contact-a-european-health-insurance-card-ehic-office.html)
  * By post 
  * [ Online ](https://www.sspcrs.ie/portal/ehic/pub) \- if you have a [ medical card ](/en/health/medical-cards-and-gp-visit-cards/medical-card/) or drug payment scheme card and you live in Ireland 

You will be asked for proof that you live in Ireland. Your card will be posted
out to you within approximately 10 working days.
