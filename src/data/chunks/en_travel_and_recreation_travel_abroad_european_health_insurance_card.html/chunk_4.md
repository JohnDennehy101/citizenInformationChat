###  You can apply:

  * **In person** : at any local health office except, Dublin North West, Cork North and South Lee 
  * **By post** : only if you live in Dublin North West, Dublin Cork North and South Lee. [ Print out an application form ](https://assets.hse.ie/media/documents/ehic-application-form-english_d57LSel.pdf) and post it along with any documents required 
  * **Online:** [ Apply through the HSE website ](https://www.sspcrs.ie/portal/ehic/pub/application) if you have a medical card or drug payment scheme card and you live in Ireland.
