####  Measures to be introduced in 2023/24 academic year:

The **income threshold** for the 50% student contribution grant will increase
from €55,240 to €62,000 per year (from 1 September 2023)

The **student contribution** will be reduced by €500 for families earning
between €62,000 and €100,000 (from 1 September 2023).

The **Postgraduate SUSI fee grant** will increase by €500.
