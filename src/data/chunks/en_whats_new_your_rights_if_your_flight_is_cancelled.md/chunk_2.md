#####  News

✈️🚫Worried about your flight getting cancelled? Make sure you know your
rights.🚫✈️

You have a choice of:

✔️ A refund within 7 days

✔️ Re-routing as soon as possible

✔️ Re-routing at a later date that suits you

If your flight is part of a package holiday, you have extra protections
covering the rest of your trip.

You can read about when compensation is and is not available.

Stay informed and make sure you get what you're entitled to 👉 [ when your
flight has been cancelled ](https://www.citizensinformation.ie/en/travel-and-
recreation/air-travel/compensation-for-overbooked-and-delayed-flights/#93e801)
.
