###  How can I find out about Local Link services in my area?

You can find out about services in your area by:

  * Checking the [ Local Link timetables ](https://www.transportforireland.ie/tfi-local-link/#door-to-door-bus-services-operating-in-your-area-header)
  * Contacting your nearest [ Local Link Office ](https://uat.transportforireland.ie/support/your-tfi-local-link-office/)
  * Looking at the [ TFI Journey Planner ](https://www.transportforireland.ie/plan-a-journey/)
