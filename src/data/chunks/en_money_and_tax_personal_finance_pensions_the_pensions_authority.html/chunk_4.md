##  Pension information services

The Pensions Authority website, [ pensionsauthority.ie
](https://www.pensionsauthority.ie/en/) , provides [ information on
understanding your pension
](https://www.pensionsauthority.ie/en/lifecycle/welcome.html) . It has useful
resources including a [ pension calculator
](https://www.pensionsauthority.ie/en/Calculators/Pension_Calculator/) , [
budget planner ](https://www.pensionsauthority.ie/en/LifeCycle/Useful-
Resources/Budget-Planner/) , [ investment risk profiler
](https://www.pensionsauthority.ie/en/LifeCycle/Useful-
Resources/Investment_Risk_profiler/) and [ a glossary of pension terms
](https://www.pensionsauthority.ie/en/I_want_to_start_a_Pension_PRSA/Glossary_of_terms/)
. You can select options that match your general circumstances to get relevant
information for your situation.
